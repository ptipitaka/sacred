<Paragraph type="center" book="pthi">
## 481\. Kosohitavatthaguyhalakkhaṇaṃ
</Paragraph>

<Division number="220" book="pthi" bookNo="8" page="131">

<Paragraph number="482" book="pthi">
“Yampi, bhikkhave, tathāgato purimaṃ jātiṃ purimaṃ bhavaṃ purimaṃ niketaṃ pubbe manussabhūto samāno cirappanaṭṭhe sucirappavāsino ñātimitte suhajje sakhino samānetā ahosi. Mātarampi puttena samānetā ahosi, puttampi mātarā samānetā ahosi, pitarampi puttena samānetā ahosi, puttampi pitarā samānetā ahosi, bhātarampi bhātarā samānetā ahosi, bhātarampi bhaginiyā samānetā ahosi, bhaginimpi bhātarā samānetā ahosi, bhaginimpi bhaginiyā samānetā ahosi, samaṅgīkatvā ca abbhanumoditā ahosi. So tassa kammassa kaṭattā …pe…  so tato cuto itthattaṃ āgato samāno imaṃ mahāpurisalakkhaṇaṃ paṭilabhati—  kosohitavatthaguyho hoti.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="483" book="pthi">
So tena lakkhaṇena samannāgato sace agāraṃ ajjhāvasati, rājā hoti cakkavattī …pe…  rājā samāno kiṃ labhati? Pahūtaputto hoti, parosahassaṃ kho panassa puttā bhavanti sūrā vīraṅgarūpā parasenappamaddanā. Rājā samāno idaṃ labhati …pe…  buddho samāno kiṃ labhati? Pahūtaputto hoti, anekasahassaṃ kho panassa puttā bhavanti sūrā vīraṅgarūpā parasenappamaddanā. Buddho samāno idaṃ labhati”. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca.
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="221" book="pthi" bookNo="8" page="132">

<Paragraph number="484" book="pthi">
Tatthetaṃ vuccati—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="485" type="verses" book="pthi">
_“Pure puratthā purimāsu jātisu,_  
_Cirappanaṭṭhe sucirappavāsino;_  
_Ñātī suhajje sakhino samānayi,_  
_Samaṅgikatvā anumoditā ahu._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="486" type="verses" book="pthi">
_So tena kammena divaṃ samakkami,_  
_Sukhañca khiḍḍāratiyo ca anvabhi;_  
_Tato cavitvā punarāgato idha,_  
_Kosohitaṃ vindati vatthachādiyaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="487" type="verses" book="pthi">
_Pahūtaputto bhavatī tathāvidho,_  
_Parosahassañca bhavanti atrajā;_  
_Sūrā ca vīrā ca amittatāpanā,_  
_Gihissa pītiṃ jananā piyaṃvadā._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="488" type="verses" book="pthi">
_Bahūtarā pabbajitassa iriyato,_  
_Bhavanti puttā vacanānusārino;_  
_Gihissa vā pabbajitassa vā puna,_  
_Taṃ lakkhaṇaṃ jāyati tadatthajotakan”ti._  


</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="489" type="center" book="pthi">
Paṭhamabhāṇavāro niṭṭhito.
</Paragraph>
