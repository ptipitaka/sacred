<Paragraph number="773" book="pthi">
Evaṃ me sutaṃ—  ekaṃ samayaṃ bhagavā mallesu cārikaṃ caramāno mahatā bhikkhusaṃghena saddhiṃ pañcamattehi bhikkhusatehi yena pāvā nāma mallānaṃ nagaraṃ tadavasari. Tatra sudaṃ bhagavā pāvāyaṃ viharati cundassa kammāraputtassa ambavane.

</Paragraph>
