<Paragraph number="474" book="a8">
Ekaṃ samayaṃ bhagavā bhaggesu viharati susumāragire bhesakaḷāvane migadāye. Atha kho nakulamātā gahapatānī yena bhagavā tenupasaṅkami; upasaṅkamitvā …pe… . Ekamantaṃ nisinnaṃ kho nakulamātaraṃ gahapatāniṃ bhagavā etadavoca—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="475" book="a8">
“Aṭṭhahi kho, nakulamāte, dhammehi samannāgato mātugāmo kāyassa bhedā paraṃ maraṇā manāpakāyikānaṃ devānaṃ sahabyataṃ upapajjati. Katamehi aṭṭhahi? Idha, nakulamāte, mātugāmo yassa mātāpitaro bhattuno denti atthakāmā hitesino anukampakā anukampaṃ upādāya tassa hoti pubbuṭṭhāyinī pacchānipātinī kiṃkārapaṭissāvinī manāpacārinī piyavādinī. (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="476" book="a8">
Ye te bhattu garuno honti—  mātāti vā pitāti vā samaṇabrāhmaṇāti vā—  te sakkaroti garuṃ karoti māneti pūjeti, abbhāgate ca āsanodakena paṭipūjeti. (2)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="477" book="a8">
Ye te bhattu abbhantarā kammantā—  uṇṇāti vā kappāsāti vā—  tattha dakkhā hoti analasā tatrupāyāya vīmaṃsāya samannāgatā alaṃ kātuṃ alaṃ saṃvidhātuṃ. (3)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="478" book="a8">
Yo so bhattu abbhantaro antojano—  dāsāti vā pessāti vā kammakarāti vā—  tesaṃ katañca katato jānāti akatañca akatato jānāti, gilānakānañca balābalaṃ jānāti khādanīyaṃ bhojanīyañcassa paccaṃsena saṃvibhajati. (4)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="479" book="a8">
Yaṃ bhattā āharati dhanaṃ vā dhaññaṃ vā rajataṃ vā jātarūpaṃ vā taṃ ārakkhena guttiyā sampādeti, tattha ca hoti adhuttī athenī asoṇḍī avināsikā. (5)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="480" book="a8">
Upāsikā kho pana hoti buddhaṃ saraṇaṃ gatā dhammaṃ saraṇaṃ gatā saṃghaṃ saraṇaṃ gatā. (6)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="481" book="a8">
Sīlavatī kho pana hoti—  pāṇātipātā paṭiviratā …pe…  surāmerayamajjapamādaṭṭhānā paṭiviratā …pe… . (7)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="482" book="a8">
Cāgavatī kho pana hoti vigatamalamaccherena cetasā agāraṃ ajjhāvasati muttacāgā payatapāṇinī vossaggaratā yācayogā dānasaṃvibhāgaratā. (8)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="483" book="a8">
Imehi kho, nakulamāte, aṭṭhahi dhammehi samannāgato mātugāmo kāyassa bhedā paraṃ maraṇā manāpakāyikānaṃ devānaṃ sahabyataṃ upapajjatīti.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="484" type="verses" book="a8">
_Yo naṃ bharati sabbadā,_  
_niccaṃ ātāpi ussuko;_  
_Taṃ sabbakāmadaṃ posaṃ,_  
_bhattāraṃ nātimaññati._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="485" type="verses" book="a8">
_Na cāpi sotthi bhattāraṃ,_  
_issāvādena rosaye;_  
_Bhattu ca garuno sabbe,_  
_paṭipūjeti paṇḍitā._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="486" type="verses" book="a8">
_Uṭṭhāhikā analasā,_  
_saṅgahitaparijjanā;_  
_Bhattu manāpaṃ carati,_  
_sambhataṃ anurakkhati._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="487" type="verses" book="a8">
_Yā evaṃ vattati nārī,_  
_bhattu chandavasānugā;_  
_Manāpā nāma te devā,_  
_yattha sā upapajjatī”ti._  


</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="488" type="center" book="a8">
Aṭṭhamaṃ.
</Paragraph>
