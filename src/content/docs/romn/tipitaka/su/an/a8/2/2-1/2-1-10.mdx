<Paragraph number="614" book="a8">
“Aṭṭhime, bhikkhave, puggalā āhuneyyā …pe…  anuttaraṃ puññakkhettaṃ lokassa. Katame aṭṭha? Sotāpanno, sotāpattiphalasacchikiriyāya paṭipanno …pe…  arahā, arahattāya paṭipanno. Ime kho, bhikkhave, aṭṭha puggalā āhuneyyā …pe…  anuttaraṃ puññakkhettaṃ lokassāti.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="615" type="verses" book="a8">
_Cattāro ca paṭipannā,_  
_cattāro ca phale ṭhitā;_  
_Esa saṃgho samukkaṭṭho,_  
_sattānaṃ aṭṭha puggalā._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="616" type="verses" book="a8">
_Yajamānānaṃ manussānaṃ,_  
_puññapekkhāna pāṇinaṃ;_  
_Karotaṃ opadhikaṃ puññaṃ,_  
_ettha dinnaṃ mahapphalan”ti._  


</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="617" type="center" book="a8">
Dasamaṃ.



</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="618" type="center" book="a8">
Gotamīvaggo paṭhamo.



</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="619" type="center" book="a8">
Tassuddānaṃ


</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="620" type="verses" book="a8">
_Gotamī ovādaṃ saṃkhittaṃ,_  
_dīghajāṇu ca ujjayo;_  
_Bhayā dve āhuneyyā ca,_  
_dve ca aṭṭha puggalāti._
</Paragraph>
