<Paragraph number="745" book="a8">
“Aṭṭhimā, bhikkhave, sampadā. Katamā aṭṭha? Uṭṭhānasampadā, ārakkhasampadā, kalyāṇamittatā, samajīvitā, saddhāsampadā, sīlasampadā, cāgasampadā, paññāsampadā—  imā kho, bhikkhave, aṭṭha sampadāti.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="746" type="verses" book="a8">
_Uṭṭhātā kammadheyyesu,_  
_appamatto vidhānavā;_  
_Samaṃ kappeti jīvikaṃ,_  
_sambhataṃ anurakkhati._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="747" type="verses" book="a8">
_Saddho sīlena sampanno,_  
_vadaññū vītamaccharo;_  
_Niccaṃ maggaṃ visodheti,_  
_sotthānaṃ samparāyikaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="748" type="verses" book="a8">
_Iccete aṭṭha dhammā ca,_  
_saddhassa gharamesino;_  
_Akkhātā saccanāmena,_  
_ubhayattha sukhāvahā._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="749" type="verses" book="a8">
_Diṭṭhadhammahitatthāya,_  
_samparāyasukhāya ca;_  
_Evametaṃ gahaṭṭhānaṃ,_  
_cāgo puññaṃ pavaḍḍhatī”ti._  


</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="750" type="center" book="a8">
Pañcamaṃ.
</Paragraph>
