<Paragraph number="1048" book="a10">
“Orimañca, bhikkhave, tīraṃ desessāmi pārimañca tīraṃ. Taṃ suṇātha, sādhukaṃ manasi karotha, bhāsissāmī”ti. “Evaṃ, bhante”ti kho te bhikkhū bhagavato paccassosuṃ. Bhagavā etadavoca—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1049" book="a10">
“Katamañca, bhikkhave, orimaṃ tīraṃ, katamañca pārimaṃ tīraṃ? Micchādiṭṭhi orimaṃ tīraṃ, sammādiṭṭhi pārimaṃ tīraṃ …pe…  micchāvimutti orimaṃ tīraṃ, sammāvimutti pārimaṃ tīraṃ. Idaṃ kho, bhikkhave, orimaṃ tīraṃ, idaṃ pārimaṃ tīranti.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1050" type="verses" book="a10">
_Appakā te manussesu,_  
_ye janā pāragāmino;_  
_Athāyaṃ itarā pajā,_  
_tīramevānudhāvati._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1051" type="verses" book="a10">
_Ye ca kho sammadakkhāte,_  
_dhamme dhammānuvattino;_  
_Te janā pāramessanti,_  
_maccudheyyaṃ suduttaraṃ._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1052" type="verses" book="a10">
_Kaṇhaṃ dhammaṃ vippahāya,_  
_sukkaṃ bhāvetha paṇḍito;_  
_Okā anokamāgamma,_  
_viveke yattha dūramaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1053" type="verses" book="a10">
_Tatrābhiratimiccheyya,_  
_hitvā kāme akiñcano;_  
_Pariyodapeyya attānaṃ,_  
_cittaklesehi paṇḍito._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1054" type="verses" book="a10">
_Yesaṃ sambodhiyaṅgesu,_  
_sammā cittaṃ subhāvitaṃ;_  
_Ādānapaṭinissagge,_  
_anupādāya ye ratā;_  
_Khīṇāsavā jutimanto,_  
_te loke parinibbutā”ti._  


</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="1055" type="center" book="a10">
Chaṭṭhaṃ.
</Paragraph>
