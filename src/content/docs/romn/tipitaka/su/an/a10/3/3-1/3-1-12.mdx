<Paragraph number="981" book="a10">
“Dasayime, bhikkhave, asekhiyā dhammā. Katame dasa? Asekhā sammādiṭṭhi, asekho sammāsaṅkappo, asekhā sammāvācā, asekho sammākammanto, asekho sammāājīvo, asekho sammāvāyāmo, asekhā sammāsati, asekho sammāsamādhi, asekhaṃ sammāñāṇaṃ, asekhā sammāvimutti—  ime kho, bhikkhave, dasa asekhiyā dhammā”ti.

</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="982" type="center" book="a10">
Dvādasamaṃ.



</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="983" type="center" book="a10">
Samaṇasaññāvaggo paṭhamo.



</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="984" type="center" book="a10">
Tassuddānaṃ


</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="985" type="verses" book="a10">
_Saññā bojjhaṅgā micchattaṃ,_  
_bījaṃ vijjāya nijjaraṃ;_  
_Dhovanaṃ tikicchā vamanaṃ,_  
_niddhamanaṃ dve asekhāti._
</Paragraph>
