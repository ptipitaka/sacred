<Paragraph number="25" book="a7">
“Sattimāni, bhikkhave, dhanāni. Katamāni satta? Saddhādhanaṃ, sīladhanaṃ, hirīdhanaṃ, ottappadhanaṃ, sutadhanaṃ, cāgadhanaṃ, paññādhanaṃ. Imāni kho, bhikkhave, satta dhanānīti.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="26" type="verses" book="a7">
_Saddhādhanaṃ sīladhanaṃ,_  
_hirī ottappiyaṃ dhanaṃ;_  
_Sutadhanañca cāgo ca,_  
_paññā ve sattamaṃ dhanaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="27" type="verses" book="a7">
_Yassa ete dhanā atthi,_  
_itthiyā purisassa vā;_  
_Adaliddoti taṃ āhu,_  
_amoghaṃ tassa jīvitaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="28" type="verses" book="a7">
_Tasmā saddhañca sīlañca,_  
_Pasādaṃ dhammadassanaṃ;_  
_Anuyuñjetha medhāvī,_  
_Saraṃ buddhāna sāsanan”ti._  


</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="29" type="center" book="a7">
Pañcamaṃ.
</Paragraph>
