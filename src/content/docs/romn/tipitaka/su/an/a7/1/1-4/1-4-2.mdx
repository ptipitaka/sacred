<Paragraph number="180" book="a7">
“Imaṃ, bhikkhave, rattiṃ aññatarā devatā abhikkantāya rattiyā abhikkantavaṇṇā kevalakappaṃ jetavanaṃ obhāsetvā yenāhaṃ tenupasaṅkami; upasaṅkamitvā maṃ abhivādetvā ekamantaṃ aṭṭhāsi. Ekamantaṃ ṭhitā kho, bhikkhave, sā devatā maṃ etadavoca—  ‘sattime, bhante, dhammā bhikkhuno aparihānāya saṃvattanti. Katame satta? Satthugāravatā, dhammagāravatā, saṃghagāravatā, sikkhāgāravatā, samādhigāravatā, hirigāravatā, ottappagāravatā. Ime kho, bhante, satta dhammā bhikkhuno aparihānāya saṃvattantī’ti. Idamavoca, bhikkhave, sā devatā. Idaṃ vatvā maṃ abhivādetvā padakkhiṇaṃ katvā tatthevantaradhāyīti.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="181" type="verses" book="a7">
_Satthugaru dhammagaru,_  
_saṃghe ca tibbagāravo;_  
_Samādhigaru ātāpī,_  
_sikkhāya tibbagāravo._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="182" type="verses" book="a7">
_Hiriottappasampanno,_  
_sappatisso sagāravo;_  
_Abhabbo parihānāya,_  
_nibbānasseva santike”ti._  


</Paragraph>
