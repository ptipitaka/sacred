<Paragraph number="9" book="a7">
Evaṃ me sutaṃ—  ekaṃ samayaṃ bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme …pe…  sattimāni, bhikkhave, balāni. Katamāni satta? Saddhābalaṃ, vīriyabalaṃ, hirībalaṃ, ottappabalaṃ, satibalaṃ, samādhibalaṃ, paññābalaṃ. Imāni kho, bhikkhave, satta balānīti.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="10" type="verses" book="a7">
_Saddhābalaṃ vīriyañca,_  
_hirī ottappiyaṃ balaṃ;_  
_Satibalaṃ samādhi ca,_  
_paññā ve sattamaṃ balaṃ;_  
_Etehi balavā bhikkhu,_  
_sukhaṃ jīvati paṇḍito._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="11" type="verses" book="a7">
_Yoniso vicine dhammaṃ,_  
_paññāyatthaṃ vipassati;_  
_Pajjotasseva nibbānaṃ,_  
_vimokkho hoti cetaso”ti._  


</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="12" type="center" book="a7">
Tatiyaṃ.
</Paragraph>
