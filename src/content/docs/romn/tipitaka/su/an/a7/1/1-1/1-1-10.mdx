<Paragraph number="53" book="a7">
“Sattimāni, bhikkhave, saṃyojanāni. Katamāni satta? Anunayasaṃyojanaṃ, paṭighasaṃyojanaṃ, diṭṭhisaṃyojanaṃ, vicikicchāsaṃyojanaṃ, mānasaṃyojanaṃ, issāsaṃyojanaṃ, macchariyasaṃyojanaṃ. Imāni kho, bhikkhave, satta saṃyojanānī”ti.

</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="54" type="center" book="a7">
Dasamaṃ.



</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="55" type="center" book="a7">
Dhanavaggo paṭhamo.



</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="56" type="center" book="a7">
Tassuddānaṃ


</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="57" type="verses" book="a7">
_Dve piyāni balaṃ dhanaṃ,_  
_Saṃkhittañceva vitthataṃ;_  
_Uggaṃ saṃyojanañceva,_  
_Pahānaṃ macchariyena cāti._
</Paragraph>
