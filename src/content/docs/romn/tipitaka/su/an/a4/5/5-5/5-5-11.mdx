<Paragraph number="1516" book="a4">
“Cattārome, bhikkhave, ariyavohārā. Katame cattāro? Diṭṭhe diṭṭhavāditā, sute sutavāditā, mute mutavāditā, viññāte viññātavāditā—  ime kho, bhikkhave, cattāro ariyavohārā”ti.

</Paragraph>

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1518" type="center" book="a4">

</Paragraph>

Tassuddānaṃ


</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1520" type="verses" book="a4">

_seyyā thūpārahena ca;_  
_Paññāvuddhi bahukārā,_  
_vohārā caturo ṭhitāti._  


</Paragraph>
