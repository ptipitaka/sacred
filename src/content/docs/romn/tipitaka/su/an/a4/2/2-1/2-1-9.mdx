<Paragraph number="427" book="a4">
“Bhojanaṃ, bhikkhave, dadamāno dāyako paṭiggāhakānaṃ cattāri ṭhānāni deti. Katamāni cattāri? Āyuṃ deti, vaṇṇaṃ deti, sukhaṃ deti, balaṃ deti. Āyuṃ kho pana datvā āyussa bhāgī hoti dibbassa vā mānusassa vā. Vaṇṇaṃ datvā…  sukhaṃ datvā…  balaṃ datvā balassa bhāgī hoti dibbassa vā mānusassa vā. Bhojanaṃ, bhikkhave, dadamāno dāyako paṭiggāhakānaṃ imāni cattāri ṭhānāni detīti.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="428" type="verses" book="a4">
_Yo saññatānaṃ paradattabhojinaṃ,_  
_Kālena sakkacca dadāti bhojanaṃ;_  
_Cattāri ṭhānāni anuppavecchati,_  
_Āyuñca vaṇṇañca sukhaṃ balañca._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="429" type="verses" book="a4">
_So āyudāyī vaṇṇadāyī,_  
_sukhaṃ balaṃ dado naro;_  
_Dīghāyu yasavā hoti,_  
_yattha yatthūpapajjatī”ti._  


</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="430" type="center" book="a4">
Navamaṃ.
</Paragraph>
