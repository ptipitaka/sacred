<Paragraph number="328" book="a4">
“Cattārimāni, bhikkhave, suvidūravidūrāni. Katamāni cattāri? Nabhañca, bhikkhave, pathavī ca; idaṃ paṭhamaṃ suvidūravidūre. Orimañca, bhikkhave, tīraṃ samuddassa pārimañca; idaṃ dutiyaṃ suvidūravidūre. Yato ca, bhikkhave, verocano abbhudeti yattha ca atthameti; idaṃ tatiyaṃ suvidūravidūre. Satañca, bhikkhave, dhammo asatañca dhammo; idaṃ catutthaṃ suvidūravidūre. Imāni kho, bhikkhave, cattāri suvidūravidūrānīti.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="329" type="verses" book="a4">
_Nabhañca dūre pathavī ca dūre,_  
_Pāraṃ samuddassa tadāhu dūre;_  
_Yato ca verocano abbhudeti,_  
_Pabhaṅkaro yattha ca atthameti;_  
_Tato have dūrataraṃ vadanti,_  
_Satañca dhammaṃ asatañca dhammaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="330" type="verses" book="a4">
_Abyāyiko hoti sataṃ samāgamo,_  
_Yāvāpi tiṭṭheyya tatheva hoti;_  
_Khippañhi veti asataṃ samāgamo,_  
_Tasmā sataṃ dhammo asabbhi ārakā”ti._  


</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="331" type="center" book="a4">
Sattamaṃ.
</Paragraph>
