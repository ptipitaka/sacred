“Cattārome, bhikkhave, puggalā santo saṃvijjamānā lokasmiṃ. Katame cattāro? Ugghaṭitaññū, vipañcitaññū, neyyo, padaparamo—  ime kho, bhikkhave, cattāro puggalā santo saṃvijjamānā lokasmin”ti.
