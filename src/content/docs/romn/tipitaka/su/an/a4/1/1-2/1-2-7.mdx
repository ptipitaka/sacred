<Paragraph number="112" book="a4">
“Cattārimāni, bhikkhave, agatigamanāni. Katamāni cattāri? Chandāgatiṃ gacchati, dosāgatiṃ gacchati, mohāgatiṃ gacchati, bhayāgatiṃ gacchati—  imāni kho, bhikkhave, cattāri agatigamanānīti.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="113" type="verses" book="a4">
_Chandā dosā bhayā mohā,_  
_yo dhammaṃ ativattati;_  
_Nihīyati tassa yaso,_  
_kāḷapakkheva candimā”ti._  


</Paragraph>
</Division>
