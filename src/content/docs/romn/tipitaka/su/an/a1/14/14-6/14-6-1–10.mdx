<Paragraph number="336" book="a1">
“Etadaggaṃ, bhikkhave, mama sāvakānaṃ upāsakānaṃ paṭhamaṃ saraṇaṃ gacchantānaṃ yadidaṃ tapussabhallikā vāṇijā. (1)
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="249" book="a1" bookNo="15" page="27">

<Paragraph number="337" book="a1">
…  Dāyakānaṃ yadidaṃ sudatto gahapati anāthapiṇḍiko. (2)
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="250" book="a1" bookNo="15" page="27">

<Paragraph number="338" book="a1">
…  Dhammakathikānaṃ yadidaṃ citto gahapati macchikāsaṇḍiko. (3)
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="251" book="a1" bookNo="15" page="27">

<Paragraph number="339" book="a1">
…  Catūhi saṅgahavatthūhi parisaṃ saṅgaṇhantānaṃ yadidaṃ hatthako āḷavako. (4)
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="252" book="a1" bookNo="15" page="27">

<Paragraph number="340" book="a1">
…  Paṇītadāyakānaṃ yadidaṃ mahānāmo sakko. (5)
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="253" book="a1" bookNo="15" page="27">

<Paragraph number="341" book="a1">
…  Manāpadāyakānaṃ yadidaṃ uggo gahapati vesāliko. (6)
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="254" book="a1" bookNo="15" page="27">

<Paragraph number="342" book="a1">
…  Saṃghupaṭṭhākānaṃ yadidaṃ hatthigāmako uggato gahapati. (7)
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="255" book="a1" bookNo="15" page="27">

<Paragraph number="343" book="a1">
…  Aveccappasannānaṃ yadidaṃ sūrambaṭṭho. (8)
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="256" book="a1" bookNo="15" page="27">

<Paragraph number="344" book="a1">
…  Puggalappasannānaṃ yadidaṃ jīvako komārabhacco. (9)
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="257" book="a1" bookNo="15" page="27">

<Paragraph number="345" book="a1">

</Paragraph>
