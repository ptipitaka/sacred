“Aṭṭhānametaṃ, bhikkhave, anavakāso yaṃ diṭṭhisampanno puggalo mātaraṃ jīvitā voropeyya. Netaṃ ṭhānaṃ vijjati. Ṭhānañca kho, bhikkhave, vijjati yaṃ puthujjano mātaraṃ jīvitā voropeyya. Ṭhānametaṃ vijjatī”ti.
