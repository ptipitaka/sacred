<Paragraph number="282" book="a3">
“Tīṇimāni, bhikkhave, paṇḍitapaññattāni sappurisapaññattāni. Katamāni tīṇi? Dānaṃ, bhikkhave, paṇḍitapaññattaṃ sappurisapaññattaṃ. Pabbajjā, bhikkhave, paṇḍitapaññattā sappurisapaññattā. Mātāpitūnaṃ, bhikkhave, upaṭṭhānaṃ paṇḍitapaññattaṃ sappurisapaññattaṃ. Imāni kho, bhikkhave, tīṇi paṇḍitapaññattāni sappurisapaññattānīti.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="283" type="verses" book="a3">
_Sabbhi dānaṃ upaññattaṃ,_  
_ahiṃsā saṃyamo damo;_  
_Mātāpitu upaṭṭhānaṃ,_  
_santānaṃ brahmacārinaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="284" type="verses" book="a3">
_Sataṃ etāni ṭhānāni,_  
_yāni sevetha paṇḍito;_  
_Ariyo dassanasampanno,_  
_sa lokaṃ bhajate sivan”ti._  


</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="285" type="center" book="a3">
Pañcamaṃ.
