<Paragraph number="2160" book="sn-maha">
“…  Nibbedhikapaññatāya saṃvattanti. Katame cattāro? Sappurisasaṃsevo, saddhammassavanaṃ, yonisomanasikāro, dhammānudhammappaṭipatti—  ime kho, bhikkhave, cattāro dhammā bhāvitā bahulīkatā nibbedhikapaññatāya saṃvattantī”ti.

</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="2161" type="center" book="sn-maha">
Terasamaṃ.



</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2162" type="center" book="sn-maha">
Mahāpaññavaggo sattamo.



</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2163" type="center" book="sn-maha">
Tassuddānaṃ


</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2164" type="verses" book="sn-maha">
_Mahā puthu vipula gambhīraṃ,_  
_Appamatta bhūri bāhullaṃ;_  
_Sīgha lahu hāsa javana,_  
_Tikkha nibbedhikāya cāti._  


</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2165" type="center" book="sn-maha">
Sotāpattisaṃyuttaṃ ekādasamaṃ.
</Paragraph>
