<Paragraph number="714" book="sn-maha">
“Tisso imā, bhikkhave, esanā. Katamā tisso? Kāmesanā, bhavesanā, brahmacariyesanā”ti vitthāretabbaṃ.

<Paragraph number="715" type="center" book="sn-maha">

</Paragraph>

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="717" type="verses" book="sn-maha">
_Esanā vidhā āsavo,_  
_Bhavo ca dukkhatā tisso;_  
_Khilaṃ malañca nīgho ca,_  
_Vedanā taṇhā tasināya cāti._  

</Paragraph>
