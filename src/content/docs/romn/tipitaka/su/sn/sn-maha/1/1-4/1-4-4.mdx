<Paragraph number="123" book="sn-maha">
Sāvatthinidānaṃ. “Aṭṭhime, bhikkhave, dhammā bhāvitā bahulīkatā apārā pāraṃ gamanāya saṃvattanti. Katame aṭṭha? Seyyathidaṃ—  sammādiṭṭhi …pe…  sammāsamādhi. Ime kho, bhikkhave, aṭṭha dhammā bhāvitā bahulīkatā apārā pāraṃ gamanāya saṃvattantī”ti.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="124" book="sn-maha">
Idamavoca bhagavā. Idaṃ vatvāna sugato athāparaṃ etadavoca satthā—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="125" type="verses" book="sn-maha">
_“Appakā te manussesu,_  
_ye janā pāragāmino;_  
_Athāyaṃ itarā pajā,_  
_tīramevānudhāvati._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="126" type="verses" book="sn-maha">
_Ye ca kho sammadakkhāte,_  
_dhamme dhammānuvattino;_  
_Te janā pāramessanti,_  
_maccudheyyaṃ suduttaraṃ._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="127" type="verses" book="sn-maha">
_Kaṇhaṃ dhammaṃ vippahāya,_  
_sukkaṃ bhāvetha paṇḍito;_  
_Okā anokamāgamma,_  
_viveke yattha dūramaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="128" type="verses" book="sn-maha">
_Tatrābhiratimiccheyya,_  
_hitvā kāme akiñcano;_  
_Pariyodapeyya attānaṃ,_  
_cittaklesehi paṇḍito._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="129" type="verses" book="sn-maha">
_Yesaṃ sambodhiyaṅgesu,_  
_sammā cittaṃ subhāvitaṃ;_  
_Ādānapaṭinissagge,_  
_anupādāya ye ratā;_  
_Khīṇāsavā jutimanto,_  
_te loke parinibbutā”ti._  


</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="130" type="center" book="sn-maha">
Catutthaṃ.
</Paragraph>
