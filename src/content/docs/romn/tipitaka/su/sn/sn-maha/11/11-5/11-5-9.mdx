<Paragraph number="2083" book="sn-maha">
Kapilavatthunidānaṃ. Ekamantaṃ nisinnaṃ kho mahānāmaṃ sakkaṃ bhagavā etadavoca—  “catūhi kho, mahānāma, dhammehi samannāgato ariyasāvako sotāpanno hoti …pe…  sambodhiparāyaṇo.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2084" book="sn-maha">
Katamehi catūhi? Idha, mahānāma, ariyasāvako buddhe …pe…  dhamme …pe…  saṃghe …pe…  ariyakantehi sīlehi samannāgato hoti akhaṇḍehi …pe…  samādhisaṃvattanikehi. Imehi kho, mahānāma, catūhi dhammehi samannāgato ariyasāvako sotāpanno hoti avinipātadhammo niyato sambodhiparāyaṇo”ti.

</Paragraph>
</Division>

Navamaṃ.
