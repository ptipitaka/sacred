<Division number="64" book="khan">

<Paragraph number="344" book="khan">
Sāvatthinidānaṃ. Atha kho aññataro bhikkhu …pe…  ekamantaṃ nisinno kho so bhikkhu bhagavantaṃ etadavoca—  “sādhu me, bhante, bhagavā saṃkhittena dhammaṃ desetu …pe…  ātāpī pahitatto vihareyyan”ti. “Maññamāno kho, bhikkhu, baddho mārassa; amaññamāno mutto pāpimato”ti. “Aññātaṃ, bhagavā, aññātaṃ, sugatā”ti.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="345" book="khan">
“Yathā kathaṃ pana tvaṃ, bhikkhu, mayā saṃkhittena bhāsitassa vitthārena atthaṃ ājānāsī”ti? “Rūpaṃ kho, bhante, maññamāno baddho mārassa; amaññamāno mutto pāpimato. Vedanaṃ…  saññaṃ…  saṅkhāre…  viññāṇaṃ maññamāno baddho mārassa; amaññamāno mutto pāpimato. Imassa khvāhaṃ, bhante, bhagavatā saṃkhittena bhāsitassa evaṃ vitthārena atthaṃ ājānāmī”ti.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="346" book="khan">
“Sādhu sādhu, bhikkhu. Sādhu kho tvaṃ, bhikkhu, mayā saṃkhittena bhāsitassa vitthārena atthaṃ ājānāsi. Rūpaṃ kho, bhikkhu, maññamāno baddho mārassa; amaññamāno mutto pāpimato. Vedanaṃ…  saññaṃ…  saṅkhāre…  viññāṇaṃ maññamāno baddho mārassa; amaññamāno mutto pāpimato. Imassa kho, bhikkhu, mayā saṃkhittena bhāsitassa evaṃ vitthārena attho daṭṭhabbo”ti …pe…  aññataro ca pana so bhikkhu arahataṃ ahosīti.

</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="347" type="center" book="khan">
Dutiyaṃ.
</Paragraph>
