<Paragraph number="364" book="khan">
Sāvatthinidānaṃ. Atha kho aññataro bhikkhu …pe…  ekamantaṃ nisinno kho so bhikkhu bhagavantaṃ etadavoca—  “sādhu me, bhante, bhagavā saṃkhittena dhammaṃ desetu …pe…  vihareyyan”ti. “Yaṃ kho, bhikkhu, anattaniyaṃ; tatra te chando pahātabbo”ti. “Aññātaṃ, bhagavā, aññātaṃ, sugatā”ti.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="365" book="khan">
“Yathā kathaṃ pana tvaṃ, bhikkhu, mayā saṃkhittena bhāsitassa vitthārena atthaṃ ājānāsī”ti? “Rūpaṃ kho, bhante, anattaniyaṃ; tatra me chando pahātabbo. Vedanā…  saññā…  saṅkhārā…  viññāṇaṃ anattaniyaṃ; tatra me chando pahātabbo. Imassa khvāhaṃ, bhante, bhagavatā saṃkhittena bhāsitassa evaṃ vitthārena atthaṃ ājānāmī”ti.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="366" book="khan">
“Sādhu sādhu, bhikkhu. Sādhu kho tvaṃ, bhikkhu, mayā saṃkhittena bhāsitassa vitthārena atthaṃ ājānāsi. Rūpaṃ kho, bhikkhu, anattaniyaṃ; tatra te chando pahātabbo. Vedanā …  saññā…  saṅkhārā…  viññāṇaṃ anattaniyaṃ; tatra te chando pahātabbo. Imassa kho, bhikkhu, mayā saṃkhittena bhāsitassa evaṃ vitthārena attho daṭṭhabbo”ti …pe…  aññataro ca pana so bhikkhu arahataṃ ahosīti.

</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="367" type="center" book="khan">
Sattamaṃ.
</Paragraph>
