<Paragraph number="553" book="sala">
“Kismiṃ nu kho, bhikkhave, sati kiṃ upādāya uppajjati ajjhattaṃ sukhaṃ dukkhan”ti?
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="554" book="sala">
“Bhagavaṃmūlakā no, bhante, dhammā …pe… .
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="555" book="sala">
“Cakkhusmiṃ kho, bhikkhave, sati cakkhuṃ upādāya uppajjati ajjhattaṃ sukhaṃ dukkhaṃ …pe…  manasmiṃ sati manaṃ upādāya uppajjati ajjhattaṃ sukhaṃ dukkhaṃ. Taṃ kiṃ maññatha, bhikkhave, cakkhu niccaṃ vā aniccaṃ vā”ti?
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="556" book="sala">
“Aniccaṃ, bhante”.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="557" book="sala">
“Yaṃ panāniccaṃ dukkhaṃ vā taṃ sukhaṃ vā”ti?
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="558" book="sala">
“Dukkhaṃ, bhante”.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="559" book="sala">
“Yaṃ panāniccaṃ dukkhaṃ vipariṇāmadhammaṃ, api nu taṃ anupādāya uppajjeyya ajjhattaṃ sukhaṃ dukkhan”ti?
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="560" book="sala">
“No hetaṃ, bhante” …pe… .
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="561" book="sala">
“Jivhā niccā vā aniccā vā”ti?
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="562" book="sala">
“Aniccā, bhante”.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="563" book="sala">
“Yaṃ panāniccaṃ dukkhaṃ vā taṃ sukhaṃ vā”ti?
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="564" book="sala">
“Dukkhaṃ, bhante”.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="565" book="sala">
“Yaṃ panāniccaṃ dukkhaṃ vipariṇāmadhammaṃ, api nu taṃ anupādāya uppajjeyya ajjhattaṃ sukhaṃ dukkhan”ti?
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="566" book="sala">
“No hetaṃ, bhante” …pe… .
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="567" book="sala">
“Mano nicco vā anicco vā”ti?
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="568" book="sala">
“Anicco, bhante”.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="569" book="sala">
“Yaṃ panāniccaṃ dukkhaṃ vā taṃ sukhaṃ vā”ti?
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="570" book="sala">
“Dukkhaṃ, bhante”.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="571" book="sala">
“Yaṃ panāniccaṃ dukkhaṃ vipariṇāmadhammaṃ, api nu taṃ anupādāya uppajjeyya ajjhattaṃ sukhaṃ dukkhan”ti?
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="572" book="sala">
“No hetaṃ, bhante”.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="573" book="sala">
“Evaṃ passaṃ, bhikkhave, sutavā ariyasāvako cakkhusmimpi nibbindati …pe…  manasmimpi nibbindati. Nibbindaṃ virajjati; virāgā vimuccati; vimuttasmiṃ vimuttamiti ñāṇaṃ hoti. ‘Khīṇā jāti, vusitaṃ brahmacariyaṃ, kataṃ karaṇīyaṃ, nāparaṃ itthattāyā’ti pajānātī”ti.

</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="574" type="center" book="sala">
Dutiyaṃ.
</Paragraph>
