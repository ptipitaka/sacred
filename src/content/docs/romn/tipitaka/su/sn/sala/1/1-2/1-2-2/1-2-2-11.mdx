<Division number="73" book="sala">

<Paragraph number="261" book="sala">
“Yo hi koci, bhikkhave, bhikkhu channaṃ phassāyatanānaṃ samudayañca atthaṅgamañca assādañca ādīnavañca nissaraṇañca yathābhūtaṃ nappajānāti. Avusitaṃ tena brahmacariyaṃ, ārakā so imasmā dhammavinayā”ti.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="262" book="sala">
Evaṃ vutte, aññataro bhikkhu bhagavantaṃ etadavoca—  “etthāhaṃ, bhante, anassasaṃ panassasaṃ. Ahañhi, bhante, channaṃ phassāyatanānaṃ samudayañca atthaṅgamañca assādañca ādīnavañca nissaraṇañca yathābhūtaṃ nappajānāmī”ti.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="263" book="sala">
“Taṃ kiṃ maññasi, bhikkhu, cakkhu niccaṃ vā aniccaṃ vā”ti?
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="264" book="sala">
“Aniccaṃ, bhante”.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="265" book="sala">
“Yaṃ panāniccaṃ dukkhaṃ vā taṃ sukhaṃ vā”ti?
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="266" book="sala">
“Dukkhaṃ, bhante”.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="267" book="sala">
“Yaṃ panāniccaṃ dukkhaṃ vipariṇāmadhammaṃ, kallaṃ nu taṃ samanupassituṃ—  ‘etaṃ mama, esohamasmi, eso me attā’”ti?
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="268" book="sala">
“No hetaṃ, bhante”.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="269" book="sala">
“Sotaṃ…  ghānaṃ…  jivhā…  kāyo…  mano nicco vā anicco vā”ti?
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="270" book="sala">
“Anicco, bhante”.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="271" book="sala">
“Yaṃ panāniccaṃ dukkhaṃ vā taṃ sukhaṃ vā”ti?
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="272" book="sala">
“Dukkhaṃ, bhante”.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="273" book="sala">
“Yaṃ panāniccaṃ dukkhaṃ vipariṇāmadhammaṃ, kallaṃ nu taṃ samanupassituṃ—  ‘etaṃ mama, esohamasmi, eso me attā’”ti?
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="274" book="sala">
“No hetaṃ, bhante”.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="275" book="sala">
“Evaṃ passaṃ, bhikkhu, sutavā ariyasāvako cakkhusmimpi nibbindati, sotasmimpi nibbindati, ghānasmimpi nibbindati, jivhāyapi nibbindati, kāyasmimpi nibbindati, manasmimpi nibbindati. Nibbindaṃ virajjati; virāgā vimuccati; vimuttasmiṃ vimuttamiti ñāṇaṃ hoti. ‘Khīṇā jāti, vusitaṃ brahmacariyaṃ, kataṃ karaṇīyaṃ, nāparaṃ itthattāyā’ti pajānātī”ti.

</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="276" type="center" book="sala">
Ekādasamaṃ.



</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="277" type="center" book="sala">
Migajālavaggo dutiyo.



</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="278" type="center" book="sala">
Tassuddānaṃ


</Paragraph>
