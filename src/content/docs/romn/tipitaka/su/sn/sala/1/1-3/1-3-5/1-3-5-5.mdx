<Paragraph number="818" book="sala">
“Nibbānasappāyaṃ vo, bhikkhave, paṭipadaṃ desessāmi. Taṃ suṇātha …pe…  katamā ca sā, bhikkhave, nibbānasappāyā paṭipadā? Taṃ kiṃ maññatha, bhikkhave, cakkhu niccaṃ vā aniccaṃ vā”ti?
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="819" book="sala">
“Aniccaṃ, bhante”.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="820" book="sala">
“Yaṃ panāniccaṃ dukkhaṃ vā taṃ sukhaṃ vā”ti?
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="821" book="sala">
“Dukkhaṃ, bhante”.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="822" book="sala">
“Yaṃ panāniccaṃ dukkhaṃ vipariṇāmadhammaṃ, kallaṃ nu taṃ samanupassituṃ—  ‘etaṃ mama, esohamasmi, eso me attā’”ti?
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="823" book="sala">
“No hetaṃ, bhante”.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="824" book="sala">
“Rūpā niccā vā aniccā vā”ti?
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="825" book="sala">
“Aniccā, bhante”.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="826" book="sala">
“Cakkhuviññāṇaṃ…  cakkhusamphasso …pe…  yampidaṃ manosamphassapaccayā uppajjati vedayitaṃ sukhaṃ vā dukkhaṃ vā adukkhamasukhaṃ vā tampi niccaṃ vā aniccaṃ vā”ti?
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="827" book="sala">
“Aniccaṃ, bhante”.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="828" book="sala">
“Yaṃ panāniccaṃ dukkhaṃ vā taṃ sukhaṃ vā”ti?
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="829" book="sala">
“Dukkhaṃ, bhante”.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="830" book="sala">
“Yaṃ panāniccaṃ dukkhaṃ vipariṇāmadhammaṃ, kallaṃ nu taṃ samanupassituṃ—  ‘etaṃ mama, esohamasmi, eso me attā’”ti?
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="831" book="sala">
“No hetaṃ, bhante”.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="832" book="sala">
“Evaṃ passaṃ, bhikkhave, sutavā ariyasāvako cakkhusmimpi nibbindati, rūpesupi nibbindati, cakkhuviññāṇepi nibbindati, cakkhusamphassepi nibbindati …pe…  yampidaṃ manosamphassapaccayā uppajjati vedayitaṃ sukhaṃ vā dukkhaṃ vā adukkhamasukhaṃ vā tasmimpi nibbindati. Nibbindaṃ virajjati; virāgā vimuccati …pe…  nāparaṃ itthattāyāti pajānāti. Ayaṃ kho sā, bhikkhave, nibbānasappāyā paṭipadā”ti.

</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="833" type="center" book="sala">
Pañcamaṃ.
</Paragraph>
