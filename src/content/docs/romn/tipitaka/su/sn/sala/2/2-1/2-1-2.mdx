<Paragraph number="1098" book="sala">
“Tisso imā, bhikkhave, vedanā. Katamā tisso? Sukhā vedanā, dukkhā vedanā, adukkhamasukhā vedanā—  imā kho, bhikkhave, tisso vedanāti.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1099" type="verses" book="sala">
_Sukhaṃ vā yadi vā dukkhaṃ,_  
_adukkhamasukhaṃ saha;_  
_Ajjhattañca bahiddhā ca,_  
_yaṃ kiñci atthi veditaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1100" type="verses" book="sala">
_Etaṃ dukkhanti ñatvāna,_  
_mosadhammaṃ palokinaṃ;_  
_Phussa phussa vayaṃ passaṃ,_  
_evaṃ tattha virajjatī”ti._  


</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="1101" type="center" book="sala">
Dutiyaṃ.
</Paragraph>
