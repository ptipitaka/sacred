Sāvatthiyaṃ viharati. “Taṃ kiṃ maññasi, rāhula, rūpā niccā vā aniccā vā”ti? “Aniccā, bhante” …pe…  saddā…  gandhā…  rasā…  phoṭṭhabbā…  dhammā… .
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1140" book="nida">
“Cakkhuviññāṇaṃ …pe…  sotaviññāṇaṃ…  ghānaviññāṇaṃ…  jivhāviññāṇaṃ…  kāyaviññāṇaṃ…  manoviññāṇaṃ… .
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1141" book="nida">

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1142" book="nida">

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1143" book="nida">

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1144" book="nida">

</Paragraph>

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1146" book="nida">
