<Paragraph number="853" book="nida">
Ekaṃ samayaṃ bhagavā rājagahe viharati gijjhakūṭe pabbate. Tatra kho bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi—  “bhikkhavo”ti. “Bhadante”ti te bhikkhū bhagavato paccassosuṃ. Bhagavā etadavoca—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="854" book="nida">
“Anamataggoyaṃ, bhikkhave, saṃsāro …pe…  ekapuggalassa, bhikkhave, kappaṃ sandhāvato saṃsarato siyā evaṃ mahā aṭṭhikaṅkalo aṭṭhipuñjo aṭṭhirāsi yathāyaṃ vepullo pabbato, sace saṃhārako assa, sambhatañca na vinasseyya. Taṃ kissa hetu? Anamataggoyaṃ, bhikkhave, saṃsāro …pe…  alaṃ vimuccitun”ti.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="855" book="nida">
Idamavoca bhagavā. Idaṃ vatvāna sugato athāparaṃ etadavoca satthā—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="856" type="verses" book="nida">
_“Ekassekena kappena,_  
_puggalassaṭṭhisañcayo;_  
_Siyā pabbatasamo rāsi,_  
_iti vuttaṃ mahesinā._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="857" type="verses" book="nida">
_So kho panāyaṃ akkhāto,_  
_vepullo pabbato mahā;_  
_Uttaro gijjhakūṭassa,_  
_magadhānaṃ giribbaje._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="858" type="verses" book="nida">
_Yato ca ariyasaccāni,_  
_sammappaññāya passati;_  
_Dukkhaṃ dukkhasamuppādaṃ,_  
_dukkhassa ca atikkamaṃ;_  
_Ariyaṃ caṭṭhaṅgikaṃ maggaṃ,_  
_dukkhūpasamagāminaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="859" type="verses" book="nida">
_Sa sattakkhattuṃparamaṃ,_  
_sandhāvitvāna puggalo;_  
_Dukkhassantakaro hoti,_  
_sabbasaṃyojanakkhayā”ti._  


</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="860" type="center" book="nida">
Dasamaṃ.



</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="861" type="center" book="nida">
Paṭhamo vaggo.



</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="862" type="center" book="nida">
Tassuddānaṃ


</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="863" type="verses" book="nida">
_Tiṇakaṭṭhañca pathavī,_  
_assu khīrañca pabbataṃ;_  
_Sāsapā sāvakā gaṅgā,_  
_daṇḍo ca puggalena cāti._
</Paragraph>
