<Paragraph number="459" book="saga">
Rājagahanidānaṃ. Ekamantaṃ ṭhito kho uttaro devaputto bhagavato santike imaṃ gāthaṃ abhāsi—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="460" type="verses" book="saga">
_“Upanīyati jīvitamappamāyu,_  
_Jarūpanītassa na santi tāṇā;_  
_Etaṃ bhayaṃ maraṇe pekkhamāno,_  
_Puññāni kayirātha sukhāvahānī”ti._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="461" type="verses" book="saga">
_“Upanīyati jīvitamappamāyu,_  
_Jarūpanītassa na santi tāṇā;_  
_Etaṃ bhayaṃ maraṇe pekkhamāno,_  
_Lokāmisaṃ pajahe santipekkho”ti._
</Paragraph>
</Division>
