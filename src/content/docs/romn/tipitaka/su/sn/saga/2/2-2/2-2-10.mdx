<Paragraph number="462" book="saga">
Ekamantaṃ ṭhito kho anāthapiṇḍiko devaputto bhagavato santike imā gāthāyo abhāsi—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="463" type="verses" book="saga">
_“Idañhi taṃ jetavanaṃ,_  
_isisaṃghanisevitaṃ;_  
_Āvutthaṃ dhammarājena,_  
_pītisañjananaṃ mama._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="464" type="verses" book="saga">
_Kammaṃ vijjā ca dhammo ca,_  
_sīlaṃ jīvitamuttamaṃ;_  
_Etena maccā sujjhanti,_  
_na gottena dhanena vā._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="465" type="verses" book="saga">
_Tasmā hi paṇḍito poso,_  
_sampassaṃ atthamattano;_  
_Yoniso vicine dhammaṃ,_  
_evaṃ tattha visujjhati._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="466" type="verses" book="saga">
_Sāriputtova paññāya,_  
_sīlena upasamena ca;_  
_Yopi pāraṅgato bhikkhu,_  
_etāvaparamo siyā”ti._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="467" book="saga">
Idamavoca anāthapiṇḍiko devaputto. Idaṃ vatvā bhagavantaṃ abhivādetvā padakkhiṇaṃ katvā tatthevantaradhāyi.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="468" book="saga">
Atha kho bhagavā tassā rattiyā accayena bhikkhū āmantesi—  “imaṃ, bhikkhave, rattiṃ aññataro devaputto abhikkantāya rattiyā abhikkantavaṇṇo kevalakappaṃ jetavanaṃ obhāsetvā yenāhaṃ tenupasaṅkami; upasaṅkamitvā maṃ abhivādetvā ekamantaṃ aṭṭhāsi. Ekamantaṃ ṭhito kho, bhikkhave, so devaputto mama santike imā gāthāyo abhāsi—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="469" type="verses" book="saga">
_‘Idañhi taṃ jetavanaṃ,_  
_isisaṃghanisevitaṃ;_  
_Āvutthaṃ dhammarājena,_  
_pītisañjananaṃ mama._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="470" type="verses" book="saga">
_Kammaṃ vijjā ca dhammo ca,_  
_sīlaṃ jīvitamuttamaṃ;_  
_Etena maccā sujjhanti,_  
_na gottena dhanena vā._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="471" type="verses" book="saga">
_Tasmā hi paṇḍito poso,_  
_sampassaṃ atthamattano;_  
_Yoniso vicine dhammaṃ,_  
_evaṃ tattha visujjhati._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="472" type="verses" book="saga">
_Sāriputtova paññāya,_  
_sīlena upasamena ca;_  
_Yopi pāraṅgato bhikkhu,_  
_etāvaparamo siyā’ti._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="473" book="saga">
Idamavoca, bhikkhave, so devaputto. Idaṃ vatvā maṃ abhivādetvā padakkhiṇaṃ katvā tatthevantaradhāyī”ti.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="474" book="saga">
Evaṃ vutte, āyasmā ānando bhagavantaṃ etadavoca—  “so hi nūna, bhante, anāthapiṇḍiko devaputto bhavissati. Anāthapiṇḍiko gahapati āyasmante sāriputte abhippasanno ahosī”ti. “Sādhu sādhu, ānanda, yāvatakaṃ kho, ānanda, takkāya pattabbaṃ anuppattaṃ taṃ tayā. Anāthapiṇḍiko hi so, ānanda, devaputto”ti.

</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="475" type="center" book="saga">
Anāthapiṇḍikavaggo dutiyo.



</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="476" type="center" book="saga">
Tassuddānaṃ


</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="477" type="verses" book="saga">
_Candimaso ca veṇḍu ca,_  
_Dīghalaṭṭhi ca nandano;_  
_Candano vāsudatto ca,_  
_Subrahmā kakudhena ca;_  
_Uttaro navamo vutto,_  
_Dasamo anāthapiṇḍikoti._
</Paragraph>
