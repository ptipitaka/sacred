<Paragraph number="1451" book="saga">
Evaṃ me sutaṃ—  ekaṃ samayaṃ bhagavā rājagahe viharati indakūṭe pabbate, indakassa yakkhassa bhavane. Atha kho indako yakkho yena bhagavā tenupasaṅkami; upasaṅkamitvā bhagavantaṃ gāthāya ajjhabhāsi—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1452" type="verses" book="saga">
_“Rūpaṃ na jīvanti vadanti buddhā,_  
_Kathaṃ nvayaṃ vindatimaṃ sarīraṃ;_  
_Kutassa aṭṭhīyakapiṇḍameti,_  
_Kathaṃ nvayaṃ sajjati gabbharasmin”ti._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1453" type="verses" book="saga">
_“Paṭhamaṃ kalalaṃ hoti,_  
_kalalā hoti abbudaṃ;_  
_Abbudā jāyate pesi,_  
_pesi nibbattatī ghano;_  
_Ghanā pasākhā jāyanti,_  
_kesā lomā nakhāpi ca._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1454" type="verses" book="saga">
_Yañcassa bhuñjatī mātā,_  
_annaṃ pānañca bhojanaṃ;_  
_Tena so tattha yāpeti,_  
_mātukucchigato naro”ti._
</Paragraph>
</Division>
