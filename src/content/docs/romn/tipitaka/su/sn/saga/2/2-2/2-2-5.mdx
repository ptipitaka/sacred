<Paragraph number="442" book="saga">
Ekamantaṃ ṭhito kho candano devaputto bhagavantaṃ gāthāya ajjhabhāsi—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="443" type="verses" book="saga">
_“Kathaṃsu tarati oghaṃ,_  
_rattindivamatandito;_  
_Appatiṭṭhe anālambe,_  
_ko gambhīre na sīdatī”ti._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="444" type="verses" book="saga">
_“Sabbadā sīlasampanno,_  
_Paññavā susamāhito;_  
_Āraddhavīriyo pahitatto,_  
_Oghaṃ tarati duttaraṃ._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="445" type="verses" book="saga">
_Virato kāmasaññāya,_  
_rūpasaṃyojanātigo;_  
_Nandīrāgaparikkhīṇo,_  
_so gambhīre na sīdatī”ti._
</Paragraph>
</Division>
