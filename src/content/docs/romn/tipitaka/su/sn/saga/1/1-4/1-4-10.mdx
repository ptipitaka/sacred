<Paragraph number="231" book="saga">
Evaṃ me sutaṃ—  ekaṃ samayaṃ bhagavā vesāliyaṃ viharati mahāvane kūṭāgārasālāyaṃ. Atha kho cūḷakokanadā pajjunnassa dhītā abhikkantāya rattiyā abhikkantavaṇṇā kevalakappaṃ mahāvanaṃ obhāsetvā yena bhagavā tenupasaṅkami; upasaṅkamitvā bhagavantaṃ abhivādetvā ekamantaṃ aṭṭhāsi. Ekamantaṃ ṭhitā kho sā devatā cūḷakokanadā pajjunnassa dhītā bhagavato santike imā gāthāyo abhāsi—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="232" type="verses" book="saga">
_“Idhāgamā vijjupabhāsavaṇṇā,_  
_Kokanadā pajjunnassa dhītā;_  
_Buddhañca dhammañca namassamānā,_  
_Gāthācimā atthavatī abhāsi._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="233" type="verses" book="saga">
_Bahunāpi kho taṃ vibhajeyyaṃ,_  
_Pariyāyena tādiso dhammo;_  
_Saṃkhittamatthaṃ lapayissāmi,_  
_Yāvatā me manasā pariyattaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="234" type="verses" book="saga">
_Pāpaṃ na kayirā vacasā manasā,_  
_Kāyena vā kiñcana sabbaloke;_  
_Kāme pahāya satimā sampajāno,_  
_Dukkhaṃ na sevetha anatthasaṃhitan”ti._  


</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="235" type="center" book="saga">
Satullapakāyikavaggo catuttho.



</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="236" type="center" book="saga">
Tassuddānaṃ


</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="237" type="verses" book="saga">
_Sabbhimaccharinā sādhu,_  
_na santujjhānasaññino;_  
_Saddhā samayo sakalikaṃ,_  
_ubho pajjunnadhītaroti._
</Paragraph>
