<Paragraph number="49" type="verses" book="saga">
_“Khattiyo dvipadaṃ seṭṭho,_  
_balībaddo catuppadaṃ;_  
_Komārī seṭṭhā bhariyānaṃ,_  
_yo ca puttāna pubbajo”ti._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="50" type="verses" book="saga">
_“Sambuddho dvipadaṃ seṭṭho,_  
_ājānīyo catuppadaṃ;_  
_Sussūsā seṭṭhā bhariyānaṃ,_  
_yo ca puttānamassavo”ti._
</Paragraph>
