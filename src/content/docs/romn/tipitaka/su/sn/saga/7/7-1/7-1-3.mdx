<Paragraph number="1150" book="saga">
Ekaṃ samayaṃ bhagavā rājagahe viharati veḷuvane kalandakanivāpe. Assosi kho asurindakabhāradvājo brāhmaṇo—  “bhāradvājagotto brāhmaṇo kira samaṇassa gotamassa santike agārasmā anagāriyaṃ pabbajito”ti kupito anattamano yena bhagavā tenupasaṅkami; upasaṅkamitvā bhagavantaṃ asabbhāhi pharusāhi vācāhi akkosati paribhāsati. Evaṃ vutte, bhagavā tuṇhī ahosi. Atha kho asurindakabhāradvājo brāhmaṇo bhagavantaṃ etadavoca—  “jitosi, samaṇa, jitosi, samaṇā”ti.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1151" type="verses" book="saga">
_“Jayaṃ ve maññati bālo,_  
_vācāya pharusaṃ bhaṇaṃ;_  
_Jayañcevassa taṃ hoti,_  
_yā titikkhā vijānato._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1152" type="verses" book="saga">
_Tasseva tena pāpiyo,_  
_yo kuddhaṃ paṭikujjhati;_  
_Kuddhaṃ appaṭikujjhanto,_  
_saṅgāmaṃ jeti dujjayaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1153" type="verses" book="saga">
_Ubhinnamatthaṃ carati,_  
_attano ca parassa ca;_  
_Paraṃ saṅkupitaṃ ñatvā,_  
_yo sato upasammati._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1154" type="verses" book="saga">
_Ubhinnaṃ tikicchantānaṃ,_  
_attano ca parassa ca;_  
_Janā maññanti bāloti,_  
_ye dhammassa akovidā”ti._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1155" book="saga">
Evaṃ vutte, asurindakabhāradvājo brāhmaṇo bhagavantaṃ etadavoca—  “abhikkantaṃ, bho gotama …pe…  abbhaññāsi. Aññataro ca panāyasmā bhāradvājo arahataṃ ahosīti.
</Paragraph>
</Division>
