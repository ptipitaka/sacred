<Paragraph number="10" book="saga">
Sāvatthinidānaṃ. Ekamantaṃ ṭhitā kho sā devatā bhagavato santike imaṃ gāthaṃ abhāsi—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="11" type="verses" book="saga">
_“Upanīyati jīvitamappamāyu,_  
_Jarūpanītassa na santi tāṇā;_  
_Etaṃ bhayaṃ maraṇe pekkhamāno,_  
_Puññāni kayirātha sukhāvahānī”ti._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="12" type="verses" book="saga">
_“Upanīyati jīvitamappamāyu,_  
_Jarūpanītassa na santi tāṇā;_  
_Etaṃ bhayaṃ maraṇe pekkhamāno,_  
_Lokāmisaṃ pajahe santipekkho”ti._
</Paragraph>
</Division>
