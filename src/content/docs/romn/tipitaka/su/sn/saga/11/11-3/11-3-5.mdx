<Paragraph number="1697" book="saga">
Evaṃ me sutaṃ—  ekaṃ samayaṃ bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tatra kho bhagavā bhikkhū …pe…  bhagavā etadavoca—  “bhūtapubbaṃ, bhikkhave, sakko devānamindo sudhammāyaṃ sabhāyaṃ deve tāvatiṃse anunayamāno tāyaṃ velāyaṃ imaṃ gāthaṃ abhāsi—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1698" type="verses" book="saga">
_‘Mā vo kodho ajjhabhavi,_  
_mā ca kujjhittha kujjhataṃ;_  
_Akkodho avihiṃsā ca,_  
_ariyesu ca paṭipadā;_  
_Atha pāpajanaṃ kodho,_  
_pabbatovābhimaddatī’”ti._  


</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="1699" type="center" book="saga">
Tatiyo vaggo.



</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1700" type="center" book="saga">
Tassuddānaṃ


</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1701" type="verses" book="saga">
_Chetvā dubbaṇṇiya māyā,_  
_accayena akodhano;_  
_Desitaṃ buddhaseṭṭhena,_  
_idañhi sakkapañcakanti._  


</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1702" type="center" book="saga">
Sakkasaṃyuttaṃ samattaṃ.



</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1703" type="center" book="saga">
Sagāthāvaggo paṭhamo.



</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1704" type="center" book="saga">
Tassuddānaṃ


</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1705" type="verses" book="saga">
_Devatā devaputto ca,_  
_rājā māro ca bhikkhunī;_  
_Brahmā brāhmaṇa vaṅgīso,_  
_vanayakkhena vāsavoti._  


</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1706" type="center" book="saga">
Sagāthāvaggasaṃyuttapāḷi niṭṭhitā.
</Paragraph>
