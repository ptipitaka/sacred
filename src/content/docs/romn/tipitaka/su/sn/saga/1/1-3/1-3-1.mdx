<Paragraph number="96" book="saga">
Sāvatthinidānaṃ. Ekamantaṃ ṭhitā kho sā devatā bhagavato santike imaṃ gāthaṃ abhāsi—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="97" type="verses" book="saga">
_“Sattiyā viya omaṭṭho,_  
_ḍayhamānova matthake;_  
_Kāmarāgappahānāya,_  
_sato bhikkhu paribbaje”ti._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="98" type="verses" book="saga">
_“Sattiyā viya omaṭṭho,_  
_ḍayhamānova matthake;_  
_Sakkāyadiṭṭhippahānāya,_  
_sato bhikkhu paribbaje”ti._
</Paragraph>
</Division>
