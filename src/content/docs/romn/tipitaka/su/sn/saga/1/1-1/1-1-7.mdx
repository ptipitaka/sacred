<Paragraph number="22" book="saga">
Sāvatthinidānaṃ. Ekamantaṃ ṭhitā kho sā devatā bhagavato santike imaṃ gāthaṃ abhāsi—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="23" type="verses" book="saga">
_“Yesaṃ dhammā appaṭividitā,_  
_Paravādesu nīyare;_  
_Suttā te nappabujjhanti,_  
_Kālo tesaṃ pabujjhitun”ti._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="24" type="verses" book="saga">
_“Yesaṃ dhammā suppaṭividitā,_  
_Paravādesu na nīyare;_  
_Te sambuddhā sammadaññā,_  
_Caranti visame saman”ti._
</Paragraph>
</Division>
