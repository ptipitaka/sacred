<Paragraph number="317" type="verses" book="saga">
_“Kiṃsu saṃyojano loko,_  
_kiṃsu tassa vicāraṇaṃ;_  
_Kissassu vippahānena,_  
_nibbānaṃ iti vuccatī”ti._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="318" type="verses" book="saga">
_“Nandīsaṃyojano loko,_  
_vitakkassa vicāraṇaṃ;_  
_Taṇhāya vippahānena,_  
_nibbānaṃ iti vuccatī”ti._
</Paragraph>
