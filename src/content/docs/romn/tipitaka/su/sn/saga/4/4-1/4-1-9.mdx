<Paragraph number="808" book="saga">
Evaṃ me sutaṃ—  ekaṃ samayaṃ bhagavā rājagahe viharati veḷuvane kalandakanivāpe. Tatra kho bhagavā bhikkhū āmantesi—  “bhikkhavo”ti. “Bhadante”ti te bhikkhū bhagavato paccassosuṃ. Bhagavā etadavoca—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="809" book="saga">
“Appamidaṃ, bhikkhave, manussānaṃ āyu. Gamanīyo samparāyo, kattabbaṃ kusalaṃ, caritabbaṃ brahmacariyaṃ. Natthi jātassa amaraṇaṃ. Yo, bhikkhave, ciraṃ jīvati, so vassasataṃ appaṃ vā bhiyyo”ti.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="810" book="saga">
Atha kho māro pāpimā yena bhagavā tenupasaṅkami; upasaṅkamitvā bhagavantaṃ gāthāya ajjhabhāsi—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="811" type="verses" book="saga">
_“Dīghamāyu manussānaṃ,_  
_na naṃ hīḷe suporiso;_  
_Careyya khīramattova,_  
_natthi maccussa āgamo”ti._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="812" type="verses" book="saga">
_“Appamāyu manussānaṃ,_  
_hīḷeyya naṃ suporiso;_  
_Careyyādittasīsova,_  
_natthi maccussa nāgamo”ti._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="813" book="saga">
Atha kho māro …pe…  tatthevantaradhāyīti.
</Paragraph>
