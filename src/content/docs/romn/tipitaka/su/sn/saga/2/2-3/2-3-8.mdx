<Paragraph number="538" book="saga">
Ekamantaṃ ṭhito kho nandivisālo devaputto bhagavantaṃ gāthāya ajjhabhāsi—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="539" type="verses" book="saga">
_“Catucakkaṃ navadvāraṃ,_  
_puṇṇaṃ lobhena saṃyutaṃ;_  
_Paṅkajātaṃ mahāvīra,_  
_kathaṃ yātrā bhavissatī”ti._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="540" type="verses" book="saga">
_“Chetvā naddhiṃ varattañca,_  
_icchālobhañca pāpakaṃ;_  
_Samūlaṃ taṇhamabbuyha,_  
_evaṃ yātrā bhavissatī”ti._
</Paragraph>
</Division>
