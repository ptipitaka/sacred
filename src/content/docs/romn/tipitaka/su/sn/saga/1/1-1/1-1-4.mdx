<Paragraph number="13" book="saga">
Sāvatthinidānaṃ. Ekamantaṃ ṭhitā kho sā devatā bhagavato santike imaṃ gāthaṃ abhāsi—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="14" type="verses" book="saga">
_“Accenti kālā tarayanti rattiyo,_  
_Vayoguṇā anupubbaṃ jahanti;_  
_Etaṃ bhayaṃ maraṇe pekkhamāno,_  
_Puññāni kayirātha sukhāvahānī”ti._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="15" type="verses" book="saga">
_“Accenti kālā tarayanti rattiyo,_  
_Vayoguṇā anupubbaṃ jahanti;_  
_Etaṃ bhayaṃ maraṇe pekkhamāno,_  
_Lokāmisaṃ pajahe santipekkho”ti._
</Paragraph>
</Division>
