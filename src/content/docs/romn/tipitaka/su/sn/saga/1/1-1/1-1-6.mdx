<Paragraph number="19" book="saga">
Sāvatthinidānaṃ. Ekamantaṃ ṭhitā kho sā devatā bhagavato santike imaṃ gāthaṃ abhāsi—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="20" type="verses" book="saga">
_“Kati jāgarataṃ suttā,_  
_kati suttesu jāgarā;_  
_Katibhi rajamādeti,_  
_katibhi parisujjhatī”ti._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="21" type="verses" book="saga">
_“Pañca jāgarataṃ suttā,_  
_pañca suttesu jāgarā;_  
_Pañcabhi rajamādeti,_  
_pañcabhi parisujjhatī”ti._
</Paragraph>
</Division>
