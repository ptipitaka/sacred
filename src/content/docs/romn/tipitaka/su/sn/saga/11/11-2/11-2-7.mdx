<Paragraph number="1653" book="saga">
Sāvatthiyaṃ jetavane. Tena kho pana samayena bhagavā divāvihāragato hoti paṭisallīno. Atha kho sakko ca devānamindo brahmā ca sahampati yena bhagavā tenupasaṅkamiṃsu; upasaṅkamitvā paccekaṃ dvārabāhaṃ nissāya aṭṭhaṃsu. Atha kho sakko devānamindo bhagavato santike imaṃ gāthaṃ abhāsi—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1654" type="verses" book="saga">
_“Uṭṭhehi vīra vijitasaṅgāma,_  
_Pannabhāra anaṇa vicara loke;_  
_Cittañca te suvimuttaṃ,_  
_Cando yathā pannarasāya rattin”ti._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1655" book="saga">
“Na kho, devānaminda, tathāgatā evaṃ vanditabbā. Evañca kho, devānaminda, tathāgatā vanditabbā—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1656" type="verses" book="saga">
_‘Uṭṭhehi vīra vijitasaṅgāma,_  
_Satthavāha anaṇa vicara loke;_  
_Desassu bhagavā dhammaṃ,_  
_Aññātāro bhavissantī’”ti._
</Paragraph>
</Division>
