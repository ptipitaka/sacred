<Paragraph number="31" book="saga">
Sāvatthinidānaṃ. Ekamantaṃ ṭhitā kho sā devatā bhagavantaṃ gāthāya ajjhabhāsi—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="32" type="verses" book="saga">
_“Araññe viharantānaṃ,_  
_santānaṃ brahmacārinaṃ;_  
_Ekabhattaṃ bhuñjamānānaṃ,_  
_kena vaṇṇo pasīdatī”ti._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="33" type="verses" book="saga">
_“Atītaṃ nānusocanti,_  
_nappajappanti nāgataṃ;_  
_Paccuppannena yāpenti,_  
_tena vaṇṇo pasīdati._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="34" type="verses" book="saga">
_Anāgatappajappāya,_  
_atītassānusocanā;_  
_Etena bālā sussanti,_  
_naḷova harito luto”ti._  


</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="35" type="center" book="saga">
Naḷavaggo paṭhamo.



</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="36" type="center" book="saga">
Tassuddānaṃ


</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="37" type="verses" book="saga">
_Oghaṃ nimokkhaṃ upaneyyaṃ,_  
_Accenti katichindi ca;_  
_Jāgaraṃ appaṭividitā,_  
_Susammuṭṭhā mānakāminā;_  
_Araññe dasamo vutto,_  
_Vaggo tena pavuccati._
</Paragraph>
