Ekamantaṃ ṭhito kho vāsudatto devaputto bhagavato santike imaṃ gāthaṃ abhāsi—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="447" type="verses" book="saga">
_“Sattiyā viya omaṭṭho,_  
_ḍayhamānova matthake;_  
_Kāmarāgappahānāya,_  
_sato bhikkhu paribbaje”ti._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="448" type="verses" book="saga">

_sato bhikkhu paribbaje”ti._
</Paragraph>
