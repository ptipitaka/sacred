<Paragraph number="28" book="saga">
Sāvatthinidānaṃ. Ekamantaṃ ṭhitā kho sā devatā bhagavato santike imaṃ gāthaṃ abhāsi—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="29" type="verses" book="saga">
_“Na mānakāmassa damo idhatthi,_  
_Na monamatthi asamāhitassa;_  
_Eko araññe viharaṃ pamatto,_  
_Na maccudheyyassa tareyya pāran”ti._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="30" type="verses" book="saga">
_“Mānaṃ pahāya susamāhitatto,_  
_Sucetaso sabbadhi vippamutto;_  
_Eko araññe viharaṃ appamatto,_  
_Sa maccudheyyassa tareyya pāran”ti._
</Paragraph>
</Division>
