<Paragraph number="1428" book="saga">
Ekaṃ samayaṃ aññataro bhikkhu kosalesu viharati aññatarasmiṃ vanasaṇḍe. Atha kho tasmiṃ vanasaṇḍe adhivatthā devatā yena so bhikkhu tenupasaṅkami; upasaṅkamitvā tassa bhikkhuno santike imaṃ gāthaṃ abhāsi—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1429" type="verses" book="saga">
_“Ṭhite majjhanhike kāle,_  
_sannisīvesu pakkhisu;_  
_Saṇateva brahāraññaṃ,_  
_taṃ bhayaṃ paṭibhāti maṃ._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1430" type="verses" book="saga">
_Ṭhite majjhanhike kāle,_  
_sannisīvesu pakkhisu;_  
_Saṇateva brahāraññaṃ,_  
_sā rati paṭibhāti man”ti._
</Paragraph>
</Division>
