<Paragraph number="395" book="saga">
Sāvatthinidānaṃ. Ekamantaṃ ṭhito kho pañcālacaṇḍo devaputto bhagavato santike imaṃ gāthaṃ abhāsi—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="396" type="verses" book="saga">
_“Sambādhe vata okāsaṃ,_  
_Avindi bhūrimedhaso;_  
_Yo jhānamabujjhi buddho,_  
_Paṭilīnanisabho munī”ti._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="397" type="verses" book="saga">
_“Sambādhe vāpi vindanti, __(pañcālacaṇḍāti bhagavā)___  
_Dhammaṃ nibbānapattiyā;_  
_Ye satiṃ paccalatthaṃsu,_  
_Sammā te susamāhitā”ti._
</Paragraph>
</Division>
