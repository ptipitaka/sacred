<Paragraph number="1402" book="saga">
Ekaṃ samayaṃ āyasmā anuruddho kosalesu viharati aññatarasmiṃ vanasaṇḍe. Atha kho aññatarā tāvatiṃsakāyikā devatā jālinī nāma āyasmato anuruddhassa purāṇadutiyikā yenāyasmā anuruddho tenupasaṅkami; upasaṅkamitvā āyasmantaṃ anuruddhaṃ gāthāya ajjhabhāsi—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1403" type="verses" book="saga">
_“Tattha cittaṃ paṇidhehi,_  
_yattha te vusitaṃ pure;_  
_Tāvatiṃsesu devesu,_  
_sabbakāmasamiddhisu;_  
_Purakkhato parivuto,_  
_devakaññāhi sobhasī”ti._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1404" type="verses" book="saga">
_“Duggatā devakaññāyo,_  
_sakkāyasmiṃ patiṭṭhitā;_  
_Te cāpi duggatā sattā,_  
_devakaññāhi patthitā”ti._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1405" type="verses" book="saga">
_“Na te sukhaṃ pajānanti,_  
_ye na passanti nandanaṃ;_  
_Āvāsaṃ naradevānaṃ,_  
_tidasānaṃ yasassinan”ti._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1406" type="verses" book="saga">
_“Na tvaṃ bāle vijānāsi,_  
_yathā arahataṃ vaco;_  
_Aniccā sabbasaṅkhārā,_  
_uppādavayadhammino;_  
_Uppajjitvā nirujjhanti,_  
_tesaṃ vūpasamo sukho._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1407" type="verses" book="saga">
_Natthi dāni punāvāso,_  
_devakāyasmi jālini;_  
_Vikkhīṇo jātisaṃsāro,_  
_natthi dāni punabbhavo”ti._
</Paragraph>
</Division>
