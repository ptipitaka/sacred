<Paragraph number="337" type="verses" book="saga">
_“Kiṃsu rathassa paññāṇaṃ,_  
_kiṃsu paññāṇamaggino;_  
_Kiṃsu raṭṭhassa paññāṇaṃ,_  
_kiṃsu paññāṇamitthiyā”ti._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="338" type="verses" book="saga">
_“Dhajo rathassa paññāṇaṃ,_  
_dhūmo paññāṇamaggino;_  
_Rājā raṭṭhassa paññāṇaṃ,_  
_bhattā paññāṇamitthiyā”ti._
</Paragraph>
