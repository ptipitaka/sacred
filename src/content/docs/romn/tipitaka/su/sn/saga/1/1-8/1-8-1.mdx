<Paragraph number="334" book="saga">
Sāvatthinidānaṃ. Ekamantaṃ ṭhitā kho sā devatā bhagavantaṃ gāthāya ajjhabhāsi—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="335" type="verses" book="saga">
_“Kiṃsu chetvā sukhaṃ seti,_  
_kiṃsu chetvā na socati;_  
_Kissassu ekadhammassa,_  
_vadhaṃ rocesi gotamā”ti._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="336" type="verses" book="saga">
_“Kodhaṃ chetvā sukhaṃ seti,_  
_kodhaṃ chetvā na socati;_  
_Kodhassa visamūlassa,_  
_madhuraggassa devate;_  
_Vadhaṃ ariyā pasaṃsanti,_  
_tañhi chetvā na socatī”ti._
</Paragraph>
</Division>
