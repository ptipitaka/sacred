<Paragraph number="55" type="verses" book="saga">
_“Dukkaraṃ duttitikkhañca,_  
_abyattena ca sāmaññaṃ;_  
_Bahūhi tattha sambādhā,_  
_yattha bālo visīdatī”ti._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="56" type="verses" book="saga">
_“Katihaṃ careyya sāmaññaṃ,_  
_cittañce na nivāraye;_  
_Pade pade visīdeyya,_  
_saṅkappānaṃ vasānugoti._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="57" type="verses" book="saga">
_Kummova aṅgāni sake kapāle,_  
_Samodahaṃ bhikkhu manovitakke;_  
_Anissito aññamaheṭhayāno,_  
_Parinibbuto nūpavadeyya kañcī”ti._
</Paragraph>
</Division>
