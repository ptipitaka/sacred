<Paragraph number="376" book="saga">
Sāvatthinidānaṃ. Atha kho māgho devaputto abhikkantāya rattiyā abhikkantavaṇṇo kevalakappaṃ jetavanaṃ obhāsetvā yena bhagavā tenupasaṅkami; upasaṅkamitvā bhagavantaṃ abhivādetvā ekamantaṃ aṭṭhāsi. Ekamantaṃ ṭhito kho māgho devaputto bhagavantaṃ gāthāya ajjhabhāsi—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="377" type="verses" book="saga">
_“Kiṃsu chetvā sukhaṃ seti,_  
_kiṃsu chetvā na socati;_  
_Kissassu ekadhammassa,_  
_vadhaṃ rocesi gotamā”ti._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="378" type="verses" book="saga">
_“Kodhaṃ chetvā sukhaṃ seti,_  
_kodhaṃ chetvā na socati;_  
_Kodhassa visamūlassa,_  
_madhuraggassa vatrabhū;_  
_Vadhaṃ ariyā pasaṃsanti,_  
_tañhi chetvā na socatī”ti._
</Paragraph>
</Division>
