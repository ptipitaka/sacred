<Paragraph number="16" book="saga">
Sāvatthinidānaṃ. Ekamantaṃ ṭhitā kho sā devatā bhagavato santike imaṃ gāthaṃ abhāsi—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="17" type="verses" book="saga">
_“Kati chinde kati jahe,_  
_kati cuttari bhāvaye;_  
_Kati saṅgātigo bhikkhu,_  
_oghatiṇṇoti vuccatī”ti._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="18" type="verses" book="saga">
_“Pañca chinde pañca jahe,_  
_pañca cuttari bhāvaye;_  
_Pañca saṅgātigo bhikkhu,_  
_oghatiṇṇoti vuccatī”ti._
</Paragraph>
</Division>
