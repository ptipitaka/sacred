<Paragraph number="43" book="saga">
Sāvatthinidānaṃ. Ekamantaṃ ṭhitā kho sā devatā bhagavato santike imaṃ gāthaṃ abhāsi—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="44" type="verses" book="saga">
_“Nandati puttehi puttimā,_  
_Gomā gohi tatheva nandati;_  
_Upadhīhi narassa nandanā,_  
_Na hi so nandati yo nirūpadhī”ti._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="45" type="verses" book="saga">
_“Socati puttehi puttimā,_  
_Gomā gohi tatheva socati;_  
_Upadhīhi narassa socanā,_  
_Na hi so socati yo nirūpadhī”ti._
</Paragraph>
</Division>
