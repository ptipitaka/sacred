Sāvatthinidānaṃ. Ekamantaṃ ṭhito kho kassapo devaputto bhagavato santike imaṃ gāthaṃ abhāsi—

<Paragraph number="375" type="verses" book="saga">
_“Bhikkhu siyā jhāyī vimuttacitto,_  
_Ākaṅkhe ce hadayassānupattiṃ;_  
_Lokassa ñatvā udayabbayañca,_  
_Sucetaso anissito tadānisaṃso”ti._
