<Paragraph number="246" type="verses" book="saga">
_“Annamevābhinandanti,_  
_ubhaye devamānusā;_  
_Atha ko nāma so yakkho,_  
_yaṃ annaṃ nābhinandatī”ti._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="247" type="verses" book="saga">
_“Ye naṃ dadanti saddhāya,_  
_vippasannena cetasā;_  
_Tameva annaṃ bhajati,_  
_asmiṃ loke paramhi ca._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="248" type="verses" book="saga">
_Tasmā vineyya maccheraṃ,_  
_dajjā dānaṃ malābhibhū;_  
_Puññāni paralokasmiṃ,_  
_patiṭṭhā honti pāṇinan”ti._
</Paragraph>
</Division>
