<Paragraph number="412" book="saga">
Sāvatthinidānaṃ. Tena kho pana samayena candimā devaputto rāhunā asurindena gahito hoti. Atha kho candimā devaputto bhagavantaṃ anussaramāno tāyaṃ velāyaṃ imaṃ gāthaṃ abhāsi—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="413" type="verses" book="saga">
_“Namo te buddha vīratthu,_  
_vippamuttosi sabbadhi;_  
_Sambādhapaṭipannosmi,_  
_tassa me saraṇaṃ bhavā”ti._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="414" book="saga">
Atha kho bhagavā candimaṃ devaputtaṃ ārabbha rāhuṃ asurindaṃ gāthāya ajjhabhāsi—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="415" type="verses" book="saga">
_“Tathāgataṃ arahantaṃ,_  
_candimā saraṇaṃ gato;_  
_Rāhu candaṃ pamuñcassu,_  
_buddhā lokānukampakā”ti._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="416" book="saga">
Atha kho rāhu asurindo candimaṃ devaputtaṃ muñcitvā taramānarūpo yena vepacitti asurindo tenupasaṅkami; upasaṅkamitvā saṃviggo lomahaṭṭhajāto ekamantaṃ aṭṭhāsi. Ekamantaṃ ṭhitaṃ kho rāhuṃ asurindaṃ vepacitti asurindo gāthāya ajjhabhāsi—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="417" type="verses" book="saga">
_“Kiṃ nu santaramānova,_  
_rāhu candaṃ pamuñcasi;_  
_Saṃviggarūpo āgamma,_  
_kiṃ nu bhītova tiṭṭhasī”ti._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="418" type="verses" book="saga">
_“Sattadhā me phale muddhā,_  
_jīvanto na sukhaṃ labhe;_  
_Buddhagāthābhigītomhi,_  
_no ce muñceyya candiman”ti._
</Paragraph>
</Division>
