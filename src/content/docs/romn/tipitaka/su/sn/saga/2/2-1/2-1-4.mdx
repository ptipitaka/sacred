<Paragraph number="379" book="saga">
Sāvatthinidānaṃ. Ekamantaṃ ṭhito kho māgadho devaputto bhagavantaṃ gāthāya ajjhabhāsi—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="380" type="verses" book="saga">
_“Kati lokasmiṃ pajjotā,_  
_yehi loko pakāsati;_  
_Bhavantaṃ puṭṭhumāgamma,_  
_kathaṃ jānemu taṃ mayan”ti._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="381" type="verses" book="saga">
_“Cattāro loke pajjotā,_  
_pañcamettha na vijjati;_  
_Divā tapati ādicco,_  
_rattimābhāti candimā._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="382" type="verses" book="saga">
_Atha aggi divārattiṃ,_  
_tattha tattha pakāsati;_  
_Sambuddho tapataṃ seṭṭho,_  
_esā ābhā anuttarā”ti._
</Paragraph>
</Division>
