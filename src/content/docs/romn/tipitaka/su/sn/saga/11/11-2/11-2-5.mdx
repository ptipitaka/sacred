<Paragraph number="1646" book="saga">
Sāvatthiyaṃ jetavane. Atha kho sakko devānamindo yena bhagavā tenupasaṅkami; upasaṅkamitvā bhagavantaṃ abhivādetvā ekamantaṃ aṭṭhāsi. Ekamantaṃ ṭhito kho sakko devānamindo bhagavantaṃ etadavoca—  “kiṃ nu kho, bhante, bhūmirāmaṇeyyakan”ti?
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1647" type="verses" book="saga">
_“Ārāmacetyā vanacetyā,_  
_pokkharañño sunimmitā;_  
_Manussarāmaṇeyyassa,_  
_kalaṃ nāgghanti soḷasiṃ._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1648" type="verses" book="saga">
_Gāme vā yadi vāraññe,_  
_Ninne vā yadi vā thale;_  
_Yattha arahanto viharanti,_  
_Taṃ bhūmirāmaṇeyyakan”ti._
</Paragraph>
</Division>
