<Paragraph number="433" book="saga">
Ekamantaṃ ṭhito kho veṇḍu devaputto bhagavato santike imaṃ gāthaṃ abhāsi—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="434" type="verses" book="saga">
_“Sukhitāva te manujā,_  
_sugataṃ payirupāsiya;_  
_Yuñjaṃ gotamasāsane,_  
_appamattā nu sikkhare”ti._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="435" type="verses" book="saga">
_“Ye me pavutte siṭṭhipade, __(veṇḍūti bhagavā)___  
_Anusikkhanti jhāyino;_  
_Kāle te appamajjantā,_  
_Na maccuvasagā siyun”ti._
</Paragraph>
</Division>
