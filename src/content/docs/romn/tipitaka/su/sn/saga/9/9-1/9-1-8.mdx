<Paragraph number="1412" book="saga">
Ekaṃ samayaṃ aññataro bhikkhu kosalesu viharati aññatarasmiṃ vanasaṇḍe. Tena kho pana samayena so bhikkhu aññatarasmiṃ kule ativelaṃ ajjhogāḷhappatto viharati. Atha kho yā tasmiṃ vanasaṇḍe adhivatthā devatā tassa bhikkhuno anukampikā atthakāmā taṃ bhikkhuṃ saṃvejetukāmā yā tasmiṃ kule kulagharaṇī, tassā vaṇṇaṃ abhinimminitvā yena so bhikkhu tenupasaṅkami; upasaṅkamitvā taṃ bhikkhuṃ gāthāya ajjhabhāsi—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1413" type="verses" book="saga">
_“Nadītīresu saṇṭhāne,_  
_sabhāsu rathiyāsu ca;_  
_Janā saṅgamma mantenti,_  
_mañca tañca kimantaran”ti._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1414" type="verses" book="saga">
_“Bahūhi saddā paccūhā,_  
_khamitabbā tapassinā;_  
_Na tena maṅku hotabbaṃ,_  
_na hi tena kilissati._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1415" type="verses" book="saga">
_Yo ca saddaparittāsī,_  
_vane vātamigo yathā;_  
_Lahucittoti taṃ āhu,_  
_nāssa sampajjate vatan”ti._
</Paragraph>
