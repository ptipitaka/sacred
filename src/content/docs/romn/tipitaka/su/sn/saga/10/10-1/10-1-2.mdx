<Paragraph number="1455" book="saga">
Ekaṃ samayaṃ bhagavā rājagahe viharati gijjhakūṭe pabbate. Atha kho sakkanāmako yakkho yena bhagavā tenupasaṅkami; upasaṅkamitvā bhagavantaṃ gāthāya ajjhabhāsi—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1456" type="verses" book="saga">
_“Sabbaganthappahīnassa,_  
_vippamuttassa te sato;_  
_Samaṇassa na taṃ sādhu,_  
_yadaññamanusāsasī”ti._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1457" type="verses" book="saga">
_“Yena kenaci vaṇṇena,_  
_saṃvāso sakka jāyati;_  
_Na taṃ arahati sappañño,_  
_manasā anukampituṃ._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1458" type="verses" book="saga">
_Manasā ce pasannena,_  
_yadaññamanusāsati;_  
_Na tena hoti saṃyutto,_  
_yānukampā anuddayā”ti._
</Paragraph>
</Division>
