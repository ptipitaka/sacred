<Paragraph number="522" book="saga">
Evaṃ me sutaṃ—  ekaṃ samayaṃ sambahulā bhikkhū, kosalesu viharanti himavantapasse araññakuṭikāya uddhatā unnaḷā capalā mukharā vikiṇṇavācā muṭṭhassatino asampajānā asamāhitā vibbhantacittā pākatindriyā.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="523" book="saga">
Atha kho jantu devaputto tadahuposathe pannarase yena te bhikkhū tenupasaṅkami; upasaṅkamitvā te bhikkhū gāthāhi ajjhabhāsi—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="524" type="verses" book="saga">
_“Sukhajīvino pure āsuṃ,_  
_bhikkhū gotamasāvakā;_  
_Anicchā piṇḍamesanā,_  
_anicchā sayanāsanaṃ;_  
_Loke aniccataṃ ñatvā,_  
_dukkhassantaṃ akaṃsu te._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="525" type="verses" book="saga">
_Dupposaṃ katvā attānaṃ,_  
_gāme gāmaṇikā viya;_  
_Bhutvā bhutvā nipajjanti,_  
_parāgāresu mucchitā._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="526" type="verses" book="saga">
_Saṃghassa añjaliṃ katvā,_  
_idhekacce vadāmahaṃ;_  
_Apaviddhā anāthā te,_  
_yathā petā tatheva te._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="527" type="verses" book="saga">
_Ye kho pamattā viharanti,_  
_te me sandhāya bhāsitaṃ;_  
_Ye appamattā viharanti,_  
_namo tesaṃ karomahan”ti._
</Paragraph>
</Division>
