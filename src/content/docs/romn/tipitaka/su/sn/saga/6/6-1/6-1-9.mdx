<Paragraph number="1074" book="saga">
Sāvatthinidānaṃ. Tena kho pana samayena kokāliko bhikkhu ābādhiko hoti dukkhito bāḷhagilāno. Atha kho turū paccekabrahmā abhikkantāya rattiyā abhikkantavaṇṇo kevalakappaṃ jetavanaṃ obhāsetvā yena kokāliko bhikkhu tenupasaṅkami; upasaṅkamitvā vehāsaṃ ṭhito kokālikaṃ bhikkhuṃ etadavoca—  “pasādehi, kokālika, sāriputtamoggallānesu cittaṃ. Pesalā sāriputtamoggallānā”ti. “Kosi tvaṃ, āvuso”ti? “Ahaṃ turū paccekabrahmā”ti. “Nanu tvaṃ, āvuso, bhagavatā anāgāmī byākato, atha kiñcarahi idhāgato? Passa, yāvañca te idaṃ aparaddhan”ti.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1075" type="verses" book="saga">
_“Purisassa hi jātassa,_  
_kuṭhārī jāyate mukhe;_  
_Yāya chindati attānaṃ,_  
_bālo dubbhāsitaṃ bhaṇaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1076" type="verses" book="saga">
_Yo nindiyaṃ pasaṃsati,_  
_Taṃ vā nindati yo pasaṃsiyo;_  
_Vicināti mukhena so kaliṃ,_  
_Kalinā tena sukhaṃ na vindati._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1077" type="verses" book="saga">
_Appamattako ayaṃ kali,_  
_Yo akkhesu dhanaparājayo;_  
_Sabbassāpi sahāpi attanā,_  
_Ayameva mahantataro kali;_  
_Yo sugatesu manaṃ padosaye._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1078" type="verses" book="saga">
_Sataṃ sahassānaṃ nirabbudānaṃ,_  
_Chattiṃsati pañca ca abbudāni;_  
_Yamariyagarahī nirayaṃ upeti,_  
_Vācaṃ manañca paṇidhāya pāpakan”ti._
</Paragraph>
