<Paragraph number="294" type="verses" book="saga">
_“Kiṃsu vatthu manussānaṃ,_  
_kiṃsūdha paramo sakhā;_  
_Kiṃsu bhūtā upajīvanti,_  
_ye pāṇā pathavissitā”ti._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="295" type="verses" book="saga">
_“Puttā vatthu manussānaṃ,_  
_bhariyā ca paramo sakhā;_  
_Vuṭṭhiṃ bhūtā upajīvanti,_  
_ye pāṇā pathavissitā”ti._
</Paragraph>
