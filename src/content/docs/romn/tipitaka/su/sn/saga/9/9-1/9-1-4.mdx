<Paragraph number="1395" book="saga">
Ekaṃ samayaṃ sambahulā bhikkhū kosalesu viharanti aññatarasmiṃ vanasaṇḍe. Atha kho te bhikkhū vassaṃvuṭṭhā temāsaccayena cārikaṃ pakkamiṃsu. Atha kho yā tasmiṃ vanasaṇḍe adhivatthā devatā te bhikkhū apassantī paridevamānā tāyaṃ velāyaṃ imaṃ gāthaṃ abhāsi—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1396" type="verses" book="saga">
_“Arati viya mejja khāyati,_  
_Bahuke disvāna vivitte āsane;_  
_Te cittakathā bahussutā,_  
_Kome gotamasāvakā gatā”ti._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1397" book="saga">
Evaṃ vutte, aññatarā devatā taṃ devataṃ gāthāya paccabhāsi—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1398" type="verses" book="saga">
_“Māgadhaṃ gatā kosalaṃ gatā,_  
_Ekacciyā pana vajjibhūmiyā;_  
_Magā viya asaṅgacārino,_  
_Aniketā viharanti bhikkhavo”ti._
</Paragraph>
</Division>
