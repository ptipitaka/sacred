<Paragraph number="1390" book="saga">
Ekaṃ samayaṃ āyasmā kassapagotto kosalesu viharati aññatarasmiṃ vanasaṇḍe. Tena kho pana samayena āyasmā kassapagotto divāvihāragato aññataraṃ chetaṃ ovadati. Atha kho yā tasmiṃ vanasaṇḍe adhivatthā devatā āyasmantaṃ kassapagottaṃ saṃvejetukāmā yenāyasmā kassapagotto tenupasaṅkami; upasaṅkamitvā āyasmantaṃ kassapagottaṃ gāthāhi ajjhabhāsi—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1391" type="verses" book="saga">
_“Giriduggacaraṃ chetaṃ,_  
_appapaññaṃ acetasaṃ;_  
_Akāle ovadaṃ bhikkhu,_  
_mandova paṭibhāti maṃ._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1392" type="verses" book="saga">
_Suṇāti na vijānāti,_  
_Āloketi na passati;_  
_Dhammasmiṃ bhaññamānasmiṃ,_  
_Atthaṃ bālo na bujjhati._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1393" type="verses" book="saga">
_Sacepi dasa pajjote,_  
_dhārayissasi kassapa;_  
_Neva dakkhati rūpāni,_  
_cakkhu hissa na vijjatī”ti._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1394" book="saga">
Atha kho āyasmā kassapagotto tāya devatāya saṃvejito saṃvegamāpādīti.
</Paragraph>
</Division>
