<Paragraph number="363" type="verses" book="saga">
_“Kesūdha araṇā loke,_  
_kesaṃ vusitaṃ na nassati;_  
_Kedha icchaṃ parijānanti,_  
_kesaṃ bhojissiyaṃ sadā._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="364" type="verses" book="saga">
_Kiṃsu mātā pitā bhātā,_  
_vandanti naṃ patiṭṭhitaṃ;_  
_Kiṃsu idha jātihīnaṃ,_  
_abhivādenti khattiyā”ti._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="365" type="verses" book="saga">
_“Samaṇīdha araṇā loke,_  
_Samaṇānaṃ vusitaṃ na nassati;_  
_Samaṇā icchaṃ parijānanti,_  
_Samaṇānaṃ bhojissiyaṃ sadā._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="366" type="verses" book="saga">
_Samaṇaṃ mātā pitā bhātā,_  
_vandanti naṃ patiṭṭhitaṃ;_  
_Samaṇīdha jātihīnaṃ,_  
_abhivādenti khattiyā”ti._  


</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="367" type="center" book="saga">
Chetvāvaggo aṭṭhamo.



</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="368" type="center" book="saga">
Tassuddānaṃ


</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="369" type="verses" book="saga">
_Chetvā rathañca cittañca,_  
_vuṭṭhi bhītā najīrati;_  
_Issaraṃ kāmaṃ pātheyyaṃ,_  
_pajjoto araṇena cāti._  


</Paragraph>

Devatāsaṃyuttaṃ samattaṃ.
