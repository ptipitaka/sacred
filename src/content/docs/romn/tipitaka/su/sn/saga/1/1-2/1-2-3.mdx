<Paragraph number="46" book="saga">
Sāvatthinidānaṃ. Ekamantaṃ ṭhitā kho sā devatā bhagavato santike imaṃ gāthaṃ abhāsi—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="47" type="verses" book="saga">
_“Natthi puttasamaṃ pemaṃ,_  
_natthi gosamitaṃ dhanaṃ;_  
_Natthi sūriyasamā ābhā,_  
_samuddaparamā sarā”ti._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="48" type="verses" book="saga">
_“Natthi attasamaṃ pemaṃ,_  
_natthi dhaññasamaṃ dhanaṃ;_  
_Natthi paññāsamā ābhā,_  
_vuṭṭhi ve paramā sarā”ti._
</Paragraph>
</Division>
