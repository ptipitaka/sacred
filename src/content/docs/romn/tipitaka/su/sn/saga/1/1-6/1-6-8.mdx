<Paragraph number="302" type="verses" book="saga">
_“Kiṃsu uppatho akkhāto,_  
_kiṃsu rattindivakkhayo;_  
_Kiṃ malaṃ brahmacariyassa,_  
_kiṃ sinānamanodakan”ti._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="303" type="verses" book="saga">
_“Rāgo uppatho akkhāto,_  
_Vayo rattindivakkhayo;_  
_Itthī malaṃ brahmacariyassa,_  
_Etthāyaṃ sajjate pajā;_  
_Tapo ca brahmacariyañca,_  
_Taṃ sinānamanodakan”ti._
</Paragraph>
