<Paragraph number="319" type="verses" book="saga">
_“Kiṃsu sambandhano loko,_  
_kiṃsu tassa vicāraṇaṃ;_  
_Kissassu vippahānena,_  
_sabbaṃ chindati bandhanan”ti._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="320" type="verses" book="saga">
_“Nandīsambandhano loko,_  
_vitakkassa vicāraṇaṃ;_  
_Taṇhāya vippahānena,_  
_sabbaṃ chindati bandhanan”ti._
</Paragraph>
