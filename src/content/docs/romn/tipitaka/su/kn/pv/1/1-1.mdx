<Paragraph number="2" type="verses" book="pv">
_“Khettūpamā arahanto,_  
_dāyakā kassakūpamā;_  
_Bījūpamaṃ deyyadhammaṃ,_  
_etto nibbattate phalaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="2" book="pv">

<Paragraph number="3" type="verses" book="pv">
_Etaṃ bījaṃ kasi khettaṃ,_  
_petānaṃ dāyakassa ca;_  
_Taṃ petā paribhuñjanti,_  
_dātā puññena vaḍḍhati._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="3" book="pv">

<Paragraph number="4" type="verses" book="pv">
_Idheva kusalaṃ katvā,_  
_Pete ca paṭipūjiya;_  
_Saggañca kamatiṭṭhānaṃ,_  
_Kammaṃ katvāna bhaddakan”ti._  


</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="5" type="center" book="pv">
Khettūpamapetavatthu paṭhamaṃ.
</Paragraph>
