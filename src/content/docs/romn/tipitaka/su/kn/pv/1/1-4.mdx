<Paragraph number="14" type="verses" book="pv">
_“Yaṃ kiñcārammaṇaṃ katvā,_  
_dajjā dānaṃ amaccharī;_  
_Pubbapete ca ārabbha,_  
_atha vā vatthudevatā._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="11" book="pv" bookNo="19" page="128">

<Paragraph number="15" type="verses" book="pv">
_Cattāro ca mahārāje,_  
_lokapāle yasassine;_  
_Kuveraṃ dhataraṭṭhañca,_  
_virūpakkhaṃ virūḷhakaṃ;_  
_Te ceva pūjitā honti,_  
_dāyakā ca anipphalā._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="12" book="pv" bookNo="19" page="129">

<Paragraph number="16" type="verses" book="pv">
_Na hi ruṇṇaṃ vā soko vā,_  
_yā caññā paridevanā;_  
_Na taṃ petassa atthāya,_  
_evaṃ tiṭṭhanti ñātayo._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="13" book="pv" bookNo="19" page="129">

<Paragraph number="17" type="verses" book="pv">
_Ayañca kho dakkhiṇā dinnā,_  
_saṃghamhi suppatiṭṭhitā;_  
_Dīgharattaṃ hitāyassa,_  
_ṭhānaso upakappatī”ti._  


</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="18" type="center" book="pv">
Piṭṭhadhītalikapetavatthu catutthaṃ.
</Paragraph>
