<Paragraph number="10" type="verses" book="pv">
_“Dibbaṃ subhaṃ dhāresi vaṇṇadhātuṃ,_  
_Vehāyasaṃ tiṭṭhasi antalikkhe;_  
_Mukhañca te kimayo pūtigandhaṃ,_  
_Khādanti kiṃ kammamakāsi pubbe”._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="11" type="verses" book="pv">
_“Samaṇo ahaṃ pāpotiduṭṭhavāco,_  
_Tapassirūpo mukhasā asaññato;_  
_Laddhā ca me tapasā vaṇṇadhātu,_  
_Mukhañca me pesuṇiyena pūti._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="9" book="pv" bookNo="19" page="128">

<Paragraph number="12" type="verses" book="pv">
_Tayidaṃ tayā nārada sāmaṃ diṭṭhaṃ,_  
_Anukampakā ye kusalā vadeyyuṃ;_  
_‘Mā pesuṇaṃ mā ca musā abhāṇi,_  
_Yakkho tuvaṃ hohisi kāmakāmī’”ti._  


</Paragraph>
</Division>
