<Paragraph number="6" type="verses" book="pv">
_“Kāyo te sabbasovaṇṇo,_  
_sabbā obhāsate disā;_  
_Mukhaṃ te sūkarasseva,_  
_kiṃ kammamakarī pure”._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="5" book="pv">

<Paragraph number="7" type="verses" book="pv">
_“Kāyena saññato āsiṃ,_  
_vācāyāsimasaññato;_  
_Tena metādiso vaṇṇo,_  
_yathā passasi nārada._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="6" book="pv">

<Paragraph number="8" type="verses" book="pv">
_Taṃ tyāhaṃ nārada brūmi,_  
_Sāmaṃ diṭṭhamidaṃ tayā;_  
_Mākāsi mukhasā pāpaṃ,_  
_Mā kho sūkaramukho ahū”ti._  


</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="9" type="center" book="pv">
Sūkaramukhapetavatthu dutiyaṃ.
</Paragraph>
