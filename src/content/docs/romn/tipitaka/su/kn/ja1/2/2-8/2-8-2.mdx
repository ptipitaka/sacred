_tāni attani passati;_  
_Kalyāṇakārī kalyāṇaṃ,_  
_pāpakārī ca pāpakaṃ;_  
_Yādisaṃ vapate bījaṃ,_  
_tādisaṃ harate phalan”ti._  


</Paragraph>
