<Division number="97" book="ja1">

<Paragraph number="804" type="verses" book="ja1">
_“Varaṃ varaṃ tvaṃ nihanaṃ pure cari,_  
_Asmiṃ padese abhibhuyya sūkare;_  
_So dāni eko byapagamma jhāyasi,_  
_Balaṃ nu te byaggha na cajja vijjati”._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="98" book="ja1">

<Paragraph number="805" type="verses" book="ja1">
_“Ime sudaṃ yanti disodisaṃ pure,_  
_Bhayaṭṭitā leṇagavesino puthū;_  
_Te dāni saṅgamma vasanti ekato,_  
_Yatthaṭṭhitā duppasahajjame mayā”._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="99" book="ja1">

<Paragraph number="806" type="verses" book="ja1">
_“Namatthu saṃghāna samāgatānaṃ,_  
_Disvā sayaṃ sakhya vadāmi abbhutaṃ;_  
_Byagghaṃ migā yattha jiniṃsu dāṭhino,_  
_Sāmaggiyā dāṭhabalesu muccare”ti._  


</Paragraph>
</Division>

Vaḍḍhakīsūkarajātakaṃ tatiyaṃ.
</Paragraph>
