<Paragraph number="1049" type="verses" book="ja1">
_“Tadeva me tvaṃ vidito,_  
_vanamajjhe rathesabha;_  
_Yassa te khaggabaddhassa,_  
_sannaddhassa tirīṭino;_  
_Assatthadumasākhāya,_  
_pakkā godhā palāyatha”._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="130" book="ja1" bookNo="22" page="272">

<Paragraph number="1050" type="verses" book="ja1">
_“Name namantassa bhaje bhajantaṃ,_  
_Kiccānukubbassa kareyya kiccaṃ;_  
_Nānatthakāmassa kareyya atthaṃ,_  
_Asambhajantampi na sambhajeyya._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="131" book="ja1" bookNo="22" page="272">

<Paragraph number="1051" type="verses" book="ja1">
_Caje cajantaṃ vanathaṃ na kayirā,_  
_Apetacittena na sambhajeyya;_  
_Dijo dumaṃ khīṇaphalanti ñatvā,_  
_Aññaṃ samekkheyya mahā hi loko”._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="132" book="ja1" bookNo="22" page="272">

<Paragraph number="1052" type="verses" book="ja1">
_“So te karissāmi yathānubhāvaṃ,_  
_Kataññutaṃ khattiye pekkhamāno;_  
_Sabbañca te issariyaṃ dadāmi,_  
_Yassicchasī tassa tuvaṃ dadāmī”ti._  


</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="1053" type="center" book="ja1">
Pakkagodhajātakaṃ tatiyaṃ.
</Paragraph>
