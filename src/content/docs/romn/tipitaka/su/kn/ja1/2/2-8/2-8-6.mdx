_“Kāle nikkhamanā sādhu,_  
_nākāle sādhu nikkhamo;_  
_Akālena hi nikkhamma,_  
_ekakampi bahujjano;_  
_Na kiñci atthaṃ joteti,_  
_dhaṅkasenāva kosiyaṃ._
