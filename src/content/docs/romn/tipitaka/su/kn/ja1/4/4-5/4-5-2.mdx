_varaṃ mayhaṃ udumbaro._  

</Paragraph>

_na khippamanubujjhati;_  
_Amittavasamanveti,_  
_pacchā ca anutappati._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>

_na ca pacchānutappatī”ti._  


</Paragraph>
