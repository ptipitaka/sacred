_“Sobhati maccho gaṅgeyyo,_  
_atho sobhati yāmuno;_  
_Catuppadoyaṃ puriso,_  
_nigrodhaparimaṇḍalo;_  
_Īsakāyatagīvo ca,_  
_sabbeva atirocati”._

_Attappasaṃsako poso,_  
_Nāyaṃ asmāka ruccatī”ti._
