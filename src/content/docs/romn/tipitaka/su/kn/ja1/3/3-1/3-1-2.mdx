_yaṃ maṃ tvaṃ tilamuṭṭhiyā;_  
_Bāhāya maṃ gahetvāna,_  
_laṭṭhiyā anutāḷayi._

_yenāsi brāhmaṇāgato;_  
_Yaṃ maṃ bāhā gahetvāna,_  
_tikkhattuṃ anutāḷayi”._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>

_Yo daṇḍena nisedhati;_  
_Sāsanaṃ taṃ na taṃ veraṃ,_  
_Iti naṃ paṇḍitā vidū”ti._  


</Paragraph>
</Division>
