_“Dakkheyyekapadaṃ tāta,_  
_anekatthapadassitaṃ;_  
_Tañca sīlena saññuttaṃ,_  
_khantiyā upapāditaṃ;_  
_Alaṃ mitte sukhāpetuṃ,_  
_amittānaṃ dukhāya cā”ti._  


</Paragraph>
