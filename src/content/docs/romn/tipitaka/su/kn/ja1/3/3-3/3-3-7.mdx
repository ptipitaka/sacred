<Paragraph number="778" type="verses" book="ja1">
_“Vassāni paññāsa samādhikāni,_  
_Vasimha selassa guhāya romaka;_  
_Asaṅkamānā abhinibbutattā,_  
_Hatthattamāyanti mamaṇḍajā pure._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="80" book="ja1">

<Paragraph number="779" type="verses" book="ja1">
_Te dāni vakkaṅga kimatthamussukā,_  
_Bhajanti aññaṃ girikandaraṃ dijā;_  
_Na nūna maññanti mamaṃ yathā pure,_  
_Cirappavutthā atha vā na te ime”._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="81" book="ja1">

<Paragraph number="780" type="verses" book="ja1">
_“Jānāma taṃ na mayaṃ sampamūḷhā,_  
_Soyeva tvaṃ te mayamasma nāññe;_  
_Cittañca te asmiṃ jane paduṭṭhaṃ,_  
_Ājīvikā tena tamuttasāmā”ti._  


</Paragraph>
</Division>

Romakajātakaṃ sattamaṃ.
