_kathaṃ atari kepukaṃ;_  
_Kathaṃ satta samuddāni,_  
_kathaṃ simbalimāruhi”._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>

_tayā atari kepukaṃ;_  
_Tayā satta samuddāni,_  
_tayā simbalimāruhiṃ”._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>

_āvahāmi vahāmi cā”ti._  


</Paragraph>
