<Paragraph number="832" type="verses" book="ja1">
_“Sīlaṃ kireva kalyāṇaṃ,_  
_sīlaṃ loke anuttaraṃ;_  
_Passa ghoraviso nāgo,_  
_sīlavāti na haññati._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="119" book="ja1">

<Paragraph number="833" type="verses" book="ja1">
_Sohaṃ sīlaṃ samādissaṃ,_  
_loke anumataṃ sivaṃ;_  
_Ariyavuttisamācāro,_  
_yena vuccati sīlavā._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="120" book="ja1">

<Paragraph number="834" type="verses" book="ja1">
_Ñātīnañca piyo hoti,_  
_mittesu ca virocati;_  
_Kāyassa bhedā sugatiṃ,_  
_upapajjati sīlavā”ti._  


</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="835" type="center" book="ja1">
Sīlavīmaṃsakajātakaṃ dasamaṃ.

  
Abbhantaravaggo catuttho.



</Paragraph>

_Anusāsaniyopi ca macchavaro,_  
_Maṇikuṇḍalakena kirena dasāti._
</Paragraph>
