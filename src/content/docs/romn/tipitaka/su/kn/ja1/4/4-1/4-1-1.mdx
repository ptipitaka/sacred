<Paragraph number="883" type="verses" book="ja1">
_“Vivarathimāsaṃ dvāraṃ,_  
_Nagaraṃ pavisantu aruṇarājassa;_  
_Sīhena susiṭṭhena,_  
_Surakkhitaṃ nandisenena”._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="2" book="ja1" bookNo="22" page="226">

<Paragraph number="884" type="verses" book="ja1">
_“Jayo kaliṅgānamasayhasāhinaṃ,_  
_Parājayo anayo assakānaṃ;_  
_Icceva te bhāsitaṃ brahmacāri,_  
_Na ujjubhūtā vitathaṃ bhaṇanti”._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="3" book="ja1" bookNo="22" page="241">

<Paragraph number="885" type="verses" book="ja1">
_“Devā musāvādamupātivattā,_  
_Saccaṃ dhanaṃ paramaṃ tesu sakka;_  
_Taṃ te musā bhāsitaṃ devarāja,_  
_Kiṃ vā paṭicca maghavā mahinda”._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="4" book="ja1" bookNo="22" page="226">

<Paragraph number="886" type="verses" book="ja1">
_“Nanu te sutaṃ brāhmaṇa bhaññamāne,_  
_Devā na issanti purisaparakkamassa;_  
_Damo samādhi manaso abhejjo,_  
_Abyaggatā nikkamanañca kāle;_  
_Daḷhañca viriyaṃ purisaparakkamo ca,_  
_Teneva āsi vijayo assakānan”ti._  


</Paragraph>
</Division>

Cūḷakāliṅgajātakaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
