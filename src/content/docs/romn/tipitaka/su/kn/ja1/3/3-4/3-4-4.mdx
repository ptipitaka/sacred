_alakkhikā bahuṃ dhanaṃ;_

_aticcaññeva pāṇino;_  
_Uppajjanti bahū bhogā,_  
_appanāyatanesupi._  

</Paragraph>

_Uppajjanti apāpassa,_  
_katapuññassa jantuno”ti._  


</Paragraph>
