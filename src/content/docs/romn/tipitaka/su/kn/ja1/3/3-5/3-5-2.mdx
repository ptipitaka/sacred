_Suphassā bhakkhitumicchati;_  
_Rañño mahānase pakkaṃ,_  
_Paccagghaṃ rājabhojanaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>

_rañño camhi idhāgato;_  
_Bhattu apacitiṃ kummi,_  
_nāsāyamakaraṃ vaṇan”ti._  


</Paragraph>
