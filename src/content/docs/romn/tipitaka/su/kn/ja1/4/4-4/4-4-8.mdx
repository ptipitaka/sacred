<Paragraph number="1074" type="verses" book="ja1">
_“Viditaṃ thusaṃ undurānaṃ,_  
_Viditaṃ pana taṇḍulaṃ;_  
_Thusaṃ thusaṃ vivajjetvā,_  
_Taṇḍulaṃ pana khādare._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="150" book="ja1" bookNo="22" page="327">

<Paragraph number="1075" type="verses" book="ja1">
_Yā mantanā araññasmiṃ,_  
_yā ca gāme nikaṇṇikā;_  
_Yañcetaṃ iti cīti ca,_  
_etampi viditaṃ mayā._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="151" book="ja1" bookNo="22" page="327">

<Paragraph number="1076" type="verses" book="ja1">
_Dhammena kira jātassa,_  
_pitā puttassa makkaṭo;_  
_Daharasseva santassa,_  
_dantehi phalamacchidā._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="152" book="ja1" bookNo="22" page="327">

<Paragraph number="1077" type="verses" book="ja1">
_Yametaṃ parisappasi,_  
_ajakāṇova sāsape;_  
_Yopāyaṃ heṭṭhato seti,_  
_etampi viditaṃ mayā”ti._  


</Paragraph>
</Division>

Thusajātakaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.
