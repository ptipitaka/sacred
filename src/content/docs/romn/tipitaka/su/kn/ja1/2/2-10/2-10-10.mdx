_“Ayaṃ isī upasamasaṃyame rato,_  
_Sa tiṭṭhati sisirabhayena aṭṭito;_

_Vinetu sītaṃ darathañca kevalaṃ”._

_Kapī ayaṃ dumavarasākhagocaro;_

_Sacevajemampi dūseyyagāran”ti. (351)_
