_“Naṅguṭṭhaṃ me avakkamma,_  
_Heṭhayitvāna eḷike;_  
_Sājja mātulavādena,_  
_Muñcitabbā nu maññasi”._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="90" book="ja1" bookNo="22" page="320">

<Paragraph number="1771" type="verses" book="ja1">
_“Puratthāmukho nisinnosi,_  
_Ahaṃ te mukhamāgatā;_  
_Pacchato tuyhaṃ naṅguṭṭhaṃ,_  
_Kathaṃ khvāhaṃ avakkamiṃ”._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="91" book="ja1" bookNo="22" page="321">

<Paragraph number="1772" type="verses" book="ja1">
_“Yāvatā caturo dīpā,_  
_sasamuddā sapabbatā;_  
_Tāvatā mayhaṃ naṅguṭṭhaṃ,_  
_kathaṃ kho tvaṃ vivajjayi”._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="92" book="ja1" bookNo="22" page="321">

<Paragraph number="1773" type="verses" book="ja1">
_“Pubbeva metamakkhiṃsu,_  
_mātā pitā ca bhātaro;_  
_Dīghaṃ duṭṭhassa naṅguṭṭhaṃ,_  
_sāmhi vehāyasāgatā”._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="93" book="ja1" bookNo="22" page="321">

<Paragraph number="1774" type="verses" book="ja1">
_“Tañca disvāna āyantiṃ,_  
_antalikkhasmi eḷike;_  
_Migasaṅgho palāyittha,_  
_bhakkho me nāsito tayā”._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="94" book="ja1" bookNo="22" page="321">

<Paragraph number="1775" type="verses" book="ja1">
_“Iccevaṃ vilapantiyā,_  
_eḷakiyā ruhagghaso;_  
_Galakaṃ anvāvamaddi,_  
_natthi duṭṭhe subhāsitaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="95" book="ja1" bookNo="22" page="321">

<Paragraph number="1776" type="verses" book="ja1">
_Neva duṭṭhe nayo atthi,_  
_Na dhammo na subhāsitaṃ;_  
_Nikkamaṃ duṭṭhe yuñjetha,_  
_So ca sabbhiṃ na rañjatī”ti. (1235)_  


</Paragraph>
</Division>
