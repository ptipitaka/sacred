_cande lohitamaddane;_

_pāṇā me cande nirujjhanti._

_Me ḍayhate nitammāmi;_  
_Tava candiyā socantiyā,_  
_Na naṃ aññehi sokehi._  

</Paragraph>

_Nadī aparipuṇṇāva sussāmi;_  
_Tava candiyā socantiyā,_  
_Na naṃ aññehi sokehi._  

</Paragraph>

_Tava candiyā socantiyā,_  
_Na naṃ aññehi sokehi”._  

</Paragraph>

_Vijjhasi vanamūlasmiṃ,_  
_Soyaṃ viddho chamā seti._  

</Paragraph>
