<Paragraph number="702" type="verses" book="ja1">
_“Nayimassa vijjāmayamatthi kiñci,_  
_Na bandhavo no pana te sahāyo;_  
_Atha kena vaṇṇena tirīṭavaccho,_  
_Tedaṇḍiko bhuñjati aggapiṇḍaṃ”._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="26" book="ja1" bookNo="22" page="203">

<Paragraph number="703" type="verses" book="ja1">
_“Āpāsu me yuddhaparājitassa,_  
_Ekassa katvā vivanasmi ghore;_  
_Pasārayī kicchagatassa pāṇiṃ,_  
_Tenūdatāriṃ dukhasampareto._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="27" book="ja1" bookNo="22" page="203">

<Paragraph number="704" type="verses" book="ja1">
_Etassa kiccena idhānupatto,_  
_Vesāyino visayā jīvaloke;_  
_Lābhāraho tāta tirīṭavaccho,_  
_Dethassa bhogaṃ yajathañca yaññan”ti._  


</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="705" type="center" book="ja1">
Tirīṭavacchajātakaṃ navamaṃ.
</Paragraph>
