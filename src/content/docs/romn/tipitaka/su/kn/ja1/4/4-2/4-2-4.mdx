<Paragraph number="950" type="verses" book="ja1">
_“Dujjīvitamajīvimha,_  
_ye sante na dadamhase;_  
_Vijjamānesu bhogesu,_  
_dīpaṃ nākamha attano”._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="54" book="ja1">

<Paragraph number="951" type="verses" book="ja1">
_“Saṭṭhi vassasahassāni,_  
_paripuṇṇāni sabbaso;_  
_Niraye paccamānānaṃ,_  
_kadā anto bhavissati”._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="55" book="ja1">

<Paragraph number="952" type="verses" book="ja1">
_“Natthi anto kuto anto,_  
_na anto paṭidissati;_  
_Tadā hi pakataṃ pāpaṃ,_  
_mama tuyhañca mārisā”._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="56" book="ja1">

<Paragraph number="953" type="verses" book="ja1">
_“Sohaṃ nūna ito gantvā,_  
_yoniṃ laddhāna mānusiṃ;_  
_Vadaññū sīlasampanno,_  
_kāhāmi kusalaṃ bahun”ti._  


</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="954" type="center" book="ja1">
Lohakumbhijātakaṃ catutthaṃ.
</Paragraph>
