_“Yo nīliyaṃ maṇḍayati,_  
_saṇḍāsena vihaññati;_  
_Tassa sā vasamanvetu,_  
_yā te ambe avāhari”._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1107" type="verses" book="ja1">
_“Vīsaṃ vā pañcavīsaṃ vā,_  
_ūnatiṃsaṃ va jātiyā;_  
_Tādisā pati mā laddhā,_  
_yā te ambe avāhari”._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="175" book="ja1" bookNo="22" page="380">

<Paragraph number="1108" type="verses" book="ja1">
_“Dīghaṃ gacchatu addhānaṃ,_  
_ekikā abhisārikā;_  
_Saṅkete pati mā adda,_  
_yā te ambe avāhari”._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>

_mālinī candanussadā;_  
_Ekikā sayane setu,_  
_yā te ambe avāharī”ti._  


</Paragraph>
