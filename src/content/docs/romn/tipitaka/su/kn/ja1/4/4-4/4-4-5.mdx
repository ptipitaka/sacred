<Paragraph number="1059" type="verses" book="ja1">
_“Brahā pavaḍḍhakāyo so,_  
_dīghadāṭho ca jambuka;_  
_Na tvaṃ tattha kule jāto,_  
_yattha gaṇhanti kuñjaraṃ”._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="138" book="ja1" bookNo="22" page="272">

<Paragraph number="1060" type="verses" book="ja1">
_“Asīho sīhamānena,_  
_yo attānaṃ vikubbati;_  
_Kotthūva gajamāsajja,_  
_seti bhūmyā anutthunaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="139" book="ja1" bookNo="22" page="273">

<Paragraph number="1061" type="verses" book="ja1">
_Yasassino uttamapuggalassa,_  
_Sañjātakhandhassa mahabbalassa;_  
_Asamekkhiya thāmabalūpapattiṃ,_  
_Sa seti nāgena hatoyaṃ jambuko._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="140" book="ja1" bookNo="22" page="273">

<Paragraph number="1062" type="verses" book="ja1">
_Yo cīdha kammaṃ kurute pamāya,_  
_Thāmabbalaṃ attani saṃviditvā;_  
_Jappena mantena subhāsitena,_  
_Parikkhavā so vipulaṃ jinātī”ti._  


</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="1063" type="center" book="ja1">
Jambukajātakaṃ pañcamaṃ.
</Paragraph>
