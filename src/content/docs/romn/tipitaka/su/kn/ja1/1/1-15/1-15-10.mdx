<Paragraph number="331" type="verses" book="ja1">
_“Asantaṃ yo paggaṇhāti,_  
_asantaṃ cūpasevati;_  
_Tameva ghāsaṃ kurute,_  
_byaggho sañjīvako yathā”ti._  


</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="332" type="center" book="ja1">
Sañjīvajātakaṃ dasamaṃ.

  
Kakaṇṭakavaggo pannarasamo.



</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="333" type="center" book="ja1">
Tassuddānaṃ


</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="334" type="verses" book="ja1">
_Sukhamedhati daṇḍavaro ca puna,_  
_Lasi vāladhi pañcamarādhavaro;_  
_Samahodadhi kattika bondi puna,_  
_Caturaṅgulabyagghavarena dasāti._  


</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="335" type="center" book="ja1">
Uparimapaṇṇāsako.



</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="336" type="center" book="ja1">
Atha vagguddānaṃ


</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="337" type="verses" book="ja1">
_Apaṇṇakaṃ sīlavaggakuruṅga,_  
_Kulāvakaṃ atthakāmena pañcamaṃ;_  
_Āsīso itthivaruṇaṃ apāyi,_  
_Littavaggena te dasa;_  
_Parosataṃ haṃci kusanāḷi,_  
_Asampadaṃ kakaṇṭakavaggo._  


</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="338" type="center" book="ja1">
Ekanipātamhilaṅkatanti.



</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="339" type="center" book="ja1">
