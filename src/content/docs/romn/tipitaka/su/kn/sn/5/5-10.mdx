_“Majjhe sarasmiṃ tiṭṭhataṃ, __(iccāyasmā kappo)___  
_Oghe jāte mahabbhaye;_  
_Jarāmaccuparetānaṃ,_  
_Dīpaṃ pabrūhi mārisa;_  
_Tvañca me dīpamakkhāhi,_
