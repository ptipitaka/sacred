<Paragraph number="1305" type="verses" book="sn">
_“Okañjahaṃ taṇhacchidaṃ anejaṃ, __(iccāyasmā bhadrāvudho)___  
_Nandiñjahaṃ oghatiṇṇaṃ vimuttaṃ;_  
_Kappañjahaṃ abhiyāce sumedhaṃ,_  
_Sutvāna nāgassa apanamissanti ito. (1)_  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="1109" book="sn">

<Paragraph number="1306" type="verses" book="sn">
_Nānājanā janapadehi saṅgatā,_  
_Tava vīra vākyaṃ abhikaṅkhamānā;_  
_Tesaṃ tuvaṃ sādhu viyākarohi,_  
_Tathā hi te vidito esa dhammo”. (2)_  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="1110" book="sn">

<Paragraph number="1307" type="verses" book="sn">
_“Ādānataṇhaṃ vinayetha sabbaṃ, __(bhadrāvudhāti bhagavā)___  
_Uddhaṃ adho tiriyañcāpi majjhe;_  
_Yaṃ yañhi lokasmimupādiyanti,_  
_Teneva māro anveti jantuṃ. (3)_  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="1111" book="sn">

<Paragraph number="1308" type="verses" book="sn">
_Tasmā pajānaṃ na upādiyetha,_  
_Bhikkhu sato kiñcanaṃ sabbaloke;_  
_Ādānasatte iti pekkhamāno,_  
_Pajaṃ imaṃ maccudheyye visattan”ti. (4)_  


</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="1309" type="center" book="sn">
Bhadrāvudhamāṇavapucchā dvādasamā.
</Paragraph>
