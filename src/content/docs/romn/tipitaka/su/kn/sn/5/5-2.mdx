<Paragraph number="1230" type="verses" book="sn">
_“Kodha santusito loke, __(iccāyasmā tissametteyyo)___  
_Kassa no santi iñjitā;_  
_Ko ubhantamabhiññāya,_  
_Majjhe mantā na lippati;_  
_Kaṃ brūsi mahāpurisoti,_  
_Ko idha sibbinimaccagā”. (1)_  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="1048" book="sn">

<Paragraph number="1231" type="verses" book="sn">
_“Kāmesu brahmacariyavā, __(metteyyāti bhagavā)___  
_Vītataṇho sadā sato;_  
_Saṅkhāya nibbuto bhikkhu,_  
_Tassa no santi iñjitā. (2)_  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="1049" book="sn">

<Paragraph number="1232" type="verses" book="sn">
_So ubhantamabhiññāya,_  
_Majjhe mantā na lippati;_  
_Taṃ brūmi mahāpurisoti,_  
_So idha sibbinimaccagā”ti. (3)_  


</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="1233" type="center" book="sn">
Tissametteyyamāṇavapucchā dutiyā.
