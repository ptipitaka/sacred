_Upasiṅghi mahāvīro,_  
_saḷalaṃ devagandhikaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

_vipassī lokanāyako;_  
_Upasiṅghi mahāvīro,_  
_pekkhamānāya me tadā._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>

_tato pabbatamāruhiṃ._  

</Paragraph>
