<Paragraph number="144" type="verses" book="cn">
_“Jhāyiṃ virajamāsīnaṃ, __(iccāyasmā udayo)___  
_Katakiccaṃ anāsavaṃ;_  
_Pāraguṃ sabbadhammānaṃ,_  
_Atthi pañhena āgamaṃ;_  
_Aññāvimokkhaṃ pabrūhi,_  
_Avijjāya pabhedanaṃ”. (1)_  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="131" book="cn" bookNo="25" page="17">

<Paragraph number="145" type="verses" book="cn">
_“Pahānaṃ kāmacchandānaṃ, __(udayāti bhagavā)___  
_Domanassāna cūbhayaṃ;_  
_Thinassa ca panūdanaṃ,_  
_Kukkuccānaṃ nivāraṇaṃ. (2)_  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="132" book="cn" bookNo="25" page="18">

<Paragraph number="146" type="verses" book="cn">
_Upekkhāsatisaṃsuddhaṃ,_  
_dhammatakkapurejavaṃ;_  
_Aññāvimokkhaṃ pabrūmi,_  
_avijjāya pabhedanaṃ”. (3)_  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="133" book="cn" bookNo="25" page="18">

<Paragraph number="147" type="verses" book="cn">
_“Kiṃsu saṃyojano loko,_  
_kiṃsu tassa vicāraṇaṃ;_  
_Kissassa vippahānena,_  
_nibbānaṃ iti vuccati”. (4)_  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="134" book="cn" bookNo="25" page="18">

<Paragraph number="148" type="verses" book="cn">
_“Nandisaṃyojano loko,_  
_vitakkassa vicāraṇaṃ;_  
_Taṇhāya vippahānena,_  
_nibbānaṃ iti vuccati”. (5)_  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="135" book="cn" bookNo="25" page="18">

<Paragraph number="149" type="verses" book="cn">
_“Kathaṃ satassa carato,_  
_viññāṇaṃ uparujjhati;_  
_Bhagavantaṃ puṭṭhumāgamma,_  
_taṃ suṇoma vaco tava”. (6)_  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="136" book="cn" bookNo="25" page="18">

<Paragraph number="150" type="verses" book="cn">
_“Ajjhattañca bahiddhā ca,_  
_Vedanaṃ nābhinandato;_  
_Evaṃ satassa carato,_  
_Viññāṇaṃ uparujjhatī”ti. (7)_  


</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="151" type="center" book="cn">
Udayamāṇavapucchā terasamā.
</Paragraph>
