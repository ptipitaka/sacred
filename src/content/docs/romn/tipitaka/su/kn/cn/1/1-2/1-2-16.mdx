<Paragraph number="162" type="verses" book="cn">
_“Jiṇṇohamasmi abalo vītavaṇṇo, __(iccāyasmā piṅgiyo)___  
_Nettā na suddhā savanaṃ na phāsu;_  
_Māhaṃ nassaṃ momuho antarāva,_  
_Ācikkha dhammaṃ yamahaṃ vijaññaṃ;_  
_Jātijarāya idha vippahānaṃ”. (1)_  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="146" book="cn">

<Paragraph number="163" type="verses" book="cn">
_“Disvāna rūpesu vihaññamāne, __(piṅgiyāti bhagavā)___  
_Ruppanti rūpesu janā pamattā;_  
_Tasmā tuvaṃ piṅgiya appamatto,_  
_Jahassu rūpaṃ apunabbhavāya”. (2)_  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="147" book="cn">

<Paragraph number="164" type="verses" book="cn">
_“Disā catasso vidisā catasso,_  
_Uddhaṃ adho dasa disā imāyo;_  
_Na tuyhaṃ adiṭṭhaṃ asutaṃ amutaṃ,_  
_Atho aviññātaṃ kiñcanamatthi loke;_  
_Ācikkha dhammaṃ yamahaṃ vijaññaṃ,_  
_Jātijarāya idha vippahānaṃ”. (3)_  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="148" book="cn">

<Paragraph number="165" type="verses" book="cn">
_“Taṇhādhipanne manuje pekkhamāno, __(piṅgiyāti bhagavā)___  
_Santāpajāte jarasā parete;_  
_Tasmā tuvaṃ piṅgiya appamatto,_  
_Jahassu taṇhaṃ apunabbhavāyā”ti. (4)_  


</Paragraph>
</Division>

Piṅgiyamāṇavapucchā soḷasamā.
