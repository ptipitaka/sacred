<Paragraph number="157" type="verses" book="cn">
_“Dvāhaṃ sakkaṃ apucchissaṃ, __(iccāyasmā mogharājā)___  
_Na me byākāsi cakkhumā;_  
_Yāvatatiyañca devīsi,_  
_Byākarotīti me sutaṃ. (1)_  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="142" book="cn" bookNo="25" page="19">

<Paragraph number="158" type="verses" book="cn">
_Ayaṃ loko paro loko,_  
_brahmaloko sadevako;_  
_Diṭṭhiṃ te nābhijānāti,_  
_gotamassa yasassino. (2)_  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="143" book="cn" bookNo="25" page="19">

<Paragraph number="159" type="verses" book="cn">
_Evaṃ abhikkantadassāviṃ,_  
_atthi pañhena āgamaṃ;_  
_Kathaṃ lokaṃ avekkhantaṃ,_  
_maccurājā na passati”. (3)_  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="144" book="cn" bookNo="25" page="19">

<Paragraph number="160" type="verses" book="cn">
_“Suññato lokaṃ avekkhassu,_  
_Mogharāja sadā sato;_  
_Attānudiṭṭhiṃ ūhacca,_  
_Evaṃ maccutaro siyā;_  
_Evaṃ lokaṃ avekkhantaṃ,_  
_Maccurājā na passatī”ti. (4)_  


</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="161" type="center" book="cn">
Mogharājamāṇavapucchā pannarasamā.
</Paragraph>
