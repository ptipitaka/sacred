<Paragraph number="79" type="verses" book="cn">
_“Pucchāmi taṃ bhagavā brūhi metaṃ, __(iccāyasmā mettagū)___  
_Maññāmi taṃ vedaguṃ bhāvitattaṃ;_  
_Kuto nu dukkhā samudāgatā ime,_  
_Ye keci lokasmimanekarūpā”. (1)_  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="75" book="cn" bookNo="25" page="9">

<Paragraph number="80" type="verses" book="cn">
_“Dukkhassa ve maṃ pabhavaṃ apucchasi, __(mettagūti bhagavā)___  
_Taṃ te pavakkhāmi yathā pajānaṃ;_  
_Upadhinidānā pabhavanti dukkhā,_  
_Ye keci lokasmimanekarūpā. (2)_  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="76" book="cn" bookNo="25" page="9">

<Paragraph number="81" type="verses" book="cn">
_Yo ve avidvā upadhiṃ karoti,_  
_Punappunaṃ dukkhamupeti mando;_  
_Tasmā pajānaṃ upadhiṃ na kayirā,_  
_Dukkhassa jātippabhavānupassī”. (3)_  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="77" book="cn" bookNo="25" page="9">

<Paragraph number="82" type="verses" book="cn">
_“Yaṃ taṃ apucchimha akittayī no,_  
_Aññaṃ taṃ pucchāma tadiṅgha brūhi;_  
_‘Kathaṃ nu dhīrā vitaranti oghaṃ,_  
_Jātiṃ jaraṃ sokapariddavañca’;_  
_Taṃ me muni sādhu viyākarohi,_  
_Tathā hi te vidito esa dhammo”. (4)_  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="78" book="cn" bookNo="25" page="9">

<Paragraph number="83" type="verses" book="cn">
_“Kittayissāmi te dhammaṃ, __(mettagūti bhagavā)___  
_Diṭṭhe dhamme anītihaṃ;_  
_Yaṃ viditvā sato caraṃ,_  
_Tare loke visattikaṃ”. (5)_  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="79" book="cn" bookNo="25" page="9">

<Paragraph number="84" type="verses" book="cn">
_“Tañcāhaṃ abhinandāmi,_  
_mahesi dhammamuttamaṃ;_  
_Yaṃ viditvā sato caraṃ,_  
_tare loke visattikaṃ”. (6)_  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="80" book="cn" bookNo="25" page="9">

<Paragraph number="85" type="verses" book="cn">
_“Yaṃ kiñci sampajānāsi, __(mettagūti bhagavā)___  
_Uddhaṃ adho tiriyañcāpi majjhe;_  
_Etesu nandiñca nivesanañca,_  
_Panujja viññāṇaṃ bhave na tiṭṭhe. (7)_  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="81" book="cn" bookNo="25" page="9">

<Paragraph number="86" type="verses" book="cn">
_Evaṃvihārī sato appamatto,_  
_Bhikkhu caraṃ hitvā mamāyitāni;_  
_Jātiṃ jaraṃ sokapariddavañca,_  
_Idheva vidvā pajaheyya dukkhaṃ”. (8)_  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="82" book="cn" bookNo="25" page="10">

<Paragraph number="87" type="verses" book="cn">
_“Etābhinandāmi vaco mahesino,_  
_Sukittitaṃ gotamanūpadhīkaṃ;_  
_Addhā hi bhagavā pahāsi dukkhaṃ,_  
_Tathā hi te vidito esa dhammo. (9)_  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="83" book="cn" bookNo="25" page="10">

<Paragraph number="88" type="verses" book="cn">
_Te cāpi nūnappajaheyyu dukkhaṃ,_  
_Ye tvaṃ muni aṭṭhitaṃ ovadeyya;_  
_Taṃ taṃ namassāmi samecca nāga,_  
_Appeva maṃ bhagavā aṭṭhitaṃ ovadeyya”. (10)_  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="84" book="cn" bookNo="25" page="10">

<Paragraph number="89" type="verses" book="cn">
_“Yaṃ brāhmaṇaṃ vedagumābhijaññā,_  
_Akiñcanaṃ kāmabhave asattaṃ;_  
_Addhā hi so oghamimaṃ atāri,_  
_Tiṇṇo ca pāraṃ akhilo akaṅkho. (11)_  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="85" book="cn" bookNo="25" page="10">

<Paragraph number="90" type="verses" book="cn">
_Vidvā ca yo vedagū naro idha,_  
_Bhavābhave saṅgamimaṃ visajja;_  
_So vītataṇho anīgho nirāso,_  
_Atāri so jātijaranti brūmī”ti. (12)_  


</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="91" type="center" book="cn">
Mettagūmāṇavapucchā catutthī.
</Paragraph>
