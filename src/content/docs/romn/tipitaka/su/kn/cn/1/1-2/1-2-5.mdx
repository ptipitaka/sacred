<Paragraph number="92" type="verses" book="cn">
_“Pucchāmi taṃ bhagavā brūhi metaṃ, (__(iccāyasmā dhotako)__)_  
_Vācābhikaṅkhāmi mahesi tuyhaṃ;_  
_Tava sutvāna nigghosaṃ,_  
_Sikkhe nibbānamattano”. (1)_  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="87" book="cn" bookNo="25" page="10">

<Paragraph number="93" type="verses" book="cn">
_“Tenahātappaṃ karohi, __(dhotakāti bhagavā)___  
_Idheva nipako sato;_  
_Ito sutvāna nigghosaṃ,_  
_Sikkhe nibbānamattano”. (2)_  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="88" book="cn" bookNo="25" page="10">

<Paragraph number="94" type="verses" book="cn">
_“Passāmahaṃ devamanussaloke,_  
_Akiñcanaṃ brāhmaṇamiriyamānaṃ;_  
_Taṃ taṃ namassāmi samantacakkhu,_  
_Pamuñca maṃ sakka kathaṃkathāhi”. (3)_  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="89" book="cn" bookNo="25" page="11">

<Paragraph number="95" type="verses" book="cn">
_“Nāhaṃ sahissāmi pamocanāya,_  
_Kathaṃkathiṃ dhotaka kañci loke;_  
_Dhammañca seṭṭhaṃ abhijānamāno,_  
_Evaṃ tuvaṃ oghamimaṃ taresi”. (4)_  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="90" book="cn" bookNo="25" page="11">

<Paragraph number="96" type="verses" book="cn">
_“Anusāsa brahme karuṇāyamāno,_  
_Vivekadhammaṃ yamahaṃ vijaññaṃ;_  
_Yathāhaṃ ākāsova abyāpajjamāno,_  
_Idheva santo asito careyyaṃ”. (5)_  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="91" book="cn" bookNo="25" page="11">

<Paragraph number="97" type="verses" book="cn">
_“Kittayissāmi te santiṃ, __(dhotakāti bhagavā)___  
_Diṭṭhe dhamme anītihaṃ;_  
_Yaṃ viditvā sato caraṃ,_  
_Tare loke visattikaṃ”. (6)_  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="92" book="cn" bookNo="25" page="11">

<Paragraph number="98" type="verses" book="cn">
_“Tañcāhaṃ abhinandāmi,_  
_mahesi santimuttamaṃ;_  
_Yaṃ viditvā sato caraṃ,_  
_tare loke visattikaṃ”. (7)_  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="93" book="cn" bookNo="25" page="11">

<Paragraph number="99" type="verses" book="cn">
_“Yaṃ kiñci sampajānāsi, __(dhotakāti bhagavā)___  
_Uddhaṃ adho tiriyañcāpi majjhe;_  
_Etaṃ viditvā saṅgoti loke,_  
_Bhavābhavāya mākāsi taṇhan”ti. (8)_  


</Paragraph>
</Division>

Dhotakamāṇavapucchā pañcamī.
