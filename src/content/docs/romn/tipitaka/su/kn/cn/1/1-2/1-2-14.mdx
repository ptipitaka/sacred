<Paragraph number="152" type="verses" book="cn">
_“Yo atītaṃ ādisati, __(iccāyasmā posālo)___  
_Anejo chinnasaṃsayo;_  
_Pāraguṃ sabbadhammānaṃ,_  
_Atthi pañhena āgamaṃ. (1)_  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="138" book="cn" bookNo="25" page="18">

<Paragraph number="153" type="verses" book="cn">
_Vibhūtarūpasaññissa,_  
_sabbakāyappahāyino;_  
_Ajjhattañca bahiddhā ca,_  
_natthi kiñcīti passato;_  
_Ñāṇaṃ sakkānupucchāmi,_  
_kathaṃ neyyo tathāvidho”. (2)_  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="139" book="cn" bookNo="25" page="18">

<Paragraph number="154" type="verses" book="cn">
_“Viññāṇaṭṭhitiyo sabbā, __(posālāti bhagavā)___  
_Abhijānaṃ tathāgato;_  
_Tiṭṭhantamenaṃ jānāti,_  
_Vimuttaṃ tapparāyaṇaṃ. (3)_  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="140" book="cn" bookNo="25" page="18">

<Paragraph number="155" type="verses" book="cn">
_Ākiñcaññasambhavaṃ ñatvā,_  
_Nandī saṃyojanaṃ iti;_  
_Evametaṃ abhiññāya,_  
_Tato tattha vipassati;_  
_Etaṃ ñāṇaṃ tathaṃ tassa,_  
_Brāhmaṇassa vusīmato”ti. (4)_  


</Paragraph>
