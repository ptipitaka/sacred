_“Yasmiṃ kāmā na vasanti, __(iccāyasmā todeyyo)___  
_Taṇhā yassa na vijjati;_  
_Kathaṃkathā ca yo tiṇṇo,_  
_Vimokkho tassa kīdiso”. (1)_  

</Paragraph>

<Division number="114" book="cn">

<Paragraph number="124" type="verses" book="cn">
_“Yasmiṃ kāmā na vasanti, __(todeyyāti bhagavā)___  
_Taṇhā yassa na vijjati;_  
_Kathaṃkathā ca yo tiṇṇo,_  
_Vimokkho tassa nāparo”. (2)_  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="115" book="cn">

<Paragraph number="125" type="verses" book="cn">
_“Nirāsaso so uda āsasāno,_  
_Paññāṇavā so uda paññakappī;_  
_Muniṃ ahaṃ sakka yathā vijaññaṃ,_  
_Taṃ me viyācikkha samantacakkhu”. (3)_  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="116" book="cn">

<Paragraph number="126" type="verses" book="cn">
_“Nirāsaso so na ca āsasāno,_  
_Paññāṇavā so na ca paññakappī;_  
_Evampi todeyya muniṃ vijāna,_  
_Akiñcanaṃ kāmabhave asattan”ti. (4)_  


</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="127" type="center" book="cn">
Todeyyamāṇavapucchā navamā.
</Paragraph>
