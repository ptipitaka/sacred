<Paragraph number="77" book="it">
Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="78" book="it">
“Nāhaṃ, bhikkhave, aññaṃ ekasaṃyojanampi samanupassāmi yena saṃyojanena saṃyuttā sattā dīgharattaṃ sandhāvanti saṃsaranti yathayidaṃ, bhikkhave, taṇhāsaṃyojanaṃ. Taṇhāsaṃyojanena hi, bhikkhave, saṃyuttā sattā dīgharattaṃ sandhāvanti saṃsarantī”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="79" type="verses" book="it">
_“Taṇhādutiyo puriso,_  
_dīghamaddhāna saṃsaraṃ;_  
_Itthabhāvaññathābhāvaṃ,_  
_saṃsāraṃ nātivattati._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="80" type="verses" book="it">
_Etamādīnavaṃ ñatvā,_  
_Taṇhaṃ dukkhassa sambhavaṃ;_  
_Vītataṇho anādāno,_  
_Sato bhikkhu paribbaje”ti._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="81" book="it">
Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="82" type="center" book="it">
Pañcamaṃ.
</Paragraph>
