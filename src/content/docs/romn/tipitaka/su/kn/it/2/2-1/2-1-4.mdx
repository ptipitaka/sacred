<Paragraph number="192" book="it">
Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="193" book="it">
“Dveme, bhikkhave, dhammā atapanīyā. Katame dve? Idha, bhikkhave, ekacco katakalyāṇo hoti, katakusalo, katabhīruttāṇo, akatapāpo, akataluddo, akatakibbiso. So ‘kataṃ me kalyāṇan’tipi na tappati, ‘akataṃ me pāpan’tipi na tappati. Ime kho, bhikkhave, dve dhammā atapanīyā”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="194" type="verses" book="it">
_“Kāyaduccaritaṃ hitvā,_  
_vacīduccaritāni ca;_  
_Manoduccaritaṃ hitvā,_  
_yañcaññaṃ dosasañhitaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="195" type="verses" book="it">
_Akatvākusalaṃ kammaṃ,_  
_katvāna kusalaṃ bahuṃ;_  
_Kāyassa bhedā sappañño,_  
_saggaṃ so upapajjatī”ti._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="196" book="it">
Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

</Paragraph>
</Division>

Catutthaṃ.
