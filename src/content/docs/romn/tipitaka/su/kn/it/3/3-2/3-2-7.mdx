<Paragraph number="425" book="it">
Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="426" book="it">
“Tīṇimāni, bhikkhave, soceyyāni. Katamāni tīṇi? Kāyasoceyyaṃ, vacīsoceyyaṃ, manosoceyyaṃ—  imāni kho, bhikkhave, tīṇi soceyyānī”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="427" type="verses" book="it">
_“Kāyasuciṃ vacīsuciṃ,_  
_cetosucimanāsavaṃ;_  
_Suciṃ soceyyasampannaṃ,_  
_āhu sabbappahāyinan”ti._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="428" book="it">
Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="429" type="center" book="it">
Sattamaṃ.
</Paragraph>
