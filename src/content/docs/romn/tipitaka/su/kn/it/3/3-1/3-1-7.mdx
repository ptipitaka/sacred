<Paragraph number="362" book="it">
Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="363" book="it">
“Tayome, bhikkhave, āsavā. Katame tayo? Kāmāsavo, bhavāsavo, avijjāsavo—  ime kho, bhikkhave, tayo āsavā”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="364" type="verses" book="it">
_“Samāhito sampajāno,_  
_sato buddhassa sāvako;_  
_Āsave ca pajānāti,_  
_āsavānañca sambhavaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="365" type="verses" book="it">
_Yattha cetā nirujjhanti,_  
_maggañca khayagāminaṃ;_  
_Āsavānaṃ khayā bhikkhu,_  
_nicchāto parinibbuto”ti._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="366" book="it">
Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="367" type="center" book="it">
Sattamaṃ.
</Paragraph>
