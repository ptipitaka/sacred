<Paragraph number="419" book="it">
Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="420" book="it">
“Tīṇimāni, bhikkhave, sucaritāni. Katamāni tīṇi? Kāyasucaritaṃ, vacīsucaritaṃ, manosucaritaṃ—  imāni kho, bhikkhave, tīṇi sucaritānī”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="421" type="verses" book="it">
_“Kāyaduccaritaṃ hitvā,_  
_vacīduccaritāni ca;_  
_Manoduccaritaṃ hitvā,_  
_yañcaññaṃ dosasaṃhitaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="422" type="verses" book="it">
_Akatvākusalaṃ kammaṃ,_  
_katvāna kusalaṃ bahuṃ;_  
_Kāyassa bhedā sappañño,_  
_saggaṃ so upapajjatī”ti._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="423" book="it">
Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="424" type="center" book="it">
Chaṭṭhaṃ.
</Paragraph>
