<Paragraph number="179" book="it">
Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="180" book="it">
“Dvīhi, bhikkhave, dhammehi samannāgato bhikkhu diṭṭheva dhamme sukhaṃ viharati avighātaṃ anupāyāsaṃ apariḷāhaṃ; kāyassa bhedā paraṃ maraṇā sugati pāṭikaṅkhā. Katamehi dvīhi? Indriyesu guttadvāratāya ca, bhojane mattaññutāya ca. Imehi kho, bhikkhave, dvīhi dhammehi samannāgato bhikkhu diṭṭheva dhamme sukhaṃ viharati avighātaṃ anupāyāsaṃ apariḷāhaṃ; kāyassa bhedā paraṃ maraṇā sugati pāṭikaṅkhā”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="181" type="verses" book="it">
_“Cakkhu sotañca ghānañca,_  
_jivhā kāyo tathā mano;_  
_Etāni yassa dvārāni,_  
_suguttāni ca bhikkhuno._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="182" type="verses" book="it">
_Bhojanamhi ca mattaññū,_  
_indriyesu ca saṃvuto;_  
_Kāyasukhaṃ cetosukhaṃ,_  
_sukhaṃ so adhigacchati._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="183" type="verses" book="it">
_Aḍayhamānena kāyena,_  
_Aḍayhamānena cetasā;_  
_Divā vā yadi vā rattiṃ,_  
_Sukhaṃ viharati tādiso”ti._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="184" book="it">
Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="185" type="center" book="it">
Dutiyaṃ.
</Paragraph>
