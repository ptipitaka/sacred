<Paragraph number="32" book="it">
Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="33" book="it">
“Sabbaṃ, bhikkhave, anabhijānaṃ aparijānaṃ tattha cittaṃ avirājayaṃ appajahaṃ abhabbo dukkhakkhayāya. Sabbañca kho, bhikkhave, abhijānaṃ parijānaṃ tattha cittaṃ virājayaṃ pajahaṃ bhabbo dukkhakkhayāyā”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="34" type="verses" book="it">
_“Yo sabbaṃ sabbato ñatvā,_  
_sabbatthesu na rajjati;_  
_Sa ve sabbapariññā so,_  
_sabbadukkhamupaccagā”ti._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="35" book="it">
Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="36" type="center" book="it">
Sattamaṃ.
</Paragraph>
