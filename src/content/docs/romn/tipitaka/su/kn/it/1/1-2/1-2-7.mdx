<Paragraph number="88" book="it">
Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="89" book="it">
“Sekhassa, bhikkhave, bhikkhuno appattamānasassa anuttaraṃ yogakkhemaṃ patthayamānassa viharato bāhiraṃ aṅganti karitvā nāññaṃ ekaṅgampi samanupassāmi yaṃ evaṃ bahūpakāraṃ yathayidaṃ, bhikkhave, kalyāṇamittatā. Kalyāṇamitto, bhikkhave, bhikkhu akusalaṃ pajahati, kusalaṃ bhāvetī”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="90" type="verses" book="it">
_“Kalyāṇamitto yo bhikkhu,_  
_sappatisso sagāravo;_  
_Karaṃ mittānaṃ vacanaṃ,_  
_sampajāno patissato;_  
_Pāpuṇe anupubbena,_  
_sabbasaṃyojanakkhayan”ti._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="91" book="it">
Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="92" type="center" book="it">
Sattamaṃ.
</Paragraph>
