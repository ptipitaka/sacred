<Paragraph number="406" book="it">
Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="407" book="it">
“Tayome, bhikkhave, addhā. Katame tayo? Atīto addhā, anāgato addhā, paccuppanno addhā—  ime kho, bhikkhave, tayo addhā”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="408" type="verses" book="it">
_“Akkheyyasaññino sattā,_  
_akkheyyasmiṃ patiṭṭhitā;_  
_Akkheyyaṃ apariññāya,_  
_yogamāyanti maccuno._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="409" type="verses" book="it">
_Akkheyyañca pariññāya,_  
_akkhātāraṃ na maññati;_  
_Phuṭṭho vimokkho manasā,_  
_santipadamanuttaraṃ._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="410" type="verses" book="it">
_Sa ve akkheyyasampanno,_  
_santo santipade rato;_  
_Saṅkhāyasevī dhammaṭṭho,_  
_saṅkhyaṃ nopeti vedagū”ti._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="411" book="it">
Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="412" type="center" book="it">
Catutthaṃ.
</Paragraph>
