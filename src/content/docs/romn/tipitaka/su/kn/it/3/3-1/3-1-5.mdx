<Paragraph number="350" book="it">
Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="351" book="it">
“Tisso imā, bhikkhave, esanā. Katamā tisso? Kāmesanā, bhavesanā, brahmacariyesanā—  imā kho, bhikkhave, tisso esanā”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="352" type="verses" book="it">
_“Samāhito sampajāno,_  
_sato buddhassa sāvako;_  
_Esanā ca pajānāti,_  
_esanānañca sambhavaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="353" type="verses" book="it">
_Yattha cetā nirujjhanti,_  
_maggañca khayagāminaṃ;_  
_Esanānaṃ khayā bhikkhu,_  
_nicchāto parinibbuto”ti._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="354" book="it">
Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

</Paragraph>
</Division>

Pañcamaṃ.
