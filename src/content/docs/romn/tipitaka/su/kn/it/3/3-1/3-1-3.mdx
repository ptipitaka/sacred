<Paragraph number="338" book="it">
Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="339" book="it">
“Tisso imā, bhikkhave, vedanā. Katamā tisso? Sukhā vedanā, dukkhā vedanā, adukkhamasukhā vedanā—  imā kho, bhikkhave, tisso vedanā”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="340" type="verses" book="it">
_“Samāhito sampajāno,_  
_sato buddhassa sāvako;_  
_Vedanā ca pajānāti,_  
_vedanānañca sambhavaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="341" type="verses" book="it">
_Yattha cetā nirujjhanti,_  
_maggañca khayagāminaṃ;_  
_Vedanānaṃ khayā bhikkhu,_  
_nicchāto parinibbuto”ti._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="342" book="it">
Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="343" type="center" book="it">
Tatiyaṃ.
</Paragraph>
