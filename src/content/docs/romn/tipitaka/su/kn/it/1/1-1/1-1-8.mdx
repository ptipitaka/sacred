<Paragraph number="37" book="it">
Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="38" book="it">
“Mānaṃ, bhikkhave, anabhijānaṃ aparijānaṃ tattha cittaṃ avirājayaṃ appajahaṃ abhabbo dukkhakkhayāya. Mānañca kho, bhikkhave, abhijānaṃ parijānaṃ tattha cittaṃ virājayaṃ pajahaṃ bhabbo dukkhakkhayāyā”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="39" type="verses" book="it">
_“Mānupetā ayaṃ pajā,_  
_mānaganthā bhave ratā;_  
_Mānaṃ aparijānantā,_  
_āgantāro punabbhavaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="40" type="verses" book="it">
_Ye ca mānaṃ pahantvāna,_  
_vimuttā mānasaṅkhaye;_  
_Te mānaganthābhibhuno,_  
_sabbadukkhamupaccagun”ti._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="41" book="it">
Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="42" type="center" book="it">
Aṭṭhamaṃ.
</Paragraph>
