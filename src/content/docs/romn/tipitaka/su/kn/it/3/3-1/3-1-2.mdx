<Paragraph number="332" book="it">
Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="333" book="it">
“Tisso imā, bhikkhave, dhātuyo. Katamā tisso? Rūpadhātu, arūpadhātu, nirodhadhātu—  imā kho, bhikkhave, tisso dhātuyo”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="334" type="verses" book="it">
_“Rūpadhātuṃ pariññāya,_  
_āruppesu asaṇṭhitā;_  
_Nirodhe ye vimuccanti,_  
_te janā maccuhāyino._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="335" type="verses" book="it">
_Kāyena amataṃ dhātuṃ,_  
_phusayitvā nirūpadhiṃ;_  
_Upadhippaṭinissaggaṃ,_  
_sacchikatvā anāsavo;_  
_Deseti sammāsambuddho,_  
_asokaṃ virajaṃ padan”ti._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="336" book="it">
Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="337" type="center" book="it">
Dutiyaṃ.
</Paragraph>
