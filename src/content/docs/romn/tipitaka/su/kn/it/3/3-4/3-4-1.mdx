<Division number="80" book="it">

<Paragraph number="535" book="it">
Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="536" book="it">
“Tayome, bhikkhave, akusalavitakkā. Katame tayo? Anavaññattipaṭisaṃyutto vitakko, lābhasakkārasilokapaṭisaṃyutto vitakko, parānuddayatāpaṭisaṃyutto vitakko. Ime kho, bhikkhave, tayo akusalavitakkā”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="537" type="verses" book="it">
_“Anavaññattisaṃyutto,_  
_lābhasakkāragāravo;_  
_Sahanandī amaccehi,_  
_ārā saṃyojanakkhayā._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="538" type="verses" book="it">
_Yo ca puttapasuṃ hitvā,_  
_Vivāhe saṃharāni ca;_  
_Bhabbo so tādiso bhikkhu,_  
_Phuṭṭhuṃ sambodhimuttaman”ti._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="539" book="it">
Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="540" type="center" book="it">
Paṭhamaṃ.
</Paragraph>
