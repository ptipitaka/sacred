<Paragraph number="287" book="it">
Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="288" book="it">
“Paṭisallānārāmā, bhikkhave, viharatha paṭisallānaratā, ajjhattaṃ cetosamathamanuyuttā, anirākatajjhānā, vipassanāya samannāgatā, brūhetāro suññāgārānaṃ. Paṭisallānārāmānaṃ, bhikkhave, viharataṃ paṭisallānaratānaṃ ajjhattaṃ cetosamathamanuyuttānaṃ anirākatajjhānānaṃ vipassanāya samannāgatānaṃ brūhetānaṃ suññāgārānaṃ dvinnaṃ phalānaṃ aññataraṃ phalaṃ pāṭikaṅkhaṃ—  diṭṭheva dhamme aññā, sati vā upādisese anāgāmitā”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="289" type="verses" book="it">
_“Ye santacittā nipakā,_  
_satimanto ca jhāyino;_  
_Sammā dhammaṃ vipassanti,_  
_kāmesu anapekkhino._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="290" type="verses" book="it">
_Appamādaratā santā,_  
_pamāde bhayadassino;_  
_Abhabbā parihānāya,_  
_nibbānasseva santike”ti._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="291" book="it">
Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="292" type="center" book="it">
Aṭṭhamaṃ.
</Paragraph>
