<Paragraph number="142" book="it">
Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="143" book="it">
“Ekadhammaṃ atītassa, bhikkhave, purisapuggalassa nāhaṃ tassa kiñci pāpakammaṃ akaraṇīyanti vadāmi. Katamaṃ ekadhammaṃ? Yadidaṃ, bhikkhave, sampajānamusāvādo”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="144" type="verses" book="it">
_“Ekadhammaṃ atītassa,_  
_musāvādissa jantuno;_  
_Vitiṇṇaparalokassa,_  
_natthi pāpaṃ akāriyan”ti._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="145" book="it">
Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

</Paragraph>
</Division>

Pañcamaṃ.
