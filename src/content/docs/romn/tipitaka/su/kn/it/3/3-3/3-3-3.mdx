<Paragraph number="465" book="it">
Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="466" book="it">
“Tisso imā, bhikkhave, nissaraṇiyā dhātuyo. Katamā tisso? Kāmānametaṃ nissaraṇaṃ yadidaṃ nekkhammaṃ, rūpānametaṃ nissaraṇaṃ yadidaṃ āruppaṃ, yaṃ kho pana kiñci bhūtaṃ saṅkhataṃ paṭiccasamuppannaṃ nirodho tassa nissaraṇaṃ—  imā kho, bhikkhave, tisso nissaraṇiyā dhātuyo”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="467" type="verses" book="it">
_“Kāmanissaraṇaṃ ñatvā,_  
_rūpānañca atikkamaṃ;_  
_Sabbasaṅkhārasamathaṃ,_  
_phusaṃ ātāpi sabbadā._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="468" type="verses" book="it">
_Sa ve sammaddaso bhikkhu,_  
_yato tattha vimuccati;_  
_Abhiññāvosito santo,_  
_sa ve yogātigo munī”ti._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="469" book="it">
Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="470" type="center" book="it">
Tatiyaṃ.
</Paragraph>
