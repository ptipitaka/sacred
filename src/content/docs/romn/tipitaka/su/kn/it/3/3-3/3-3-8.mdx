<Division number="77" book="it">

<Paragraph number="510" book="it">
Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="511" book="it">
“Bhidurāyaṃ, bhikkhave, kāyo, viññāṇaṃ virāgadhammaṃ, sabbe upadhī aniccā dukkhā vipariṇāmadhammā”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="512" type="verses" book="it">
_“Kāyañca bhiduraṃ ñatvā,_  
_Viññāṇañca virāgunaṃ;_  
_Upadhīsu bhayaṃ disvā,_  
_Jātimaraṇamaccagā;_  
_Sampatvā paramaṃ santiṃ,_  
_Kālaṃ kaṅkhati bhāvitatto”ti._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="513" book="it">
Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="514" type="center" book="it">
Aṭṭhamaṃ.
</Paragraph>
