<Paragraph number="272" book="it">
Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="273" book="it">
“Atthi, bhikkhave, ajātaṃ abhūtaṃ akataṃ asaṅkhataṃ. No cetaṃ, bhikkhave, abhavissa ajātaṃ abhūtaṃ akataṃ asaṅkhataṃ, nayidha jātassa bhūtassa katassa saṅkhatassa nissaraṇaṃ paññāyetha. Yasmā ca kho, bhikkhave, atthi ajātaṃ abhūtaṃ akataṃ asaṅkhataṃ, tasmā jātassa bhūtassa katassa saṅkhatassa nissaraṇaṃ paññāyatī”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="274" type="verses" book="it">
_“Jātaṃ bhūtaṃ samuppannaṃ,_  
_kataṃ saṅkhatamaddhuvaṃ;_  
_Jarāmaraṇasaṅghāṭaṃ,_  
_roganīḷaṃ pabhaṅguraṃ._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="275" type="verses" book="it">
_Āhāranettippabhavaṃ,_  
_nālaṃ tadabhinandituṃ;_  
_Tassa nissaraṇaṃ santaṃ,_  
_atakkāvacaraṃ dhuvaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="276" type="verses" book="it">
_Ajātaṃ asamuppannaṃ,_  
_asokaṃ virajaṃ padaṃ;_  
_Nirodho dukkhadhammānaṃ,_  
_saṅkhārūpasamo sukho”ti._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="277" book="it">
Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="278" type="center" book="it">
Chaṭṭhaṃ.
</Paragraph>
