<Paragraph number="203" book="it">
Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="204" book="it">
“Dvīhi, bhikkhave, dhammehi samannāgato puggalo yathābhataṃ nikkhitto evaṃ sagge. Katamehi dvīhi? Bhaddakena ca sīlena, bhaddikāya ca diṭṭhiyā. Imehi kho, bhikkhave, dvīhi dhammehi samannāgato puggalo yathābhataṃ nikkhitto evaṃ sagge”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="205" type="verses" book="it">
_“Bhaddakena ca sīlena,_  
_bhaddikāya ca diṭṭhiyā;_  
_Etehi dvīhi dhammehi,_  
_yo samannāgato naro;_  
_Kāyassa bhedā sappañño,_  
_saggaṃ so upapajjatī”ti._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="206" book="it">
Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

</Paragraph>
</Division>

Chaṭṭhaṃ.
</Paragraph>
