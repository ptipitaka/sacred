<Paragraph number="413" book="it">
Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="414" book="it">
“Tīṇimāni, bhikkhave, duccaritāni. Katamāni tīṇi? Kāyaduccaritaṃ, vacīduccaritaṃ, manoduccaritaṃ—  imāni kho, bhikkhave, tīṇi duccaritānī”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="415" type="verses" book="it">
_“Kāyaduccaritaṃ katvā,_  
_vacīduccaritāni ca;_  
_Manoduccaritaṃ katvā,_  
_yañcaññaṃ dosasaṃhitaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="416" type="verses" book="it">
_Akatvā kusalaṃ kammaṃ,_  
_katvānākusalaṃ bahuṃ;_  
_Kāyassa bhedā duppañño,_  
_nirayaṃ sopapajjatī”ti._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="417" book="it">
Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="418" type="center" book="it">
Pañcamaṃ.
</Paragraph>
