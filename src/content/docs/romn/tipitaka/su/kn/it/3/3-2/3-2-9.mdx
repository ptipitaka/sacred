<Paragraph number="435" book="it">
Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="436" book="it">
“Yassa kassaci, bhikkhave, rāgo appahīno, doso appahīno, moho appahīno—  ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, ‘baddho mārassa paṭimukkassa mārapāso yathākāmakaraṇīyo pāpimato’. Yassa kassaci, bhikkhave, rāgo pahīno, doso pahīno, moho pahīno—  ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, ‘abaddho mārassa omukkassa mārapāso na yathākāmakaraṇīyo pāpimato’”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="437" type="verses" book="it">
_“Yassa rāgo ca doso ca,_  
_avijjā ca virājitā;_  
_Taṃ bhāvitattaññataraṃ,_  
_brahmabhūtaṃ tathāgataṃ;_  
_Buddhaṃ verabhayātītaṃ,_  
_āhu sabbappahāyinan”ti._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="438" book="it">
Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="439" type="center" book="it">
Navamaṃ.
