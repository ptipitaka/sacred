<Paragraph number="198" book="it">
Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="199" book="it">
“Dvīhi, bhikkhave, dhammehi samannāgato puggalo yathābhataṃ nikkhitto evaṃ niraye. Katamehi dvīhi? Pāpakena ca sīlena, pāpikāya ca diṭṭhiyā. Imehi kho, bhikkhave, dvīhi dhammehi samannāgato puggalo yathābhataṃ nikkhitto evaṃ niraye”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="200" type="verses" book="it">
_“Pāpakena ca sīlena,_  
_pāpikāya ca diṭṭhiyā;_  
_Etehi dvīhi dhammehi,_  
_yo samannāgato naro;_  
_Kāyassa bhedā duppañño,_  
_nirayaṃ sopapajjatī”ti._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="201" book="it">
Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

</Paragraph>
</Division>

Pañcamaṃ.
