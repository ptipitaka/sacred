<Paragraph number="48" book="it">
Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="49" book="it">
“Dosaṃ, bhikkhave, anabhijānaṃ aparijānaṃ tattha cittaṃ avirājayaṃ appajahaṃ abhabbo dukkhakkhayāya. Dosañca kho, bhikkhave, abhijānaṃ parijānaṃ tattha cittaṃ virājayaṃ pajahaṃ bhabbo dukkhakkhayāyā”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="50" type="verses" book="it">
_“Yena dosena duṭṭhāse,_  
_sattā gacchanti duggatiṃ;_  
_Taṃ dosaṃ sammadaññāya,_  
_pajahanti vipassino;_  
_Pahāya na punāyanti,_  
_imaṃ lokaṃ kudācanan”ti._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="51" book="it">
Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

</Paragraph>
</Division>
