<Paragraph number="12" book="it">
Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="13" book="it">
“Ekadhammaṃ, bhikkhave, pajahatha; ahaṃ vo pāṭibhogo anāgāmitāya. Katamaṃ ekadhammaṃ? Mohaṃ, bhikkhave, ekadhammaṃ pajahatha; ahaṃ vo pāṭibhogo anāgāmitāyā”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="14" type="verses" book="it">
_“Yena mohena mūḷhāse,_  
_sattā gacchanti duggatiṃ;_  
_Taṃ mohaṃ sammadaññāya,_  
_pajahanti vipassino;_  
_Pahāya na punāyanti,_  
_imaṃ lokaṃ kudācanan”ti._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="15" book="it">
Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="16" type="center" book="it">
Tatiyaṃ.
</Paragraph>
