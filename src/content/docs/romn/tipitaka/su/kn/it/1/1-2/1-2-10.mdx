<Paragraph number="103" book="it">
Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="104" book="it">
“Idhāhaṃ, bhikkhave, ekaccaṃ puggalaṃ paduṭṭhacittaṃ evaṃ cetasā ceto paricca pajānāmi—  ‘imamhi cāyaṃ samaye puggalo kālaṃ kareyya yathābhataṃ nikkhitto evaṃ niraye’. Taṃ kissa hetu? Cittaṃ hissa, bhikkhave, paduṭṭhaṃ. Cetopadosahetu kho pana, bhikkhave, evamidhekacce sattā kāyassa bhedā paraṃ maraṇā apāyaṃ duggatiṃ vinipātaṃ nirayaṃ upapajjantī”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="105" type="verses" book="it">
_“Paduṭṭhacittaṃ ñatvāna,_  
_ekaccaṃ idha puggalaṃ;_  
_Etamatthañca byākāsi,_  
_buddho bhikkhūna santike._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="106" type="verses" book="it">
_Imamhi cāyaṃ samaye,_  
_kālaṃ kayirātha puggalo;_  
_Nirayaṃ upapajjeyya,_  
_cittaṃ hissa padūsitaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="107" type="verses" book="it">
_Yathā haritvā nikkhipeyya,_  
_evamevaṃ tathāvidho;_  
_Cetopadosahetu hi,_  
_sattā gacchanti duggatin”ti._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="108" book="it">
Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

</Paragraph>
</Division>
