<Paragraph number="306" book="it">
Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="307" book="it">
“Dveme, bhikkhave, āpāyikā nerayikā idamappahāya. Katame dve? Yo ca abrahmacārī brahmacāripaṭiñño, yo ca paripuṇṇaṃ parisuddhaṃ brahmacariyaṃ carantaṃ amūlakena abrahmacariyena anuddhaṃseti. Ime kho, bhikkhave, dve āpāyikā nerayikā idamappahāyā”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="308" type="verses" book="it">
_“Abhūtavādī nirayaṃ upeti,_  
_Yo vāpi katvā na karomi cāha;_  
_Ubhopi te pecca samā bhavanti,_  
_Nihīnakammā manujā parattha._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="309" type="verses" book="it">
_Kāsāvakaṇṭhā bahavo,_  
_pāpadhammā asaññatā;_  
_Pāpā pāpehi kammehi,_  
_nirayaṃ te upapajjare._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="310" type="verses" book="it">
_Seyyo ayoguḷo bhutto,_  
_tatto aggisikhūpamo;_  
_Yañce bhuñjeyya dussīlo,_  
_raṭṭhapiṇḍamasaññato”ti._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="311" book="it">
Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="312" type="center" book="it">
Ekādasamaṃ.
</Paragraph>
