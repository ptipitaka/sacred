<Paragraph number="387" book="it">
Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="388" book="it">
“Tīṇimāni, bhikkhave, puññakiriyavatthūni. Katamāni tīṇi? Dānamayaṃ puññakiriyavatthu, sīlamayaṃ puññakiriyavatthu, bhāvanāmayaṃ puññakiriyavatthu—  imāni kho, bhikkhave, tīṇi puññakiriyavatthūnī”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="389" type="verses" book="it">
_“Puññameva so sikkheyya,_  
_āyataggaṃ sukhudrayaṃ;_  
_Dānañca samacariyañca,_  
_mettacittañca bhāvaye._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="390" type="verses" book="it">
_Ete dhamme bhāvayitvā,_  
_tayo sukhasamuddaye;_  
_Abyābajjhaṃ sukhaṃ lokaṃ,_  
_paṇḍito upapajjatī”ti._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="391" book="it">
Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="392" type="center" book="it">
Paṭhamaṃ.
</Paragraph>
