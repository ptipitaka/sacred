<Paragraph number="220" book="it">
Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="221" book="it">
“Nayidaṃ, bhikkhave, brahmacariyaṃ vussati janakuhanatthaṃ, na janalapanatthaṃ, na lābhasakkārasilokānisaṃsatthaṃ, na ‘iti maṃ jano jānātū’ti. Atha kho idaṃ, bhikkhave, brahmacariyaṃ vussati abhiññatthañceva pariññatthañcā”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="222" type="verses" book="it">
_“Abhiññatthaṃ pariññatthaṃ,_  
_brahmacariyaṃ anītihaṃ;_  
_Adesayi so bhagavā,_  
_nibbānogadhagāminaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="223" type="verses" book="it">
_Esa maggo mahattehi,_  
_anuyāto mahesibhi;_  
_Ye ye taṃ paṭipajjanti,_  
_yathā buddhena desitaṃ;_  
_Dukkhassantaṃ karissanti,_  
_satthusāsanakārino”ti._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="224" book="it">
Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="225" type="center" book="it">
Navamaṃ.
</Paragraph>
