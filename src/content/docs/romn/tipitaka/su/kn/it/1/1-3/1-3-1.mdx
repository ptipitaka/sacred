<Paragraph number="113" book="it">
Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="114" book="it">
“Idhāhaṃ, bhikkhave, ekaccaṃ puggalaṃ pasannacittaṃ evaṃ cetasā ceto paricca pajānāmi—  ‘imamhi cāyaṃ samaye puggalo kālaṃ kareyya yathābhataṃ nikkhitto evaṃ sagge’. Taṃ kissa hetu? Cittaṃ hissa, bhikkhave, pasannaṃ. Cetopasādahetu kho pana, bhikkhave, evamidhekacce sattā kāyassa bhedā paraṃ maraṇā sugatiṃ saggaṃ lokaṃ upapajjantī”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="115" type="verses" book="it">
_“Pasannacittaṃ ñatvāna,_  
_ekaccaṃ idha puggalaṃ;_  
_Etamatthañca byākāsi,_  
_buddho bhikkhūna santike._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="116" type="verses" book="it">
_Imamhi cāyaṃ samaye,_  
_kālaṃ kayirātha puggalo;_  
_Sugatiṃ upapajjeyya,_  
_cittaṃ hissa pasāditaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="117" type="verses" book="it">
_Yathā haritvā nikkhipeyya,_  
_evamevaṃ tathāvidho;_  
_Cetopasādahetu hi,_  
_sattā gacchanti suggatin”ti._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="118" book="it">
Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

</Paragraph>
</Division>

Paṭhamaṃ.
</Paragraph>
