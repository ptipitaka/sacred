<Paragraph number="368" book="it">
Vuttañhetaṃ bhagavatā vuttamarahatāti me sutaṃ—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="369" book="it">
“Tayome, bhikkhave, āsavā. Katame tayo? Kāmāsavo, bhavāsavo, avijjāsavo—  ime kho, bhikkhave, tayo āsavā”ti. Etamatthaṃ bhagavā avoca. Tatthetaṃ iti vuccati—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="370" type="verses" book="it">
_“Yassa kāmāsavo khīṇo,_  
_avijjā ca virājitā;_  
_Bhavāsavo parikkhīṇo,_  
_vippamutto nirūpadhi;_  
_Dhāreti antimaṃ dehaṃ,_  
_jetvā māraṃ savāhinin”ti._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="371" book="it">
Ayampi attho vutto bhagavatā, iti me sutanti.

</Paragraph>

Aṭṭhamaṃ.
