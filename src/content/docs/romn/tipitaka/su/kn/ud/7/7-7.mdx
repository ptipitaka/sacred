<Paragraph number="635" book="ud">
Evaṃ me sutaṃ—  ekaṃ samayaṃ bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena bhagavā attano papañcasaññāsaṅkhāpahānaṃ paccavekkhamāno nisinno hoti.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="636" book="ud">
Atha kho bhagavā attano papañcasaññāsaṅkhāpahānaṃ viditvā tāyaṃ velāyaṃ imaṃ udānaṃ udānesi—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="637" type="verses" book="ud">
_“Yassa papañcā ṭhiti ca natthi,_  
_Sandānaṃ palighañca vītivatto;_  
_Taṃ nittaṇhaṃ muniṃ carantaṃ,_  
_Nāvajānāti sadevakopi loko”ti. (80)_  


</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="638" type="center" book="ud">
Sattamaṃ.
</Paragraph>
