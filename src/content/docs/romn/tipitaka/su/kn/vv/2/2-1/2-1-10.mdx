_Padumesu ca tūriyagaṇā pavajjare,_  
_Imā ca naccanti manoharāyo._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>

<Paragraph number="806" type="verses" book="vv">

_Manussabhūto kimakāsi puññaṃ;_  
_Kenāsi evaṃ jalitānubhāvo,_  
_Vaṇṇo ca te sabbadisā pabhāsatī”ti._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>

_yassa kammassidaṃ phalaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
