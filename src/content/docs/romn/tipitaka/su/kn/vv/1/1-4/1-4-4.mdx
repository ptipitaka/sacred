_yā tvaṃ tiṭṭhasi devate;_

_osadhī viya tārakā._  


(712–713.)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="561" type="verses" book="vv">

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="562" type="verses" book="vv">

_ …pe… _  
_yassa kammassidaṃ phalaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="715" book="vv" bookNo="19" page="59">

<Paragraph number="563" type="verses" book="vv">
_“Ahañca bārāṇasiyaṃ,_  
_buddhassādiccabandhuno;_  
_Adāsiṃ sukkhakummāsaṃ,_  
_pasannā sehi pāṇibhi._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="716" book="vv" bookNo="19" page="59">

<Paragraph number="564" type="verses" book="vv">
_Sukkhāya aloṇikāya ca,_  
_Passa phalaṃ kummāsapiṇḍiyā;_  
_Alomaṃ sukhitaṃ disvā,_  
_Ko puññaṃ na karissati._  


(717–718.)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="565" type="verses" book="vv">
_Tena metādiso vaṇṇo,_  
_ …pe… _  
_Vaṇṇo ca me sabbadisā pabhāsatī”ti._  


</Paragraph>
