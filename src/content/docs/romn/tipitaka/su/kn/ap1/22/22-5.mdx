<Paragraph number="2850" type="verses" book="ap1">
_“Udentaṃ sataraṃsiṃva,_  
_pītaraṃsiṃva bhāṇumaṃ;_  
_Byagghūsabhaṃva pavaraṃ,_  
_sujātaṃ pabbatantare._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="22" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="235">

<Paragraph number="2851" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Buddhassa ānubhāvo so,_  
_jalate pabbatantare;_  
_Raṃse cittaṃ pasādetvā,_  
_kappaṃ saggamhi modahaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="23" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="235">

<Paragraph number="2852" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Avasesesu kappesu,_  
_kusalaṃ caritaṃ mayā;_  
_Tena cittappasādena,_  
_buddhānussatiyāpi ca._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="24" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="235">

<Paragraph number="2853" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Tiṃsakappasahasseto,_  
_yaṃ saññamalabhiṃ tadā;_  
_Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,_  
_buddhasaññāyidaṃ phalaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="25" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="235">

<Paragraph number="2854" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Sattapaññāsakappamhi,_  
_eko āsiṃ janādhipo;_  
_Sujāto nāma nāmena,_  
_cakkavattī mahabbalo._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="26" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="235">

<Paragraph number="2855" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Paṭisambhidā catasso,_  
_ …pe… _  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2856" book="ap1">
Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā raṃsisaññako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="2857" type="center" book="ap1">
Raṃsisaññakattherassāpadānaṃ pañcamaṃ.
</Paragraph>
