_guhāyaṃ pupphamokiriṃ._  

</Paragraph>

_Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,_  
_Pupphadānassidaṃ phalaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>

_cakkavattī mahabbalo._  

</Paragraph>
