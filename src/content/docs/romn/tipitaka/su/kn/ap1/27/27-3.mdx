<Paragraph number="3171" type="verses" book="ap1">
_“Tissassa kho bhagavato,_  
_bodhiyā pādaputtame;_  
_Aḍḍhacandaṃ mayā dinnaṃ,_  
_dharaṇīruhapādape._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="11" book="ap1">

<Paragraph number="3172" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Dvenavute ito kappe,_  
_yaṃ candamabhiropayiṃ;_  
_Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,_  
_bodhipūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="12" book="ap1">

<Paragraph number="3173" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Pañcavīse ito kappe,_  
_devalo nāma khattiyo;_  
_Sattaratanasampanno,_  
_cakkavattī mahabbalo._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="13" book="ap1">

<Paragraph number="3174" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Paṭisambhidā catasso,_  
_ …pe… _  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="3175" book="ap1">

</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="3176" type="center" book="ap1">
Aḍḍhacandiyattherassāpadānaṃ tatiyaṃ.
</Paragraph>
