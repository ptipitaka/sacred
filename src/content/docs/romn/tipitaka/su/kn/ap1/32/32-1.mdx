<Paragraph number="3506" type="verses" book="ap1">
_“Dhammadassissa munino,_  
_vati kārāpitā mayā;_  
_Ārakkho ca mayā dinno,_  
_dvipadindassa tādino._

<Division number="2" book="ap1">

<Paragraph number="3507" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Aṭṭhārase kappasate,_  
_yaṃ kammamakariṃ tadā;_  
_Tena kammavisesena,_  
_patto me āsavakkhayo._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="3" book="ap1">

<Paragraph number="3508" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Paṭisambhidā catasso,_  
_vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;_  
_Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,_  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="3509" book="ap1">
Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā ārakkhadāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

</Paragraph>
</Division>
