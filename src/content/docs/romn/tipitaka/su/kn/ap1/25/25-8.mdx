_“Satta sattalipupphāni,_  
_sīse katvānahaṃ tadā;_  
_Buddhassa abhiropesiṃ,_  
_vessabhumhi naruttame._

_Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,_  
_buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="3077" book="ap1">

</Paragraph>
