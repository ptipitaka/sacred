<Paragraph number="2612" type="verses" book="ap1">
_“Udentaṃ sataraṃsiṃva,_  
_pītaraṃsiṃva bhāṇumaṃ;_  
_Kakudhaṃ vilasantaṃva,_  
_padumuttaranāyakaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

<Division number="25" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="215">

<Paragraph number="2613" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Isimuggāni pisitvā,_  
_madhukhudde anīḷake;_  
_Pāsādeva ṭhito santo,_  
_adāsiṃ lokabandhuno._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="26" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="215">

<Paragraph number="2614" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Aṭṭhasatasahassāni,_  
_ahesuṃ buddhasāvakā;_  
_Sabbesaṃ pattapūrentaṃ,_  
_tato cāpi bahuttaraṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="27" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="215">

<Paragraph number="2615" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Tena cittappasādena,_  
_sukkamūlena codito;_  
_Kappānaṃ satasahassaṃ,_  
_duggatiṃ nupapajjahaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="28" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="215">

<Paragraph number="2616" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Cattālīsamhi sahasse,_  
_kappānaṃ aṭṭhatiṃsa te;_  
_Isimuggasanāmā te,_  
_cakkavattī mahabbalā._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="29" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="216">

<Paragraph number="2617" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Paṭisambhidā catasso,_  
_ …pe… _  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2618" book="ap1">
Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā isimuggadāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="2619" type="center" book="ap1">
Isimuggadāyakattherassāpadānaṃ pañcamaṃ.
