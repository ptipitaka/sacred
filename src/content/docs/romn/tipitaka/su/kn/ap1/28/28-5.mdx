_niyate paṇṇasanthare;_  
_Vutthokāso mayā dinno,_  
_samaṇassa mahesino._

<Division number="20" book="ap1">

<Paragraph number="3250" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Tissassa dvipadindassa,_  
_lokanāthassa tādino;_  
_Añjaliṃ paggahetvāna,_  
_santhariṃ pupphasantharaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="21" book="ap1">

<Paragraph number="3251" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Dvenavute ito kappe,_  
_yaṃ kariṃ pupphasantharaṃ;_  
_Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,_  
_santharassa idaṃ phalaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="22" book="ap1">

<Paragraph number="3252" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Ito cuddasakappamhi,_  
_ahosiṃ manujādhipo;_  
_Ekaañjaliko nāma,_  
_cakkavattī mahabbalo._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>

_ …pe… _  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="3254" book="ap1">

</Paragraph>
