<Paragraph number="3358" type="verses" book="ap1">
_“Ajito nāma nāmena,_  
_ahosiṃ brāhmaṇo tadā;_  
_Āhutiṃ yiṭṭhukāmohaṃ,_  
_nānāpupphaṃ samānayiṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="2" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="276">

<Paragraph number="3359" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Jalantaṃ citakaṃ disvā,_  
_sikhino lokabandhuno;_  
_Tañca pupphaṃ samānetvā,_  
_citake okiriṃ ahaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="3" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="276">

<Paragraph number="3360" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Ekattiṃse ito kappe,_  
_yaṃ pupphamabhipūjayiṃ;_  
_Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,_  
_buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="4" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="276">

<Paragraph number="3361" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Sattavīse ito kappe,_  
_sattāsuṃ manujādhipā;_  
_Supajjalitanāmā te,_  
_cakkavattī mahabbalā._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="5" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="276">

<Paragraph number="3362" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Paṭisambhidā catasso,_  
_vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;_  
_Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,_  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="3363" book="ap1">
Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā citakapūjako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="3364" type="center" book="ap1">
Citakapūjakattherassāpadānaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
</Paragraph>
