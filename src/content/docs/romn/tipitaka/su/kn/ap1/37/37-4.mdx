<Paragraph number="4050" type="verses" book="ap1">
_“Vijjādharo tadā āsiṃ,_  
_himavantamhi pabbate;_  
_Addasaṃ virajaṃ buddhaṃ,_  
_caṅkamantaṃ mahāyasaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="13" book="ap1">

<Paragraph number="4051" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Tīṇi kesarapupphāni,_  
_sīse katvānahaṃ tadā;_  
_Upasaṅkamma sambuddhaṃ,_  
_vessabhuṃ abhipūjayiṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="14" book="ap1">

<Paragraph number="4052" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Ekattiṃse ito kappe,_  
_yaṃ kammamakariṃ tadā;_  
_Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,_  
_buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="15" book="ap1">

<Paragraph number="4053" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Paṭisambhidā catasso,_  
_ …pe… _  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="4054" book="ap1">
Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā kesarapupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="4055" type="center" book="ap1">
Kesarapupphiyattherassāpadānaṃ catutthaṃ.
</Paragraph>
