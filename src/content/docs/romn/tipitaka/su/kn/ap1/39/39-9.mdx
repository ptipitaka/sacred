_“Vipassino pāvacane,_  
_ekaṃ leṇaṃ mayā kataṃ;_  
_Cātuddisassa saṃghassa,_  
_bandhumārājadhāniyā._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="50" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="345">

<Paragraph number="4242" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Dussehi bhūmiṃ leṇassa,_  
_santharitvā pariccajiṃ;_  
_Udaggacitto sumano,_  
_akāsiṃ paṇidhiṃ tadā._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="51" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="345">

<Paragraph number="4243" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Ārādhayeyyaṃ sambuddhaṃ,_  
_pabbajjañca labheyyahaṃ;_  
_Anuttarañca nibbānaṃ,_  
_phuseyyaṃ santimuttamaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="52" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="345">

<Paragraph number="4244" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Teneva sukkamūlena,_  
_kappe navuti saṃsariṃ;_  
_Devabhūto manusso ca,_  
_katapuñño virocahaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="53" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="345">

<Paragraph number="4245" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Tato kammāvasesena,_  
_idha pacchimake bhave;_  
_Campāyaṃ aggaseṭṭhissa,_  
_jātomhi ekaputtako._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="54" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="346">

<Paragraph number="4246" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Jātamattassa me sutvā,_  
_pitu chando ayaṃ ahu;_  
_Dadāmahaṃ kumārassa,_  
_vīsakoṭī anūnakā._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="55" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="346">

<Paragraph number="4247" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Caturaṅgulā ca me lomā,_  
_jātā pādatale ubho;_  
_Sukhumā mudusamphassā,_  
_tūlāpicusamā subhā._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="56" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="346">

<Paragraph number="4248" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Atītā navuti kappā,_  
_ayaṃ eko ca uttari;_  
_Nābhijānāmi nikkhitte,_  
_pāde bhūmyā asanthate._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="57" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="346">

<Paragraph number="4249" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Ārādhito me sambuddho,_  
_pabbajiṃ anagāriyaṃ;_  
_Arahattañca me pattaṃ,_  
_sītibhūtomhi nibbuto._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="58" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="346">

<Paragraph number="4250" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Aggo āraddhavīriyānaṃ,_  
_niddiṭṭho sabbadassinā;_  
_Khīṇāsavomhi arahā,_  
_chaḷabhiñño mahiddhiko._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="59" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="346">

<Paragraph number="4251" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Ekanavutito kappe,_  
_yaṃ dānamadadiṃ tadā;_  
_Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,_  
_leṇadānassidaṃ phalaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>

<Division number="63" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="346">

<Paragraph number="4255" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Thero koṭivīso soṇo,_  
_bhikkhusaṃghassa aggato;_  
_Pañhaṃ puṭṭho viyākāsi,_  
_anotatte mahāsareti._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="4256" book="ap1">
Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā soṇo koṭivīso thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

</Paragraph>
