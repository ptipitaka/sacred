_Ubhohatthehi paggayha,_  
_pūjayiṃ aparājitaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>

_Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,_  
_buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>

<Paragraph number="4076" type="verses" book="ap1">

_ …pe… _  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="4077" book="ap1">

</Paragraph>
