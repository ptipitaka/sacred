_“Nagare bandhumatiyā,_  
_ārāmiko ahaṃ tadā;_  
_Addasaṃ virajaṃ buddhaṃ,_  
_gacchantaṃ anilañjase._  

</Paragraph>

<Division number="33" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="431">

<Paragraph number="6014" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Labujaṃ phalamādāya,_  
_buddhaseṭṭhassadāsahaṃ;_  
_Ākāseva ṭhito santo,_  
_paṭiggaṇhi mahāyaso._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="34" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="432">

<Paragraph number="6015" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Vittisañjanano mayhaṃ,_  
_diṭṭhadhammasukhāvaho;_  
_Phalaṃ buddhassa datvāna,_  
_vippasannena cetasā._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="35" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="432">

<Paragraph number="6016" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Adhigañchiṃ tadā pītiṃ,_  
_vipulaṃ sukhamuttamaṃ;_  
_Uppajjateva ratanaṃ,_  
_nibbattassa tahiṃ tahiṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="36" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="432">

<Paragraph number="6017" type="verses" book="ap1">

_yaṃ phalaṃ adadiṃ tadā;_  
_Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,_  
_phaladānassidaṃ phalaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
