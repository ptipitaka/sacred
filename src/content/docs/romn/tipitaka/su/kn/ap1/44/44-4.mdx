* Ñāṇatthavikattheraapadāna
</Paragraph>

<Division number="35" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="432">

<Paragraph number="5704" type="verses" book="ap1">
_“Kaṇikāraṃva jalitaṃ,_  
_dīparukkhaṃva jotitaṃ;_  
_Kañcanaṃva virocantaṃ,_  
_addasaṃ dvipaduttamaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="36" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="432">

<Paragraph number="5705" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Kamaṇḍaluṃ ṭhapetvāna,_  
_vākacīrañca kuṇḍikaṃ;_  
_Ekaṃsaṃ ajinaṃ katvā,_  
_buddhaseṭṭhaṃ thaviṃ ahaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="37" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="432">

<Paragraph number="5706" type="verses" book="ap1">
_‘Tamandhakāraṃ vidhamaṃ,_  
_mohajālasamākulaṃ;_  
_Ñāṇālokaṃ dassetvāna,_  
_nittiṇṇosi mahāmuni._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="38" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="432">

<Paragraph number="5707" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Samuddharasimaṃ lokaṃ,_  
_sabbāvantamanuttaraṃ;_  
_Ñāṇe te upamā natthi,_  
_yāvatājagatogati._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="39" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="432">

<Paragraph number="5708" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Tena ñāṇena sabbaññū,_  
_iti buddho pavuccati;_  
_Vandāmi taṃ mahāvīraṃ,_  
_sabbaññutamanāvaraṃ’._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="40" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="432">

<Paragraph number="5709" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Satasahassito kappe,_  
_buddhaseṭṭhaṃ thaviṃ ahaṃ;_  
_Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,_  
_ñāṇatthavāyidaṃ phalaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="41" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="432">

<Paragraph number="5710" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,_  
_ …pe… _  
_viharāmi anāsavo._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="42" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="432">

<Paragraph number="5711" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,_  
_ …pe… _  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="43" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="432">

<Paragraph number="5712" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Paṭisambhidā catasso,_  
_ …pe… _  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="5713" book="ap1">
Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā ñāṇatthaviko thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="5714" type="center" book="ap1">
Ñāṇatthavikattherassāpadānaṃ catutthaṃ.
</Paragraph>
