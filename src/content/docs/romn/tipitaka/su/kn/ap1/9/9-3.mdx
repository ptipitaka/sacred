<Paragraph number="1705" type="verses" book="ap1">
_“Rukkhamūle nisinnohaṃ,_  
_byādhito paramena ca;_  
_Paramakāruññapattomhi,_  
_araññe kānane ahaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="17" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="141">

<Paragraph number="1706" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Anukampaṃ upādāya,_  
_tisso satthā upesi maṃ;_  
_Sohaṃ nipannako santo,_  
_sire katvāna añjaliṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="18" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="141">

<Paragraph number="1707" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Pasannacitto sumano,_  
_sabbasattānamuttamaṃ;_  
_Sambuddhaṃ abhivādetvā,_  
_tattha kālaṅkato ahaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="19" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="141">

<Paragraph number="1708" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Dvenavute ito kappe,_  
_yaṃ vandiṃ purisuttamaṃ;_  
_Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,_  
_vandanāya idaṃ phalaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="20" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="142">

<Paragraph number="1709" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Ito pañcamake kappe,_  
_pañcevāsuṃ mahāsikhā;_  
_Sattaratanasampannā,_  
_cakkavattī mahabbalā._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="21" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="142">

<Paragraph number="1710" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Paṭisambhidā catasso,_  
_ …pe… _  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1711" book="ap1">
Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā nipannañjaliko thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="1712" type="center" book="ap1">
Nipannañjalikattherassāpadānaṃ tatiyaṃ.
</Paragraph>
