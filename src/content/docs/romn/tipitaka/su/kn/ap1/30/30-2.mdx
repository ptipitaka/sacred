_ajinuttaravāsano;_  
_Abhiññā pañca nibbattā,_  
_candassa parimajjako._

<Division number="7" book="ap1">

<Paragraph number="3366" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Vipassiṃ lokapajjotaṃ,_  
_disvā abhigataṃ mamaṃ;_  
_Pāricchattakapupphāni,_  
_dhāresiṃ satthuno ahaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="8" book="ap1">

<Paragraph number="3367" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Ekanavutito kappe,_  
_yaṃ pupphamabhipūjayiṃ;_  
_Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,_  
_dhāraṇāya idaṃ phalaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="9" book="ap1">

<Paragraph number="3368" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Sattāsītimhito kappe,_  
_eko āsiṃ mahīpati;_  
_Samantadhāraṇo nāma,_  
_cakkavattī mahabbalo._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="10" book="ap1">

<Paragraph number="3369" type="verses" book="ap1">

_ …pe… _  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="3370" book="ap1">

</Paragraph>
