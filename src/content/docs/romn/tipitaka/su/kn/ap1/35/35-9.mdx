_“Kaṇikāraṃva jotantaṃ,_

_oghatiṇṇamanāsavaṃ._

<Paragraph number="3956" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Tindukaṃ saphalaṃ disvā,_  
_bhinditvāna sakosakaṃ;_  
_Pasannacitto sumano,_  
_sayambhussa madāsahaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="61" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="324">

<Paragraph number="3957" type="verses" book="ap1">

_yaṃ phalamadadiṃ tadā;_  
_Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,_  
_phaladānassidaṃ phalaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>

_ …pe… _  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="3959" book="ap1">

</Paragraph>
