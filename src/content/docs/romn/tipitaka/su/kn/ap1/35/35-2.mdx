<Paragraph number="3893" type="verses" book="ap1">
_“Candabhāgānadītīre,_  
_ahosiṃ vānaro tadā;_  
_Addasaṃ virajaṃ buddhaṃ,_  
_nisinnaṃ pabbatantare._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="12" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="319">

<Paragraph number="3894" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Obhāsentaṃ disā sabbā,_  
_sālarājaṃva phullitaṃ;_  
_Lakkhaṇabyañjanūpetaṃ,_  
_disvā attamano ahuṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="13" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="319">

<Paragraph number="3895" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Udaggacitto sumano,_  
_pītiyā haṭṭhamānaso;_  
_Tīṇi uppalapupphāni,_  
_matthake abhiropayiṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="14" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="319">

<Paragraph number="3896" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Pupphāni abhiropetvā,_  
_vipassissa mahesino;_  
_Sagāravo bhavitvāna,_  
_pakkāmiṃ uttarāmukho._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="15" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="319">

<Paragraph number="3897" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Gacchanto paṭikuṭiko,_  
_vippasannena cetasā;_  
_Selantare patitvāna,_  
_pāpuṇiṃ jīvitakkhayaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="16" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="319">

<Paragraph number="3898" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Tena kammena sukatena,_  
_cetanāpaṇidhīhi ca;_  
_Jahitvā mānusaṃ dehaṃ,_  
_tāvatiṃsamagacchahaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="17" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="319">

<Paragraph number="3899" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Satānaṃ tīṇikkhattuñca,_  
_devarajjaṃ akārayiṃ;_  
_Satānaṃ pañcakkhattuñca,_  
_cakkavattī ahosahaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="18" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="319">

<Paragraph number="3900" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Ekanavutito kappe,_  
_yaṃ pupphamabhipūjayiṃ;_  
_Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,_  
_buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="19" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="319">

<Paragraph number="3901" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Paṭisambhidā catasso,_  
_ …pe… _  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="3902" book="ap1">
Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā tīṇuppalamāliyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

</Paragraph>
</Division>

Tīṇuppalamāliyattherassāpadānaṃ dutiyaṃ.
