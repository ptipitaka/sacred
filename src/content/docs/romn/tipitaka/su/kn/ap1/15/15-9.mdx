_ganthitvāna vaṭaṃsakaṃ;_  
_Buddhassa abhiropesiṃ,_  
_sammukhā lokanāyakaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

_yaṃ pupphamabhiropayiṃ;_  
_Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,_  
_buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>

_cakkavattī mahabbalā._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
