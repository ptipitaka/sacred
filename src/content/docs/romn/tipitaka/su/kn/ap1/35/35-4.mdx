<Paragraph number="3913" type="verses" book="ap1">
_“Himavantassāvidūre,_  
_bhūtagaṇo nāma pabbato;_  
_Tatthaddasaṃ paṃsukūlaṃ,_  
_dumaggamhi vilaggitaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="28" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="320">

<Paragraph number="3914" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Tīṇi kiṅkaṇipupphāni,_  
_ocinitvānahaṃ tadā;_  
_Haṭṭho haṭṭhena cittena,_  
_paṃsukūlaṃ apūjayiṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="29" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="320">

<Paragraph number="3915" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Ekattiṃse ito kappe,_  
_yaṃ kammamakariṃ tadā;_  
_Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,_  
_tiṇṇaṃ pupphānidaṃ phalaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="30" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="320">

<Paragraph number="3916" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Paṭisambhidā catasso,_  
_ …pe… _  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="3917" book="ap1">
Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā tikiṅkaṇipūjako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="3918" type="center" book="ap1">
