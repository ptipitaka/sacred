<Paragraph number="1736" type="verses" book="ap1">
_“Pabbate himavantamhi,_  
_kharājinadharo ahaṃ;_  
_Phussaṃ jinavaraṃ disvā,_  
_phalahattho phalaṃ adaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="40" book="ap1">

<Paragraph number="1737" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Yamahaṃ phalamadāsiṃ,_  
_vippasannena cetasā;_  
_Bhave nibbattamānamhi,_  
_phalaṃ nibbattate mama._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="41" book="ap1">

<Paragraph number="1738" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Dvenavute ito kappe,_  
_yaṃ phalaṃ adadiṃ ahaṃ;_  
_Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,_  
_phaladānassidaṃ phalaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="42" book="ap1">

<Paragraph number="1739" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Paṭisambhidā catasso,_  
_ …pe… _  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1740" book="ap1">
Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā phaladāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="1741" type="center" book="ap1">
Phaladāyakattherassāpadānaṃ sattamaṃ.
</Paragraph>
