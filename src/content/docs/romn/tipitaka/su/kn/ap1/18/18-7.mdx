<Paragraph number="2551" type="verses" book="ap1">
_“Padumuttarassa munino,_  
_saḷale bodhimuttame;_  
_Pasannacitto sumano,_  
_ekadīpaṃ adāsahaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="31" book="ap1">

<Paragraph number="2552" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Bhave nibbattamānamhi,_  
_nibbatte puññasañcaye;_  
_Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,_  
_dīpadānassidaṃ phalaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="32" book="ap1">

<Paragraph number="2553" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Soḷase kappasahasse,_  
_ito te caturo janā;_  
_Candābhā nāma nāmena,_  
_cakkavattī mahabbalā._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="33" book="ap1">

<Paragraph number="2554" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Paṭisambhidā catasso,_  
_ …pe… _  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2555" book="ap1">
Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā ekadīpiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="2556" type="center" book="ap1">
Ekadīpiyattherassāpadānaṃ sattamaṃ.

  
Navamaṃ bhāṇavāraṃ.
</Paragraph>
