<Paragraph number="1030" type="verses" book="ap1">
_“Siddhatthassa bhagavato,_  
_lokajeṭṭhassa tādino;_  
_Kuṭidhūpaṃ mayā dinnaṃ,_  
_vippasannena cetasā._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="162" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="87">

<Paragraph number="1031" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Yaṃ yaṃ yonupapajjāmi,_  
_devattaṃ atha mānusaṃ;_  
_Sabbesampi piyo homi,_  
_dhūpadānassidaṃ phalaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="163" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="87">

<Paragraph number="1032" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Catunnavutito kappe,_  
_yaṃ dhūpamadadiṃ tadā;_  
_Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,_  
_dhūpadānassidaṃ phalaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="164" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="87">

<Paragraph number="1033" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Paṭisambhidā catasso,_  
_vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;_  
_Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,_  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1034" book="ap1">
Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā dhūpadāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="1035" type="center" book="ap1">
Dhūpadāyakattherassāpadānaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
</Paragraph>
