<Paragraph number="2703" type="verses" book="ap1">
_“Nagare dvāravatiyā,_  
_mālāvaccho mamaṃ ahu;_  
_Udapāno ca tattheva,_  
_pādapānaṃ virohano._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="29" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="223">

<Paragraph number="2704" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Sabalena upatthaddho,_  
_siddhattho aparājito;_  
_Mamānukampamāno so,_  
_gacchate anilañjase._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="30" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="223">

<Paragraph number="2705" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Aññaṃ kiñci na passāmi,_  
_pūjāyoggaṃ mahesino;_  
_Asokaṃ pallavaṃ disvā,_  
_ākāse ukkhipiṃ ahaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="31" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="223">

<Paragraph number="2706" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Buddhassa te kisalayā,_  
_gacchato yanti pacchato;_  
_Tāhaṃ disvāna saṃvijiṃ,_  
_aho buddhassuḷāratā._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="32" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="223">

<Paragraph number="2707" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Catunnavutito kappe,_  
_pallavaṃ abhiropayiṃ;_  
_Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,_  
_buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="33" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="223">

<Paragraph number="2708" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Sattatiṃse ito kappe,_  
_eko ekissaro ahu;_  
_Sattaratanasampanno,_  
_cakkavattī mahabbalo._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="34" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="223">

<Paragraph number="2709" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Paṭisambhidā catasso,_  
_ …pe… _  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2710" book="ap1">
Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā kisalayapūjako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="2711" type="center" book="ap1">
