_sambuddhaṃ aparājitaṃ;_  
_Tāhaṃ maghavapupphena,_  
_sayambhuṃ pūjayiṃ tadā._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>

_buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
