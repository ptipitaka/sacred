_abhibhomi narāmare;_  
_Dhāremi antimaṃ dehaṃ,_  
_sammāsambuddhasāsane._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>

_Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,_  
_pupphapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>

_cāturantā janādhipā._  

</Paragraph>
