<Paragraph number="2728" type="verses" book="ap1">
_“Sumaṅgaloti nāmena,_  
_sayambhū aparājito;_  
_Pavanā nikkhamitvāna,_  
_nagaraṃ pāvisī jino._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="48" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="225">

<Paragraph number="2729" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Piṇḍacāraṃ caritvāna,_  
_nikkhamma nagarā muni;_  
_Katakiccova sambuddho,_  
_so vasī vanamantare._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="49" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="225">

<Paragraph number="2730" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Kiṃkaṇipupphaṃ paggayha,_  
_buddhassa abhiropayiṃ;_  
_Pasannacitto sumano,_  
_sayambhussa mahesino._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="50" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="225">

<Paragraph number="2731" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Catunnavutito kappe,_  
_yaṃ pupphamabhiropayiṃ;_  
_Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,_  
_buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="51" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="225">

<Paragraph number="2732" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Chaḷāsītimhito kappe,_  
_apilāsisanāmako;_  
_Sattaratanasampanno,_  
_cakkavattī mahabbalo._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="52" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="225">

<Paragraph number="2733" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Paṭisambhidā catasso,_  
_ …pe… _  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2734" book="ap1">
Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā kiṃkaṇikapupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="2735" type="center" book="ap1">
Kiṃkaṇikapupphiyattherassāpadānaṃ navamaṃ.
</Paragraph>
