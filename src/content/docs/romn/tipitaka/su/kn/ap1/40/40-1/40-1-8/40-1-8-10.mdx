<Paragraph number="4415" type="verses" book="ap1">

_datvā dhammakaruttame;_  
_Pañcānisaṃse anubhomi,_  
_kammānucchavike mama._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>

_dibbaṃ āyuṃ labhāmahaṃ;_  
_Appasayho sadā homi,_  
_corapaccatthikehi vā._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>

_vihesampi na kubbate;_  
_Antarāmaraṇaṃ natthi,_  
_tesaṃ nissandato mama._
</Paragraph>
