<Paragraph number="3432" type="verses" book="ap1">
_“Isisaṃghe ahaṃ pubbe,_  
_āsiṃ mātaṅgavāraṇo;_  
_Mahesīnaṃ pasādena,_  
_padmakesaramokiriṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="2" book="ap1">

<Paragraph number="3433" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Paccekajinaseṭṭhesu,_  
_dhutarāgesu tādisu;_  
_Tesu cittaṃ pasādetvā,_  
_kappaṃ saggamhi modahaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="3" book="ap1">

<Paragraph number="3434" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Ekanavutito kappe,_  
_kesaraṃ okiriṃ tadā;_  
_Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,_  
_buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="4" book="ap1">

<Paragraph number="3435" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Paṭisambhidā catasso,_  
_vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;_  
_Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,_  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="3436" book="ap1">
Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā padumakesariyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="3437" type="center" book="ap1">
Padumakesariyattherassāpadānaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
</Paragraph>
