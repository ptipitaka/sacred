_vāsaṃ kappesahaṃ pure;_  
_Ajinuttaravāsohaṃ,_  
_vasāmi pabbatantare._

<Paragraph number="1724" type="verses" book="ap1">

_Sataraṃsiṃva bhāṇumaṃ;_  
_Vanantaragataṃ disvā,_  
_Sālarājaṃva pupphitaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>

<Paragraph number="1725" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Raṃsyā cittaṃ pasādetvā,_  
_vipassissa mahesino;_  
_Paggayha añjaliṃ vandiṃ,_  
_sirasā ukkuṭī ahaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="33" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="143">

<Paragraph number="1726" type="verses" book="ap1">

_yaṃ kammamakariṃ tadā;_  
_Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,_  
_raṃsisaññāyidaṃ phalaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
