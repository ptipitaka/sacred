_Vipassissābhiropayiṃ;_  
_Udaggacitto sumano,_  
_Dvipadindassa tādino._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>

_yaṃ pupphamabhiropayiṃ;_  
_Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,_  
_buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>

_cakkavattī mahabbalo._  

</Paragraph>
