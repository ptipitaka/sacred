_“Nibbute lokanāthamhi,_  
_atthadassīnaruttame;_  
_Upaṭṭhahiṃ bhikkhusaṃghaṃ,_  
_vippasannena cetasā._

<Division number="85" book="ap1">

<Paragraph number="1167" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Nimantetvā bhikkhusaṃghaṃ,_  
_Ujubhūtaṃ samāhitaṃ;_  
_Ucchunā maṇḍapaṃ katvā,_  
_Bhojesiṃ saṃghamuttamaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="86" book="ap1">

<Paragraph number="1168" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Yaṃ yaṃ yonupapajjāmi,_  
_devattaṃ atha mānusaṃ;_  
_Sabbe satte abhibhomi,_  
_puññakammassidaṃ phalaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="87" book="ap1">

<Paragraph number="1169" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Aṭṭhārase kappasate,_  
_yaṃ dānamadadiṃ tadā;_  
_Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,_  
_ucchudānassidaṃ phalaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>

<Paragraph number="1170" type="verses" book="ap1">

_ …pe… _  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1171" book="ap1">

</Paragraph>
