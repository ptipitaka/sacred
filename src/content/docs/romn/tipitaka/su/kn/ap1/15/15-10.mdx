_lokajeṭṭhassa tādino;_  
_Pallaṅko hi mayā dinno,_  
_sauttarasapacchado._

<Division number="49" book="ap1">

<Paragraph number="2344" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Sattaratanasampanno,_  
_pallaṅko āsi so tadā;_  
_Mama saṅkappamaññāya,_  
_nibbattati sadā mama._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="50" book="ap1">

<Paragraph number="2345" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Tiṃsakappasahassamhi,_  
_pallaṅkamadadiṃ tadā;_  
_Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,_  
_pallaṅkassa idaṃ phalaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="51" book="ap1">

<Paragraph number="2346" type="verses" book="ap1">

_suvaṇṇābhā tayo janā;_  
_Sattaratanasampannā,_  
_cakkavattī mahabbalā._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
