<Paragraph number="2520" type="verses" book="ap1">
_“Koṇḍaññassa bhagavato,_  
_lokajeṭṭhassa tādino;_  
_Ārohatthāya pāsādaṃ,_  
_nisseṇī kāritā mayā._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="10" book="ap1">

<Paragraph number="2521" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Tena cittappasādena,_  
_anubhotvāna sampadā;_  
_Dhāremi antimaṃ dehaṃ,_  
_sammāsambuddhasāsane._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="11" book="ap1">

<Paragraph number="2522" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Ekattiṃsamhi kappānaṃ,_  
_sahassamhi tayo ahuṃ;_  
_Sambahulā nāma rājāno,_  
_cakkavattī mahabbalā._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="12" book="ap1">

<Paragraph number="2523" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Paṭisambhidā catasso,_  
_ …pe… _  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2524" book="ap1">
Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā nisseṇidāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="2525" type="center" book="ap1">
Nisseṇidāyakattherassāpadānaṃ dutiyaṃ.
</Paragraph>
