<Paragraph number="2278" type="verses" book="ap1">
_“Parinibbute bhagavati,_  
_atthadassīnaruttame;_  
_Chattātichattaṃ kāretvā,_  
_thūpamhi abhiropayiṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="2" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="188">

<Paragraph number="2279" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Kālena kālamāgantvā,_  
_namassiṃ lokanāyakaṃ;_  
_Pupphacchadanaṃ katvāna,_  
_chattamhi abhiropayiṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="3" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="188">

<Paragraph number="2280" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Sattarase kappasate,_  
_devarajjamakārayiṃ;_  
_Manussattaṃ na gacchāmi,_  
_thūpapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="4" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="188">

<Paragraph number="2281" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Paṭisambhidā catasso,_  
_vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;_  
_Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,_  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2282" book="ap1">
Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā atichattiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="2283" type="center" book="ap1">
Atichattiyattherassāpadānaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
</Paragraph>
