_“Tisso nāma ahu satthā,_  
_lokajeṭṭho narāsabho;_  
_Tayopadhikkhaye disvā,_  
_dhajaṃ āropitaṃ mayā._  

</Paragraph>

<Division number="21" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="320">

<Paragraph number="3905" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Tena kammena sukatena,_  
_cetanāpaṇidhīhi ca;_  
_Jahitvā mānusaṃ dehaṃ,_  
_tāvatiṃsamagacchahaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="22" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="320">

<Paragraph number="3906" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Satānaṃ tīṇikkhattuñca,_  
_devarajjaṃ akārayiṃ;_  
_Satānaṃ pañcakkhattuñca,_  
_cakkavattī ahosahaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="23" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="320">

<Paragraph number="3907" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Padesarajjaṃ vipulaṃ,_  
_gaṇanāto asaṅkhiyaṃ;_  
_Anubhomi sakaṃ kammaṃ,_  
_pubbe sukatamattano._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="24" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="320">

<Paragraph number="3908" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Dvenavute ito kappe,_  
_yaṃ kammamakariṃ tadā;_  
_Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,_  
_dhajadānassidaṃ phalaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="25" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="320">

<Paragraph number="3909" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Icchamāno cahaṃ ajja,_  
_sakānanaṃ sapabbataṃ;_  
_Khomadussena chādeyyaṃ,_  
_tadā mayhaṃ kate phalaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
