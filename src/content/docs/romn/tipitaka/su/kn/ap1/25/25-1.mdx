<Paragraph number="3034" type="verses" book="ap1">
_“Migaluddo pure āsiṃ,_  
_araññe kānane ahaṃ;_  
_Bharitvā tuvaramādāya,_  
_saṃghassa adadiṃ ahaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="2" book="ap1">

<Paragraph number="3035" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Ekanavutito kappe,_  
_yaṃ dānamadadiṃ tadā;_  
_Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,_  
_tuvarassa idaṃ phalaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="3" book="ap1">

<Paragraph number="3036" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Paṭisambhidā catasso,_  
_vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;_  
_Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,_  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="3037" book="ap1">
Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā tuvaradāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="3038" type="center" book="ap1">
Tuvaradāyakattherassāpadānaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
</Paragraph>
