<Paragraph number="3229" type="verses" book="ap1">
_“Mama saṅkappamaññāya,_  
_satthā lokagganāyako;_  
_Manomayena kāyena,_  
_iddhiyā upasaṅkami._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="6" book="ap1">

<Paragraph number="3230" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Satthāraṃ upasaṅkantaṃ,_  
_vanditvā purisuttamaṃ;_  
_Pasannacitto sumano,_  
_tilamuṭṭhimadāsahaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="7" book="ap1">

<Paragraph number="3231" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Ekanavutito kappe,_  
_yaṃ dānamadadiṃ tadā;_  
_Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,_  
_tilamuṭṭhiyidaṃ phalaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="8" book="ap1">

<Paragraph number="3232" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Ito soḷasakappamhi,_  
_tantiso nāma khattiyo;_  
_Sattaratanasampanno,_  
_cakkavattī mahabbalo._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="9" book="ap1">

<Paragraph number="3233" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Paṭisambhidā catasso,_  
_ …pe… _  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="3234" book="ap1">
Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā tilamuṭṭhidāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="3235" type="center" book="ap1">
Tilamuṭṭhidāyakattherassāpadānaṃ dutiyaṃ.
</Paragraph>
