<Paragraph number="4056" type="verses" book="ap1">
_“Padumo nāma sambuddho,_  
_cittakūṭe vasī tadā;_  
_Disvāna taṃ ahaṃ buddhaṃ,_  
_sayambhuṃ aparājitaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="17" book="ap1">

<Paragraph number="4057" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Aṅkolaṃ pupphitaṃ disvā,_  
_ocinitvānahaṃ tadā;_  
_Upagantvāna sambuddhaṃ,_  
_pūjayiṃ padumaṃ jinaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="18" book="ap1">

<Paragraph number="4058" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Ekattiṃse ito kappe,_  
_yaṃ pupphamabhipūjayiṃ;_  
_Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,_  
_buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="19" book="ap1">

<Paragraph number="4059" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Paṭisambhidā catasso,_  
_ …pe… _  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="4060" book="ap1">
Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā aṅkolapupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="4061" type="center" book="ap1">
Aṅkolapupphiyattherassāpadānaṃ pañcamaṃ.
</Paragraph>
