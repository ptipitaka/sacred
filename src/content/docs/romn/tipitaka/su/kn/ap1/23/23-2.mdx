_“Ekattiṃse ito kappe,_  
_gaṇasatthārako ahaṃ;_  
_Addasaṃ virajaṃ buddhaṃ,_  
_āhutīnaṃ paṭiggahaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="7" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="239">

<Paragraph number="2907" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Cammakhaṇḍaṃ mayā dinnaṃ,_  
_sikhino lokabandhuno;_  
_Tena kammena dvipadinda,_  
_lokajeṭṭha narāsabha._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="8" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="239">

<Paragraph number="2908" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Sampattiṃ anubhotvāna,_  
_kilese jhāpayiṃ ahaṃ;_  
_Dhāremi antimaṃ dehaṃ,_  
_sammāsambuddhasāsane._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="9" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="239">

<Paragraph number="2909" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Ekattiṃse ito kappe,_  
_ajinaṃ yaṃ adāsahaṃ;_  
_Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,_  
_ajinassa idaṃ phalaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="10" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="239">

<Paragraph number="2910" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Ito pañcamake kappe,_  
_rājā āsiṃ sudāyako;_  
_Sattaratanasampanno,_  
_cakkavattī mahabbalo._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="11" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="240">

<Paragraph number="2911" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Paṭisambhidā catasso,_  
_ …pe… _  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2912" book="ap1">
Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā ajinadāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

</Paragraph>
</Division>

Ajinadāyakattherassāpadānaṃ dutiyaṃ.
