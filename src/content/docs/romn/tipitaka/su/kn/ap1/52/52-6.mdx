_“Candabhāgānadītīre,_  
_ahosiṃ kinnaro tadā;_  
_Addasaṃ virajaṃ buddhaṃ,_  
_sayambhuṃ aparājitaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="29" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="431">

<Paragraph number="6729" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Pasannacitto sumano,_  
_vedajāto katañjalī;_  
_Gahetvā ajjunaṃ pupphaṃ,_  
_sayambhuṃ abhipūjayiṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="30" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="431">

<Paragraph number="6730" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Tena kammena sukatena,_  
_cetanāpaṇidhīhi ca;_  
_Jahitvā kinnaraṃ dehaṃ,_  
_tāvatiṃsamagacchahaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="31" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="431">

<Paragraph number="6731" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Chattiṃsakkhattuṃ devindo,_  
_devarajjamakārayiṃ;_  
_Dasakkhattuṃ cakkavattī,_  
_mahārajjamakārayiṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="32" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="431">

<Paragraph number="6732" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Padesarajjaṃ vipulaṃ,_  
_gaṇanāto asaṅkhiyaṃ;_  
_Sukhette vappitaṃ bījaṃ,_  
_sayambhumhi aho mama._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="33" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="431">

<Paragraph number="6733" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Kusalaṃ vijjate mayhaṃ,_  
_pabbajiṃ anagāriyaṃ;_  
_Pūjāraho ahaṃ ajja,_  
_sakyaputtassa sāsane._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="34" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="432">

<Paragraph number="6734" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,_  
_ …pe… _  
_viharāmi anāsavo._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="35" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="432">

<Paragraph number="6735" type="verses" book="ap1">

_ …pe… _  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="36" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="432">

_ …pe… _  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="6737" book="ap1">

</Paragraph>
</Division>
