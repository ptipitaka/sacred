<Paragraph number="449" type="verses" book="ap1">
_“Ajjhāyako mantadharo,_  
_tiṇṇaṃ vedāna pāragū;_  
_Purakkhatomhi sissehi,_  
_upagacchiṃ naruttamaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="435" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="39">

<Paragraph number="450" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Padumuttaro lokavidū,_  
_āhutīnaṃ paṭiggaho;_  
_Mama kammaṃ pakittesi,_  
_saṃkhittena mahāmuni._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="436" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="40">

<Paragraph number="451" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Tāhaṃ dhammaṃ suṇitvāna,_  
_Abhivādetvāna satthuno;_  
_Añjaliṃ paggahetvāna,_  
_Pakkamiṃ dakkhiṇāmukho._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="437" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="40">

<Paragraph number="452" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Saṃkhittena suṇitvāna,_  
_vitthārena abhāsayiṃ;_  
_Sabbe sissā attamanā,_  
_sutvāna mama bhāsato;_  
_Sakaṃ diṭṭhiṃ vinodetvā,_  
_buddhe cittaṃ pasādayuṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="438" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="40">

<Paragraph number="453" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Saṃkhittenapi desemi,_  
_vitthārena tathevahaṃ;_  
_Abhidhammanayaññūhaṃ,_  
_kathāvatthuvisuddhiyā;_  
_Sabbesaṃ viññāpetvāna,_  
_viharāmi anāsavo._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="439" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="40">

<Paragraph number="454" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Ito pañcasate kappe,_  
_caturo suppakāsakā;_  
_Sattaratanasampannā,_  
_catudīpamhi issarā._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="440" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="40">

<Paragraph number="455" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Paṭisambhidā catasso,_  
_vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;_  
_Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,_  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="456" book="ap1">
Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā puṇṇo mantāṇiputto thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="457" type="center" book="ap1">
Puṇṇamantāṇiputtattherassāpadānaṃ pañcamaṃ.
</Paragraph>
