<Paragraph number="1589" type="verses" book="ap1">
_“Āpāṭaliṃ ahaṃ pupphaṃ,_  
_ujjhitaṃ sumahāpathe;_  
_Thūpamhi abhiropesiṃ,_  
_sikhino lokabandhuno._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="2" book="ap1">

<Paragraph number="1590" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Ekattiṃse ito kappe,_  
_yaṃ kammamakariṃ tadā;_  
_Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,_  
_thūpapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="3" book="ap1">

<Paragraph number="1591" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Ito pannarase kappe,_  
_bhūmiyo nāma khattiyo;_  
_Sattaratanasampanno,_  
_cakkavattī mahabbalo._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="4" book="ap1">

<Paragraph number="1592" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Paṭisambhidā catasso,_  
_vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;_  
_Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,_  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1593" book="ap1">
Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā nāgasamālo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="1594" type="center" book="ap1">
Nāgasamālattherassāpadānaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
</Paragraph>
