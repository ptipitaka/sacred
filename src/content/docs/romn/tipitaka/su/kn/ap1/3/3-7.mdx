<Paragraph number="1036" type="verses" book="ap1">
_“Vipassissa bhagavato,_  
_bodhiyā pādaputtame;_  
_Purāṇapulinaṃ hitvā,_  
_suddhapulinamākiriṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="166" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="88">

<Paragraph number="1037" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Ekanavutito kappe,_  
_yaṃ pulinamadāsahaṃ;_  
_Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,_  
_pulinadānassidaṃ phalaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="167" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="88">

<Paragraph number="1038" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Tiṃsatime ito kappe,_  
_rājā āsiṃ janādhibhū;_  
_Mahāpulinanāmena,_  
_cakkavattī mahabbalo._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="168" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="88">

<Paragraph number="1039" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Paṭisambhidā catasso,_  
_vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;_  
_Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,_  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1040" book="ap1">
Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā pulinapūjako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="1041" type="center" book="ap1">
Pulinapūjakattherassāpadānaṃ sattamaṃ.
</Paragraph>
