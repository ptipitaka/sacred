_“Yadā nibbāyi sambuddho,_  
_mahesī padumuttaro;_  
_Samāgamma janā sabbe,_  
_sarīraṃ nīharanti te._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="23" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="337">

<Paragraph number="4124" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Nīharante sarīramhi,_  
_vajjamānāsu bherisu;_  
_Pasannacitto sumano,_  
_paṭṭipupphaṃ apūjayiṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="24" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="337">

<Paragraph number="4125" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Satasahassito kappe,_  
_yaṃ pupphamabhipūjayiṃ;_  
_Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,_  
_sarīrapūjite phalaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="25" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="337">

<Paragraph number="4126" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,_  
_bhavā sabbe samūhatā;_  
_Nāgova bandhanaṃ chetvā,_  
_viharāmi anāsavo._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="26" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="337">

<Paragraph number="4127" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,_  
_Mama buddhassa santike;_  
_Tisso vijjā anuppattā,_  
_Kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="27" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="337">

<Paragraph number="4128" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Paṭisambhidā catasso,_  
_ …pe… _  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="4129" book="ap1">

</Paragraph>
</Division>
