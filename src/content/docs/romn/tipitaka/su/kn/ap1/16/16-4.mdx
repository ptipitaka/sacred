_“Devaputto ahaṃ santo,_  
_pūjayiṃ sikhināyakaṃ;_  
_Kakkārupupphaṃ paggayha,_  
_buddhassa abhiropayiṃ._

<Division number="22" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="196">

<Paragraph number="2380" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Ekattiṃse ito kappe,_  
_yaṃ pupphamabhiropayiṃ;_  
_Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,_  
_buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="23" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="196">

<Paragraph number="2381" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Ito ca navame kappe,_  
_rājā sattuttamo ahuṃ;_  
_Sattaratanasampanno,_  
_cakkavattī mahabbalo._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="24" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="196">

<Paragraph number="2382" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Paṭisambhidā catasso,_  
_ …pe… _  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2383" book="ap1">
Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā kakkārupupphapūjako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="2384" type="center" book="ap1">
