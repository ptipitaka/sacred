_“Ajjhāyako mantadharo,_  
_tiṇṇaṃ vedāna pāragū;_  
_Himavantassāvidūre,_  
_vasāmi assame ahaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

<Division number="76" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="176">

<Paragraph number="2141" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Aggihuttañca me atthi,_  
_puṇḍarīkaphalāni ca;_  
_Puṭake nikkhipitvāna,_  
_dumagge laggitaṃ mayā._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="77" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="177">

<Paragraph number="2142" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Padumuttaro lokavidū,_  
_āhutīnaṃ paṭiggaho;_  
_Mamuddharitukāmo so,_  
_bhikkhanto mamupāgami._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="78" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="177">

<Paragraph number="2143" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Pasannacitto sumano,_  
_phalaṃ buddhassadāsahaṃ;_  
_Vittisañjanano mayhaṃ,_  
_diṭṭhadhammasukhāvaho._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="79" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="177">

<Paragraph number="2144" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Suvaṇṇavaṇṇo sambuddho,_  
_āhutīnaṃ paṭiggaho;_  
_Antalikkhe ṭhito satthā,_  
_imaṃ gāthaṃ abhāsatha._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="80" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="177">

<Paragraph number="2145" type="verses" book="ap1">
_‘Iminā phaladānena,_  
_cetanāpaṇidhīhi ca;_  
_Kappānaṃ satasahassaṃ,_  
_duggatiṃ nupapajjati’._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="81" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="177">

<Paragraph number="2146" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Teneva sukkamūlena,_  
_anubhotvāna sampadā;_  
_Pattomhi acalaṃ ṭhānaṃ,_  
_hitvā jayaparājayaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="82" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="177">

<Paragraph number="2147" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Ito sattasate kappe,_  
_rājā āsiṃ sumaṅgalo;_  
_Sattaratanasampanno,_  
_cakkavattī mahabbalo._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>

_ …pe… _  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”._  

</Paragraph>

</Paragraph>
