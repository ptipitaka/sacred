_bodhiyā okiriṃ ahaṃ;_  
_Mocayissati no mutto,_  
_nibbāpessati nibbuto._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>

_yaṃ bodhimabhisiñcayiṃ;_  
_Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,_  
_bodhisiñcāyidaṃ phalaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>

_kappe āsuṃ janādhipā;_  
_Udakasecanā nāma,_  
_aṭṭhete cakkavattino._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
