<Paragraph number="6691" type="verses" book="ap1">
_“Migaluddo pure āsiṃ,_  
_vipine vicaraṃ ahaṃ;_  
_Addasaṃ virajaṃ buddhaṃ,_  
_sabbadhammāna pāraguṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="2" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="429">

<Paragraph number="6692" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Kurañciyaphalaṃ gayha,_  
_buddhaseṭṭhassadāsahaṃ;_  
_Puññakkhettassa tādino,_  
_pasanno sehi pāṇibhi._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="3" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="429">

<Paragraph number="6693" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Ekatiṃse ito kappe,_  
_yaṃ phalaṃ adadiṃ tadā;_  
_Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,_  
_phaladānassidaṃ phalaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="4" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="429">

<Paragraph number="6694" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,_  
_bhavā sabbe samūhatā;_  
_Nāgova bandhanaṃ chetvā,_  
_viharāmi anāsavo._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="5" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="429">

<Paragraph number="6695" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,_  
_buddhaseṭṭhassa santike;_  
_Tisso vijjā anuppattā,_  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="6" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="429">

<Paragraph number="6696" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Paṭisambhidā catasso,_  
_vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;_  
_Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,_  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="6697" book="ap1">
Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā kurañciyaphaladāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="6698" type="center" book="ap1">
Kurañciyaphaladāyakattherassāpadānaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
</Paragraph>
