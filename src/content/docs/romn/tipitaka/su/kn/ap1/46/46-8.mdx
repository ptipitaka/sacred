<Paragraph number="5941" type="verses" book="ap1">
_“Parinibbute bhagavati,_  
_padumuttare mahāyase;_  
_Pupphavaṭaṃsake katvā,_  
_sarīramabhiropayiṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="50" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="433">

<Paragraph number="5942" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Tattha cittaṃ pasādetvā,_  
_nimmānaṃ agamāsahaṃ;_  
_Devalokagato santo,_  
_puññakammaṃ sarāmahaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="51" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="433">

<Paragraph number="5943" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Ambarā pupphavasso me,_  
_sabbakālaṃ pavassati;_  
_Saṃsarāmi manusse ce,_  
_rājā homi mahāyaso._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="52" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="433">

<Paragraph number="5944" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Tahiṃ kusumavasso me,_  
_abhivassati sabbadā;_  
_Tasseva pupphapūjāya,_  
_vāhasā sabbadassino._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="53" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="433">

<Paragraph number="5945" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Ayaṃ pacchimako mayhaṃ,_  
_carimo vattate bhavo;_  
_Ajjāpi pupphavasso me,_  
_abhivassati sabbadā._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="54" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="433">

<Paragraph number="5946" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Satasahassito kappe,_  
_yaṃ pupphamabhiropayiṃ;_  
_Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,_  
_dehapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="55" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="433">

<Paragraph number="5947" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Kilesā jhāpitā mayhaṃ,_  
_ …pe… _  
_viharāmi anāsavo._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="56" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="433">

<Paragraph number="5948" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Svāgataṃ vata me āsi,_  
_ …pe… _  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="57" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="433">

<Paragraph number="5949" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Paṭisambhidā catasso,_  
_ …pe… _  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="5950" book="ap1">
Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā jātipupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="5951" type="center" book="ap1">
Jātipupphiyattherassāpadānaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.
</Paragraph>
