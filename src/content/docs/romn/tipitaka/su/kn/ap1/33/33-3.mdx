<Paragraph number="3603" type="verses" book="ap1">

_hāso me udapajjatha;_  
_Añjaliṃ paggahetvāna,_  
_bhiyyo cittaṃ pasādayiṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>

_Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,_  
_buddhasaññāyidaṃ phalaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>

</Paragraph>
