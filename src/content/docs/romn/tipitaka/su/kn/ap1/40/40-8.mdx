_Aññe devetirocāmi,_  
_puññakammassidaṃ phalaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>

_caṅkamanto ṭhito cahaṃ;_  
_Channo saḷalapupphehi,_  
_puññakammassidaṃ phalaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>

_Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,_  
_buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
