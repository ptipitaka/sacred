_āhutīnaṃ paṭiggahaṃ._

<Paragraph number="1608" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Bhisāluvañca pādāsiṃ,_  
_udakaṃ hatthadhovanaṃ;_  
_Vanditvā sirasā pāde,_  
_pakkāmi uttarāmukho._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="15" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="133">

<Paragraph number="1609" type="verses" book="ap1">

_bhisāluvamadaṃ tadā;_  
_Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,_  
_puññakammassidaṃ phalaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="16" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="133">

<Paragraph number="1610" type="verses" book="ap1">

_bhisasammatakhattiyo;_  
_Sattaratanasampanno,_  
_cakkavattī mahabbalo._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>

_ …pe… _  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1612" book="ap1">
