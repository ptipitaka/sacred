_Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,_  
_dīpadānassidaṃ phalaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>

_Samantacakkhunāmena,_  
_cakkavattī mahabbalo._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
