_subbate abhivādiya;_  
_Ālambadaṇḍaṃ datvāna,_  
_pakkāmiṃ uttarāmukho._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>

_yaṃ daṇḍamadadiṃ tadā;_  
_Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,_  
_daṇḍadānassidaṃ phalaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
