<Paragraph number="3929" type="verses" book="ap1">
_“Himavantassāvidūre,_  
_jāpalo nāma pabbato;_  
_Buddho sudassano nāma,_  
_vihāsi pabbatantare._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="40" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="321">

<Paragraph number="3930" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Pupphaṃ hemavantaṃ gayha,_  
_Gacchaṃ vehāyasenahaṃ;_  
_Addasaṃ virajaṃ buddhaṃ,_  
_Oghatiṇṇamanāsavaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="41" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="322">

<Paragraph number="3931" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Satta campakapupphāni,_  
_sīse katvānahaṃ tadā;_  
_Buddhassa abhiropesiṃ,_  
_sayambhussa mahesino._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="42" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="322">

<Paragraph number="3932" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Ekattiṃse ito kappe,_  
_yaṃ pupphamabhiropayiṃ;_  
_Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,_  
_buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="43" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="322">

<Paragraph number="3933" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Paṭisambhidā catasso,_  
_ …pe… _  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="3934" book="ap1">
Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā campakapupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

</Paragraph>
</Division>

Campakapupphiyattherassāpadānaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
