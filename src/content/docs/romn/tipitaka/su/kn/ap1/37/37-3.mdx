_āgañchi mama santike._

_mahākāruṇike jine;_  
_Bhisamuḷālaṃ paggayha,_  
_buddhaseṭṭhassadāsahaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>

_yaṃ bhisamadadiṃ tadā;_  
_Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,_  
_bhisadānassidaṃ phalaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>

</Paragraph>
