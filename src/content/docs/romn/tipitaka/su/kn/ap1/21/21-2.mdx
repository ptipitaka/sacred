_“Suvaṇṇavaṇṇo bhagavā,_  
_sataraṃsī patāpavā;_  
_Caṅkamanaṃ samārūḷho,_  
_mettacitto sikhīsabho._  

</Paragraph>

<Division number="6" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="227">

<Paragraph number="2757" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Pasannacitto sumano,_  
_vanditvā ñāṇamuttamaṃ;_  
_Minelapupphaṃ paggayha,_  
_buddhassa abhiropayiṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="7" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="227">

<Paragraph number="2758" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Ekattiṃse ito kappe,_  
_yaṃ pupphamabhipūjayiṃ;_  
_Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,_  
_buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="8" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="227">

<Paragraph number="2759" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Ekūnatiṃsakappamhi,_  
_sumeghaghananāmako;_  
_Sattaratanasampanno,_  
_cakkavattī mahabbalo._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="9" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="228">

<Paragraph number="2760" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Paṭisambhidā catasso,_  
_ …pe… _  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2761" book="ap1">
Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā minelapupphiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

</Paragraph>
</Division>
