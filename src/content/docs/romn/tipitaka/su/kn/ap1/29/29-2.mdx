_“Sinerusamasantoso,_  
_dharaṇīsamasādiso;_  
_Vuṭṭhahitvā samādhimhā,_  
_bhikkhāya mamupaṭṭhito._

<Division number="6" book="ap1">

<Paragraph number="3294" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Harītakaṃ āmalakaṃ,_  
_ambajambuvibhītakaṃ;_  
_Kolaṃ bhallātakaṃ billaṃ,_  
_phārusakaphalāni ca._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="7" book="ap1">

<Paragraph number="3295" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Siddhatthassa mahesissa,_  
_sabbalokānukampino;_  
_Tañca sabbaṃ mayā dinnaṃ,_  
_vippasannena cetasā._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="8" book="ap1">

<Paragraph number="3296" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Catunnavutito kappe,_  
_yaṃ phalaṃ adadiṃ tadā;_  
_Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,_  
_phaladānassidaṃ phalaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="9" book="ap1">

<Paragraph number="3297" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Sattapaññāsito kappe,_  
_ekajjho nāma khattiyo;_  
_Sattaratanasampanno,_  
_cakkavattī mahabbalo._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="10" book="ap1">

<Paragraph number="3298" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Paṭisambhidā catasso,_  
_ …pe… _  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="3299" book="ap1">
Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā phaladāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="3300" type="center" book="ap1">
Phaladāyakattherassāpadānaṃ dutiyaṃ.
</Paragraph>
