<Paragraph number="2799" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Koraṇḍaṃ pupphitaṃ disvā,_  
_samūlaṃ pūjitaṃ mayā;_  
_Haṭṭho haṭṭhena cittena,_  
_avandiṃ padamuttamaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="37" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="231">

<Paragraph number="2800" type="verses" book="ap1">

_yaṃ pupphamabhipūjayiṃ;_  
_Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,_  
_buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>

_eko vītamalo ahuṃ;_  
_Sattaratanasampanno,_  
_cakkavattī mahabbalo._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>

_ …pe… _  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2803" book="ap1">
