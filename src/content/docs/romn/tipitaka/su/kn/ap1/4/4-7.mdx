<Paragraph number="1173" type="verses" book="ap1">
_“Himavantassāvidūre,_  
_tāpaso āsahaṃ tadā;_  
_Lasuṇaṃ upajīvāmi,_  
_lasuṇaṃ mayhabhojanaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="90" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="99">

<Paragraph number="1174" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Khāriyo pūrayitvāna,_  
_saṃghārāmamagacchahaṃ;_  
_Haṭṭho haṭṭhena cittena,_  
_saṃghassa lasuṇaṃ adaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="91" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="99">

<Paragraph number="1175" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Vipassissa naraggassa,_  
_sāsane niratassahaṃ;_  
_Saṃghassa lasuṇaṃ datvā,_  
_kappaṃ saggamhi modahaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="92" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="99">

<Paragraph number="1176" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Ekanavutito kappe,_  
_lasuṇaṃ yamadaṃ tadā;_  
_Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,_  
_lasuṇassa idaṃ phalaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="93" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="99">

<Paragraph number="1177" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Paṭisambhidā catasso,_  
_ …pe… _  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1178" book="ap1">
Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā lasuṇadāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

</Paragraph>
</Division>

Lasuṇadāyakattherassāpadānaṃ sattamaṃ.
