_“Phusso nāmāsi bhagavā,_  
_āhutīnaṃ paṭiggaho;_  
_Gacchate vīthiyaṃ vīro,_  
_nibbāpento mahājanaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="29" book="ap1">

<Paragraph number="2471" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Anupubbena bhagavā,_  
_āgacchi mama santikaṃ;_  
_Tato taṃ pattaṃ paggayha,_  
_sappitelamadāsahaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="30" book="ap1">

<Paragraph number="2472" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Dvenavute ito kappe,_  
_yaṃ sappimadadiṃ tadā;_  
_Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,_  
_sappidānassidaṃ phalaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="31" book="ap1">

<Paragraph number="2473" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Chappaññāse ito kappe,_  
_eko āsi samodako;_  
_Sattaratanasampanno,_  
_cakkavattī mahabbalo._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="32" book="ap1">

<Paragraph number="2474" type="verses" book="ap1">

_ …pe… _  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2475" book="ap1">

</Paragraph>
