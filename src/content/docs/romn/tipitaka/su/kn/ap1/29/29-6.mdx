_“Rathiyaṃ paṭipajjantaṃ,_  
_āhutīnaṃ paṭiggahaṃ;_  
_Dvipadindaṃ mahānāgaṃ,_  
_lokajeṭṭhaṃ narāsabhaṃ._

<Division number="25" book="ap1">

<Paragraph number="3321" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Pakkosāpiya tassāhaṃ,_  
_sabbalokahitesino;_  
_Upaṭṭhāko mayā dinno,_  
_siddhatthassa mahesino._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="26" book="ap1">

<Paragraph number="3322" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Paṭiggahetvā sambuddho,_  
_niyyādesi mahāmuni;_  
_Uṭṭhāya āsanā tamhā,_  
_pakkāmi pācināmukho._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="27" book="ap1">

<Paragraph number="3323" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Catunnavutito kappe,_  
_upaṭṭhākamadaṃ tadā;_  
_Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,_  
_upaṭṭhānassidaṃ phalaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="28" book="ap1">

<Paragraph number="3324" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Sattapaññāsito kappe,_  
_balasenasanāmako;_  
_Sattaratanasampanno,_  
_cakkavattī mahabbalo._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="29" book="ap1">

<Paragraph number="3325" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Paṭisambhidā catasso,_  
_ …pe… _  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="3326" book="ap1">
Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā upaṭṭhākadāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="3327" type="center" book="ap1">
Upaṭṭhākadāyakattherassāpadānaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
