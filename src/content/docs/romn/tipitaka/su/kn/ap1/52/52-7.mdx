_vehāsaṃ agamāsahaṃ;_  
_Tatthaddasāsiṃ sambuddhaṃ,_  
_oghatiṇṇamanāsavaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>

_sire katvāna añjaliṃ;_  
_Buddhassa abhiropesiṃ,_  
_sayambhussa mahesino._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>

_buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
