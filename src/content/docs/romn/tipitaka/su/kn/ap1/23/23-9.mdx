<Paragraph number="2957" type="verses" book="ap1">
_“Asito nāma nāmena,_  
_mālākāro ahaṃ tadā;_  
_Āveḷaṃ paggahetvāna,_  
_rañño dātuṃ vajāmahaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="44" book="ap1">

<Paragraph number="2958" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Asampattomhi rājānaṃ,_  
_addasaṃ sikhināyakaṃ;_  
_Haṭṭho haṭṭhena cittena,_  
_buddhassa abhiropayiṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="45" book="ap1">

<Paragraph number="2959" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Ekattiṃse ito kappe,_  
_yaṃ pupphamabhipūjayiṃ;_  
_Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,_  
_buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="46" book="ap1">

<Paragraph number="2960" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Pañcavīse ito kappe,_  
_rājāhosiṃ mahabbalo;_  
_Vebhāro nāma nāmena,_  
_cakkavattī mahabbalo._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="47" book="ap1">

<Paragraph number="2961" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Paṭisambhidā catasso,_  
_ …pe… _  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2962" book="ap1">
Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā sukatāveḷiyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="2963" type="center" book="ap1">
Sukatāveḷiyattherassāpadānaṃ navamaṃ.
</Paragraph>
