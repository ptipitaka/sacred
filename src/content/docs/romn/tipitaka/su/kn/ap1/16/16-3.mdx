<Paragraph number="2371" type="verses" book="ap1">
_“Udentaṃ sataraṃsiṃva,_  
_pītaraṃsiṃva bhāṇumaṃ;_  
_Pannarase yathā candaṃ,_  
_niyyantaṃ lokanāyakaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="16" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="195">

<Paragraph number="2372" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Aṭṭhasaṭṭhisahassāni,_  
_sabbe khīṇāsavā ahuṃ;_  
_Parivāriṃsu sambuddhaṃ,_  
_dvipadindaṃ narāsabhaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="17" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="195">

<Paragraph number="2373" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Sammajjitvāna taṃ vīthiṃ,_  
_niyyante lokanāyake;_  
_Ussāpesiṃ dhajaṃ tattha,_  
_vippasannena cetasā._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="18" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="195">

<Paragraph number="2374" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Ekanavutito kappe,_  
_yaṃ dhajaṃ abhiropayiṃ;_  
_Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,_  
_dhajadānassidaṃ phalaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="19" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="195">

<Paragraph number="2375" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Ito catutthake kappe,_  
_rājāhosiṃ mahabbalo;_  
_Sabbākārena sampanno,_  
_sudhajo iti vissuto._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="20" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="196">

<Paragraph number="2376" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Paṭisambhidā catasso,_  
_ …pe… _  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2377" book="ap1">
Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā vīthisammajjako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="2378" type="center" book="ap1">
Vīthisammajjakattherassāpadānaṃ tatiyaṃ.
</Paragraph>
