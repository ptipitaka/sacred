<Paragraph number="1180" type="verses" book="ap1">
_“Nibbute lokanāthamhi,_  
_sikhimhi vadataṃ vare;_  
_Haṭṭho haṭṭhena cittena,_  
_avandiṃ thūpamuttamaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="95" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="99">

<Paragraph number="1181" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Vaḍḍhakīhi kathāpetvā,_  
_mūlaṃ datvānahaṃ tadā;_  
_Haṭṭho haṭṭhena cittena,_  
_āyāgaṃ kārapesahaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="96" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="99">

<Paragraph number="1182" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Aṭṭha kappāni devesu,_  
_abbokiṇṇaṃ vasiṃ ahaṃ;_  
_Avasesesu kappesu,_  
_vokiṇṇaṃ saṃsariṃ ahaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="97" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="99">

<Paragraph number="1183" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Kāye visaṃ na kamati,_  
_satthāni na ca hanti me;_  
_Udakehaṃ na miyyāmi,_  
_āyāgassa idaṃ phalaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="98" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="99">

<Paragraph number="1184" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Yadicchāmi ahaṃ vassaṃ,_  
_mahāmegho pavassati;_  
_Devāpi me vasaṃ enti,_  
_puññakammassidaṃ phalaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="99" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="99">

<Paragraph number="1185" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Sattaratanasampanno,_  
_tiṃsakkhattuṃ ahosahaṃ;_  
_Na maṃ kecāvajānanti,_  
_puññakammassidaṃ phalaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="100" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="99">

<Paragraph number="1186" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Ekattiṃse ito kappe,_  
_āyāgaṃ yamakārayiṃ;_  
_Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,_  
_āyāgassa idaṃ phalaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="101" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="100">

<Paragraph number="1187" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Paṭisambhidā catasso,_  
_ …pe… _  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1188" book="ap1">
Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā āyāgadāyako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="1189" type="center" book="ap1">
Āyāgadāyakattherassāpadānaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.
