<Paragraph number="726" type="verses" book="ap1">
_“Padumuttarassa bhagavato,_  
_Lokajeṭṭhassa tādino;_  
_Vatthaṃ khomaṃ mayā dinnaṃ,_  
_Sayambhussa mahesino._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="28" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="62">

<Paragraph number="727" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Taṃ me buddho viyākāsi,_  
_jalajuttaranāmako;_  
_‘Iminā vatthadānena,_  
_hemavaṇṇo bhavissasi._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="29" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="62">

<Paragraph number="728" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Dve sampattī anubhotvā,_  
_kusalamūlehi codito;_  
_Gotamassa bhagavato,_  
_kaniṭṭho tvaṃ bhavissasi._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="30" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="62">

<Paragraph number="729" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Rāgaratto sukhasīlo,_  
_kāmesu gedhamāyuto;_  
_Buddhena codito santo,_  
_tadā tvaṃ pabbajissasi._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="31" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="62">

<Paragraph number="730" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Pabbajitvāna tvaṃ tattha,_  
_kusalamūlena codito;_  
_Sabbāsave pariññāya,_  
_nibbāyissasināsavo’._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="32" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="63">

<Paragraph number="731" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Satta kappasahassamhi,_  
_caturo ceḷanāmakā;_  
_Saṭṭhi kappasahassamhi,_  
_upacelā catujjanā._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="33" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="63">

<Paragraph number="732" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Pañca kappasahassamhi,_  
_ceḷāva caturo janā;_  
_Sattaratanasampannā,_  
_catudīpamhi issarā._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="34" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="63">

<Paragraph number="733" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Paṭisambhidā catasso,_  
_vimokkhāpi ca aṭṭhime;_  
_Chaḷabhiññā sacchikatā,_  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="734" book="ap1">
Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā nando thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="735" type="center" book="ap1">
Nandattherassāpadānaṃ tatiyaṃ.
</Paragraph>
