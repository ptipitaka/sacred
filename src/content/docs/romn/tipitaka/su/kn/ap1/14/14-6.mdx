_“Kaṇikāraṃva jotantaṃ,_  
_nisinnaṃ pabbatantare;_  
_Obhāsentaṃ disā sabbā,_  
_osadhiṃ viya tārakaṃ._

<Division number="42" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="184">

<Paragraph number="2233" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Tayo māṇavakā āsuṃ,_  
_sake sippe susikkhitā;_  
_Khāribhāraṃ gahetvāna,_  
_anventi mama pacchato._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="43" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="184">

<Paragraph number="2234" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Puṭake satta pupphāni,_  
_nikkhittāni tapassinā;_  
_Gahetvā tāni ñāṇamhi,_  
_vessabhussābhiropayiṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="44" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="184">

<Paragraph number="2235" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Ekattiṃse ito kappe,_  
_yaṃ pupphamabhiropayiṃ;_  
_Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,_  
_ñāṇapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="45" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="184">

<Paragraph number="2236" type="verses" book="ap1">

_vipulābhasanāmako;_  
_Sattaratanasampanno,_  
_cakkavattī mahabbalo._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>

_ …pe… _  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2238" book="ap1">

</Paragraph>
