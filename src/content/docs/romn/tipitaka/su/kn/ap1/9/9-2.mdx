<Paragraph number="1697" type="verses" book="ap1">
_“Jātiyā sattavassohaṃ,_  
_pabbajiṃ anagāriyaṃ;_  
_Avandiṃ satthuno pāde,_  
_vippasannena cetasā._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="11" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="141">

<Paragraph number="1698" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Sattanaṅgalakīpupphe,_  
_ākāse ukkhipiṃ ahaṃ;_  
_Tissaṃ buddhaṃ samuddissa,_  
_anantaguṇasāgaraṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="12" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="141">

<Paragraph number="1699" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Sugatānugataṃ maggaṃ,_  
_pūjetvā haṭṭhamānaso;_  
_Añjaliñca tadākāsiṃ,_  
_pasanno sehi pāṇibhi._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="13" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="141">

<Paragraph number="1700" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Dvenavute ito kappe,_  
_yaṃ kammamakariṃ tadā;_  
_Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,_  
_buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="14" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="141">

<Paragraph number="1701" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Ito aṭṭhamake kappe,_  
_tayo aggisikhā ahu;_  
_Sattaratanasampannā,_  
_cakkavattī mahabbalā._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>

_ …pe… _  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1703" book="ap1">
Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā gatasaññako thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

</Paragraph>
</Division>
