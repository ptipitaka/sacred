Tassuddānaṃ


</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="6561" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Kiṅkaṇī paṃsukūlañca,_  
_koraṇḍamatha kiṃsukaṃ;_  
_Upaḍḍhadussī ghatado,_  
_udakaṃ thūpakārako._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="6562" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Naḷakārī ca navamo,_  
_piyālaphaladāyako;_  
_Satamekañca gāthānaṃ,_  
_navakañca taduttari._  


</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="6563" type="center" book="ap1">

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="6564" type="verses" book="ap1">

_sakiṃsammajjakopi ca;_  
_Ekavihārī vibhītakī,_  
_jagatī sālapupphiyo._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="6565" type="verses" book="ap1">

_catuddasasatāni ca._  


</Paragraph>
