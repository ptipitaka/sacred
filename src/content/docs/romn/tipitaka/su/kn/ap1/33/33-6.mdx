_“Suvaṇṇavaṇṇaṃ sambuddhaṃ,_

_Bāttiṃsavaralakkhaṇaṃ._

<Division number="35" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="298">

_anejaṃ aparājitaṃ;_  
_Sambuddhaṃ atināmetvā,_  
_bhojayiṃ taṃ mahāmuniṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="36" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="298">

<Paragraph number="3631" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Muni kāruṇiko loke,_  
_obhāsayi mamaṃ tadā;_  
_Buddhe cittaṃ pasādetvā,_  
_kappaṃ saggamhi modahaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="37" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="298">

<Paragraph number="3632" type="verses" book="ap1">

_yaṃ dānamadadiṃ tadā;_  
_Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,_  
_bhikkhādānassidaṃ phalaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>

_ …pe… _  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="3634" book="ap1">

</Paragraph>
