_addasaṃ lokanāyakaṃ._

_nibbāpeti mahājanaṃ;_  
_Yannūnāhaṃ upāseyyaṃ,_  
_devadevaṃ narāsabhaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="62" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="186">

<Paragraph number="2259" type="verses" book="ap1">

_sakosaṃ pupphamāhariṃ;_  
_Upagantvāna sambuddhaṃ,_  
_adāsiṃ pupphamuttamaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="63" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="186">

<Paragraph number="2260" type="verses" book="ap1">

_yaṃ pupphamabhiropayiṃ;_  
_Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,_  
_pupphadānassidaṃ phalaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>

_virocanasanāmakā;_  
_Tayo āsiṃsu rājāno,_  
_cakkavattī mahabbalā._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
