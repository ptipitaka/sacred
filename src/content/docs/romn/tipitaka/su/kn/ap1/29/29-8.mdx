<Paragraph number="3333" type="verses" book="ap1">
_“Vipassissa bhagavato,_  
_saṃgho sakkatamānito;_  
_Byasanaṃ me anuppattaṃ,_  
_ñātibhedo pure ahu._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="34" book="ap1">

<Paragraph number="3334" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Pabbajjaṃ upagantvāna,_  
_byasanupasamāyahaṃ;_  
_Sattāhābhirato tattha,_  
_satthusāsanakamyatā._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="35" book="ap1">

<Paragraph number="3335" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Ekanavutito kappe,_  
_yamahaṃ pabbajiṃ tadā;_  
_Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,_  
_pabbajjāya idaṃ phalaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="36" book="ap1">

<Paragraph number="3336" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Sattasaṭṭhimhito kappe,_  
_satta āsuṃ mahīpatī;_  
_Sunikkhamāti ñāyanti,_  
_cakkavattī mahabbalā._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="37" book="ap1">

<Paragraph number="3337" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Paṭisambhidā catasso,_  
_ …pe… _  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="3338" book="ap1">
Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā sattāhapabbajito thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

</Paragraph>
</Division>
