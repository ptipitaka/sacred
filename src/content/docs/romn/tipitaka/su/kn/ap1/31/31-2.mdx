_yaṃ vatthamadadiṃ pure;_  
_Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,_  
_gandhadānassidaṃ phalaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>

_suceḷo nāma khattiyo;_  
_Sattaratanasampanno,_  
_cakkavattī mahabbalo._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
