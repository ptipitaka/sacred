<Paragraph number="2596" type="verses" book="ap1">
_“Kaṇikāraṃva jotantaṃ,_  
_nisinnaṃ pabbatantare;_  
_Appameyyaṃva udadhiṃ,_  
_vitthataṃ dharaṇiṃ yathā._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="13" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="214">

<Paragraph number="2597" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Pūjitaṃ devasaṅghena,_  
_nisabhājāniyaṃ yathā;_  
_Haṭṭho haṭṭhena cittena,_  
_upāgacchiṃ naruttamaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="14" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="214">

<Paragraph number="2598" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Sattapupphāni paggayha,_  
_koṭumbarasamākulaṃ;_  
_Buddhassa abhiropesiṃ,_  
_sikhino lokabandhuno._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="15" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="214">

<Paragraph number="2599" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Ekattiṃse ito kappe,_  
_yaṃ pupphamabhiropayiṃ;_  
_Duggatiṃ nābhijānāmi,_  
_buddhapūjāyidaṃ phalaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="16" book="ap1" bookNo="20" page="214">

<Paragraph number="2600" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Ito vīsatikappamhi,_  
_mahānelasanāmako;_  
_Eko āsi mahātejo,_  
_cakkavattī mahabbalo._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>

<Paragraph number="2601" type="verses" book="ap1">
_Paṭisambhidā catasso,_  
_ …pe… _  
_kataṃ buddhassa sāsanaṃ”._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2602" book="ap1">
Itthaṃ sudaṃ āyasmā koṭumbariyo thero imā gāthāyo abhāsitthāti.

</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="2603" type="center" book="ap1">
Koṭumbariyattherassāpadānaṃ tatiyaṃ.
