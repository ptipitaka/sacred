_mahāpakkho mahiddhiko;_  
_Dhammo nāma mahāyakkho,_  
_sabbalokānukampako._

_samādapento mahājanaṃ;_  
_Carāmi gāmanigamaṃ,_  
_samitto saparijjano._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="68" book="cp" bookNo="21" page="405">

<Paragraph number="236" type="verses" book="cp">
_Pāpo kadariyo yakkho,_  
_dīpento dasa pāpake;_  
_Sopettha mahiyā carati,_  
_samitto saparijjano._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="69" book="cp" bookNo="21" page="405">

<Paragraph number="237" type="verses" book="cp">
_Dhammavādī adhammo ca,_  
_ubho paccanikā mayaṃ;_  
_Dhure dhuraṃ ghaṭṭayantā,_  
_samimhā paṭipathe ubho._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="70" book="cp" bookNo="21" page="405">

<Paragraph number="238" type="verses" book="cp">
_Kalaho vattatī bhesmā,_  
_kalyāṇapāpakassa ca;_  
_Maggā okkamanatthāya,_  
_mahāyuddho upaṭṭhito._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="71" book="cp" bookNo="21" page="405">

<Paragraph number="239" type="verses" book="cp">
_Yadihaṃ tassa kuppeyyaṃ,_  
_yadi bhinde tapoguṇaṃ;_  
_Sahaparijanaṃ tassa,_  
_rajabhūtaṃ kareyyahaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="72" book="cp" bookNo="21" page="405">

<Paragraph number="240" type="verses" book="cp">
_Api cāhaṃ sīlarakkhāya,_  
_nibbāpetvāna mānasaṃ;_  
_Saha janenokkamitvā,_  
_pathaṃ pāpassa dāsahaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="73" book="cp" bookNo="21" page="405">

_katvā cittassa nibbutiṃ;_  
_Vivaraṃ adāsi pathavī,_  
_pāpayakkhassa tāvade”ti._  


</Paragraph>
