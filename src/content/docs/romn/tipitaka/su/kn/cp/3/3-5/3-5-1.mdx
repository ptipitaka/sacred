Tassuddānaṃ

<Paragraph number="408" type="verses" book="cp">
_Yudhañjayo somanasso,_  
_ayogharabhisena ca;_  
_Soṇanando mūgapakkho,_  
_kapirājā saccasavhayo._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="409" type="verses" book="cp">
_Vaṭṭako maccharājā ca,_  
_kaṇhadīpāyano isi;_  
_Sutasomo puna āsiṃ,_  
_sāmo ca ekarājahu;_  
_Upekkhāpāramī āsi,_  
_iti vutthaṃ mahesinā._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="410" type="verses" book="cp">
_Evaṃ bahubbidhaṃ dukkhaṃ,_  
_sampattī ca bahubbidhā;_  
_Bhavābhave anubhavitvā,_  
_patto sambodhimuttamaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="411" type="verses" book="cp">
_Datvā dātabbakaṃ dānaṃ,_  
_sīlaṃ pūretvā asesato;_  
_Nekkhamme pāramiṃ gantvā,_  
_patto sambodhimuttamaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="412" type="verses" book="cp">
_Paṇḍite paripucchitvā,_  
_vīriyaṃ katvāna muttamaṃ;_  
_Khantiyā pāramiṃ gantvā,_  
_patto sambodhimuttamaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="413" type="verses" book="cp">
_Katvā daḷhamadhiṭṭhānaṃ,_  
_saccavācānurakkhiya;_  
_Mettāya pāramiṃ gantvā,_  
_patto sambodhimuttamaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="414" type="verses" book="cp">
_Lābhālābhe yasāyase,_  
_sammānanāvamānane;_  
_Sabbattha samako hutvā,_  
_patto sambodhimuttamaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="415" type="verses" book="cp">
_Kosajjaṃ bhayato disvā,_  
_vīriyārambhañca khemato;_  
_Āraddhavīriyā hotha,_  
_esā buddhānusāsanī._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="416" type="verses" book="cp">
_Vivādaṃ bhayato disvā,_  
_avivādañca khemato;_  
_Samaggā sakhilā hotha,_  
_esā buddhānusāsanī._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="417" type="verses" book="cp">
_Pamādaṃ bhayato disvā,_  
_appamādañca khemato;_  
_Bhāvethaṭṭhaṅgikaṃ maggaṃ,_  
_esā buddhānusāsanī._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="418" book="cp">
Itthaṃ sudaṃ bhagavā attano pubbacariyaṃ sambhāvayamāno buddhāpadāniyaṃ nāma dhammapariyāyaṃ abhāsitthāti.

</Paragraph>
