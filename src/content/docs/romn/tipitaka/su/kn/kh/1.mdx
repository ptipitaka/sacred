<Paragraph number="2" type="verses" book="kh">
_Buddhaṃ saraṇaṃ gacchāmi,_  
_Dhammaṃ saraṇaṃ gacchāmi,_  
_Saṃghaṃ saraṇaṃ gacchāmi._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="3" type="verses" book="kh">
_Dutiyampi buddhaṃ saraṇaṃ gacchāmi,_  
_Dutiyampi dhammaṃ saraṇaṃ gacchāmi,_  
_Dutiyampi saṃghaṃ saraṇaṃ gacchāmi._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="4" type="verses" book="kh">
_Tatiyampi buddhaṃ saraṇaṃ gacchāmi,_  
_Tatiyampi dhammaṃ saraṇaṃ gacchāmi,_  
_Tatiyampi saṃghaṃ saraṇaṃ gacchāmi._  


</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="5" type="center" book="kh">
Saraṇattayaṃ.
