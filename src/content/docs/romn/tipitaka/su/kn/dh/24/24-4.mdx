_Na taṃ daḷhaṃ bandhanamāhu dhīrā,_  
_Yadāyasaṃ dārujapabbajañca;_

_Ohārinaṃ sithilaṃ duppamuñcaṃ;_  
_Etampi chetvāna paribbajanti,_  
_Anapekkhino kāmasukhaṃ pahāya._
