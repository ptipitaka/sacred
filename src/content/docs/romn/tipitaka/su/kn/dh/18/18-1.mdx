_Yamapurisāpi ca te upaṭṭhitā;_  
_Uyyogamukhe ca tiṭṭhasi,_  
_Pātheyyampi ca te na vijjati._

_Khippaṃ vāyama paṇḍito bhava;_  
_Niddhantamalo anaṅgaṇo,_  
_Dibbaṃ ariyabhūmiṃ upehisi._  

</Paragraph>

_Vāso te natthi antarā,_  
_Pātheyyampi ca te na vijjati._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
