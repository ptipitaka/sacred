_khippameva nigacchati._

_sarīrassa va bhedanaṃ;_  
_Garukaṃ vāpi ābādhaṃ,_  
_cittakkhepaṃ va pāpuṇe._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>

_Abbhakkhānaṃ va dāruṇaṃ;_  
_Parikkhayaṃ va ñātīnaṃ,_  
_Bhogānaṃ va pabhaṅguraṃ._  

</Paragraph>
