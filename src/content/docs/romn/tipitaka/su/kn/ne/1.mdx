<Paragraph number="2" type="verses" book="ne">
_Yaṃ loko pūjayate,_  
_Salokapālo sadā namassati ca;_  
_Tasseta sāsanavaraṃ,_  
_Vidūhi ñeyyaṃ naravarassa._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="3" type="verses" book="ne">
_Dvādasa padāni suttaṃ,_  
_Taṃ sabbaṃ byañjanañca attho ca;_  
_Taṃ viññeyyaṃ ubhayaṃ,_  
_Ko attho byañjanaṃ katamaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="4" type="verses" book="ne">
_Soḷasahārā netti,_  
_Pañcanayā sāsanassa pariyeṭṭhi;_  
_Aṭṭhārasamūlapadā,_  
_Mahakaccānena niddiṭṭhā._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="5" type="verses" book="ne">
_Hārā byañjanavicayo,_  
_Suttassa nayā tayo ca suttattho;_  
_Ubhayaṃ pariggahītaṃ,_  
_Vuccati suttaṃ yathāsuttaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="6" type="verses" book="ne">
_Yā ceva desanā yañca,_  
_Desitaṃ ubhayameva viññeyyaṃ;_  
_Tatrāyamānupubbī,_  
_Navavidhasuttantapariyeṭṭhīti._  


</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="7" type="center" book="ne">
Saṅgahavāro.
</Paragraph>
