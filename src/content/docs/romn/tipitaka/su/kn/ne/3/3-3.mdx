<Paragraph number="47" type="verses" book="ne">
_Akkharaṃ padaṃ byañjanaṃ,_  
_nirutti tatheva niddeso;_  
_Ākārachaṭṭhavacanaṃ,_  
_ettāva byañjanaṃ sabbaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="48" type="verses" book="ne">
_Saṅkāsanā pakāsanā,_  
_Vivaraṇā vibhajanuttānīkammapaññatti;_  
_Etehi chahi padehi,_  
_Attho kammañca niddiṭṭhaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="49" type="verses" book="ne">
_Tīṇi ca nayā anūnā,_  
_Atthassa ca chappadāni gaṇitāni;_  
_Navahi padehi bhagavato,_  
_Vacanassattho samāyutto._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="50" type="verses" book="ne">
_Atthassa navappadāni,_  
_Byañjanapariyeṭṭhiyā catubbīsa;_  
_Ubhayaṃ saṅkalayitvā,_  
_Tettiṃsā ettikā nettīti._  


</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="51" type="center" book="ne">
Niddesavāro.
</Paragraph>
