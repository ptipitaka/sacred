Kathaṃ <Emphasis>adhiṭṭhānaṭṭhena indriyāni daṭṭhabbāni?</Emphasis> Saddhindriyassa bhāvanāya chando uppajjati—  chandavasena saddhāvasena saddhindriyaṃ adhiṭṭhāti. Chandavasena pāmojjaṃ uppajjati—  pāmojjavasena saddhāvasena saddhindriyaṃ adhiṭṭhāti …pe…  evaṃ adhiṭṭhānaṭṭhena indriyāni daṭṭhabbāni.
