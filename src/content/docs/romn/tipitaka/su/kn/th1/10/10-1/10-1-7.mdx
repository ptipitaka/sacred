* (Apara) Gotamattheragāthā
</Paragraph>

<Division number="587" book="th1" bookNo="19" page="306">

<Paragraph number="896" type="verses" book="th1">
_“Vijāneyya sakaṃ atthaṃ,_  
_Avalokeyyātha pāvacanaṃ;_  
_Yañcettha assa patirūpaṃ,_  
_Sāmaññaṃ ajjhupagatassa._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="588" book="th1" bookNo="19" page="306">

<Paragraph number="897" type="verses" book="th1">
_Mittaṃ idha ca kalyāṇaṃ,_  
_Sikkhā vipulaṃ samādānaṃ;_  
_Sussūsā ca garūnaṃ,_  
_Etaṃ samaṇassa patirūpaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="589" book="th1" bookNo="19" page="306">

<Paragraph number="898" type="verses" book="th1">
_Buddhesu sagāravatā,_  
_Dhamme apaciti yathābhūtaṃ;_  
_Saṃghe ca cittikāro,_  
_Etaṃ samaṇassa patirūpaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="590" book="th1" bookNo="19" page="306">

<Paragraph number="899" type="verses" book="th1">
_Ācāragocare yutto,_  
_Ājīvo sodhito agārayho;_  
_Cittassa ca saṇṭhapanaṃ,_  
_Etaṃ samaṇassa patirūpaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="591" book="th1" bookNo="19" page="306">

<Paragraph number="900" type="verses" book="th1">
_Cārittaṃ atha vārittaṃ,_  
_Iriyāpathiyaṃ pasādaniyaṃ;_  
_Adhicitte ca āyogo,_  
_Etaṃ samaṇassa patirūpaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="592" book="th1" bookNo="19" page="306">

<Paragraph number="901" type="verses" book="th1">
_Āraññakāni senāsanāni,_  
_Pantāni appasaddāni;_  
_Bhajitabbāni muninā,_  
_Etaṃ samaṇassa patirūpaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="593" book="th1" bookNo="19" page="306">

<Paragraph number="902" type="verses" book="th1">
_Sīlañca bāhusaccañca,_  
_Dhammānaṃ pavicayo yathābhūtaṃ;_  
_Saccānaṃ abhisamayo,_  
_Etaṃ samaṇassa patirūpaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="594" book="th1" bookNo="19" page="306">

<Paragraph number="903" type="verses" book="th1">
_Bhāveyya ca aniccanti,_  
_Anattasaññaṃ asubhasaññañca;_  
_Lokamhi ca anabhiratiṃ,_  
_Etaṃ samaṇassa patirūpaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="595" book="th1" bookNo="19" page="306">

<Paragraph number="904" type="verses" book="th1">
_Bhāveyya ca bojjhaṅge,_  
_Iddhipādāni indriyāni balāni;_  
_Aṭṭhaṅgamaggamariyaṃ,_  
_Etaṃ samaṇassa patirūpaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="596" book="th1" bookNo="19" page="306">

<Paragraph number="905" type="verses" book="th1">
_Taṇhaṃ pajaheyya muni,_  
_Samūlake āsave padāleyya;_  
_Vihareyya vippamutto,_  
_Etaṃ samaṇassa patirūpan”ti._  


</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="906" type="center" book="th1">
…  Gotamo thero… .



</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="907" type="center" book="th1">
Dasakanipāto niṭṭhito.



</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="908" type="center" book="th1">
Tatruddānaṃ


</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="909" type="verses" book="th1">
_Kāḷudāyī ca so thero,_  
_ekavihārī ca kappino;_  
_Cūḷapanthako kappo ca,_  
_upaseno ca gotamo;_  
_Sattime dasake therā,_  
_gāthāyo cettha sattatīti._
</Paragraph>
