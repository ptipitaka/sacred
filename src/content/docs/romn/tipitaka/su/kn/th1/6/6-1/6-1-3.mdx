</Paragraph>

_dhammarājassa sāsane._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="390" book="th1">

<Paragraph number="660" type="verses" book="th1">
_Yassa sabrahmacārīsu,_  
_gāravo upalabbhati;_  
_Na vihāyati saddhammā,_  
_maccho bavhodake yathā._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>

_gāravo upalabbhati;_

_khette bījaṃva bhaddakaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
