<Paragraph number="11" type="verses" book="th1">
_“Sabbhireva samāsetha,_  
_paṇḍitehatthadassibhi;_  
_Atthaṃ mahantaṃ gambhīraṃ,_  
_duddasaṃ nipuṇaṃ aṇuṃ;_  
_Dhīrā samadhigacchanti,_  
_appamattā vicakkhaṇā”ti._  

</Paragraph>
