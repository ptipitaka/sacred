<Paragraph number="548" type="verses" book="th1">
_“Dhammo have rakkhati dhammacāriṃ,_  
_Dhammo suciṇṇo sukhamāvahati;_  
_Esānisaṃso dhamme suciṇṇe,_  
_Na duggatiṃ gacchati dhammacārī._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="304" book="th1" bookNo="19" page="272">

<Paragraph number="549" type="verses" book="th1">
_Na hi dhammo adhammo ca,_  
_ubho samavipākino;_  
_Adhammo nirayaṃ neti,_  
_dhammo pāpeti suggatiṃ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="305" book="th1" bookNo="19" page="272">

<Paragraph number="550" type="verses" book="th1">
_Tasmā hi dhammesu kareyya chandaṃ,_  
_Iti modamāno sugatena tādinā;_  
_Dhamme ṭhitā sugatavarassa sāvakā,_  
_Nīyanti dhīrā saraṇavaraggagāmino”._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>

_Taṇhājālo samūhato;_  
_So khīṇasaṃsāro na catthi kiñcanaṃ,_  
_Cando yathā dosinā puṇṇamāsiyan”ti._
