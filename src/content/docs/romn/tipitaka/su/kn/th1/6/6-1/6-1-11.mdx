_viharaṃ kānane vane;_  
_Paviddhagocare lūkhe,_  
_kathaṃ bhikkhu karissasi”._

<Division number="436" book="th1" bookNo="19" page="288">

<Paragraph number="714" type="verses" book="th1">
_“Pītisukhena vipulena,_  
_pharitvāna samussayaṃ;_  
_Lūkhampi abhisambhonto,_  
_viharissāmi kānane._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="437" book="th1" bookNo="19" page="288">

<Paragraph number="715" type="verses" book="th1">
_Bhāvento satta bojjhaṅge,_  
_indriyāni balāni ca;_  
_Jhānasokhummasampanno,_  
_viharissaṃ anāsavo._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="438" book="th1" bookNo="19" page="288">

<Paragraph number="716" type="verses" book="th1">
_Vippamuttaṃ kilesehi,_  
_suddhacittaṃ anāvilaṃ;_  
_Abhiṇhaṃ paccavekkhanto,_  
_viharissaṃ anāsavo._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="439" book="th1" bookNo="19" page="288">

<Paragraph number="717" type="verses" book="th1">
_Ajjhattañca bahiddhā ca,_  
_ye me vijjiṃsu āsavā;_  
_Sabbe asesā ucchinnā,_  
_na ca uppajjare puna._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>

_tiṭṭhanti chinnamūlakā;_  
_Dukkhakkhayo anuppatto,_  
_natthi dāni punabbhavo”ti._  


</Paragraph>
</Division>
