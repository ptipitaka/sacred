<Paragraph number="185">
»  Cakkhu cakkhāyatananti?
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="186">
Dibbacakkhu paññācakkhu cakkhu, na cakkhāyatanaṃ. Cakkhāyatanaṃ cakkhu ceva cakkhāyatanañca.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="187">
«  Cakkhāyatanaṃ cakkhūti? Āmantā.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="188">
»  Sotaṃ sotāyatananti?
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="189">

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="190">
«  Sotāyatanaṃ sotanti? Āmantā.
</Paragraph>
