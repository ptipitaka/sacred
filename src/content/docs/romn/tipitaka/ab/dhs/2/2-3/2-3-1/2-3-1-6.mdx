Katame dhammā savitakkasavicārā? Savitakkasavicārabhūmiyaṃ kāmāvacare, rūpāvacare, apariyāpanne, vitakkavicāre ṭhapetvā; taṃsampayutto vedanākkhandho …pe…  viññāṇakkhandho—  ime dhammā savitakkasavicārā.

</Paragraph>
