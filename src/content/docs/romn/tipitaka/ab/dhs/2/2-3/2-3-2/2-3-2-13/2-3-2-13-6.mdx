Katame dhammā savicārā? Savicārabhūmiyaṃ kāmāvacare rūpāvacare apariyāpanne, vicāraṃ ṭhapetvā, taṃsampayutto vedanākkhandho, saññākkhandho, saṅkhārakkhandho, viññāṇakkhandho—  ime dhammā savicārā.
