Kusalaṃ cittasampayuttaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nakusalo nacittasampayutto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. Kusalaṃ cittasampayuttaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naakusalo nacittasampayutto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā.
