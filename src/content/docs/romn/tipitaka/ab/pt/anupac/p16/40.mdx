Maggārammaṇaṃ hetuṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca namaggārammaṇo nahetu dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā pañcatiṃsa, ārammaṇe chabbīsa …pe…  avigate pañcatiṃsa.
</Paragraph>
