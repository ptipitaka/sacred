Anidassanaappaṭighaṃ niyyānikaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naanidassanaappaṭigho naniyyāniko dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā cha, adhipatiyā cha …pe…  avigate cha.
