Akusalaṃ hīnaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naakusalo nahīno dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi, adhipatiyā tīṇi …pe…  avigate tīṇi.

</Paragraph>
