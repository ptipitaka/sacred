Akusalaṃ micchattaniyataṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naakusalo namicchattaniyato dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi, adhipatiyā tīṇi …pe…  avigate tīṇi.
