Nasanidassanasappaṭighaṃ naparittaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca sanidassanasappaṭigho paritto dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā ekavīsa, adhipatiyā ekavīsa …pe…  aññamaññe tīṇi …pe…  avigate ekavīsa.
