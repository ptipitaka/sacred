Nasanidassanasappaṭighaṃ nahīnaṃ dhammaṃ paccayā anidassanaappaṭigho hīno dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Hetuyā tīṇi …pe…  avigate tīṇi.

</Paragraph>
