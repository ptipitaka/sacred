<Division number="39">

<Paragraph number="4150">
Bhāvanāya pahātabbo dhammo bhāvanāya pahātabbassa dhammassa adhipatipaccayena paccayo—  ārammaṇādhipati sahajātādhipati. <Emphasis>Ārammaṇādhipati</Emphasis>—  bhāvanāya pahātabbaṃ rāgaṃ garuṃ katvā assādeti abhinandati, taṃ garuṃ katvā bhāvanāya pahātabbo rāgo uppajjati. <Emphasis>Sahajātādhipati</Emphasis>—  bhāvanāya pahātabbādhipati sampayuttakānaṃ khandhānaṃ adhipatipaccayena paccayo. (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="4151">
Bhāvanāya pahātabbo dhammo nabhāvanāya pahātabbassa dhammassa adhipatipaccayena paccayo—  ārammaṇādhipati, sahajātādhipati. <Emphasis>Ārammaṇādhipati</Emphasis>—  bhāvanāya pahātabbaṃ rāgaṃ garuṃ katvā assādeti abhinandati, taṃ garuṃ katvā nabhāvanāya pahātabbo rāgo uppajjati, diṭṭhi uppajjati. <Emphasis>Sahajātādhipati</Emphasis>—  bhāvanāya pahātabbādhipati cittasamuṭṭhānānaṃ rūpānaṃ adhipatipaccayena paccayo. (2)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="4152">
Bhāvanāya pahātabbo dhammo bhāvanāya pahātabbassa ca nabhāvanāya pahātabbassa ca dhammassa adhipatipaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Sahajātādhipati</Emphasis>—  bhāvanāya pahātabbādhipati sampayuttakānaṃ khandhānaṃ cittasamuṭṭhānānañca rūpānaṃ adhipatipaccayena paccayo. (3)
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="40">

<Paragraph number="4153">
Nabhāvanāya pahātabbo dhammo nabhāvanāya pahātabbassa dhammassa adhipatipaccayena paccayo—  ārammaṇādhipati, sahajātādhipati. <Emphasis>Ārammaṇādhipati</Emphasis>—  dānaṃ datvā sīlaṃ …pe…  uposathakammaṃ katvā taṃ garuṃ katvā paccavekkhati assādeti abhinandati, taṃ garuṃ katvā nabhāvanāya pahātabbo rāgo uppajjati. <Emphasis>Sahajātādhipati</Emphasis>—  nabhāvanāya pahātabbādhipati sampayuttakānaṃ khandhānaṃ cittasamuṭṭhānānañca rūpānaṃ adhipatipaccayena paccayo. (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="4154">
Nabhāvanāya pahātabbo dhammo bhāvanāya pahātabbassa dhammassa adhipatipaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Ārammaṇādhipati</Emphasis>—  dānaṃ …pe…  jhānaṃ …pe…  cakkhuṃ …pe…  vatthuṃ nabhāvanāya pahātabbe khandhe garuṃ katvā assādeti abhinandati, taṃ garuṃ katvā bhāvanāya pahātabbo rāgo uppajjati. (2)
</Paragraph>
</Division>
