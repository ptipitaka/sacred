<Division number="227">

<Paragraph number="2876">
Cittasamuṭṭhāno dhammo cittasamuṭṭhānassa dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2877">
Cittasamuṭṭhāno dhammo nocittasamuṭṭhānassa dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ. <Emphasis>Sahajātaṃ</Emphasis>—  paṭisandhikkhaṇe. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) (2)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2878">
Cittasamuṭṭhāno dhammo cittasamuṭṭhānassa ca nocittasamuṭṭhānassa ca dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo…  pacchājātaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) (3)
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="228">

<Paragraph number="2879">
Nocittasamuṭṭhāno dhammo nocittasamuṭṭhānassa dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, purejātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ. <Emphasis>Sahajātaṃ</Emphasis>—  paṭisandhikkhaṇe cittaṃ kaṭattā rūpānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo, cittaṃ vatthussa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo, vatthu cittassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Purejātaṃ</Emphasis>—  cakkhāyatanaṃ cakkhuviññāṇassa …pe…  kāyāyatanaṃ kāyaviññāṇassa …pe…  vatthu cittassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Pacchājātā</Emphasis>—  nocittasamuṭṭhānā khandhā purejātassa imassa nocittasamuṭṭhānassa kāyassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2880">
Nocittasamuṭṭhāno dhammo cittasamuṭṭhānassa dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, purejātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ (Saṃkhittaṃ.) (2)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2881">
Nocittasamuṭṭhāno dhammo cittasamuṭṭhānassa ca nocittasamuṭṭhānassa ca dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, purejātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) (3)
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="229">

<Paragraph number="2882">
Cittasamuṭṭhāno ca nocittasamuṭṭhāno ca dhammā cittasamuṭṭhānassa dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2883">
Cittasamuṭṭhāno ca nocittasamuṭṭhāno ca dhammā nocittasamuṭṭhānassa dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) (2)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2884">
Cittasamuṭṭhāno ca nocittasamuṭṭhāno ca dhammā cittasamuṭṭhānassa ca nocittasamuṭṭhānassa ca dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo…  pacchājātaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) (3)
</Paragraph>
</Division>
