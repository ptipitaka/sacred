<Division number="31">

<Paragraph number="2509">
Maggārammaṇo dhammo maggārammaṇassa dhammassa adhipatipaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Sahajātādhipati</Emphasis>—  maggārammaṇādhipati sampayuttakānaṃ khandhānaṃ adhipatipaccayena paccayo. (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2510">
Maggārammaṇo dhammo maggādhipatissa dhammassa adhipatipaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Sahajātādhipati</Emphasis>—  maggārammaṇādhipati sampayuttakānaṃ maggādhipatīnaṃ khandhānaṃ adhipatipaccayena paccayo. (2)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2511">
Maggārammaṇo dhammo maggārammaṇassa ca maggādhipatissa ca dhammassa adhipatipaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Sahajātādhipati</Emphasis>—  maggārammaṇādhipati sampayuttakānaṃ maggārammaṇānañca maggādhipatīnañca khandhānaṃ adhipatipaccayena paccayo. (3)
</Paragraph>
