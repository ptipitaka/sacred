<Paragraph number="1393">

</Paragraph>

<Division number="30">

<Paragraph number="1395">
Nosaññojano dhammo nosaññojanassa dhammassa atthipaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, purejātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ, āhāraṃ, indriyaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1396">
Nosaññojano dhammo saññojanassa dhammassa atthipaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, purejātaṃ. <Emphasis>Sahajātā</Emphasis>—  nosaññojanā khandhā sampayuttakānaṃ saññojanānaṃ atthipaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Purejātaṃ</Emphasis>—  cakkhuṃ …pe…  vatthuṃ assādeti abhinandati, taṃ ārabbha rāgo uppajjati …pe…  domanassaṃ uppajjati, vatthu saññojanānaṃ atthipaccayena paccayo. (2)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1397">
Nosaññojano dhammo saññojanassa ca nosaññojanassa ca dhammassa atthipaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, purejātaṃ. <Emphasis>Sahajāto</Emphasis>—  nosaññojano …pe… . (Saṃkhittaṃ, āsavasadisaṃ.) (3)
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="31">

<Paragraph number="1398">
Saññojano ca nosaññojano ca dhammā saññojanassa dhammassa atthipaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, purejātaṃ. (Āsavasadisaṃ.) (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1399">
Saññojano ca nosaññojano ca dhammā nosaññojanassa dhammassa atthipaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, purejātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ, āhāraṃ, indriyaṃ. (Vibhajitabbaṃ āsavasadisaṃ.) (2)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1400">

</Paragraph>
