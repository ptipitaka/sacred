<Division number="27">

<Paragraph number="1039">
Noāsavo dhammo noāsavassa dhammassa upanissayapaccayena paccayo—  ārammaṇūpanissayo, anantarūpanissayo, pakatūpanissayo …pe… . <Emphasis>Pakatūpanissayo</Emphasis>—  saddhaṃ upanissāya dānaṃ deti …pe…  samāpattiṃ uppādeti, mānaṃ jappeti, diṭṭhiṃ gaṇhāti; sīlaṃ …pe…  senāsanaṃ upanissāya dānaṃ deti …pe…  saṃghaṃ bhindati; saddhā …pe…  senāsanaṃ saddhāya …pe…  phalasamāpattiyā upanissayapaccayena paccayo. (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1040">
Noāsavo dhammo āsavassa dhammassa upanissayapaccayena paccayo—  ārammaṇūpanissayo, anantarūpanissayo, pakatūpanissayo …pe… . <Emphasis>Pakatūpanissayo</Emphasis>—  saddhaṃ upanissāya mānaṃ jappeti, diṭṭhiṃ gaṇhāti; sīlaṃ …pe…  saṃghaṃ bhindati, saddhā …pe…  senāsanaṃ rāgassa …pe…  patthanāya upanissayapaccayena paccayo. (2)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1041">

(3)
</Paragraph>
