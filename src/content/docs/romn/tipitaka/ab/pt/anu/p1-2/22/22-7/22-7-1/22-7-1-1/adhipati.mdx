<Division number="14">

<Paragraph number="2897">
Ajjhattārammaṇo dhammo ajjhattārammaṇassa dhammassa adhipatipaccayena paccayo—  ārammaṇādhipati, sahajātādhipati. <Emphasis>Ārammaṇādhipati</Emphasis>—  ajjhattaṃ viññāṇañcāyatanaṃ garuṃ katvā paccavekkhati, nevasaññānāsaññāyatanaṃ garuṃ katvā paccavekkhati. Ajjhattārammaṇaṃ ajjhattaṃ dibbaṃ cakkhuṃ garuṃ katvā …pe…  dibbaṃ sotadhātuṃ …pe…  iddhividhañāṇaṃ…  pubbenivāsānussatiñāṇaṃ…  yathākammūpagañāṇaṃ…  anāgataṃsañāṇaṃ garuṃ katvā …pe…  ajjhattārammaṇe ajjhatte khandhe garuṃ katvā assādeti abhinandati, taṃ garuṃ katvā ajjhattārammaṇo rāgo uppajjati, diṭṭhi uppajjati. <Emphasis>Sahajātādhipati</Emphasis>—  ajjhattārammaṇādhipati sampayuttakānaṃ khandhānaṃ adhipatipaccayena paccayo. (1)
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="15">

<Paragraph number="2898">
Bahiddhārammaṇo dhammo bahiddhārammaṇassa dhammassa adhipatipaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Sahajātādhipati</Emphasis>—  bahiddhārammaṇādhipati sampayuttakānaṃ khandhānaṃ adhipatipaccayena paccayo. (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2899">
Bahiddhārammaṇo dhammo ajjhattārammaṇassa dhammassa adhipatipaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Ārammaṇādhipati</Emphasis>—  dānaṃ datvā sīlaṃ samādiyitvā uposathakammaṃ katvā taṃ garuṃ katvā paccavekkhati, pubbe suciṇṇāni paccavekkhati, jhānā vuṭṭhahitvā …pe…  ariyā maggā vuṭṭhahitvā maggaṃ…  phalaṃ garuṃ katvā paccavekkhanti. Bahiddhārammaṇaṃ ajjhattaṃ dibbaṃ cakkhuṃ garuṃ katvā …pe…  dibbaṃ sotadhātuṃ…  iddhividhañāṇaṃ…  cetopariyañāṇaṃ…  pubbenivāsānussatiñāṇaṃ…  yathākammūpagañāṇaṃ…  anāgataṃsañāṇaṃ garuṃ katvā paccavekkhati, bahiddhārammaṇe ajjhatte khandhe garuṃ katvā assādeti abhinandati, taṃ garuṃ katvā ajjhattārammaṇo rāgo uppajjati, diṭṭhi uppajjati. (2)
</Paragraph>
</Division>
