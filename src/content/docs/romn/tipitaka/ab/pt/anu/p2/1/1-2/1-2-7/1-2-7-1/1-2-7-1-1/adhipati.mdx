<Division number="101">

<Paragraph number="272">
Sahetuko dhammo sahetukassa dhammassa adhipatipaccayena paccayo—  ārammaṇādhipati, sahajātādhipati. <Emphasis>Ārammaṇādhipati</Emphasis>—  dānaṃ datvā sīlaṃ …pe…  uposathakammaṃ katvā taṃ garuṃ katvā paccavekkhati, jhānā …pe…  ariyā maggā vuṭṭhahitvā maggaṃ garuṃ katvā …pe…  phalaṃ …pe…  sahetuke khandhe garuṃ katvā assādeti abhinandati; taṃ garuṃ katvā rāgo uppajjati, diṭṭhi uppajjati. <Emphasis>Sahajātādhipati</Emphasis>—  sahetukādhipati sampayuttakānaṃ khandhānaṃ adhipatipaccayena paccayo. (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="273">
Sahetuko dhammo ahetukassa dhammassa adhipatipaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Sahajātādhipati</Emphasis>—  sahetukādhipati cittasamuṭṭhānānaṃ rūpānaṃ adhipatipaccayena paccayo. (2)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="274">
Sahetuko dhammo sahetukassa ca ahetukassa ca dhammassa adhipatipaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Sahajātādhipati</Emphasis>—  sahetukādhipati sampayuttakānaṃ khandhānaṃ cittasamuṭṭhānānañca rūpānaṃ adhipatipaccayena paccayo. (3)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="275">
Ahetuko dhammo sahetukassa dhammassa adhipatipaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Ārammaṇādhipati</Emphasis>—  ariyā nibbānaṃ garuṃ katvā paccavekkhanti; nibbānaṃ gotrabhussa, vodānassa, maggassa, phalassa adhipatipaccayena paccayo; cakkhuṃ …pe…  vatthuṃ…  ahetuke khandhe garuṃ katvā assādeti abhinandati; taṃ garuṃ katvā rāgo uppajjati, diṭṭhi uppajjati. (1)
</Paragraph>
</Division>
