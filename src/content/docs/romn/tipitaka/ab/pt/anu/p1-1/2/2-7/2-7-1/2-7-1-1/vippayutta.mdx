<Division number="298">

<Paragraph number="981">
Kusalo dhammo abyākatassa dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ. <Emphasis>Sahajātā</Emphasis>—  kusalā khandhā cittasamuṭṭhānānaṃ rūpānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Pacchājātā</Emphasis>—  kusalā khandhā purejātassa imassa kāyassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="982">
Akusalo dhammo abyākatassa dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ. <Emphasis>Sahajātā</Emphasis>—  akusalā khandhā cittasamuṭṭhānānaṃ rūpānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Pacchājātā</Emphasis>—  akusalā khandhā purejātassa imassa kāyassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="983">
Abyākato dhammo abyākatassa dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, purejātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ. <Emphasis>Sahajātā</Emphasis>—  vipākābyākatā kiriyābyākatā khandhā cittasamuṭṭhānānaṃ rūpānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. Paṭisandhikkhaṇe vipākābyākatā khandhā kaṭattārūpānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. Khandhā vatthussa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. Vatthu khandhānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Purejātaṃ</Emphasis>—  cakkhāyatanaṃ cakkhuviññāṇassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. Sotāyatanaṃ sotaviññāṇassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. Ghānāyatanaṃ ghānaviññāṇassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. Jivhāyatanaṃ jivhāviññāṇassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. Kāyāyatanaṃ kāyaviññāṇassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. Vatthu vipākābyākatānaṃ kiriyābyākatānaṃ khandhānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Pacchājātā</Emphasis>—  vipākābyākatā kiriyābyākatā khandhā purejātassa imassa kāyassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="984">
Abyākato dhammo kusalassa dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Purejātaṃ</Emphasis>—  vatthu kusalānaṃ khandhānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. (2)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="985">
Abyākato dhammo akusalassa dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Purejātaṃ</Emphasis>—  vatthu akusalānaṃ khandhānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. (3)
</Paragraph>
</Division>
