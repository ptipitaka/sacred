<Paragraph number="856">
Hetuṃ appamāṇārammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca hetu appamāṇārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā…  tīṇi.

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="858">
