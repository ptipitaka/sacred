</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="3895">
Nokileso dhammo nokilesassa dhammassa atthipaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, purejātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ, āhāraṃ, indriyaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="3896">

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="3897">

<Division number="31">

<Paragraph number="3898">
Kileso ca nokileso ca dhammā kilesassa dhammassa atthipaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, purejātaṃ. <Emphasis>Sahajāto</Emphasis>—  lobho ca sampayuttakā ca khandhā mohassa, diṭṭhiyā, thinassa, uddhaccassa, ahirikassa, anottappassa atthipaccayena paccayo (cakkaṃ). <Emphasis>Sahajāto</Emphasis>—  lobho ca vatthu ca mohassa, diṭṭhiyā, thinassa, uddhaccassa, ahirikassa, anottappassa atthipaccayena paccayo. (Cakkaṃ.) (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="3899">
Kileso ca nokileso ca dhammā nokilesassa dhammassa atthipaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, purejātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ, āhāraṃ, indriyaṃ. <Emphasis>Sahajāto</Emphasis>—  nokileso eko khandho ca kileso ca tiṇṇannaṃ khandhānaṃ cittasamuṭṭhānānañca rūpānaṃ atthipaccayena paccayo …pe…  dve khandhā ca …pe… . <Emphasis>Sahajātā</Emphasis>—  kilesā ca mahābhūtā ca cittasamuṭṭhānānaṃ rūpānaṃ atthipaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Sahajātā</Emphasis>—  kilesā ca vatthu ca nokilesānaṃ khandhānaṃ atthipaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Pacchājātā</Emphasis>—  kilesā ca sampayuttakā khandhā ca purejātassa imassa kāyassa atthipaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Pacchājātā</Emphasis>—  kilesā ca sampayuttakā ca khandhā kabaḷīkāro āhāro ca imassa kāyassa atthipaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Pacchājātā</Emphasis>—  kilesā ca sampayuttakā ca khandhā rūpajīvitindriyañca kaṭattārūpānaṃ atthipaccayena paccayo. (2)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="3900">
Kileso ca nokileso ca dhammā kilesassa ca nokilesassa ca dhammassa atthipaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, purejātaṃ. <Emphasis>Sahajāto</Emphasis>—  nokileso eko khandho ca lobho ca tiṇṇannaṃ khandhānaṃ cittasamuṭṭhānānañca rūpānaṃ mohassa ca, diṭṭhiyā, thinassa, uddhaccassa, ahirikassa, anottappassa atthipaccayena paccayo …pe…  dve khandhā ca …pe… . <Emphasis>Sahajāto</Emphasis>—  lobho ca vatthu ca mohassa, diṭṭhiyā, thinassa, uddhaccassa, ahirikassa, anottappassa sampayuttakānañca khandhānaṃ atthipaccayena paccayo. (Cakkaṃ.) (3)
</Paragraph>

</Paragraph>
