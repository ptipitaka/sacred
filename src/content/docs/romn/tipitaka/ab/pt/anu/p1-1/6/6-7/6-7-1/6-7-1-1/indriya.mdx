<Paragraph number="2885">
Saṃkiliṭṭhasaṃkilesiko dhammo saṃkiliṭṭhasaṃkilesikassa dhammassa indriyapaccayena paccayo…  tīṇi.

<Paragraph number="2886">
Asaṃkiliṭṭhasaṃkilesiko dhammo asaṃkiliṭṭhasaṃkilesikassa dhammassa indriyapaccayena paccayo—  asaṃkiliṭṭhasaṃkilesikā indriyā sampayuttakānaṃ khandhānaṃ cittasamuṭṭhānānañca rūpānaṃ indriyapaccayena paccayo; paṭisandhikkhaṇe …pe…  cakkhundriyaṃ cakkhuviññāṇassa …pe…  kāyindriyaṃ kāyaviññāṇassa indriyapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Rūpajīvitindriyaṃ</Emphasis>—  kaṭattārūpānaṃ indriyapaccayena paccayo.
</Paragraph>
