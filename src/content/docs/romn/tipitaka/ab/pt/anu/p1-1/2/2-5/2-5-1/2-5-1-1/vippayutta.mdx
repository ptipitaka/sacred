<Paragraph number="705">
Kusalaṃ dhammaṃ saṃsaṭṭho kusalo dhammo uppajjati vippayuttapaccayā—  kusalaṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ saṃsaṭṭhā tayo khandhā …pe…  dve khandhe saṃsaṭṭhā dve khandhā; vatthuṃ vippayuttapaccayā.
