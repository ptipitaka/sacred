<Division number="83">

<Paragraph number="728">
Sappaṭigho dhammo sappaṭighassa dhammassa atthipaccayena paccayo…  ekaṃ. (Paṭiccasadisā paṭhamapañhā.) (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="729">
Sappaṭigho dhammo appaṭighassa dhammassa atthipaccayena paccayo sahajātaṃ, purejātaṃ. <Emphasis>Sahajātā</Emphasis>—  sappaṭighā mahābhūtā āpodhātuyā atthipaccayena paccayo, sappaṭighā mahābhūtā appaṭighānaṃ cittasamuṭṭhānānaṃ rūpānaṃ kaṭattārūpānaṃ upādārūpānaṃ atthipaccayena paccayo; phoṭṭhabbāyatanaṃ itthindriyassa …pe…  kabaḷīkārassa āhārassa atthipaccayena paccayo; bāhiraṃ…  āhārasamuṭṭhānaṃ…  utusamuṭṭhānaṃ…  asaññasattānaṃ …pe… . <Emphasis>Purejātaṃ</Emphasis>—  cakkhuṃ …pe…  phoṭṭhabbe aniccato …pe…  domanassaṃ uppajjati; dibbena cakkhunā rūpaṃ passati, dibbāya sotadhātuyā saddaṃ suṇāti, rūpāyatanañca cakkhāyatanañca cakkhuviññāṇassa …pe…  phoṭṭhabbāyatanañca kāyāyatanañca kāyaviññāṇassa atthipaccayena paccayo. (2)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="730">
Sappaṭigho dhammo sappaṭighassa ca appaṭighassa ca dhammassa atthipaccayena paccayo—  sappaṭighaṃ ekaṃ mahābhūtaṃ dvinnaṃ mahābhūtānaṃ āpodhātuyā ca atthipaccayena paccayo …pe… . (Paṭiccasadisaṃ yāva asaññasattā.) (3)
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="84">

<Paragraph number="731">
Appaṭigho dhammo appaṭighassa dhammassa atthipaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, purejātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ, āhāraṃ, indriyaṃ. <Emphasis>Sahajāto</Emphasis>—  appaṭigho eko khandho tiṇṇannaṃ khandhānaṃ …pe…  (yāva asaññasattā). <Emphasis>Purejātaṃ</Emphasis>—  vatthuṃ …pe…  itthindriyaṃ…  purisindriyaṃ…  jīvitindriyaṃ…  āpodhātuṃ…  kabaḷīkāraṃ āhāraṃ aniccato …pe…  domanassaṃ uppajjati; vatthu appaṭighānaṃ khandhānaṃ atthipaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Pacchājātā</Emphasis>—  appaṭighā khandhā purejātassa imassa appaṭighassa kāyassa atthipaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Kabaḷīkāro āhāro</Emphasis>—  imassa appaṭighassa kāyassa atthipaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Rūpajīvitindriyaṃ</Emphasis>—  appaṭighānaṃ kaṭattārūpānaṃ atthipaccayena paccayo. (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="732">
Appaṭigho dhammo sappaṭighassa dhammassa atthipaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ, āhāraṃ, indriyaṃ. <Emphasis>Sahajātā</Emphasis>—  appaṭighā khandhā sappaṭighānaṃ cittasamuṭṭhānānaṃ rūpānaṃ atthipaccayena paccayo; paṭisandhikkhaṇe …pe…  āpodhātu sappaṭighānaṃ mahābhūtānaṃ atthipaccayena paccayo; āpodhātu sappaṭighānaṃ cittasamuṭṭhānānaṃ rūpānaṃ kaṭattārūpānaṃ upādārūpānaṃ atthipaccayena paccayo; āpodhātu cakkhāyatanassa …pe…  phoṭṭhabbāyatanassa atthipaccayena paccayo; bāhiraṃ, āhārasamuṭṭhānaṃ, utusamuṭṭhānaṃ, asaññasattānaṃ …pe… . <Emphasis>Pacchājātā</Emphasis>—  appaṭighā khandhā purejātassa imassa sappaṭighassa kāyassa atthipaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Kabaḷīkāro āhāro</Emphasis>—  imassa sappaṭighassa kāyassa atthipaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Rūpajīvitindriyaṃ</Emphasis>—  sappaṭighānaṃ kaṭattārūpānaṃ atthipaccayena paccayo. (2)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="733">
Appaṭigho dhammo sappaṭighassa ca appaṭighassa ca dhammassa atthipaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ, āhāraṃ, indriyaṃ. <Emphasis>Sahajāto</Emphasis>—  appaṭigho eko khandho tiṇṇannaṃ khandhānaṃ sappaṭighānañca appaṭighānañca …pe… . (Paṭiccasadisaṃ yāva asaññasattā.) <Emphasis>Pacchājātā</Emphasis>—  appaṭighā khandhā purejātassa imassa sappaṭighassa ca appaṭighassa ca kāyassa atthipaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Kabaḷīkāro āhāro</Emphasis>—  imassa sappaṭighassa ca appaṭighassa ca kāyassa atthipaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Rūpajīvitindriyaṃ</Emphasis>—  sappaṭighānañca appaṭighānañca kaṭattārūpānaṃ atthipaccayena paccayo. (3)
</Paragraph>

</Division>

<Paragraph number="734">
Sappaṭigho ca appaṭigho ca dhammā sappaṭighassa dhammassa atthipaccayena paccayo. (Paṭiccasadisaṃ yāva asaññasattā.) (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="735">
Sappaṭigho ca appaṭigho ca dhammā appaṭighassa dhammassa atthipaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, purejātaṃ. <Emphasis>Sahajātā</Emphasis>—  appaṭighā khandhā ca mahābhūtā ca appaṭighānaṃ cittasamuṭṭhānānaṃ rūpānaṃ …pe…  (paṭiccasadisaṃ yāva asaññasattā). <Emphasis>Sahajāto</Emphasis>—  cakkhuviññāṇasahagato eko khandho ca cakkhāyatanañca tiṇṇannaṃ khandhānaṃ …pe…  dve khandhā ca …pe…  kāyaviññāṇasahagato eko khandho ca kāyāyatanañca tiṇṇannaṃ khandhānaṃ atthipaccayena paccayo …pe…  dve khandhā ca …pe… . (2)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="736">
Sappaṭigho ca appaṭigho ca dhammā sappaṭighassa ca appaṭighassa ca dhammassa atthipaccayena paccayo. (Paṭiccasadisaṃ.) (3)
</Paragraph>
