<Division number="83">

<Paragraph number="2523">
Citto dhammo nocittassa dhammassa atthipaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2524">
Nocitto dhammo nocittassa dhammassa atthipaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, purejātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ, āhāraṃ, indriyaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2525">
Nocitto dhammo cittassa dhammassa atthipaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, purejātaṃ. <Emphasis>Sahajātā</Emphasis>—  nocittā khandhā cittassa atthipaccayena paccayo; paṭisandhikkhaṇe …pe…  paṭisandhikkhaṇe vatthu cittassa atthipaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Purejātaṃ</Emphasis>—  cakkhuṃ …pe…  vatthuṃ aniccato …pe… . (Purejātasadisaṃ, saṃkhittaṃ.) (2)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2526">
Nocitto dhammo cittassa ca nocittassa ca dhammassa atthipaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, purejātaṃ. <Emphasis>Sahajāto</Emphasis>—  nocitto eko khandho dvinnaṃ khandhānaṃ cittassa ca cittasamuṭṭhānānañca rūpānaṃ atthipaccayena paccayo; paṭisandhikkhaṇe nocitto eko khandho …pe…  paṭisandhikkhaṇe vatthu cittassa sampayuttakānañca khandhānaṃ atthipaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Purejātaṃ</Emphasis>—  cakkhuṃ …pe…  vatthuṃ …pe… . (Purejātasadisaṃ, saṃkhittaṃ.) (3)
</Paragraph>

<Division number="84">

<Paragraph number="2527">
Citto ca nocitto ca dhammā nocittassa dhammassa atthipaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, purejātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ, āhāraṃ, indriyaṃ. <Emphasis>Sahajāto</Emphasis>—  nocitto eko khandho ca cittañca dvinnaṃ khandhānaṃ cittasamuṭṭhānānañca rūpānaṃ atthipaccayena paccayo; dve khandhā ca …pe… . <Emphasis>Sahajātaṃ</Emphasis>—  cittañca vatthuñca nocittānaṃ khandhānaṃ atthipaccayena paccayo (paṭisandhikkhaṇepi dve). <Emphasis>Sahajātaṃ</Emphasis>—  cittañca sampayuttakā ca khandhā cittasamuṭṭhānānaṃ rūpānaṃ atthipaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Sahajātaṃ</Emphasis>—  cittañca mahābhūtā ca cittasamuṭṭhānānaṃ rūpānaṃ atthipaccayena paccayo (paṭisandhikkhaṇepi dve). <Emphasis>Pacchājātaṃ</Emphasis>—  cittañca sampayuttakā ca khandhā purejātassa imassa kāyassa atthipaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Pacchājātaṃ</Emphasis>—  cittañca sampayuttakā ca khandhā kabaḷīkāro āhāro ca imassa kāyassa atthipaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Pacchājātaṃ</Emphasis>—  cittañca sampayuttakā ca khandhā rūpajīvitindriyañca kaṭattārūpānaṃ atthipaccayena paccayo. (1)
</Paragraph>
