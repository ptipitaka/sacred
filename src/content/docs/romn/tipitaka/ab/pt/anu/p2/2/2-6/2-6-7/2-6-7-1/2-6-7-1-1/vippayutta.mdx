<Division number="166">

<Paragraph number="924">
Lokiyo dhammo lokiyassa dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, purejātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ. <Emphasis>Sahajātā</Emphasis>—  lokiyā khandhā cittasamuṭṭhānānaṃ rūpānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo; paṭisandhikkhaṇe …pe…  khandhā vatthussa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo; vatthu khandhānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Purejātaṃ</Emphasis>—  cakkhāyatanaṃ cakkhuviññāṇassa …pe…  kāyāyatanaṃ kāyaviññāṇassa …pe…  vatthu lokiyānaṃ khandhānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Pacchājātā</Emphasis>—  lokiyā khandhā purejātassa imassa kāyassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="925">
Lokiyo dhammo lokuttarassa dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Purejātaṃ</Emphasis>—  vatthu lokuttarānaṃ khandhānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. (2)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="926">
Lokuttaro dhammo lokiyassa dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ. <Emphasis>Sahajātā</Emphasis>—  lokuttarā khandhā cittasamuṭṭhānānaṃ rūpānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Pacchājātā</Emphasis>—  lokuttarā khandhā purejātassa imassa kāyassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. (1)
</Paragraph>
</Division>
