<Division number="34">

<Paragraph number="1057">
Āsavo dhammo noāsavassa dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ. <Emphasis>Sahajātā</Emphasis>—  āsavā cittasamuṭṭhānānaṃ rūpānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Pacchājātā</Emphasis>—  āsavā purejātassa imassa kāyassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. (1)
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="35">

<Paragraph number="1058">
Noāsavo dhammo noāsavassa dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, purejātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ. <Emphasis>Sahajātā</Emphasis>—  noāsavā khandhā cittasamuṭṭhānānaṃ rūpānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo; paṭisandhikkhaṇe …pe…  khandhā vatthussa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo, vatthu khandhānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Purejātaṃ</Emphasis>—  cakkhāyatanaṃ cakkhuviññāṇassa …pe…  kāyāyatanaṃ kāyaviññāṇassa …pe…  vatthu noāsavānaṃ khandhānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Pacchājātā</Emphasis>—  noāsavā khandhā purejātassa imassa kāyassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1059">
Noāsavo dhammo āsavassa dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Purejātaṃ</Emphasis>—  vatthu āsavānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. (2)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1060">
Noāsavo dhammo āsavassa ca noāsavassa ca dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Purejātaṃ</Emphasis>—  vatthu āsavānaṃ sampayuttakānañca khandhānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. (3)
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="36">

<Paragraph number="1061">
Āsavo ca noāsavo ca dhammā noāsavassa dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ. <Emphasis>Sahajātā</Emphasis>—  āsavā ca sampayuttakā ca khandhā cittasamuṭṭhānānaṃ rūpānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Pacchājātā</Emphasis>—  āsavā ca sampayuttakā ca khandhā purejātassa imassa kāyassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. (1)
</Paragraph>
</Division>
