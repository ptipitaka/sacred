<Division number="81">

<Paragraph number="2115">
Paritto dhammo parittassa dhammassa āhārapaccayena paccayo …pe…  indriyapaccayena paccayo…  jhānapaccayena paccayo…  maggapaccayena paccayo…  sampayuttapaccayena paccayo…  vippayuttapaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, purejātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ. <Emphasis>Sahajātā</Emphasis>—  parittā khandhā cittasamuṭṭhānānaṃ rūpānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. Paṭisandhikkhaṇe parittā khandhā kaṭattārūpānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. Khandhā vatthussa …pe…  vatthu khandhānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Purejātaṃ</Emphasis>—  cakkhāyatanaṃ cakkhuviññāṇassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo …pe…  kāyāyatanaṃ kāyaviññāṇassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. Vatthu parittānaṃ khandhānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Pacchājātā</Emphasis>—  parittā khandhā purejātassa imassa kāyassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2116">
Paritto dhammo mahaggatassa dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, purejātaṃ. <Emphasis>Sahajātaṃ</Emphasis>—  paṭisandhikkhaṇe vatthu mahaggatānaṃ khandhānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Purejātaṃ</Emphasis>—  vatthu mahaggatānaṃ khandhānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. (2)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2117">
Paritto dhammo appamāṇassa dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Purejātaṃ</Emphasis>—  vatthu appamāṇānaṃ khandhānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. (3)
</Paragraph>

Paṭisandhikkhaṇe …pe…  <Emphasis>pacchājātā</Emphasis>—  mahaggatā khandhā purejātassa imassa kāyassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. (1)
