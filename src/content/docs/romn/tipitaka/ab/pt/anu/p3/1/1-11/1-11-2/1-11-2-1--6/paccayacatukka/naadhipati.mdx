<Paragraph number="712">
Naadhipatiyā cha, napurejāte nava, napacchājāte nava, naāsevane nava, navippayutte nava. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="713">
Hetupaccayā naadhipatiyā cha. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="714">

</Paragraph>
