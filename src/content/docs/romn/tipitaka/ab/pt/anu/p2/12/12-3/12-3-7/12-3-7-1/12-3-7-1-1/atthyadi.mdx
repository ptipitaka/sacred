<Division number="65">

<Paragraph number="3983">
Saṃkiliṭṭho dhammo saṃkiliṭṭhassa dhammassa atthipaccayena paccayo…  ekaṃ. (Paṭiccavārasadisaṃ.) Saṃkiliṭṭho dhammo asaṃkiliṭṭhassa dhammassa atthipaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Saṃkiliṭṭho dhammo saṃkiliṭṭhassa ca asaṃkiliṭṭhassa ca dhammassa atthipaccayena paccayo. (Paṭiccasadisaṃ.) (3)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="3984">
Asaṃkiliṭṭho dhammo asaṃkiliṭṭhassa dhammassa atthipaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, purejātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ, āhāraṃ, indriyaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) Asaṃkiliṭṭho dhammo saṃkiliṭṭhassa dhammassa atthipaccayena paccayo—  purejātaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) (2)
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="66">

<Paragraph number="3985">
Saṃkiliṭṭho ca asaṃkiliṭṭho ca dhammā saṃkiliṭṭhassa dhammassa atthipaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, purejātaṃ. <Emphasis>Sahajāto</Emphasis>—  saṃkiliṭṭho eko khandho ca vatthu ca tiṇṇannaṃ khandhānaṃ atthipaccayena paccayo …pe…  dve khandhā ca …pe…  (Saṃkhittaṃ.) (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="3986">
Saṃkiliṭṭho ca asaṃkiliṭṭho ca dhammā asaṃkiliṭṭhassa dhammassa atthipaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ, āhāraṃ, indriyaṃ. <Emphasis>Sahajātā</Emphasis>—  saṃkiliṭṭhā khandhā ca mahābhūtā ca cittasamuṭṭhānānaṃ rūpānaṃ atthipaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Pacchājātā</Emphasis>—  saṃkiliṭṭhā khandhā ca kabaḷīkāro āhāro ca imassa kāyassa atthipaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Pacchājātā</Emphasis>—  saṃkiliṭṭhā khandhā ca rūpajīvitindriyañca kaṭattārūpānaṃ atthipaccayena paccayo. (2)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="3987">
Natthipaccayena paccayo…  vigatapaccayena paccayo…  avigatapaccayena paccayo.
</Paragraph>
