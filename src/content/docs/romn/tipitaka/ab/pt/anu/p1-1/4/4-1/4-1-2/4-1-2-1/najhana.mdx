Vipākaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca vipāko dhammo uppajjati najhānapaccayā—  pañcaviññāṇasahagataṃ ekaṃ khandhaṃ paṭicca tayo khandhā …pe…  dve khandhe paṭicca dve khandhā.
