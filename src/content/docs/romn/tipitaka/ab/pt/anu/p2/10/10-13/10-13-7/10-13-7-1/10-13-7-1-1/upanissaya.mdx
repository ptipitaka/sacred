<Paragraph number="3329">
Upādā dhammo noupādā dhammassa upanissayapaccayena paccayo—  ārammaṇūpanissayo, pakatūpanissayo …pe… . <Emphasis>Pakatūpanissayo</Emphasis>—  cakkhusampadaṃ …pe…  kāyasampadaṃ…  vaṇṇasampadaṃ…  saddasampadaṃ…  gandhasampadaṃ…  rasasampadaṃ…  bhojanaṃ upanissāya dānaṃ deti …pe…  saṃghaṃ bhindati, cakkhusampadā …pe…  kāyasampadā…  vaṇṇasampadā…  saddasampadā…  gandhasampadā…  rasasampadā…  bhojanaṃ saddhāya …pe…  phalasamāpattiyā upanissayapaccayena paccayo.
