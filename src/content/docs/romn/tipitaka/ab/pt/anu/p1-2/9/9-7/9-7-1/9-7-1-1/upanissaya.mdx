<Division number="83">

<Paragraph number="990">
Dassanena pahātabbo dhammo dassanena pahātabbassa dhammassa upanissayapaccayena paccayo—  ārammaṇūpanissayo, anantarūpanissayo, pakatūpanissayo …pe… . <Emphasis>Pakatūpanissayo</Emphasis>—  dassanena pahātabbaṃ rāgaṃ upanissāya pāṇaṃ hanati, adinnaṃ ādiyati …pe…  saṃghaṃ bhindati. Dassanena pahātabbaṃ dosaṃ…  mohaṃ…  diṭṭhiṃ…  patthanaṃ upanissāya pāṇaṃ hanati …pe…  saṃghaṃ bhindati. Dassanena pahātabbo rāgo…  doso…  moho…  diṭṭhi…  patthanā dassanena pahātabbassa rāgassa…  dosassa…  mohassa…  diṭṭhiyā…  patthanāya upanissayapaccayena paccayo. (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="991">
Dassanena pahātabbo dhammo nevadassanena nabhāvanāya pahātabbassa dhammassa upanissayapaccayena paccayo—  anantarūpanissayo, pakatūpanissayo …pe… . <Emphasis>Pakatūpanissayo</Emphasis>—  dassanena pahātabbaṃ rāgaṃ upanissāya dānaṃ deti, sīlaṃ samādiyati, uposathakammaṃ karoti …pe…  samāpattiṃ uppādeti. Dassanena pahātabbaṃ dosaṃ …pe…  patthanaṃ upanissāya dānaṃ deti …pe…  samāpattiṃ uppādeti. Dassanena pahātabbo rāgo…  doso…  moho…  diṭṭhi…  patthanā saddhāya …pe…  paññāya kāyikassa sukhassa, kāyikassa dukkhassa phalasamāpattiyā upanissayapaccayena paccayo. (2)
</Paragraph>

</Division>
