<Division number="85">

<Paragraph number="1800">
Ganthavippayutto dhammo ganthavippayuttassa dhammassa purejātapaccayena paccayo—  ārammaṇapurejātaṃ, vatthupurejātaṃ. <Emphasis>Ārammaṇapurejātaṃ</Emphasis>—  cakkhuṃ …pe…  vatthuṃ aniccato vipassati, assādeti abhinandati, taṃ ārabbha ganthavippayutto rāgo uppajjati, vicikicchā …pe…  uddhaccaṃ …pe…  domanassaṃ uppajjati; dibbena cakkhunā rūpaṃ passati, dibbāya sotadhātuyā saddaṃ suṇāti. Rūpāyatanaṃ cakkhuviññāṇassa …pe…  phoṭṭhabbāyatanaṃ kāyaviññāṇassa …pe… . <Emphasis>Vatthupurejātaṃ</Emphasis>—  cakkhāyatanaṃ cakkhuviññāṇassa …pe…  kāyāyatanaṃ kāyaviññāṇassa …pe…  vatthu ganthavippayuttānaṃ khandhānaṃ diṭṭhigatavippayuttassa lobhassa ca paṭighassa ca purejātapaccayena paccayo. (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1801">
Ganthavippayutto dhammo ganthasampayuttassa dhammassa purejātapaccayena paccayo—  ārammaṇapurejātaṃ, vatthupurejātaṃ. <Emphasis>Ārammaṇapurejātaṃ</Emphasis>—  cakkhuṃ …pe…  vatthuṃ assādeti abhinandati, taṃ ārabbha ganthasampayutto rāgo uppajjati, diṭṭhi …pe…  domanassaṃ uppajjati. <Emphasis>Vatthupurejātaṃ</Emphasis>—  vatthu ganthasampayuttakānaṃ khandhānaṃ purejātapaccayena paccayo. (2)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1802">
Ganthavippayutto dhammo ganthasampayuttassa ca ganthavippayuttassa ca dhammassa purejātapaccayena paccayo—  ārammaṇapurejātaṃ, vatthupurejātaṃ. <Emphasis>Ārammaṇapurejātaṃ</Emphasis>—  cakkhuṃ …pe…  vatthuṃ ārabbha diṭṭhigatavippayuttalobhasahagatā khandhā ca lobho ca domanassasahagatā khandhā ca paṭighañca uppajjanti. <Emphasis>Vatthupurejātaṃ</Emphasis>—  vatthu diṭṭhigatavippayuttalobhasahagatānaṃ khandhānaṃ lobhassa ca domanassasahagatānaṃ khandhānaṃ paṭighassa ca purejātapaccayena paccayo. (3)
</Paragraph>
