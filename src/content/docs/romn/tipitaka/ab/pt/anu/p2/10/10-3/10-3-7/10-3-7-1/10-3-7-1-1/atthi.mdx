<Division number="135">

<Paragraph number="2649">
Acetasiko dhammo acetasikassa dhammassa atthipaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, purejātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ, āhāraṃ, indriyaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2650">
Acetasiko dhammo cetasikassa dhammassa atthipaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, purejātaṃ. <Emphasis>Sahajātaṃ</Emphasis>—  cittaṃ cetasikānaṃ khandhānaṃ atthipaccayena paccayo; paṭisandhikkhaṇe cittaṃ …pe…  paṭisandhikkhaṇe vatthu cetasikānaṃ khandhānaṃ atthipaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Purejātaṃ</Emphasis>—  cakkhuṃ …pe…  vatthuṃ aniccato …pe… . (Purejātasadisaṃ ninnānākaraṇaṃ.) (2)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2651">
Acetasiko dhammo cetasikassa ca acetasikassa ca dhammassa atthipaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, purejātaṃ. <Emphasis>Sahajātaṃ</Emphasis>—  cittaṃ sampayuttakānaṃ khandhānaṃ cittasamuṭṭhānānañca rūpānaṃ atthipaccayena paccayo; paṭisandhikkhaṇe cittaṃ …pe…  paṭisandhikkhaṇe vatthu cetasikānaṃ khandhānaṃ cittassa ca atthipaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Purejātaṃ</Emphasis>—  cakkhuṃ …pe…  vatthuṃ aniccato …pe… . (Purejātasadisaṃ ninnānaṃ.) (3)
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="136">

<Paragraph number="2652">
Cetasiko ca acetasiko ca dhammā cetasikassa dhammassa atthipaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, purejātaṃ. <Emphasis>Sahajāto</Emphasis>—  cakkhuviññāṇasahagato eko khandho ca cakkhāyatanañca dvinnaṃ khandhānaṃ …pe…  kāyaviññāṇasahagato …pe…  cetasiko eko khandho ca vatthu ca dvinnaṃ khandhānaṃ atthipaccayena paccayo; dve khandhā ca …pe… . <Emphasis>Sahajāto</Emphasis>—  cetasiko eko khandho ca cittañca dvinnaṃ khandhānaṃ atthipaccayena paccayo; dve khandhā ca …pe…  (paṭisandhikkhaṇe dvepi kātabbā.) (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2653">
Cetasiko ca acetasiko ca dhammā acetasikassa dhammassa atthipaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, purejātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ, āhāraṃ, indriyaṃ. <Emphasis>Sahajātā</Emphasis>—  cakkhuviññāṇasahagatā khandhā ca cakkhāyatanañca cakkhuviññāṇassa …pe…  kāyaviññāṇassa …pe…  cetasikā khandhā ca cittañca cittasamuṭṭhānānaṃ rūpānaṃ atthipaccayena paccayo; cetasikā khandhā ca cittañca mahābhūtā ca cittasamuṭṭhānānaṃ rūpānaṃ atthipaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Sahajātā</Emphasis>—  cetasikā khandhā ca vatthu ca cittassa atthipaccayena paccayo (paṭisandhikkhaṇe tīṇipi kātabbā). <Emphasis>Pacchājātā</Emphasis>—  cetasikā khandhā ca cittañca purejātassa imassa kāyassa atthipaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Pacchājātā</Emphasis>—  cetasikā khandhā ca cittañca kabaḷīkāro āhāro ca imassa kāyassa atthipaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Pacchājātā</Emphasis>—  cetasikā khandhā ca cittañca rūpajīvitindriyañca kaṭattārūpānaṃ atthipaccayena paccayo. (2)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2654">
Cetasiko ca acetasiko ca dhammā cetasikassa ca acetasikassa ca dhammassa atthipaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, purejātaṃ. <Emphasis>Sahajāto</Emphasis>—  cakkhuviññāṇasahagato eko khandho ca cakkhāyatanañca dvinnaṃ khandhānaṃ cakkhuviññāṇassa ca atthipaccayena paccayo …pe…  kāyaviññāṇasahagato eko khandho ca kāyāyatanañca dvinnaṃ khandhānaṃ kāyaviññāṇassa ca atthipaccayena paccayo; dve khandhā ca …pe… . <Emphasis>Sahajāto</Emphasis>—  cetasiko eko khandho ca vatthu ca dvinnaṃ khandhānaṃ cittassa ca atthipaccayena paccayo; dve khandhā ca …pe… . (Paṭisandhiyā dve kātabbā.) (3)
</Paragraph>
