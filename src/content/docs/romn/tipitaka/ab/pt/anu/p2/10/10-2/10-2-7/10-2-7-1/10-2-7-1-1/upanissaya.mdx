<Division number="77">

<Paragraph number="2503">
Citto dhammo cittassa dhammassa upanissayapaccayena paccayo—  ārammaṇūpanissayo, anantarūpanissayo, pakatūpanissayo …pe… . <Emphasis>Pakatūpanissayo</Emphasis>—  cittaṃ cittassa upanissayapaccayena paccayo…  tīṇi.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2504">
Nocitto dhammo nocittassa dhammassa upanissayapaccayena paccayo—  ārammaṇūpanissayo, anantarūpanissayo, pakatūpanissayo …pe… . <Emphasis>Pakatūpanissayo</Emphasis>—  saddhaṃ upanissāya dānaṃ deti …pe…  mānaṃ jappeti, diṭṭhiṃ gaṇhāti; sīlaṃ …pe…  senāsanaṃ upanissāya dānaṃ deti …pe…  saṃghaṃ bhindati; saddhā …pe…  senāsanaṃ saddhāya …pe…  maggassa, phalasamāpattiyā upanissayapaccayena paccayo. (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2505">
Nocitto dhammo cittassa dhammassa upanissayapaccayena paccayo. (Ime dvepi pūretukāmena sabbattha kātabbā, paṭhamagamanasadisaṃ ninnānākaraṇaṃ.)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2506">
Citto ca nocitto ca dhammā cittassa dhammassa upanissayapaccayena paccayo—  ārammaṇūpanissayo, anantarūpanissayo, pakatūpanissayo …pe… . <Emphasis>Pakatūpanissayo</Emphasis>—  cittañca sampayuttakā ca khandhā cittassa upanissayapaccayena paccayo…  tīṇi.
</Paragraph>
</Division>
