<Division number="54">

<Paragraph number="3959">
Saṃkiliṭṭho dhammo saṃkiliṭṭhassa dhammassa adhipatipaccayena paccayo—  ārammaṇādhipati, sahajātādhipati. <Emphasis>Ārammaṇādhipati</Emphasis>—  rāgaṃ garuṃ katvā assādeti abhinandati, taṃ garuṃ katvā rāgo uppajjati, diṭṭhi uppajjati; diṭṭhiṃ garuṃ katvā assādeti abhinandati, taṃ garuṃ katvā rāgo uppajjati, diṭṭhi uppajjati. <Emphasis>Sahajātādhipati</Emphasis>—  saṃkiliṭṭhādhipati sampayuttakānaṃ khandhānaṃ adhipatipaccayena paccayo. (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="3960">
Saṃkiliṭṭho dhammo asaṃkiliṭṭhassa dhammassa adhipatipaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Sahajātādhipati</Emphasis>—  saṃkiliṭṭhādhipati cittasamuṭṭhānānaṃ rūpānaṃ adhipatipaccayena paccayo. (2)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="3961">
Saṃkiliṭṭho dhammo saṃkiliṭṭhassa ca asaṃkiliṭṭhassa ca dhammassa adhipatipaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Sahajātādhipati</Emphasis>—  saṃkiliṭṭhādhipati sampayuttakānaṃ khandhānaṃ cittasamuṭṭhānānañca rūpānaṃ adhipatipaccayena paccayo. (3)
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="55">

<Paragraph number="3962">
Asaṃkiliṭṭho dhammo asaṃkiliṭṭhassa dhammassa adhipatipaccayena paccayo—  ārammaṇādhipati, sahajātādhipati. <Emphasis>Ārammaṇādhipati</Emphasis>—  dānaṃ …pe…  sīlaṃ …pe…  uposathakammaṃ …pe…  pubbe …pe…  jhānā vuṭṭhahitvā jhānaṃ garuṃ katvā paccavekkhati, ariyā maggā vuṭṭhahitvā maggaṃ garuṃ katvā paccavekkhanti …pe…  nibbānaṃ phalassa adhipatipaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Sahajātādhipati</Emphasis>—  asaṃkiliṭṭhādhipati sampayuttakānaṃ khandhānaṃ cittasamuṭṭhānānañca rūpānaṃ adhipatipaccayena paccayo. (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="3963">
Asaṃkiliṭṭho dhammo saṃkiliṭṭhassa dhammassa adhipatipaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Ārammaṇādhipati</Emphasis>—  dānaṃ …pe…  sīlaṃ …pe…  uposathakammaṃ …pe…  pubbe …pe…  jhānā vuṭṭhahitvā …pe…  cakkhuṃ …pe…  vatthuṃ asaṃkiliṭṭhe khandhe garuṃ katvā assādeti abhinandati, taṃ garuṃ katvā rāgo uppajjati, diṭṭhi uppajjati. (2)
</Paragraph>
</Division>
