<Division number="352">

<Paragraph number="4816">
Saraṇo dhammo saraṇassa dhammassa adhipatipaccayena paccayo—  ārammaṇādhipati, sahajātādhipati. <Emphasis>Ārammaṇādhipati</Emphasis>—  rāgaṃ garuṃ katvā assādeti abhinandati, taṃ garuṃ katvā rāgo uppajjati…  diṭṭhi uppajjati; diṭṭhiṃ garuṃ katvā assādeti abhinandati. <Emphasis>Sahajātādhipati</Emphasis>—  saraṇādhipati sampayuttakānaṃ khandhānaṃ adhipatipaccayena paccayo. Saraṇo dhammo araṇassa dhammassa adhipatipaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Sahajātādhipati</Emphasis>—  saraṇādhipati cittasamuṭṭhānānaṃ rūpānaṃ adhipatipaccayena paccayo. Saraṇo dhammo saraṇassa ca araṇassa ca dhammassa adhipatipaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Sahajātādhipati</Emphasis>—  saraṇādhipati sampayuttakānaṃ khandhānaṃ cittasamuṭṭhānānañca rūpānaṃ adhipatipaccayena paccayo. (3)
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="353">

<Paragraph number="4817">
Araṇo dhammo araṇassa dhammassa adhipatipaccayena paccayo—  ārammaṇādhipati, sahajātādhipati. <Emphasis>Ārammaṇādhipati</Emphasis>—  dānaṃ …pe…  sīlaṃ …pe…  uposathakammaṃ katvā taṃ garuṃ katvā paccavekkhati, pubbe …pe…  jhānā …pe…  ariyā maggā vuṭṭhahitvā maggaṃ garuṃ katvā paccavekkhanti, phalaṃ …pe…  nibbānaṃ garuṃ katvā paccavekkhanti, nibbānaṃ gotrabhussa, vodānassa, maggassa, phalassa, adhipatipaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Sahajātādhipati</Emphasis>—  araṇādhipati sampayuttakānaṃ khandhānaṃ cittasamuṭṭhānānañca rūpānaṃ adhipatipaccayena paccayo. Araṇo dhammo saraṇassa dhammassa adhipatipaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Ārammaṇādhipati</Emphasis>—  dānaṃ …pe…  sīlaṃ …pe…  uposathakammaṃ katvā taṃ garuṃ katvā assādeti abhinandati, taṃ garuṃ katvā rāgo uppajjati…  diṭṭhi uppajjati; pubbe suciṇṇāni …pe…  jhānā …pe…  cakkhuṃ …pe…  vatthuṃ, araṇe khandhe garuṃ katvā assādeti abhinandati, taṃ garuṃ katvā rāgo uppajjati…  diṭṭhi uppajjati. (2)
</Paragraph>
</Division>
