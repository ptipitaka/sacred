<Division number="65">

<Paragraph number="1833">
Sekkho dhammo sekkhassa dhammassa atthipaccayena paccayo—  sekkho eko khandho tiṇṇannaṃ khandhānaṃ …pe… . (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1834">
Sekkho dhammo nevasekkhanāsekkhassa dhammassa atthipaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ. <Emphasis>Sahajātā</Emphasis>—  sekkhā khandhā cittasamuṭṭhānānaṃ rūpānaṃ atthipaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Pacchājātā</Emphasis>—  sekkhā khandhā purejātassa imassa kāyassa atthipaccayena paccayo. (2)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1835">
Sekkho dhammo sekkhassa ca nevasekkhanāsekkhassa ca dhammassa atthipaccayena paccayo—  sekkho eko khandho tiṇṇannaṃ khandhānaṃ cittasamuṭṭhānānañca rūpānaṃ atthipaccayena paccayo …pe…  dve khandhā …pe… . (3)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1836">
Asekkho dhammo asekkhassa dhammassa atthipaccayena paccayo …pe…  tīṇi. (Sekkhasadisaṃ.)
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="66">

<Paragraph number="1837">
Nevasekkhanāsekkho dhammo nevasekkhanāsekkhassa dhammassa atthipaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, purejātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ, āhāraṃ, indriyaṃ. <Emphasis>Sahajāto</Emphasis>—  nevasekkhanāsekkho eko khandho tiṇṇannaṃ khandhānaṃ cittasamuṭṭhānānañca rūpānaṃ atthipaccayena paccayo …pe…  dve khandhā …pe…  paṭisandhikkhaṇe …pe…  khandhā vatthussa atthipaccayena paccayo. Vatthu khandhānaṃ atthipaccayena paccayo, ekaṃ mahābhūtaṃ …pe…  bāhiraṃ …pe…  asaññasattānaṃ …pe… . <Emphasis>Purejātaṃ</Emphasis>—  cakkhuṃ aniccato dukkhato anattato vipassati; assādeti abhinandati; taṃ ārabbha rāgo uppajjati …pe…  domanassaṃ uppajjati. Sotaṃ …pe…  vatthuṃ aniccato …pe…  vipassati, dibbena cakkhunā rūpaṃ passati, dibbāya sotadhātuyā saddaṃ suṇāti, rūpāyatanaṃ cakkhuviññāṇassa …pe…  phoṭṭhabbāyatanaṃ kāyaviññāṇassa …pe…  cakkhāyatanaṃ cakkhuviññāṇassa …pe…  kāyāyatanaṃ kāyaviññāṇassa…  vatthu nevasekkhanāsekkhānaṃ khandhānaṃ atthipaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Pacchājātā</Emphasis>—  nevasekkhanāsekkhā khandhā purejātassa imassa kāyassa atthipaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Kabaḷīkāro āhāro</Emphasis>—  imassa kāyassa atthipaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Rūpajīvitindriyaṃ</Emphasis>—  kaṭattārūpānaṃ atthipaccayena paccayo. (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1838">
Nevasekkhanāsekkho dhammo sekkhassa dhammassa atthipaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Purejātaṃ</Emphasis>—  vatthu sekkhānaṃ khandhānaṃ atthipaccayena paccayo. (2)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1839">
Nevasekkhanāsekkho dhammo asekkhassa dhammassa atthipaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Purejātaṃ</Emphasis>—  vatthu asekkhānaṃ khandhānaṃ atthipaccayena paccayo. (3)
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="67">

<Paragraph number="1840">
Sekkho ca nevasekkhanāsekkho ca dhammā sekkhassa dhammassa atthipaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, purejātaṃ. <Emphasis>Sahajāto</Emphasis>—  sekkho eko khandho ca vatthu ca tiṇṇannaṃ khandhānaṃ atthipaccayena paccayo …pe…  dve khandhā …pe… . (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1841">
Sekkho ca nevasekkhanāsekkho ca dhammā nevasekkhanāsekkhassa dhammassa atthipaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ, āhāraṃ, indriyaṃ. <Emphasis>Sahajātā</Emphasis>—  sekkhā khandhā ca mahābhūtā ca cittasamuṭṭhānānaṃ rūpānaṃ atthipaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Pacchājātā</Emphasis>—  sekkhā khandhā ca kabaḷīkāro āhāro ca imassa kāyassa atthipaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Pacchājātā</Emphasis>—  sekkhā khandhā ca rūpajīvitindriyañca kaṭattārūpānaṃ atthipaccayena paccayo. (2)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1842">
Asekkho ca nevasekkhanāsekkho ca dhammā asekkhassa dhammassa atthipaccayena paccayo …pe… . (Dve pañhā kātabbā, sekkhasadisā.)
</Paragraph>
</Division>
