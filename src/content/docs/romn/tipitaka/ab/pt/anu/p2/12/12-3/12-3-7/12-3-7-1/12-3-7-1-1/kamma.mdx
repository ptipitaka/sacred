<Division number="62">

<Paragraph number="3975">
Saṃkiliṭṭho dhammo saṃkiliṭṭhassa dhammassa kammapaccayena paccayo—  saṃkiliṭṭhā cetanā sampayuttakānaṃ khandhānaṃ kammapaccayena paccayo. (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="3976">
Saṃkiliṭṭho dhammo asaṃkiliṭṭhassa dhammassa kammapaccayena paccayo—  sahajātā, nānākkhaṇikā. <Emphasis>Sahajātā</Emphasis>—  saṃkiliṭṭhā cetanā cittasamuṭṭhānānaṃ rūpānaṃ kammapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Nānākkhaṇikā</Emphasis>—  saṃkiliṭṭhā cetanā vipākānaṃ khandhānaṃ kaṭattā ca rūpānaṃ kammapaccayena paccayo. (Mūlaṃ kātabbaṃ.) Saṃkiliṭṭhā cetanā sampayuttakānaṃ khandhānaṃ cittasamuṭṭhānānañca rūpānaṃ kammapaccayena paccayo. (3)
</Paragraph>
