<Division number="265">

<Paragraph number="2968">
Cittasahabhū dhammo cittasahabhussa dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Sahajātā</Emphasis>—  cittasahabhū khandhā cittasahabhūnaṃ cittasamuṭṭhānānaṃ rūpānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2969">
Cittasahabhū dhammo nocittasahabhussa dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ. <Emphasis>Sahajātā</Emphasis>—  cittasahabhū khandhā nocittasahabhūnaṃ cittasamuṭṭhānānaṃ rūpānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo; paṭisandhikkhaṇe …pe… . <Emphasis>Pacchājātā</Emphasis>—  cittasahabhū khandhā purejātassa imassa nocittasahabhussa kāyassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. (2)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2970">
Cittasahabhū dhammo cittasahabhussa ca nocittasahabhussa ca dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Sahajātā</Emphasis>—  cittasahabhū khandhā cittasahabhūnañca nocittasahabhūnañca cittasamuṭṭhānānaṃ rūpānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. (3)
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="266">

<Paragraph number="2971">
Nocittasahabhū dhammo nocittasahabhussa dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, purejātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ. <Emphasis>Sahajātaṃ</Emphasis>—  cittaṃ nocittasahabhūnaṃ cittasamuṭṭhānānaṃ rūpānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo; paṭisandhikkhaṇe cittaṃ kaṭattārūpānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo; cittaṃ vatthussa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo; vatthu cittassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Purejātaṃ</Emphasis>—  cakkhāyatanaṃ cakkhuviññāṇassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo …pe…  kāyāyatanaṃ …pe…  vatthu cittassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Pacchājātaṃ</Emphasis>—  cittaṃ purejātassa imassa nocittasahabhussa kāyassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2972">
Nocittasahabhū dhammo cittasahabhussa dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, purejātaṃ. <Emphasis>Sahajātaṃ</Emphasis>—  cittaṃ cittasahabhūnaṃ cittasamuṭṭhānānaṃ rūpānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Purejātaṃ</Emphasis>—  cakkhāyatanaṃ cakkhuviññāṇasahagatānaṃ khandhānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo …pe…  kāyāyatanaṃ …pe…  vatthu cittasahabhūnaṃ khandhānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. (2)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2973">
Nocittasahabhū dhammo cittasahabhussa ca nocittasahabhussa ca dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, purejātaṃ. <Emphasis>Sahajātaṃ</Emphasis>—  cittaṃ cittasahabhūnañca nocittasahabhūnañca cittasamuṭṭhānānaṃ rūpānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo; paṭisandhikkhaṇe …pe… . <Emphasis>Vatthupurejātaṃ</Emphasis>—  cakkhāyatanaṃ cakkhuviññāṇassa sampayuttakānañca rūpānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo …pe…  kāyāyatanaṃ …pe…  vatthu cittassa sampayuttakānañca khandhānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. (3)
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="267">

<Paragraph number="2974">
Cittasahabhū ca nocittasahabhū ca dhammā cittasahabhussa dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Sahajātā</Emphasis>—  cittasahabhū khandhā ca cittañca cittasahabhūnaṃ cittasamuṭṭhānānaṃ rūpānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2975">
Cittasahabhū ca nocittasahabhū ca dhammā nocittasahabhussa dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ. <Emphasis>Sahajātā</Emphasis>—  cittasahabhū khandhā ca cittañca nocittasahabhūnaṃ cittasamuṭṭhānānaṃ rūpānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo; paṭisandhikkhaṇe …pe… . <Emphasis>Pacchājātā</Emphasis>—  cittasahabhū khandhā ca cittañca purejātassa imassa nocittasahabhussa kāyassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. (2)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2976">
Cittasahabhū ca nocittasahabhū ca dhammā cittasahabhussa ca nocittasahabhussa ca dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo—  cittasahabhū khandhā ca cittañca cittasahabhūnañca nocittasahabhūnañca cittasamuṭṭhānānaṃ rūpānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. (3)
</Paragraph>
</Division>
