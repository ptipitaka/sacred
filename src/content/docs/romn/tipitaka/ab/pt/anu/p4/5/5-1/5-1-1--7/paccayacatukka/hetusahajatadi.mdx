<Paragraph number="1343">
Abyākataṃ anārammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca abyākato anārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

<Paragraph number="1344">

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1345">

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1346">
