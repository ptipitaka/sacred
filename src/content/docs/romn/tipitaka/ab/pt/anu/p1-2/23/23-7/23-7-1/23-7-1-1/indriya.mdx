<Division number="46">

<Paragraph number="3037">
Anidassanasappaṭigho dhammo anidassanaappaṭighassa dhammassa indriyapaccayena paccayo—  cakkhundriyaṃ cakkhuviññāṇassa …pe…  kāyindriyaṃ kāyaviññāṇassa indriyapaccayena paccayo. (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="3038">
Anidassanaappaṭigho dhammo anidassanaappaṭighassa dhammassa indriyapaccayena paccayo—  anidassanaappaṭighā indriyā sampayuttakānaṃ khandhānaṃ anidassanaappaṭighānaṃ cittasamuṭṭhānānañca rūpānaṃ indriyapaccayena paccayo. Paṭisandhikkhaṇe anidassanaappaṭighā indriyā sampayuttakānaṃ khandhānaṃ anidassanaappaṭighānañca kaṭattārūpānaṃ indriyapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Rūpajīvitindriyaṃ</Emphasis>—  anidassanaappaṭighānaṃ kaṭattārūpānaṃ indriyapaccayena paccayo. (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="3039">
Anidassanaappaṭigho dhammo sanidassanasappaṭighassa dhammassa indriyapaccayena paccayo—  anidassanaappaṭighā indriyā sanidassanasappaṭighānaṃ cittasamuṭṭhānānaṃ rūpānaṃ indriyapaccayena paccayo. Paṭisandhikkhaṇe anidassanaappaṭighā indriyā sanidassanasappaṭighānaṃ kaṭattārūpānaṃ indriyapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Rūpajīvitindriyaṃ</Emphasis>—  sanidassanasappaṭighānaṃ kaṭattārūpānaṃ indriyapaccayena paccayo. (2)
</Paragraph>

</Paragraph>

Anidassanasappaṭigho ca anidassanaappaṭigho ca dhammā anidassanaappaṭighassa dhammassa indriyapaccayena paccayo—  cakkhundriyañca cakkhuviññāṇañca cakkhuviññāṇasahagatānaṃ khandhānaṃ indriyapaccayena paccayo …pe…  kāyindriyañca kāyaviññāṇañca kāyaviññāṇasahagatānaṃ khandhānaṃ indriyapaccayena paccayo.
