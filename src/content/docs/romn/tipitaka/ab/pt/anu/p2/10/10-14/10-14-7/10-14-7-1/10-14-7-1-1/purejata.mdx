<Paragraph number="3462">
Upādinno dhammo upādinnassa dhammassa purejātapaccayena paccayo—  ārammaṇapurejātaṃ, vatthupurejātaṃ. <Emphasis>Ārammaṇapurejātaṃ</Emphasis>—  cakkhuṃ …pe…  kāyaṃ upādinne rūpe…  gandhe…  rase…  phoṭṭhabbe…  vatthuṃ aniccato …pe…  domanassaṃ uppajjati; kusalākusale niruddhe vipāko tadārammaṇatā uppajjati, upādinnaṃ rūpāyatanaṃ cakkhuviññāṇassa …pe…  gandhāyatanaṃ …pe…  phoṭṭhabbāyatanaṃ kāyaviññāṇassa …pe… . <Emphasis>Vatthupurejātaṃ</Emphasis>—  cakkhāyatanaṃ cakkhuviññāṇassa …pe…  kāyāyatanaṃ kāyaviññāṇassa …pe…  vatthu upādinnānaṃ khandhānaṃ purejātapaccayena paccayo. (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="3463">
Upādinno dhammo anupādinnassa dhammassa purejātapaccayena paccayo—  ārammaṇapurejātaṃ, vatthupurejātaṃ. <Emphasis>Ārammaṇapurejātaṃ</Emphasis>—  cakkhuṃ …pe…  kāyaṃ, upādinne rūpe…  gandhe…  rase…  phoṭṭhabbe vatthuṃ aniccato …pe…  domanassaṃ uppajjati; dibbena cakkhunā upādinnaṃ rūpaṃ passati. <Emphasis>Vatthupurejātaṃ</Emphasis>—  vatthu anupādinnānaṃ khandhānaṃ purejātapaccayena paccayo. (2)
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="454">

<Paragraph number="3464">
Anupādinno dhammo anupādinnassa dhammassa purejātapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Ārammaṇapurejātaṃ</Emphasis>—  anupādinne rūpe…  sadde…  gandhe…  rase…  phoṭṭhabbe aniccato …pe…  domanassaṃ uppajjati; dibbena cakkhunā anupādinnaṃ rūpaṃ passati, dibbāya sotadhātuyā saddaṃ suṇāti. (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="3465">
Anupādinno dhammo upādinnassa dhammassa purejātapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Ārammaṇapurejātaṃ</Emphasis>—  anupādinne rūpe…  sadde…  gandhe…  rase…  phoṭṭhabbe aniccato …pe…  domanassaṃ uppajjati; kusalākusale niruddhe vipāko tadārammaṇatā uppajjati, anupādinnaṃ rūpāyatanaṃ cakkhuviññāṇassa …pe…  saddāyatanaṃ …pe…  phoṭṭhabbāyatanaṃ kāyaviññāṇassa purejātapaccayena paccayo. (2)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="3466">
Upādinno ca anupādinno ca dhammā upādinnassa dhammassa purejātapaccayena paccayo—  <Emphasis>ārammaṇapurejātaṃ, vatthupurejātaṃ</Emphasis>. Anupādinnaṃ rūpāyatanañca cakkhāyatanañca cakkhuviññāṇassa …pe…  saddāyatanañca sotāyatanañca …pe…  phoṭṭhabbāyatanañca kāyāyatanañca kāyaviññāṇassa purejātapaccayena paccayo; anupādinnaṃ rūpāyatanañca vatthu ca …pe…  phoṭṭhabbāyatanañca vatthu ca upādinnānaṃ khandhānaṃ purejātapaccayena paccayo. (1)
</Paragraph>
