Hetuyā nava, ārammaṇe nava (arūpaṃ sabbaṃ uddharitabbaṃ. Cittasamuṭṭhānadukasadisaṃ.) Adhipatiyā nava (mahābhūtā chasupi pañhesu kātabbā, adhipatiyā tīsu natthi.)
