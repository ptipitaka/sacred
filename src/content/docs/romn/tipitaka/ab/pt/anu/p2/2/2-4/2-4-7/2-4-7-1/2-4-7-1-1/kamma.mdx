<Paragraph number="716">
Appaṭigho dhammo appaṭighassa dhammassa kammapaccayena paccayo—  sahajātā, nānākkhaṇikā. <Emphasis>Sahajātā</Emphasis>—  appaṭighā cetanā sampayuttakānaṃ khandhānaṃ appaṭighānañca cittasamuṭṭhānānaṃ rūpānaṃ kammapaccayena paccayo; paṭisandhikkhaṇe …pe… . <Emphasis>Nānākkhaṇikā</Emphasis>—  appaṭighā cetanā vipākānaṃ khandhānaṃ appaṭighānañca kaṭattārūpānaṃ kammapaccayena paccayo. (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="717">

<Paragraph number="718">

(3)
