<Paragraph number="4156">
Bhāvanāya pahātabbo dhammo bhāvanāya pahātabbassa dhammassa upanissayapaccayena paccayo—  ārammaṇūpanissayo, anantarūpanissayo, pakatūpanissayo …pe… . <Emphasis>Pakatūpanissayo</Emphasis>—  bhāvanāya pahātabbo rāgo…  doso…  moho…  māno…  patthanā bhāvanāya pahātabbassa rāgassa…  dosassa…  mohassa…  mānassa…  patthanāya upanissayapaccayena paccayo. (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="4157">
Bhāvanāya pahātabbo dhammo nabhāvanāya pahātabbassa dhammassa upanissayapaccayena paccayo—  ārammaṇūpanissayo, anantarūpanissayo, pakatūpanissayo …pe… . <Emphasis>Pakatūpanissayo</Emphasis>—  bhāvanāya pahātabbaṃ rāgaṃ upanissāya dānaṃ deti …pe…  samāpattiṃ uppādeti, pāṇaṃ hanati …pe…  saṃghaṃ bhindati; bhāvanāya pahātabbaṃ dosaṃ…  mohaṃ…  mānaṃ…  patthanaṃ upanissāya dānaṃ deti …pe…  samāpattiṃ uppādeti, pāṇaṃ hanati …pe…  saṃghaṃ bhindati; bhāvanāya pahātabbo rāgo …pe…  patthanā saddhāya …pe…  paññāya nabhāvanāya pahātabbassa, rāgassa…  dosassa…  mohassa…  diṭṭhiyā…  patthanāya…  kāyikassa sukhassa…  kāyikassa dukkhassa…  maggassa…  phalasamāpattiyā upanissayapaccayena paccayo. (2)
</Paragraph>

<Division number="43">

<Paragraph number="4158">
Nabhāvanāya pahātabbo dhammo nabhāvanāya pahātabbassa dhammassa upanissayapaccayena paccayo—  ārammaṇūpanissayo, anantarūpanissayo, pakatūpanissayo …pe… . <Emphasis>Pakatūpanissayo</Emphasis>—  saddhaṃ upanissāya dānaṃ deti …pe…  samāpattiṃ uppādeti, diṭṭhiṃ gaṇhāti; sīlaṃ …pe…  paññaṃ, nabhāvanāya pahātabbaṃ rāgaṃ…  dosaṃ…  mohaṃ…  diṭṭhiṃ…  patthanaṃ…  kāyikaṃ sukhaṃ…  kāyikaṃ dukkhaṃ …pe…  senāsanaṃ upanissāya dānaṃ deti …pe…  samāpattiṃ uppādeti, pāṇaṃ hanati …pe…  saṃghaṃ bhindati; saddhā …pe…  senāsanaṃ saddhāya …pe…  paññāya nabhāvanāya pahātabbassa rāgassa…  dosassa…  mohassa…  diṭṭhiyā…  patthanāya…  kāyikassa sukhassa…  kāyikassa dukkhassa…  maggassa…  phalasamāpattiyā upanissayapaccayena paccayo. (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="4159">
Nabhāvanāya pahātabbo dhammo bhāvanāya pahātabbassa dhammassa upanissayapaccayena paccayo—  ārammaṇūpanissayo, anantarūpanissayo, pakatūpanissayo …pe… . <Emphasis>Pakatūpanissayo</Emphasis>—  saddhaṃ upanissāya mānaṃ jappeti …pe…  sīlaṃ …pe…  paññaṃ…  rāgaṃ …pe…  kāyikaṃ sukhaṃ…  kāyikaṃ dukkhaṃ…  senāsanaṃ upanissāya mānaṃ jappeti; saddhā …pe…  senāsanaṃ bhāvanāya pahātabbassa rāgassa…  dosassa…  mohassa…  mānassa…  patthanāya upanissayapaccayena paccayo. (2)
</Paragraph>
