<Paragraph number="2054">
Nīvaraṇasampayutto dhammo nīvaraṇavippayuttassa dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2055">
Nīvaraṇavippayutto dhammo nīvaraṇavippayuttassa dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, purejātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2056">
Nīvaraṇavippayutto dhammo nīvaraṇasampayuttassa dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Purejātaṃ</Emphasis>—  vatthu nīvaraṇasampayuttakānaṃ khandhānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. (2)
</Paragraph>
