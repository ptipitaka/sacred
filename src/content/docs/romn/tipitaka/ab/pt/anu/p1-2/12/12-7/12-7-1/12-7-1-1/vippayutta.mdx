<Division number="64">

<Paragraph number="1828">
Sekkho dhammo nevasekkhanāsekkhassa dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ. <Emphasis>Sahajātā</Emphasis>—  sekkhā khandhā cittasamuṭṭhānānaṃ rūpānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Pacchājātā</Emphasis>—  sekkhā khandhā purejātassa imassa kāyassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1829">
Asekkho dhammo nevasekkhanāsekkhassa dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ. (Sekkhasadisaṃ.)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1830">
Nevasekkhanāsekkho dhammo nevasekkhanāsekkhassa dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, purejātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ. <Emphasis>Sahajātā</Emphasis>—  nevasekkhanāsekkhā khandhā cittasamuṭṭhānānaṃ rūpānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. Paṭisandhikkhaṇe nevasekkhanāsekkhā khandhā kaṭattārūpānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. Khandhā vatthussa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. Vatthu khandhānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Purejātaṃ</Emphasis>—  cakkhāyatanaṃ cakkhuviññāṇassa …pe…  kāyāyatanaṃ kāyaviññāṇassa, vatthu nevasekkhanāsekkhānaṃ khandhānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Pacchājātā</Emphasis>—  nevasekkhanāsekkhā khandhā purejātassa imassa kāyassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1831">
Nevasekkhanāsekkho dhammo sekkhassa dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Purejātaṃ</Emphasis>—  vatthu sekkhānaṃ khandhānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. (2)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1832">
Nevasekkhanāsekkho dhammo asekkhassa dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Purejātaṃ</Emphasis>—  vatthu asekkhānaṃ khandhānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. (3)
</Paragraph>
</Division>
