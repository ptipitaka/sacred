<Division number="32">

<Paragraph number="3007">
Sanidassanasappaṭigho dhammo anidassanaappaṭighassa dhammassa adhipatipaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Ārammaṇādhipati</Emphasis>—  rūpaṃ garuṃ katvā assādeti abhinandati, taṃ garuṃ katvā rāgo uppajjati, diṭṭhi uppajjati. (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="3008">
Anidassanasappaṭigho dhammo anidassanaappaṭighassa dhammassa adhipatipaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Ārammaṇādhipati</Emphasis>—  cakkhuṃ …pe…  kāyaṃ…  sadde…  gandhe…  rase…  phoṭṭhabbe…  garuṃ katvā assādeti abhinandati, taṃ garuṃ katvā rāgo uppajjati, diṭṭhi uppajjati. (1)
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="33">

<Paragraph number="3009">
Anidassanaappaṭigho dhammo anidassanaappaṭighassa dhammassa adhipatipaccayena paccayo—  ārammaṇādhipati, sahajātādhipati. <Emphasis>Ārammaṇādhipati</Emphasis>—  dānaṃ datvā sīlaṃ samādiyitvā uposathakammaṃ katvā taṃ garuṃ katvā …pe…  jhānā vuṭṭhahitvā …pe…  ariyā maggā vuṭṭhahitvā …pe…  phalā vuṭṭhahitvā …pe…  phalaṃ garuṃ katvā …pe…  nibbānaṃ garuṃ katvā…  nibbānaṃ gotrabhussa, vodānassa, maggassa, phalassa adhipatipaccayena paccayo. Vatthuṃ…  itthindriyaṃ…  purisindriyaṃ…  jīvitindriyaṃ…  āpodhātuṃ…  kabaḷīkāraṃ āhāraṃ…  anidassanaappaṭighe khandhe garuṃ katvā assādeti abhinandati, taṃ garuṃ katvā rāgo uppajjati, diṭṭhi uppajjati. <Emphasis>Sahajātādhipati</Emphasis>—  anidassanaappaṭighādhipati sampayuttakānaṃ khandhānaṃ anidassanaappaṭighānañca cittasamuṭṭhānānaṃ rūpānaṃ adhipatipaccayena paccayo. (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="3010">
Anidassanaappaṭigho dhammo sanidassanasappaṭighassa dhammassa adhipatipaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Sahajātādhipati</Emphasis>—  anidassanaappaṭighādhipati sanidassanasappaṭighānaṃ cittasamuṭṭhānānaṃ rūpānaṃ adhipatipaccayena paccayo. (2)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="3011">
(Anidassanaappaṭighamūlake sattapi pañhā vibhajitabbā, adhipati tividharūpasaṅgahena.)
</Paragraph>
</Division>
