<Division number="263">

<Paragraph number="2964">
Cittasahabhū dhammo cittasahabhussa dhammassa kammapaccayena paccayo—  sahajātā, nānākkhaṇikā. <Emphasis>Sahajātā</Emphasis>—  cittasahabhū cetanā sampayuttakānaṃ khandhānaṃ cittasahabhūnaṃ cittasamuṭṭhānānañca rūpānaṃ kammapaccayena paccayo; paṭisandhikkhaṇe …pe… . <Emphasis>Nānākkhaṇikā</Emphasis>—  cittasahabhū cetanā vipākānaṃ cittasahabhūnaṃ khandhānaṃ kammapaccayena paccayo. (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2965">
Cittasahabhū dhammo nocittasahabhussa dhammassa kammapaccayena paccayo—  sahajātā, nānākkhaṇikā. <Emphasis>Sahajātā</Emphasis>—  cittasahabhū cetanā cittassa nocittasahabhūnaṃ cittasamuṭṭhānānañca rūpānaṃ kammapaccayena paccayo; paṭisandhikkhaṇe …pe… . <Emphasis>Nānākkhaṇikā</Emphasis>—  cittasahabhū cetanā vipākassa cittassa kaṭattā ca rūpānaṃ kammapaccayena paccayo. (2)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2966">
Cittasahabhū dhammo cittasahabhussa ca nocittasahabhussa ca dhammassa kammapaccayena paccayo—  sahajātā, nānākkhaṇikā. <Emphasis>Sahajātā</Emphasis>—  cittasahabhū cetanā sampayuttakānaṃ khandhānaṃ cittassa ca cittasahabhūnañca nocittasahabhūnañca cittasamuṭṭhānānañca rūpānaṃ kammapaccayena paccayo; paṭisandhikkhaṇe …pe… . <Emphasis>Nānākkhaṇikā</Emphasis>—  cittasahabhū cetanā vipākānaṃ khandhānaṃ cittassa kaṭattā ca rūpānaṃ kammapaccayena paccayo. (3)
</Paragraph>
</Division>
