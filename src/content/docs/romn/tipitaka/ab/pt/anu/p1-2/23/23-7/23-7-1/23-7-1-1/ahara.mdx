<Paragraph number="3034">
Anidassanaappaṭigho dhammo anidassanaappaṭighassa dhammassa āhārapaccayena paccayo—  anidassanaappaṭighā āhārā sampayuttakānaṃ khandhānaṃ anidassanaappaṭighānañca cittasamuṭṭhānānaṃ rūpānaṃ āhārapaccayena paccayo. Paṭisandhikkhaṇe anidassanaappaṭighā āhārā sampayuttakānaṃ khandhānaṃ anidassanaappaṭighānañca kaṭattārūpānaṃ āhārapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Kabaḷīkāro āhāro</Emphasis>—  imassa anidassanaappaṭighassa kāyassa āhārapaccayena paccayo. (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="3035">
Anidassanaappaṭigho dhammo sanidassanasappaṭighassa dhammassa āhārapaccayena paccayo—  anidassanaappaṭighā āhārā sanidassanasappaṭighānaṃ cittasamuṭṭhānānaṃ rūpānaṃ āhārapaccayena paccayo. Paṭisandhikkhaṇe anidassanaappaṭighā āhārā sanidassanasappaṭighānaṃ kaṭattārūpānaṃ āhārapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Kabaḷīkāro āhāro</Emphasis>—  imassa sanidassanasappaṭighassa kāyassa āhārapaccayena paccayo. (2)
</Paragraph>
