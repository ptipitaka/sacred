<Division number="55">

<Paragraph number="2393">
Micchattaniyato dhammo aniyatassa dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ. <Emphasis>Sahajātā</Emphasis>—  micchattaniyatā khandhā cittasamuṭṭhānānaṃ rūpānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Pacchājātā</Emphasis>—  micchattaniyatā khandhā purejātassa imassa kāyassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2394">
Sammattaniyato dhammo aniyatassa dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ. <Emphasis>Sahajātā</Emphasis>—  sammattaniyatā khandhā cittasamuṭṭhānānaṃ rūpānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Pacchājātā</Emphasis>—  sammattaniyatā khandhā purejātassa imassa kāyassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2395">
Aniyato dhammo aniyatassa dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, purejātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ. <Emphasis>Sahajātā</Emphasis>—  aniyatā khandhā cittasamuṭṭhānānaṃ rūpānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. Paṭisandhikkhaṇe …pe…  khandhā vatthussa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. Vatthu khandhānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Purejātaṃ</Emphasis>—  cakkhāyatanaṃ cakkhuviññāṇassa …pe…  kāyāyatanaṃ kāyaviññāṇassa…  vatthu aniyatānaṃ khandhānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Pacchājātā</Emphasis>—  aniyatā khandhā purejātassa imassa kāyassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2396">
Aniyato dhammo micchattaniyatassa dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Purejātaṃ</Emphasis>—  vatthu micchattaniyatānaṃ khandhānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. (2)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2397">
Aniyato dhammo sammattaniyatassa dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Purejātaṃ</Emphasis>—  vatthu sammattaniyatānaṃ khandhānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. (3)
</Paragraph>
