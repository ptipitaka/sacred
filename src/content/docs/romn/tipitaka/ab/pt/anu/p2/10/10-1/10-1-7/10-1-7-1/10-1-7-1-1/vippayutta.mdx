<Paragraph number="2401">
Sārammaṇo dhammo anārammaṇassa dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2402">
Anārammaṇo dhammo sārammaṇassa dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, purejātaṃ. <Emphasis>Sahajātaṃ</Emphasis>—  paṭisandhikkhaṇe vatthu sārammaṇānaṃ khandhānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Purejātaṃ</Emphasis>—  cakkhāyatanaṃ cakkhuviññāṇassa …pe…  kāyāyatanaṃ kāyaviññāṇassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo; vatthu sārammaṇānaṃ khandhānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. (1)
</Paragraph>
