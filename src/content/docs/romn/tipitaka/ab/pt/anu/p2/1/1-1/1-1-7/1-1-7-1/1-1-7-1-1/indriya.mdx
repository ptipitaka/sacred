<Paragraph number="96">
Nahetu dhammo nahetussa dhammassa indriyapaccayena paccayo—  nahetū indriyā sampayuttakānaṃ khandhānaṃ cittasamuṭṭhānānañca rūpānaṃ indriyapaccayena paccayo; paṭisandhikkhaṇe …pe…  cakkhundriyaṃ cakkhuviññāṇassa …pe…  kāyindriyaṃ kāyaviññāṇassa …pe… . <Emphasis>Rūpajīvitindriyaṃ</Emphasis>—  kaṭattārūpānaṃ indriyapaccayena paccayo. (Evaṃ indriyapaccayā vitthāretabbā. Nava.)
</Paragraph>
