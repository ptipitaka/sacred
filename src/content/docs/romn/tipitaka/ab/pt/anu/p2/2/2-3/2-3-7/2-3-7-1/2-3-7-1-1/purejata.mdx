<Division number="40">

<Paragraph number="626">
Sanidassano dhammo anidassanassa dhammassa purejātapaccayena paccayo—  sanidassanaṃ rūpaṃ aniccato …pe…  domanassaṃ uppajjati; dibbena cakkhunā rūpaṃ passati, rūpāyatanaṃ cakkhuviññāṇassa purejātapaccayena paccayo. (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="627">
Anidassano dhammo anidassanassa dhammassa purejātapaccayena paccayo—  ārammaṇapurejātaṃ, vatthupurejātaṃ. <Emphasis>Ārammaṇapurejātaṃ</Emphasis>—  cakkhuṃ …pe…  vatthuṃ aniccato …pe…  domanassaṃ uppajjati, dibbāya sotadhātuyā saddaṃ suṇāti, saddāyatanaṃ sotaviññāṇassa …pe…  phoṭṭhabbāyatanaṃ kāyaviññāṇassa purejātapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Vatthupurejātaṃ</Emphasis>—  cakkhāyatanaṃ cakkhuviññāṇassa …pe…  kāyāyatanaṃ kāyaviññāṇassa …pe…  vatthu anidassanānaṃ khandhānaṃ purejātapaccayena paccayo. (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="628">
Sanidassano ca anidassano ca dhammā anidassanassa dhammassa purejātapaccayena paccayo—  <Emphasis>ārammaṇapurejātaṃ, vatthupurejātaṃ</Emphasis>. Rūpāyatanañca vatthu ca anidassanānaṃ khandhānaṃ purejātapaccayena paccayo; rūpāyatanañca cakkhāyatanañca cakkhuviññāṇassa purejātapaccayena paccayo. (1)
</Paragraph>
</Division>
