<Division number="35">

<Paragraph number="1958">
Nonīvaraṇo dhammo nonīvaraṇassa dhammassa upanissayapaccayena paccayo—  ārammaṇūpanissayo, anantarūpanissayo, pakatūpanissayo …pe… . <Emphasis>Pakatūpanissayo</Emphasis>—  saddhaṃ upanissāya dānaṃ deti …pe…  sīlaṃ …pe…  uposathakammaṃ …pe…  jhānaṃ uppādeti, vipassanaṃ …pe…  maggaṃ …pe…  abhiññaṃ …pe…  samāpattiṃ uppādeti, mānaṃ jappeti, diṭṭhiṃ gaṇhāti; sīlaṃ …pe…  paññaṃ …pe…  mānaṃ…  diṭṭhiṃ…  patthanaṃ…  kāyikaṃ sukhaṃ…  kāyikaṃ dukkhaṃ…  utuṃ…  bhojanaṃ…  senāsanaṃ upanissāya dānaṃ deti …pe…  saṃghaṃ bhindati; saddhā …pe…  senāsanaṃ saddhāya …pe…  paññāya, rāgassa …pe…  patthanāya, kāyikassa sukhassa, kāyikassa dukkhassa, maggassa, phalasamāpattiyā upanissayapaccayena paccayo. (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1959">
Nonīvaraṇo dhammo nīvaraṇassa dhammassa upanissayapaccayena paccayo (tīṇi upanissayā). <Emphasis>Pakatūpanissayo</Emphasis>—  saddhaṃ upanissāya mānaṃ jappeti, diṭṭhiṃ gaṇhāti; sīlaṃ …pe…  senāsanaṃ upanissāya pāṇaṃ hanati …pe…  saṃghaṃ bhindati; saddhā …pe…  senāsanaṃ rāgassa …pe…  patthanāya upanissayapaccayena paccayo. (2)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1960">
Nonīvaraṇo dhammo nīvaraṇassa ca nonīvaraṇassa ca dhammassa upanissayapaccayena paccayo—  ārammaṇūpanissayo, anantarūpanissayo, pakatūpanissayo …pe… . <Emphasis>Pakatūpanissayo</Emphasis>—  saddhaṃ upanissāya mānaṃ jappeti, diṭṭhiṃ gaṇhāti; sīlaṃ …pe…  senāsanaṃ upanissāya pāṇaṃ hanati …pe…  saṃghaṃ bhindati; saddhā …pe…  senāsanaṃ nīvaraṇānaṃ sampayuttakānañca khandhānaṃ upanissayapaccayena paccayo. (3)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1961">
Nīvaraṇo ca nonīvaraṇo ca dhammā nīvaraṇassa dhammassa upanissayapaccayena paccayo—  ārammaṇūpanissayo, anantarūpanissayo, pakatūpanissayo …pe… . <Emphasis>Pakatūpanissayo</Emphasis>—  nīvaraṇā ca sampayuttakā ca khandhā nīvaraṇānaṃ upanissayapaccayena paccayo…  tīṇi.
</Paragraph>
