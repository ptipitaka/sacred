<Division number="344">

<Paragraph number="3169">
Ajjhattiko dhammo ajjhattikassa dhammassa upanissayapaccayena paccayo—  ārammaṇūpanissayo, anantarūpanissayo, pakatūpanissayo …pe… . <Emphasis>Pakatūpanissayo</Emphasis>—  cittaṃ cittassa upanissayapaccayena paccayo…  tīṇi.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="3170">
Bāhiro dhammo bāhirassa dhammassa upanissayapaccayena paccayo—  ārammaṇūpanissayo, anantarūpanissayo, pakatūpanissayo …pe… . <Emphasis>Pakatūpanissayo</Emphasis>—  saddhaṃ upanissāya dānaṃ deti …pe…  mānaṃ jappeti, diṭṭhiṃ gaṇhāti; sīlaṃ …pe…  senāsanaṃ upanissāya dānaṃ deti …pe…  saṃghaṃ bhindati; saddhā …pe…  senāsanaṃ saddhāya …pe…  phalasamāpattiyā upanissayapaccayena paccayo. (Tīṇipi pūretvā kātabbā, cittassāti kātabbā, sampayuttakānañcāti kātabbā.)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="3171">
Ajjhattiko ca bāhiro ca dhammā ajjhattikassa dhammassa upanissayapaccayena paccayo…  tīṇi.
</Paragraph>
</Division>
