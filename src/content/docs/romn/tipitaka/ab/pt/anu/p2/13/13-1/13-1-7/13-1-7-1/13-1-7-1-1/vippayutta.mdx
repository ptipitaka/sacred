<Paragraph number="4116">
Dassanena pahātabbo dhammo nadassanena pahātabbassa dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="4117">
Nadassanena pahātabbo dhammo nadassanena pahātabbassa dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, purejātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="4118">
Nadassanena pahātabbo dhammo dassanena pahātabbassa dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Purejātaṃ</Emphasis>—  vatthu dassanena pahātabbānaṃ khandhānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. (2)
</Paragraph>
