<Division number="33">

<Paragraph number="1687">
Gantho dhammo noganthassa dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1688">
Nogantho dhammo noganthassa dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, purejātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ (Saṃkhittaṃ.) (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1689">
Nogantho dhammo ganthassa dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Purejātaṃ</Emphasis>—  vatthu ganthānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. (2)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1690">
Nogantho dhammo ganthassa ca noganthassa ca dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Purejātaṃ</Emphasis>—  vatthu ganthānaṃ sampayuttakānañca khandhānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. (3)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1691">
Gantho ca nogantho ca dhammā noganthassa dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) (1)
</Paragraph>
</Division>
