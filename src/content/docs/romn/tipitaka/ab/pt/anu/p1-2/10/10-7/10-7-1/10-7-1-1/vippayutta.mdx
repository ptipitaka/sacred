<Division number="102">

<Paragraph number="1367">
Dassanena pahātabbahetuko dhammo nevadassanena nabhāvanāya pahātabbahetukassa dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ. (Dassanattikasadisaṃ.)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1368">
Bhāvanāya pahātabbahetuko dhammo nevadassanena nabhāvanāya pahātabbahetukassa dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ. (Dassanattikasadisaṃ.)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1369">
Nevadassanena nabhāvanāya pahātabbahetuko dhammo nevadassanena nabhāvanāya pahātabbahetukassa dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, purejātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ. (Dassanattikasadisaṃ.) <Emphasis>Pacchājātā</Emphasis>—  nevadassanena nabhāvanāya pahātabbahetukā khandhā ca moho ca purejātassa imassa kāyassa …pe… . (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1370">
Nevadassanena nabhāvanāya pahātabbahetuko dhammo dassanena pahātabbahetukassa dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Purejātaṃ</Emphasis>—  vatthu dassanena pahātabbahetukānaṃ khandhānaṃ …pe… . (2)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1371">
Nevadassanena nabhāvanāya pahātabbahetuko dhammo bhāvanāya pahātabbahetukassa dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Purejātaṃ</Emphasis>—  vatthu bhāvanāya pahātabbahetukānaṃ khandhānaṃ …pe… . (3)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1372">
Nevadassanena nabhāvanāya pahātabbahetuko dhammo dassanena pahātabbahetukassa ca nevadassanena nabhāvanāya pahātabbahetukassa ca dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Purejātaṃ</Emphasis>—  vatthu vicikicchāsahagatānaṃ khandhānaṃ mohassa ca vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. (4)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1373">
Nevadassanena nabhāvanāya pahātabbahetuko dhammo bhāvanāya pahātabbahetukassa ca nevadassanena nabhāvanāya pahātabbahetukassa ca dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Purejātaṃ</Emphasis>—  vatthu uddhaccasahagatānaṃ khandhānaṃ mohassa ca vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. (5)
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="103">

<Paragraph number="1374">
Dassanena pahātabbahetuko ca nevadassanena nabhāvanāya pahātabbahetuko ca dhammā nevadassanena nabhāvanāya pahātabbahetukassa dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ. <Emphasis>Sahajātā</Emphasis>—  vicikicchāsahagatā khandhā ca moho ca cittasamuṭṭhānānaṃ rūpānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Pacchājātā</Emphasis>—  vicikicchāsahagatā khandhā ca moho ca purejātassa imassa kāyassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1375">
Bhāvanāya pahātabbahetuko ca nevadassanena nabhāvanāya pahātabbahetuko ca dhammā nevadassanena nabhāvanāya pahātabbahetukassa dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ. <Emphasis>Sahajātā</Emphasis>—  uddhaccasahagatā khandhā ca moho ca cittasamuṭṭhānānaṃ rūpānaṃ …pe… . <Emphasis>Pacchājātā</Emphasis>—  uddhaccasahagatā khandhā ca moho ca purejātassa imassa kāyassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. (1)
</Paragraph>
</Division>
