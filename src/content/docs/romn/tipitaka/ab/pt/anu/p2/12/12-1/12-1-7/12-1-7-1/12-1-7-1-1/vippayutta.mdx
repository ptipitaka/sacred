<Division number="29">

<Paragraph number="3887">
Kileso dhammo nokilesassa dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="3888">
Nokileso dhammo nokilesassa dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, purejātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="3889">
Nokileso dhammo kilesassa dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Purejātaṃ</Emphasis>—  vatthu kilesānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. (2)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="3890">
Nokileso dhammo kilesassa ca nokilesassa ca dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Purejātaṃ</Emphasis>—  vatthu kilesānaṃ sampayuttakānañca khandhānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. (3)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="3891">
Kileso ca nokileso ca dhammā nokilesassa dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ. Vitthāretabbaṃ.)
</Paragraph>
</Division>
