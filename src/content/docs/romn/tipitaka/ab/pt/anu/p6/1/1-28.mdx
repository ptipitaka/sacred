Hetuṃ cittasaṃsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhānaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca hetu cittasaṃsaṭṭhasamuṭṭhāno dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Sabbattha nava.)

<Paragraph number="151">

</Paragraph>
