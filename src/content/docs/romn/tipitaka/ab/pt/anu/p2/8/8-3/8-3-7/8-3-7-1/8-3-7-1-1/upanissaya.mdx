<Paragraph number="2044">
Nīvaraṇasampayutto dhammo nīvaraṇasampayuttassa dhammassa upanissayapaccayena paccayo—  ārammaṇūpanissayo, anantarūpanissayo, pakatūpanissayo …pe… . <Emphasis>Pakatūpanissayo</Emphasis>—  rāgaṃ upanissāya pāṇaṃ hanati …pe…  saṃghaṃ bhindati; dosaṃ…  mohaṃ…  mānaṃ…  diṭṭhiṃ…  patthanaṃ upanissāya pāṇaṃ hanati …pe…  saṃghaṃ bhindati; rāgo …pe…  patthanā rāgassa …pe…  patthanāya upanissayapaccayena paccayo. </Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2045">

(2)
</Paragraph>
