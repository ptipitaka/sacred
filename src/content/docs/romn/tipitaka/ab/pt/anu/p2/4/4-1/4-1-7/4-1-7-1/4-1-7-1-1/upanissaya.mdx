<Paragraph number="1374">
Saññojano dhammo saññojanassa dhammassa upanissayapaccayena paccayo—  ārammaṇūpanissayo, anantarūpanissayo, pakatūpanissayo …pe… . <Emphasis>Pakatūpanissayo</Emphasis>—  saññojanā saññojanānaṃ upanissayapaccayena paccayo. (Evaṃ tīṇipi.)

<Division number="23">

<Paragraph number="1375">
Nosaññojano dhammo nosaññojanassa dhammassa upanissayapaccayena paccayo—  ārammaṇūpanissayo, anantarūpanissayo, pakatūpanissayo …pe… . <Emphasis>Pakatūpanissayo</Emphasis>—  saddhaṃ upanissāya dānaṃ deti …pe…  samāpattiṃ uppādeti, mānaṃ jappeti, diṭṭhiṃ gaṇhāti; sīlaṃ …pe…  paññaṃ, rāgaṃ …pe…  patthanaṃ …pe…  senāsanaṃ upanissāya dānaṃ deti …pe…  saṃghaṃ bhindati; saddhā …pe…  senāsanaṃ saddhāya …pe…  phalasamāpattiyā upanissayapaccayena paccayo. (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1376">
Nosaññojano dhammo saññojanassa dhammassa upanissayapaccayena paccayo—  ārammaṇūpanissayo, anantarūpanissayo, pakatūpanissayo …pe… . <Emphasis>Pakatūpanissayo</Emphasis>—  saddhaṃ upanissāya mānaṃ jappeti, diṭṭhiṃ gaṇhāti; sīlaṃ …pe…  senāsanaṃ upanissāya pāṇaṃ hanati …pe…  saṃghaṃ bhindati; saddhā …pe…  senāsanaṃ rāgassa …pe…  patthanāya upanissayapaccayena paccayo. (2)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1377">
Nosaññojano dhammo saññojanassa ca nosaññojanassa ca dhammassa upanissayapaccayena paccayo—  ārammaṇūpanissayo, anantarūpanissayo, pakatūpanissayo …pe… . <Emphasis>Pakatūpanissayo</Emphasis>—  saddhaṃ upanissāya mānaṃ jappeti, diṭṭhiṃ gaṇhāti; sīlaṃ …pe…  senāsanaṃ upanissāya pāṇaṃ hanati …pe…  saṃghaṃ bhindati; saddhā …pe…  senāsanaṃ saññojanānaṃ sampayuttakānañca khandhānaṃ upanissayapaccayena paccayo. (3)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1378">
Saññojano ca nosaññojano ca dhammā saññojanassa dhammassa upanissayapaccayena paccayo…  tīṇi.
</Paragraph>
</Division>
