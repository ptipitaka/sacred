<Division number="19">

<Paragraph number="1366">
Saññojano dhammo saññojanassa dhammassa adhipatipaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Ārammaṇādhipati</Emphasis>—  saññojanaṃ garuṃ katvā …pe…  tīṇi. (Garukārammaṇā.)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1367">
Nosaññojano dhammo nosaññojanassa dhammassa adhipatipaccayena paccayo—  ārammaṇādhipati, sahajātādhipati. <Emphasis>Ārammaṇādhipati</Emphasis>—  dānaṃ datvā sīlaṃ …pe…  tīṇi (tiṇṇampi ārammaṇādhipati, sahajātādhipatipi kātabbā, vibhajitabbā tīṇipi).
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1368">
Saññojano ca nosaññojano ca dhammā saññojanassa dhammassa adhipatipaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Ārammaṇādhipati</Emphasis>—  saññojane ca sampayuttake ca khandhe garuṃ katvā …pe…  tīṇi.
</Paragraph>
</Division>
