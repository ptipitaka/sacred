<Division number="31">

<Paragraph number="1683">
Nogantho dhammo noganthassa dhammassa kammapaccayena paccayo—  sahajātā, nānākkhaṇikā. <Emphasis>Sahajātā</Emphasis>—  noganthā cetanā sampayuttakānaṃ khandhānaṃ cittasamuṭṭhānānañca rūpānaṃ kammapaccayena paccayo; paṭisandhikkhaṇe …pe… . <Emphasis>Nānākkhaṇikā</Emphasis>—  noganthā cetanā vipākānaṃ khandhānaṃ kaṭattā ca rūpānaṃ kammapaccayena paccayo. (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1684">
Nogantho dhammo ganthassa dhammassa kammapaccayena paccayo—  noganthā cetanā sampayuttakānaṃ ganthānaṃ kammapaccayena paccayo. (2)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1685">
Nogantho dhammo ganthassa ca noganthassa ca dhammassa kammapaccayena paccayo—  noganthā cetanā sampayuttakānaṃ khandhānaṃ ganthānañca cittasamuṭṭhānānañca rūpānaṃ kammapaccayena paccayo. (3)
</Paragraph>
