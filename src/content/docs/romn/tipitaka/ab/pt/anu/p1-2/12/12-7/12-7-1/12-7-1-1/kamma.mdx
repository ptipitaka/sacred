<Division number="59">

<Paragraph number="1816">
Sekkho dhammo sekkhassa dhammassa kammapaccayena paccayo—  sahajātā, nānākkhaṇikā. <Emphasis>Sahajātā</Emphasis>—  sekkhā cetanā sampayuttakānaṃ khandhānaṃ kammapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Nānākkhaṇikā</Emphasis>—  sekkhā cetanā vipākānaṃ sekkhānaṃ khandhānaṃ kammapaccayena paccayo. (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1817">
Sekkho dhammo asekkhassa dhammassa kammapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Nānākkhaṇikā</Emphasis>—  sekkhā cetanā asekkhānaṃ khandhānaṃ kammapaccayena paccayo. (2)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1818">
Sekkho dhammo nevasekkhanāsekkhassa dhammassa kammapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Sahajātā</Emphasis>—  sekkhā cetanā cittasamuṭṭhānānaṃ rūpānaṃ kammapaccayena paccayo. (3)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1819">
Sekkho dhammo sekkhassa ca nevasekkhanāsekkhassa ca dhammassa kammapaccayena paccayo—  sekkhā cetanā sampayuttakānaṃ khandhānaṃ cittasamuṭṭhānānañca rūpānaṃ kammapaccayena paccayo. (4)
</Paragraph>

</Division>

<Paragraph number="1820">

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1821">
Asekkho dhammo nevasekkhanāsekkhassa dhammassa kammapaccayena paccayo—  asekkhā cetanā cittasamuṭṭhānānaṃ rūpānaṃ kammapaccayena paccayo. (2)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1822">
Asekkho dhammo asekkhassa ca nevasekkhanāsekkhassa ca dhammassa kammapaccayena paccayo—  asekkhā cetanā sampayuttakānaṃ khandhānaṃ cittasamuṭṭhānānañca rūpānaṃ kammapaccayena paccayo. (3)
</Paragraph>

</Division>
