<Division number="127">

<Paragraph number="554">
Savitakkasavicāro dhammo avitakkaavicārassa dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ. <Emphasis>Sahajātā</Emphasis>—  savitakkasavicārā khandhā cittasamuṭṭhānānaṃ rūpānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. Paṭisandhikkhaṇe savitakkasavicārā khandhā kaṭattārūpānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Pacchājātā</Emphasis>—  savitakkasavicārā khandhā purejātassa imassa kāyassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="555">
Avitakkavicāramatto dhammo avitakkaavicārassa dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ. <Emphasis>Sahajātā</Emphasis>—  avitakkavicāramattā khandhā cittasamuṭṭhānānaṃ rūpānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo, vitakko cittasamuṭṭhānānaṃ rūpānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. Paṭisandhikkhaṇe avitakkavicāramattā khandhā kaṭattārūpānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. Vitakko kaṭattārūpānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Pacchājātā</Emphasis>—  avitakkavicāramattā khandhā ca vitakko ca purejātassa imassa kāyassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. (1)
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="128">

<Paragraph number="556">
Avitakkaavicāro dhammo avitakkaavicārassa dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, purejātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ. <Emphasis>Sahajātā</Emphasis>—  avitakkaavicārā khandhā cittasamuṭṭhānānaṃ rūpānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. Vicāro cittasamuṭṭhānānaṃ rūpānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. Paṭisandhikkhaṇe avitakkaavicārā khandhā kaṭattārūpānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. Vicāro kaṭattārūpānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo; khandhā vatthussa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo, vatthu khandhānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo; vicāro vatthussa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo; vatthu vicārassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Purejātaṃ</Emphasis>—  cakkhāyatanaṃ cakkhuviññāṇassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo …pe…  kāyāyatanaṃ kāyaviññāṇassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo, vatthu avitakkaavicārānaṃ khandhānaṃ vicārassa ca vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Pacchājātā</Emphasis>—  avitakkaavicārā khandhā ca vicāro ca purejātassa imassa kāyassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="557">
Avitakkaavicāro dhammo savitakkasavicārassa dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, purejātaṃ. <Emphasis>Sahajātaṃ</Emphasis>—  paṭisandhikkhaṇe vatthu savitakkasavicārānaṃ khandhānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Purejātaṃ</Emphasis>—  vatthu savitakkasavicārānaṃ khandhānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. (2)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="558">
Avitakkaavicāro dhammo avitakkavicāramattassa dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, purejātaṃ. <Emphasis>Sahajātaṃ</Emphasis>—  paṭisandhikkhaṇe vatthu avitakkavicāramattānaṃ khandhānaṃ vitakkassa ca vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Purejātaṃ</Emphasis>—  vatthu avitakkavicāramattānaṃ khandhānaṃ vitakkassa ca vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. (3)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="559">
Avitakkaavicāro dhammo avitakkavicāramattassa ca avitakkaavicārassa ca dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, purejātaṃ. <Emphasis>Sahajātaṃ</Emphasis>—  paṭisandhikkhaṇe vatthu avitakkavicāramattānaṃ khandhānaṃ vicārassa ca vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Purejātaṃ</Emphasis>—  vatthu avitakkavicāramattānaṃ khandhānaṃ vicārassa ca vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. (4)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="560">
Avitakkaavicāro dhammo savitakkasavicārassa ca avitakkavicāramattassa ca dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, purejātaṃ. <Emphasis>Sahajātaṃ</Emphasis>—  paṭisandhikkhaṇe vatthu savitakkasavicārānaṃ khandhānaṃ vitakkassa ca vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Purejātaṃ</Emphasis>—  vatthu savitakkasavicārānaṃ khandhānaṃ vitakkassa ca vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. (5)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="561">
Avitakkavicāramatto ca avitakkaavicāro ca dhammā avitakkaavicārassa dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ. <Emphasis>Sahajātā</Emphasis>—  avitakkavicāramattā khandhā ca vicāro ca cittasamuṭṭhānānaṃ rūpānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. Paṭisandhikkhaṇe avitakkavicāramattā khandhā ca vicāro ca kaṭattārūpānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Pacchājātā</Emphasis>—  avitakkavicāramattā khandhā ca vicāro ca purejātassa imassa kāyassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. (1)
</Paragraph>

Paṭisandhikkhaṇe savitakkasavicārā khandhā ca vitakko ca kaṭattārūpānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Pacchājātā</Emphasis>—  savitakkasavicārā khandhā ca vitakko ca purejātassa imassa kāyassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. (1)
