* Kamma-vipāka
</Paragraph>

<Division number="221">

<Paragraph number="2865">
Cittasamuṭṭhāno dhammo cittasamuṭṭhānassa dhammassa kammapaccayena paccayo—  sahajātā, nānākkhaṇikā. <Emphasis>Sahajātā</Emphasis>—  cittasamuṭṭhānā cetanā sampayuttakānaṃ khandhānaṃ cittasamuṭṭhānānañca rūpānaṃ kammapaccayena paccayo; paṭisandhikkhaṇe …pe… . <Emphasis>Nānākkhaṇikā</Emphasis>—  cittasamuṭṭhānā cetanā vipākānaṃ khandhānaṃ kammapaccayena paccayo. (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2866">
Cittasamuṭṭhāno dhammo nocittasamuṭṭhānassa dhammassa kammapaccayena paccayo—  sahajātā, nānākkhaṇikā. <Emphasis>Sahajātā</Emphasis>—  cittasamuṭṭhānā cetanā cittassa kammapaccayena paccayo; paṭisandhikkhaṇe …pe… . <Emphasis>Nānākkhaṇikā</Emphasis>—  cittasamuṭṭhānā cetanā vipākassa cittassa kaṭattā ca rūpānaṃ kammapaccayena paccayo. (2)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2867">
Cittasamuṭṭhāno dhammo cittasamuṭṭhānassa ca nocittasamuṭṭhānassa ca dhammassa kammapaccayena paccayo—  sahajātā, nānākkhaṇikā. <Emphasis>Sahajātā</Emphasis>—  cittasamuṭṭhānā cetanā sampayuttakānaṃ khandhānaṃ cittassa ca cittasamuṭṭhānānañca rūpānaṃ kammapaccayena paccayo; paṭisandhikkhaṇe …pe… . <Emphasis>Nānākkhaṇikā</Emphasis>—  cittasamuṭṭhānā cetanā vipākānaṃ khandhānaṃ cittassa ca kaṭattā ca rūpānaṃ kammapaccayena paccayo. (3)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2868">
Cittasamuṭṭhāno dhammo cittasamuṭṭhānassa dhammassa vipākapaccayena paccayo…  nava.
</Paragraph>
</Division>
