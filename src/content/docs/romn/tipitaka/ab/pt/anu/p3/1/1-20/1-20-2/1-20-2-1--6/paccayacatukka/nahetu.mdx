<Paragraph number="1164">
Nahetuṃ bahiddhā dhammaṃ paṭicca nahetu bahiddhā dhammo uppajjati nahetupaccayā. Nahetuṃ bahiddhā dhammaṃ paṭicca hetu bahiddhā dhammo uppajjati nahetupaccayā. (2) (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

</Paragraph>
