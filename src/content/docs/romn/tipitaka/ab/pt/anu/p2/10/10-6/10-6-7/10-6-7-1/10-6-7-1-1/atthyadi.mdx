<Division number="230">

<Paragraph number="2885">
Cittasamuṭṭhāno dhammo cittasamuṭṭhānassa dhammassa atthipaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, purejātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2886">
Cittasamuṭṭhāno dhammo nocittasamuṭṭhānassa dhammassa atthipaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, purejātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ, āhāraṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) (2)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2887">
Cittasamuṭṭhāno dhammo cittasamuṭṭhānassa ca nocittasamuṭṭhānassa ca dhammassa atthipaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, purejātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) (3)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2888">
Nocittasamuṭṭhāno dhammo nocittasamuṭṭhānassa dhammassa atthipaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, purejātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ, āhāraṃ, indriyaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2889">
Nocittasamuṭṭhāno dhammo cittasamuṭṭhānassa dhammassa atthipaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, purejātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) (2)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2890">
Nocittasamuṭṭhāno dhammo cittasamuṭṭhānassa ca nocittasamuṭṭhānassa ca dhammassa atthipaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, purejātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ, āhāraṃ (Saṃkhittaṃ.) (3)
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="231">

<Paragraph number="2891">
Cittasamuṭṭhāno ca nocittasamuṭṭhāno ca dhammā cittasamuṭṭhānassa dhammassa atthipaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, purejātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ. <Emphasis>Sahajāto</Emphasis>—  cakkhuviññāṇasahagato eko khandho …pe…  (Saṃkhittaṃ.) (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2892">
Cittasamuṭṭhāno ca nocittasamuṭṭhāno ca dhammā nocittasamuṭṭhānassa dhammassa atthipaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, purejātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ, āhāraṃ, indriyaṃ. <Emphasis>Sahajātā</Emphasis>—  cakkhuviññāṇasahagatā khandhā ca cakkhāyatanañca cakkhuviññāṇassa atthipaccayena paccayo …pe…  kāyaviññāṇasahagatā …pe… . <Emphasis>Sahajātā</Emphasis>—  cittasamuṭṭhānā …pe…  (paccayavārasadisaṃ paṭisandhipi pavattipi kātabbā sabbesampi pañhānaṃ.) <Emphasis>Pacchājātā</Emphasis>—  cittasamuṭṭhānā khandhā ca cittañca purejātassa imassa nocittasamuṭṭhānassa kāyassa atthipaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Pacchājātā</Emphasis>—  cittasamuṭṭhānā khandhā ca cittañca kabaḷīkāro āhāro ca imassa nocittasamuṭṭhānassa kāyassa atthipaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Pacchājātā</Emphasis>—  cittasamuṭṭhānā khandhā ca cittañca rūpajīvitindriyañca kaṭattārūpānaṃ atthipaccayena paccayo. (2)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2893">
Cittasamuṭṭhāno ca nocittasamuṭṭhāno ca dhammā cittasamuṭṭhānassa ca nocittasamuṭṭhānassa ca dhammassa atthipaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, purejātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ. <Emphasis>Sahajāto</Emphasis>—  cakkhuviññāṇasahagato …pe…  (Saṃkhittaṃ.) (3)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2894">
Natthipaccayena paccayo…  vigatapaccayena paccayo…  avigatapaccayena paccayo.
</Paragraph>
</Division>
