<Division number="114">

<Paragraph number="312">
Sahetuko dhammo sahetukassa dhammassa indriyapaccayena paccayo…  tīṇi.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="313">
Ahetuko dhammo ahetukassa dhammassa indriyapaccayena paccayo—  ahetukā indriyā sampayuttakānaṃ khandhānaṃ cittasamuṭṭhānānañca rūpānaṃ …pe…  paṭisandhikkhaṇe …pe…  cakkhundriyaṃ cakkhuviññāṇassa …pe…  kāyindriyaṃ kāyaviññāṇassa indriyapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Rūpajīvitindriyaṃ</Emphasis>—  kaṭattārūpānaṃ indriyapaccayena paccayo. (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="314">
Sahetuko dhammo sahetukassa dhammassa jhānapaccayena paccayo…  tīṇi.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="315">
Ahetuko dhammo ahetukassa dhammassa jhānapaccayena paccayo—  ahetukāni jhānaṅgāni sampayuttakānaṃ khandhānaṃ cittasamuṭṭhānānañca rūpānaṃ …pe…  paṭisandhikkhaṇe …pe… . (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="316">
Sahetuko dhammo sahetukassa dhammassa maggapaccayena paccayo…  tīṇi.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="317">
Sahetuko dhammo sahetukassa dhammassa sampayuttapaccayena paccayo. (Paṭiccavāre sampayuttasadisā cha pañhā.)
</Paragraph>
</Division>
