<Division number="28">

<Paragraph number="1387">
Saññojano dhammo nosaññojanassa dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ. (Vibhajitabbaṃ.) (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1388">
Nosaññojano dhammo nosaññojanassa dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, purejātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ. (Vibhajitabbaṃ.) (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1389">
Nosaññojano dhammo saññojanassa dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Purejātaṃ</Emphasis>—  vatthu saññojanānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. (2)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1390">
Nosaññojano dhammo saññojanassa ca nosaññojanassa ca dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Purejātaṃ</Emphasis>—  vatthu saññojanānaṃ sampayuttakānañca khandhānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. (3)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1391">
Saññojano ca nosaññojano ca dhammā nosaññojanassa dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ. (Vibhajitabbaṃ.) (1)
</Paragraph>
</Division>
