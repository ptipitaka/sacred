<Paragraph number="2741">
Cittasampayutto dhammo cittavippayuttassa dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2742">
Cittavippayutto dhammo cittasampayuttassa dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, purejātaṃ. <Emphasis>Sahajātaṃ</Emphasis>—  paṭisandhikkhaṇe vatthu cittasampayuttakānaṃ khandhānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Purejātaṃ</Emphasis>—  cakkhāyatanaṃ cakkhuviññāṇasahagatānaṃ khandhānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo …pe…  kāyāyatanaṃ …pe…  vatthu cittasampayuttakānaṃ khandhānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. (1)
