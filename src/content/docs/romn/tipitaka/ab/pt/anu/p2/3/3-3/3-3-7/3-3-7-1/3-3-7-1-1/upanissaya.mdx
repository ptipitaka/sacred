<Division number="86">

<Paragraph number="1204">
Āsavasampayutto dhammo āsavasampayuttassa dhammassa upanissayapaccayena paccayo—  ārammaṇūpanissayo, anantarūpanissayo, pakatūpanissayo …pe… . <Emphasis>Pakatūpanissayo</Emphasis>—  āsavasampayuttā khandhā āsavasampayuttānaṃ khandhānaṃ upanissayapaccayena paccayo. (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1205">
Āsavasampayutto dhammo āsavavippayuttassa dhammassa upanissayapaccayena paccayo—  anantarūpanissayo, pakatūpanissayo …pe… . <Emphasis>Pakatūpanissayo</Emphasis>—  āsavasampayuttā khandhā āsavavippayuttānaṃ khandhānaṃ mohassa ca upanissayapaccayena paccayo. (2)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1206">
Āsavasampayutto dhammo āsavasampayuttassa ca āsavavippayuttassa ca dhammassa upanissayapaccayena paccayo—  anantarūpanissayo, pakatūpanissayo …pe… . <Emphasis>Pakatūpanissayo</Emphasis>—  āsavasampayuttakā khandhā domanassasahagatānaṃ vicikicchāsahagatānaṃ uddhaccasahagatānaṃ khandhānaṃ mohassa ca upanissayapaccayena paccayo. (3)
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="87">

<Paragraph number="1207">
Āsavavippayutto dhammo āsavavippayuttassa dhammassa upanissayapaccayena paccayo—  ārammaṇūpanissayo, anantarūpanissayo, pakatūpanissayo …pe… . <Emphasis>Pakatūpanissayo</Emphasis>—  saddhaṃ upanissāya dānaṃ deti …pe…  sīlaṃ …pe…  paññaṃ…  kāyikaṃ sukhaṃ …pe…  senāsanaṃ…  mohaṃ upanissāya dānaṃ deti …pe…  samāpattiṃ uppādeti; saddhā …pe…  paññā…  kāyikaṃ sukhaṃ…  kāyikaṃ dukkhaṃ…  moho ca saddhāya …pe…  mohassa ca upanissayapaccayena paccayo. (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1208">
Āsavavippayutto dhammo āsavasampayuttassa dhammassa upanissayapaccayena paccayo—  ārammaṇūpanissayo, anantarūpanissayo, pakatūpanissayo …pe… . <Emphasis>Pakatūpanissayo</Emphasis>—  saddhaṃ upanissāya mānaṃ jappeti, diṭṭhiṃ gaṇhāti; sīlaṃ …pe…  paññaṃ…  kāyikaṃ sukhaṃ…  kāyikaṃ dukkhaṃ…  utuṃ…  bhojanaṃ…  senāsanaṃ…  mohaṃ upanissāya pāṇaṃ hanati …pe…  saṃghaṃ bhindati; saddhā …pe…  moho ca rāgassa …pe…  patthanāya upanissayapaccayena paccayo. (2)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1209">
Āsavavippayutto dhammo āsavasampayuttassa ca āsavavippayuttassa ca dhammassa upanissayapaccayena paccayo—  anantarūpanissayo, pakatūpanissayo …pe… . <Emphasis>Pakatūpanissayo</Emphasis>—  saddhā…  sīlaṃ …pe…  moho domanassasahagatānaṃ vicikicchāsahagatānaṃ uddhaccasahagatānaṃ khandhānaṃ mohassa ca upanissayapaccayena paccayo. (3)
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="88">

<Paragraph number="1210">
Āsavasampayutto ca āsavavippayutto ca dhammā āsavasampayuttassa dhammassa upanissayapaccayena paccayo—  anantarūpanissayo, pakatūpanissayo …pe… . <Emphasis>Pakatūpanissayo</Emphasis>—  domanassasahagatā vicikicchāsahagatā uddhaccasahagatā khandhā ca moho ca āsavasampayuttakānaṃ khandhānaṃ upanissayapaccayena paccayo. (Pucchitabbaṃ mūlaṃ.) Domanassasahagatā vicikicchāsahagatā uddhaccasahagatā khandhā ca moho ca āsavasampayuttakānaṃ khandhānaṃ upanissayapaccayena paccayo. (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1211">
Āsavasampayutto ca āsavavippayutto ca dhammā āsavasampayuttassa ca āsavavippayuttassa ca dhammassa upanissayapaccayena paccayo—  anantarūpanissayo, pakatūpanissayo …pe… . <Emphasis>Pakatūpanissayo</Emphasis>—  domanassasahagatā vicikicchāsahagatā uddhaccasahagatā khandhā ca moho ca domanassasahagatānaṃ vicikicchāsahagatānaṃ uddhaccasahagatānaṃ khandhānaṃ mohassa ca upanissayapaccayena paccayo. (2)
</Paragraph>
