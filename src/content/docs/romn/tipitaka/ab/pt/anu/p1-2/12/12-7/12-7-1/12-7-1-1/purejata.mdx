<Division number="56">

<Paragraph number="1808">
Nevasekkhanāsekkho dhammo nevasekkhanāsekkhassa dhammassa purejātapaccayena paccayo—  ārammaṇapurejātaṃ, vatthupurejātaṃ. <Emphasis>Ārammaṇapurejātaṃ</Emphasis>—  cakkhuṃ aniccato …pe…  vipassati, assādeti abhinandati; taṃ ārabbha rāgo uppajjati …pe…  domanassaṃ uppajjati. Sotaṃ …pe…  vatthuṃ aniccato dukkhato anattato vipassati …pe…  domanassaṃ uppajjati, dibbena cakkhunā rūpaṃ passati, dibbāya sotadhātuyā saddaṃ suṇāti, rūpāyatanaṃ cakkhuviññāṇassa …pe…  phoṭṭhabbāyatanaṃ kāyaviññāṇassa purejātapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Vatthupurejātaṃ</Emphasis>—  cakkhāyatanaṃ cakkhuviññāṇassa …pe…  kāyāyatanaṃ kāyaviññāṇassa, vatthu nevasekkhanāsekkhānaṃ khandhānaṃ purejātapaccayena paccayo. (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1809">
Nevasekkhanāsekkho dhammo sekkhassa dhammassa purejātapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Vatthupurejātaṃ</Emphasis>—  vatthu sekkhānaṃ khandhānaṃ purejātapaccayena paccayo. (2)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1810">
Nevasekkhanāsekkho dhammo asekkhassa dhammassa purejātapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Vatthupurejātaṃ</Emphasis>—  vatthu asekkhānaṃ khandhānaṃ purejātapaccayena paccayo. (3)
</Paragraph>
