<Paragraph number="3883">
Nokileso dhammo nokilesassa dhammassa kammapaccayena paccayo—  sahajātā, nānākkhaṇikā. <Emphasis>Sahajātā</Emphasis>—  nokilesā cetanā sampayuttakānaṃ khandhānaṃ cittasamuṭṭhānānañca rūpānaṃ kammapaccayena paccayo; paṭisandhikkhaṇe …pe… . <Emphasis>Nānākkhaṇikā</Emphasis>—  nokilesā cetanā vipākānaṃ khandhānaṃ kaṭattā ca rūpānaṃ kammapaccayena paccayo. (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="3884">
Nokileso dhammo kilesassa dhammassa kammapaccayena paccayo—  nokilesā cetanā kilesānaṃ kammapaccayena paccayo. (2)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="3885">
Nokileso dhammo kilesassa ca nokilesassa ca dhammassa kammapaccayena paccayo—  nokilesā cetanā sampayuttakānaṃ khandhānaṃ kilesānaṃ cittasamuṭṭhānānañca rūpānaṃ kammapaccayena paccayo. (3)
</Paragraph>
