<Division number="462">

<Paragraph number="3496">
Upādinno dhammo upādinnassa dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, purejātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ. <Emphasis>Sahajātā</Emphasis>—  paṭisandhikkhaṇe upādinnā khandhā kaṭattārūpānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo, khandhā vatthussa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo, vatthu khandhānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Purejātaṃ</Emphasis>—  cakkhāyatanaṃ cakkhuviññāṇassa …pe…  kāyāyatanaṃ kāyaviññāṇassa …pe…  vatthu upādinnānaṃ khandhānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Pacchājātā</Emphasis>—  upādinnā khandhā purejātassa imassa upādinnassa kāyassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="3497">
Upādinno dhammo anupādinnassa dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, purejātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ. <Emphasis>Sahajātā</Emphasis>—  upādinnā khandhā cittasamuṭṭhānānaṃ rūpānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Purejātaṃ</Emphasis>—  vatthu anupādinnānaṃ khandhānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Pacchājātā</Emphasis>—  upādinnā khandhā purejātassa imassa anupādinnassa kāyassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. (2)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="3498">
Upādinno dhammo upādinnassa ca anupādinnassa ca dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Pacchājātā</Emphasis>—  upādinnā khandhā purejātassa imassa upādinnassa ca anupādinnassa ca kāyassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. (3)
</Paragraph>

</Division>
