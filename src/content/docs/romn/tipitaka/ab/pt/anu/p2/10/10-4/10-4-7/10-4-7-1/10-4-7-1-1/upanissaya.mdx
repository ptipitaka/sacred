<Paragraph number="2726">
Cittasampayutto dhammo cittasampayuttassa dhammassa upanissayapaccayena paccayo—  ārammaṇūpanissayo, anantarūpanissayo, pakatūpanissayo …pe… . <Emphasis>Pakatūpanissayo</Emphasis>—  saddhaṃ upanissāya dānaṃ deti …pe…  mānaṃ jappeti, diṭṭhiṃ gaṇhāti; sīlaṃ …pe…  patthanaṃ kāyikaṃ sukhaṃ…  kāyikaṃ dukkhaṃ upanissāya dānaṃ deti …pe…  pāṇaṃ hanati …pe…  saṃghaṃ bhindati; saddhā …pe…  kāyikaṃ dukkhaṃ saddhāya …pe…  maggassa, phalasamāpattiyā upanissayapaccayena paccayo.
