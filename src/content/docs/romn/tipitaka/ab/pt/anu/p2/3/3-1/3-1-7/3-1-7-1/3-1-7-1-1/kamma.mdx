<Division number="31">

<Paragraph number="1050">
Noāsavo dhammo noāsavassa dhammassa kammapaccayena paccayo—  sahajātā, nānākkhaṇikā. <Emphasis>Sahajātā</Emphasis>—  noāsavā cetanā sampayuttakānaṃ khandhānaṃ cittasamuṭṭhānānañca rūpānaṃ kammapaccayena paccayo; paṭisandhikkhaṇe …pe… . <Emphasis>Nānākkhaṇikā</Emphasis>—  noāsavā cetanā vipākānaṃ khandhānaṃ kaṭattā ca rūpānaṃ kammapaccayena paccayo. (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1051">
Noāsavo dhammo āsavassa dhammassa kammapaccayena paccayo—  noāsavā cetanā sampayuttakānaṃ āsavānaṃ kammapaccayena paccayo. (2)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1052">
Noāsavo dhammo āsavassa ca noāsavassa ca dhammassa kammapaccayena paccayo—  noāsavā cetanā sampayuttakānaṃ khandhānaṃ āsavānaṃ cittasamuṭṭhānānañca rūpānaṃ kammapaccayena paccayo. (3)
</Paragraph>
