<Division number="82">

<Paragraph number="724">
Sappaṭigho dhammo appaṭighassa dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Purejātaṃ</Emphasis>—  cakkhāyatanaṃ cakkhuviññāṇassa …pe…  kāyāyatanaṃ kāyaviññāṇassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="725">
Appaṭigho dhammo appaṭighassa dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, purejātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ. <Emphasis>Sahajātā</Emphasis>—  appaṭighā khandhā appaṭighānaṃ cittasamuṭṭhānānaṃ rūpānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo; paṭisandhikkhaṇe …pe…  khandhā vatthussa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo, vatthu khandhānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Purejātaṃ</Emphasis>—  vatthu appaṭighānaṃ khandhānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Pacchājātā</Emphasis>—  appaṭighā khandhā purejātassa imassa appaṭighassa kāyassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="726">
Appaṭigho dhammo sappaṭighassa dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ. <Emphasis>Sahajātā</Emphasis>—  appaṭighā khandhā sappaṭighānaṃ cittasamuṭṭhānānaṃ rūpānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo; paṭisandhikkhaṇe …pe… . <Emphasis>Pacchājātā</Emphasis>—  appaṭighā khandhā purejātassa imassa sappaṭighassa kāyassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. (2)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="727">
Appaṭigho dhammo sappaṭighassa ca appaṭighassa ca dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ. <Emphasis>Sahajātā</Emphasis>—  appaṭighā khandhā sappaṭighānañca appaṭighānañca cittasamuṭṭhānānaṃ rūpānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo; paṭisandhikkhaṇe …pe… . <Emphasis>Pacchājātā</Emphasis>—  appaṭighā khandhā purejātassa imassa sappaṭighassa ca appaṭighassa ca kāyassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. (3)
</Paragraph>
</Division>
