<Division number="91">

<Paragraph number="1813">
Ganthasampayutto dhammo ganthavippayuttassa dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ, vibhajitabbaṃ.) (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1814">
Ganthavippayutto dhammo ganthavippayuttassa dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, purejātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1815">
Ganthavippayutto dhammo ganthasampayuttassa dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Purejātaṃ</Emphasis>—  vatthu ganthasampayuttakānaṃ khandhānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. (2)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1816">
Ganthavippayutto dhammo ganthasampayuttassa ca ganthavippayuttassa ca dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Purejātaṃ</Emphasis>—  vatthu diṭṭhigatavippayuttalobhasahagatānaṃ khandhānaṃ lobhassa ca domanassasahagatānaṃ khandhānaṃ paṭighassa ca vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. (3)
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="92">

<Paragraph number="1817">
Ganthasampayutto ca ganthavippayutto ca dhammā ganthavippayuttassa dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ. <Emphasis>Sahajātā</Emphasis>—  diṭṭhigatavippayuttalobhasahagatā khandhā ca lobho ca cittasamuṭṭhānānaṃ rūpānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo; domanassasahagatā khandhā ca paṭighañca cittasamuṭṭhānānaṃ rūpānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Pacchājātā</Emphasis>—  diṭṭhigatavippayuttalobhasahagatā khandhā ca lobho ca domanassasahagatā khandhā ca paṭighañca purejātassa imassa kāyassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. (1)
</Paragraph>
</Division>
