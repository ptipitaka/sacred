<Division number="23">

<Paragraph number="1663">
Gantho dhammo ganthassa dhammassa adhipatipaccayena paccayo…  tīṇi. (Ārammaṇasadisā, garukārammaṇā kātabbā.)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1664">
Nogantho dhammo noganthassa dhammassa adhipatipaccayena paccayo—  ārammaṇādhipati, sahajātādhipati. <Emphasis>Ārammaṇādhipati</Emphasis>—  dānaṃ …pe…  sīlaṃ …pe…  uposathakammaṃ …pe…  pubbe …pe…  jhānā …pe…  ariyā maggā …pe…  phalaṃ …pe…  nibbānaṃ …pe…  nibbānaṃ gotrabhussa, vodānassa, maggassa, phalassa adhipatipaccayena paccayo; cakkhuṃ …pe…  vatthuṃ noganthe khandhe garuṃ katvā assādeti abhinandati, taṃ garuṃ katvā rāgo uppajjati, diṭṭhi uppajjati. <Emphasis>Sahajātādhipati</Emphasis>—  noganthādhipati sampayuttakānaṃ khandhānaṃ cittasamuṭṭhānānañca rūpānaṃ adhipatipaccayena paccayo. (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1665">
Nogantho dhammo ganthassa dhammassa adhipatipaccayena paccayo—  ārammaṇādhipati, sahajātādhipati. <Emphasis>Ārammaṇādhipati</Emphasis>—  dānaṃ …pe…  sīlaṃ …pe…  uposathakammaṃ …pe…  pubbe suciṇṇāni …pe…  jhānā …pe…  cakkhuṃ …pe…  vatthuṃ noganthe khandhe garuṃ katvā assādeti abhinandati, taṃ garuṃ katvā rāgo uppajjati, diṭṭhi uppajjati. <Emphasis>Sahajātādhipati</Emphasis>—  noganthādhipati ganthānaṃ adhipatipaccayena paccayo. (2)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1666">
Nogantho dhammo ganthassa ca noganthassa ca dhammassa adhipatipaccayena paccayo—  ārammaṇādhipati, sahajātādhipati. <Emphasis>Ārammaṇādhipati</Emphasis>—  dānaṃ …pe…  noganthe khandhe garuṃ katvā assādeti abhinandati, taṃ garuṃ katvā ganthā ca sampayuttakā ca khandhā uppajjanti. <Emphasis>Sahajātādhipati</Emphasis>—  noganthādhipati sampayuttakānaṃ khandhānaṃ ganthānañca cittasamuṭṭhānānañca rūpānaṃ adhipatipaccayena paccayo. (3)
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="24">

<Paragraph number="1667">
Gantho ca nogantho ca dhammā ganthassa dhammassa adhipatipaccayena paccayo—  ārammaṇādhipati…  tīṇi.
</Paragraph>
</Division>
