<Division number="8">

<Paragraph number="2385">
Nabhāvanāya pahātabbaṃ kusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca nabhāvanāya pahātabbo kusalo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

<Paragraph number="2386">

<Paragraph number="2389">
