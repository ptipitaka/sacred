<Division number="342">

<Paragraph number="3162">
Ajjhattiko dhammo ajjhattikassa dhammassa adhipatipaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Ārammaṇādhipati</Emphasis>—  cittaṃ garuṃ katvā cittaṃ uppajjati. (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="3163">
Ajjhattiko dhammo bāhirassa dhammassa adhipatipaccayena paccayo—  ārammaṇādhipati, sahajātādhipati. <Emphasis>Ārammaṇādhipati</Emphasis>—  cittaṃ garuṃ katvā bāhirā khandhā uppajjanti. <Emphasis>Sahajātādhipati</Emphasis>—  cittādhipati sampayuttakānaṃ khandhānaṃ cittasamuṭṭhānānañca rūpānaṃ adhipatipaccayena paccayo. (Mūlaṃ.) <Emphasis>Ārammaṇādhipati</Emphasis>—  ajjhattikaṃ cittaṃ garuṃ katvā cittañca sampayuttakā ca khandhā uppajjanti. (3)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="3164">
Bāhiro dhammo bāhirassa dhammassa adhipatipaccayena paccayo—  ārammaṇādhipati, sahajātādhipati. Ārammaṇādhipati—  dānaṃ datvā …pe…  tīṇi. (Dve adhipatī tiṇṇampi kātabbā.) (3)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="3165">
Ajjhattiko ca bāhiro ca dhammā ajjhattikassa dhammassa adhipatipaccayena paccayo…  tīṇi. (Tiṇṇampi ekāyeva adhipati.)
</Paragraph>
</Division>
