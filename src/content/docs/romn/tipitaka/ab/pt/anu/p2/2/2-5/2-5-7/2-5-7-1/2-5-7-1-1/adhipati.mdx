<Division number="114">

<Paragraph number="805">
Rūpī dhammo arūpissa dhammassa adhipatipaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Ārammaṇādhipati</Emphasis>—  cakkhuṃ …pe…  kabaḷīkāraṃ āhāraṃ garuṃ katvā assādeti abhinandati, taṃ garuṃ katvā rāgo uppajjati, diṭṭhi uppajjati. (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="806">
Arūpī dhammo arūpissa dhammassa adhipatipaccayena paccayo—  ārammaṇādhipati, sahajātādhipati. <Emphasis>Ārammaṇādhipati</Emphasis>—  dānaṃ …pe…  (saṃkhittaṃ) nibbānaṃ maggassa, phalassa adhipatipaccayena paccayo; arūpino khandhe garuṃ katvā assādeti …pe…  rāgo uppajjati, diṭṭhi uppajjati. <Emphasis>Sahajātādhipati</Emphasis>—  arūpī adhipati sampayuttakānaṃ khandhānaṃ adhipatipaccayena paccayo. (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="807">
Arūpī dhammo rūpissa dhammassa adhipatipaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Sahajātādhipati</Emphasis>—  arūpī adhipati cittasamuṭṭhānānaṃ rūpānaṃ adhipatipaccayena paccayo. (2)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="808">
Arūpī dhammo rūpissa ca arūpissa ca dhammassa adhipatipaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Sahajātādhipati</Emphasis>—  arūpī adhipati sampayuttakānaṃ khandhānaṃ cittasamuṭṭhānānañca rūpānaṃ adhipatipaccayena paccayo. (3)
</Paragraph>
</Division>
