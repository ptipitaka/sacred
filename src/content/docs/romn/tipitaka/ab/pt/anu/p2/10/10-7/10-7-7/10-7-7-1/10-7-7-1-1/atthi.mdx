<Paragraph number="2983">
Cittasahabhū ca nocittasahabhū ca dhammā cittasahabhussa dhammassa atthipaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, purejātaṃ. <Emphasis>Sahajāto</Emphasis>—  cakkhuviññāṇasahagato eko khandho ca cakkhāyatanañca cakkhuviññāṇañca dvinnaṃ khandhānaṃ …pe…  dve khandhā ca …pe… . (Sabbaṃ paṭisandhiyaṃ kātabbaṃ, sahajātaṃ purejātampi.) (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2984">
Cittasahabhū ca nocittasahabhū ca dhammā nocittasahabhussa dhammassa atthipaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, purejātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ, āhāraṃ, indriyaṃ. <Emphasis>Sahajātā</Emphasis>—  cakkhuviññāṇasahagatā khandhā cakkhāyatanañca cakkhuviññāṇassa atthipaccayena paccayo …pe…  kāyaviññāṇasahagatā …pe…  cittasahabhū khandhā ca cittañca nocittasahabhūnaṃ cittasamuṭṭhānānaṃ rūpānaṃ atthipaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Sahajātā</Emphasis>—  cittasahabhū khandhā ca vatthu ca cittassa atthipaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Sahajātā</Emphasis>—  cittasahabhū khandhā ca mahābhūtā ca nocittasahabhūnaṃ cittasamuṭṭhānānaṃ rūpānaṃ atthipaccayena paccayo (paṭisandhikkhaṇe tīṇipi kātabbā). <Emphasis>Pacchājātā</Emphasis>—  cittasahabhū khandhā ca cittañca purejātassa imassa nocittasahabhussa kāyassa atthipaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Pacchājātā</Emphasis>—  cittasahabhū khandhā ca kabaḷīkāro āhāro ca imassa nocittasahabhussa kāyassa atthipaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Pacchājātā</Emphasis>—  cittasahabhū khandhā ca rūpajīvitindriyañca kaṭattārūpānaṃ atthipaccayena paccayo. (2)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2985">

</Paragraph>
