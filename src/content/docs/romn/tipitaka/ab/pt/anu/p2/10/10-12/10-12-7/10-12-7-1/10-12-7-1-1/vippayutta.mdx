<Division number="357">

<Paragraph number="3203">
Ajjhattiko dhammo ajjhattikassa dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, purejātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ. <Emphasis>Sahajātaṃ</Emphasis>—  paṭisandhikkhaṇe cittaṃ ajjhattikānaṃ kaṭattārūpānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Purejātaṃ</Emphasis>—  cakkhāyatanaṃ cakkhuviññāṇassa …pe…  kāyāyatanaṃ kāyaviññāṇassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Pacchājātā</Emphasis>—  ajjhattikā khandhā purejātassa imassa ajjhattikassa kāyassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="3204">
Ajjhattiko dhammo bāhirassa dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, purejātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ. <Emphasis>Sahajātā</Emphasis>—  ajjhattikā khandhā cittasamuṭṭhānānaṃ rūpānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo; paṭisandhikkhaṇe …pe… . <Emphasis>Purejātaṃ</Emphasis>—  cakkhāyatanaṃ cakkhuviññāṇasahagatānaṃ khandhānaṃ …pe…  kāyāyatanaṃ kāyaviññāṇasahagatānaṃ khandhānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Pacchājātā</Emphasis>—  ajjhattikā khandhā purejātassa imassa bāhirassa kāyassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. (2)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="3205">
Ajjhattiko dhammo ajjhattikassa ca bāhirassa ca dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, purejātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ. <Emphasis>Sahajātā</Emphasis>—  paṭisandhikkhaṇe ajjhattikā khandhā ajjhattikānañca bāhirānañca kaṭattārūpānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Purejātaṃ</Emphasis>—  cakkhāyatanaṃ cakkhuviññāṇassa sampayuttakānañca khandhānaṃ …pe…  kāyāyatanaṃ kāyaviññāṇassa sampayuttakānaṃ khandhānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Pacchājātā</Emphasis>—  ajjhattikā khandhā purejātassa imassa ajjhattikassa ca bāhirassa ca kāyassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. (3)
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="358">

<Paragraph number="3206">
Bāhiro dhammo bāhirassa dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, purejātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ. <Emphasis>Sahajātā</Emphasis>—  bāhirā khandhā cittasamuṭṭhānānaṃ rūpānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo; paṭisandhikkhaṇe …pe…  khandhā vatthussa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo; vatthu khandhānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Purejātaṃ</Emphasis>—  vatthu bāhirānaṃ khandhānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Pacchājātā</Emphasis>—  bāhirā khandhā purejātassa imassa bāhirassa kāyassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="3207">
Bāhiro dhammo ajjhattikassa dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, purejātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ. <Emphasis>Sahajātā</Emphasis>—  paṭisandhikkhaṇe bāhirā khandhā ajjhattikānaṃ kaṭattārūpānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo; paṭisandhikkhaṇe vatthu cittassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Purejātaṃ</Emphasis>—  vatthu cittassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Pacchājātā</Emphasis>—  bāhirā khandhā purejātassa imassa ajjhattikassa kāyassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. (2)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="3208">
Bāhiro dhammo ajjhattikassa ca bāhirassa ca dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, purejātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) (3)
</Paragraph>

</Division>
