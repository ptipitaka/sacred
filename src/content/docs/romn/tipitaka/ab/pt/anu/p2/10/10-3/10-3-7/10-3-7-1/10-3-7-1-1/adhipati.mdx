<Division number="123">

<Paragraph number="2616">
Cetasiko dhammo cetasikassa dhammassa adhipatipaccayena paccayo—  ārammaṇādhipati, sahajātādhipati. <Emphasis>Ārammaṇādhipati</Emphasis>—  cetasike khandhe garuṃ katvā cetasikā khandhā uppajjanti. <Emphasis>Sahajātādhipati</Emphasis>—  cetasikādhipati sampayuttakānaṃ khandhānaṃ adhipatipaccayena paccayo. (Mūlaṃ pucchitabbaṃ.) <Emphasis>Ārammaṇādhipati</Emphasis>—  cetasike khandhe garuṃ katvā cittaṃ uppajjati. <Emphasis>Sahajātādhipati</Emphasis>—  cetasikādhipati cittassa cittasamuṭṭhānānañca rūpānaṃ adhipatipaccayena paccayo. (Mūlaṃ pucchitabbaṃ.) <Emphasis>Ārammaṇādhipati</Emphasis>—  cetasike khandhe garuṃ katvā cetasikā khandhā ca cittañca uppajjanti. <Emphasis>Sahajātādhipati</Emphasis>—  cetasikādhipati sampayuttakānaṃ khandhānaṃ cittassa ca cittasamuṭṭhānānañca rūpānaṃ adhipatipaccayena paccayo. (3)
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="124">

<Paragraph number="2617">
Acetasiko dhammo acetasikassa dhammassa adhipatipaccayena paccayo—  ārammaṇādhipati, sahajātādhipati. <Emphasis>Ārammaṇādhipati</Emphasis>—  ariyā maggā vuṭṭhahitvā …pe…  nibbānaṃ garuṃ katvā paccavekkhanti, nibbānaṃ gotrabhussa, vodānassa. Maggassa, phalassa adhipatipaccayena paccayo; cakkhuṃ …pe…  vatthuṃ acetasike khandhe garuṃ katvā assādeti abhinandati, taṃ garuṃ katvā cittaṃ uppajjati. <Emphasis>Sahajātādhipati</Emphasis>—  acetasikādhipati cittasamuṭṭhānānaṃ rūpānaṃ adhipatipaccayena paccayo. (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2618">
Acetasiko dhammo cetasikassa dhammassa adhipatipaccayena paccayo—  ārammaṇādhipati, sahajātādhipati. <Emphasis>Ārammaṇādhipati</Emphasis>—  ariyā maggā vuṭṭhahitvā …pe…  nibbānaṃ garuṃ katvā …pe…  (paṭhamagamanasadisaṃ); cakkhuṃ …pe…  vatthuṃ acetasike khandhe garuṃ katvā assādeti abhinandati, taṃ garuṃ katvā rāgo uppajjati, diṭṭhi uppajjati. <Emphasis>Sahajātādhipati</Emphasis>—  acetasikādhipati sampayuttakānaṃ khandhānaṃ adhipatipaccayena paccayo. (2)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2619">
Acetasiko dhammo cetasikassa ca acetasikassa ca dhammassa adhipatipaccayena paccayo—  ārammaṇādhipati, sahajātādhipati. <Emphasis>Ārammaṇādhipati</Emphasis>—  ariyā maggā vuṭṭhahitvā …pe…  nibbānaṃ …pe…  (paṭhamagamanaṃ) acetasike khandhe garuṃ katvā assādeti abhinandati, taṃ garuṃ katvā cetasikā khandhā ca cittañca uppajjanti. <Emphasis>Sahajātādhipati</Emphasis>—  acetasikādhipati sampayuttakānaṃ khandhānaṃ cittasamuṭṭhānānañca rūpānaṃ adhipatipaccayena paccayo. (3)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2620">
Cetasiko ca acetasiko ca dhammā cetasikassa dhammassa adhipatipaccayena paccayo—  ārammaṇādhipati…  tīṇi. (Ārammaṇādhipatiyeva.)
</Paragraph>
</Division>
