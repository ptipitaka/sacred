<Paragraph number="3969">
Saṃkiliṭṭho dhammo saṃkiliṭṭhassa dhammassa upanissayapaccayena paccayo—  ārammaṇūpanissayo, anantarūpanissayo, pakatūpanissayo …pe… . <Emphasis>Pakatūpanissayo</Emphasis>—  rāgaṃ upanissāya pāṇaṃ hanati …pe…  saṃghaṃ bhindati, dosaṃ …pe…  patthanaṃ upanissāya pāṇaṃ hanati …pe…  saṃghaṃ bhindati, rāgo …pe…  patthanā rāgassa …pe…  patthanāya upanissayapaccayena paccayo. (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="3970">
Saṃkiliṭṭho dhammo asaṃkiliṭṭhassa dhammassa upanissayapaccayena paccayo—  anantarūpanissayo, pakatūpanissayo …pe… . <Emphasis>Pakatūpanissayo</Emphasis>—  rāgaṃ upanissāya dānaṃ deti …pe…  samāpattiṃ uppādeti, dosaṃ …pe…  patthanaṃ upanissāya dānaṃ deti …pe…  samāpattiṃ uppādeti, rāgo …pe…  patthanā saddhāya …pe…  paññāya kāyikassa sukhassa…  kāyikassa dukkhassa…  maggassa, phalasamāpattiyā upanissayapaccayena paccayo. (2)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="3971">

(1)
</Paragraph>

(2)
