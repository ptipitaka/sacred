<Division number="75">

<Paragraph number="711">
Sappaṭigho dhammo appaṭighassa dhammassa purejātapaccayena paccayo—  ārammaṇapurejātaṃ, vatthupurejātaṃ. <Emphasis>Ārammaṇapurejātaṃ</Emphasis>—  cakkhuṃ …pe…  phoṭṭhabbe aniccato …pe…  domanassaṃ uppajjati; dibbena cakkhunā rūpaṃ passati, dibbāya sotadhātuyā saddaṃ suṇāti, rūpāyatanaṃ cakkhuviññāṇassa …pe…  phoṭṭhabbāyatanaṃ kāyaviññāṇassa purejātapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Vatthupurejātaṃ</Emphasis>—  cakkhāyatanaṃ cakkhuviññāṇassa …pe…  kāyāyatanaṃ kāyaviññāṇassa purejātapaccayena paccayo. (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="712">
Appaṭigho dhammo appaṭighassa dhammassa purejātapaccayena paccayo—  ārammaṇapurejātaṃ, vatthupurejātaṃ. <Emphasis>Ārammaṇapurejātaṃ</Emphasis>—  vatthuṃ …pe…  itthindriyaṃ, purisindriyaṃ, jīvitindriyaṃ, āpodhātuṃ, kabaḷīkāraṃ āhāraṃ aniccato …pe…  domanassaṃ uppajjati. <Emphasis>Vatthupurejātaṃ</Emphasis>—  vatthu appaṭighānaṃ khandhānaṃ purejātapaccayena paccayo. (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="713">
Sappaṭigho ca appaṭigho ca dhammā appaṭighassa dhammassa purejātapaccayena paccayo—  <Emphasis>ārammaṇapurejātaṃ, vatthupurejātaṃ</Emphasis>. Cakkhāyatanañca vatthu ca …pe…  phoṭṭhabbāyatanañca vatthu ca appaṭighānaṃ khandhānaṃ purejātapaccayena paccayo. (1)
</Paragraph>
</Division>
