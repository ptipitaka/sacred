<Division number="174">

<Paragraph number="4458">
Nasukhasahagato dhammo nasukhasahagatassa dhammassa purejātapaccayena paccayo—  ārammaṇapurejātaṃ, vatthupurejātaṃ. <Emphasis>Ārammaṇapurejātaṃ</Emphasis>—  cakkhuṃ …pe…  vatthuṃ nasukhasahagatena cittena aniccato …pe…  vipassati assādeti abhinandati, taṃ ārabbha nasukhasahagato rāgo uppajjati …pe…  domanassaṃ uppajjati, sukhaṃ uppajjati, dibbena cakkhunā rūpaṃ passati …pe…  phoṭṭhabbāyatanaṃ kāyaviññāṇassa …pe… . <Emphasis>Vatthupurejātaṃ</Emphasis>—  cakkhāyatanaṃ cakkhuviññāṇassa …pe…  kāyāyatanaṃ kāyaviññāṇassa …pe…  vatthu nasukhasahagatānaṃ khandhānaṃ sukhassa ca purejātapaccayena paccayo. (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="4459">
Nasukhasahagato dhammo sukhasahagatassa dhammassa purejātapaccayena paccayo—  ārammaṇapurejātaṃ, vatthupurejātaṃ. <Emphasis>Ārammaṇapurejātaṃ</Emphasis>—  cakkhuṃ …pe…  vatthuṃ sukhasahagatena cittena aniccato …pe…  vipassati, assādeti abhinandati, taṃ ārabbha sukhasahagato rāgo uppajjati, diṭṭhi uppajjati. <Emphasis>Vatthupurejātaṃ</Emphasis>—  vatthu sukhasahagatānaṃ khandhānaṃ purejātapaccayena paccayo. (2)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="4460">
Nasukhasahagato dhammo sukhasahagatassa ca nasukhasahagatassa ca dhammassa purejātapaccayena paccayo—  ārammaṇapurejātaṃ, vatthupurejātaṃ. <Emphasis>Ārammaṇapurejātaṃ</Emphasis>—  cakkhuṃ …pe…  vatthuṃ sukhasahagatena cittena aniccato …pe…  vipassati, assādeti abhinandati, taṃ ārabbha sukhañca sampayuttakā khandhā ca uppajjanti. <Emphasis>Vatthupurejātaṃ</Emphasis>—  vatthu sukhasahagatānaṃ khandhānaṃ sukhassa ca purejātapaccayena paccayo. (3)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="4461">
Pacchājātapaccayena paccayo…  tīṇi…  āsevanapaccayena paccayo…  nava.
</Paragraph>
