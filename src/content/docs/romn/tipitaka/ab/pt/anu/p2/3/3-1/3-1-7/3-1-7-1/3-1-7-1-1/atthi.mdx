<Division number="37">

<Paragraph number="1062">
Āsavo dhammo āsavassa dhammassa atthipaccayena paccayo—  kāmāsavo diṭṭhāsavassa avijjāsavassa atthipaccayena paccayo. (Cakkaṃ.) (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1063">
Āsavo dhammo noāsavassa dhammassa atthipaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ. <Emphasis>Sahajātā</Emphasis>—  āsavā sampayuttakānaṃ khandhānaṃ cittasamuṭṭhānānañca rūpānaṃ atthipaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Pacchājātā</Emphasis>—  āsavā purejātassa imassa kāyassa atthipaccayena paccayo. (2)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1064">
Āsavo dhammo āsavassa ca noāsavassa ca dhammassa atthipaccayena paccayo—  kāmāsavo diṭṭhāsavassa avijjāsavassa sampayuttakānaṃ khandhānaṃ cittasamuṭṭhānānañca rūpānaṃ atthipaccayena paccayo. (Cakkaṃ.) (3)
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="38">

<Paragraph number="1065">
Noāsavo dhammo noāsavassa dhammassa atthipaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, purejātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ, āhāraṃ, indriyaṃ. <Emphasis>Sahajāto</Emphasis>—  noāsavo eko khandho tiṇṇannaṃ khandhānaṃ …pe…  (yāva asaññasattā). <Emphasis>Purejātaṃ</Emphasis>—  cakkhuṃ …pe…  vatthuṃ aniccato …pe…  domanassaṃ uppajjati; dibbena cakkhunā rūpaṃ passati, dibbāya sotadhātuyā saddaṃ suṇāti. Rūpāyatanaṃ cakkhuviññāṇassa …pe…  phoṭṭhabbāyatanaṃ kāyaviññāṇassa …pe…  cakkhāyatanaṃ cakkhuviññāṇassa …pe…  kāyāyatanaṃ kāyaviññāṇassa …pe…  vatthu noāsavānaṃ khandhānaṃ atthipaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Pacchājātā</Emphasis>—  noāsavā khandhā purejātassa imassa kāyassa atthipaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Kabaḷīkāro āhāro</Emphasis>—  imassa kāyassa atthipaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Rūpajīvitindriyaṃ</Emphasis>—  kaṭattārūpānaṃ atthipaccayena paccayo. (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1066">
Noāsavo dhammo āsavassa dhammassa atthipaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, purejātaṃ. <Emphasis>Sahajātā</Emphasis>—  noāsavā khandhā āsavānaṃ atthipaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Purejātaṃ</Emphasis>—  cakkhuṃ …pe…  vatthuṃ assādeti abhinandati, taṃ ārabbha āsavā uppajjanti, vatthu āsavānaṃ atthipaccayena paccayo. (2)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1067">
Noāsavo dhammo āsavassa ca noāsavassa ca dhammassa atthipaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, purejātaṃ. <Emphasis>Sahajāto</Emphasis>—  noāsavo eko khandho tiṇṇannaṃ khandhānaṃ āsavānaṃ cittasamuṭṭhānānañca rūpānaṃ atthipaccayena paccayo …pe…  dve khandhā …pe… . (Cakkaṃ.) (3)
</Paragraph>

<Division number="39">

<Paragraph number="1068">
Āsavo ca noāsavo ca dhammā āsavassa dhammassa atthipaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, purejātaṃ. <Emphasis>Sahajāto</Emphasis>—  kāmāsavo ca sampayuttakā ca khandhā diṭṭhāsavassa avijjāsavassa atthipaccayena paccayo (cakkaṃ). Kāmāsavo ca vatthu ca diṭṭhāsavassa avijjāsavassa atthipaccayena paccayo. (Cakkaṃ.) (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1069">
Āsavo ca noāsavo ca dhammā noāsavassa dhammassa atthipaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, purejātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ, āhāraṃ, indriyaṃ. <Emphasis>Sahajāto</Emphasis>—  noāsavo eko khandho ca āsavā ca tiṇṇannaṃ khandhānaṃ cittasamuṭṭhānānañca rūpānaṃ atthipaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Sahajātā</Emphasis>—  āsavā mahābhūtā ca cittasamuṭṭhānānaṃ rūpānaṃ atthipaccayena paccayo; āsavā ca vatthu ca noāsavānaṃ khandhānaṃ atthipaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Pacchājātā</Emphasis>—  āsavā ca kabaḷīkāro āhāro ca imassa kāyassa atthipaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Pacchājātā</Emphasis>—  āsavā ca rūpajīvitindriyañca kaṭattārūpānaṃ atthipaccayena paccayo. (2)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1070">
Āsavo ca noāsavo ca dhammā āsavassa ca noāsavassa ca dhammassa atthipaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, purejātaṃ. <Emphasis>Sahajāto</Emphasis>—  noāsavo eko khandho ca kāmāsavo ca tiṇṇannaṃ khandhānaṃ diṭṭhāsavassa avijjāsavassa cittasamuṭṭhānānañca rūpānaṃ atthipaccayena paccayo …pe…  dve khandhā ca …pe…  (cakkaṃ). <Emphasis>Sahajāto</Emphasis>—  kāmāsavo ca vatthu ca diṭṭhāsavassa avijjāsavassa sampayuttakānañca khandhānaṃ atthipaccayena paccayo. (Cakkaṃ.) (3)
</Paragraph>
