<Paragraph number="3729">
Upādānasampayutto dhammo upādānavippayuttassa dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="3730">
Upādānavippayutto dhammo upādānavippayuttassa dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo—  sahajātaṃ, purejātaṃ, pacchājātaṃ. (Saṃkhittaṃ.) (1)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="3731">
Upādānavippayutto dhammo upādānasampayuttassa dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Purejātaṃ</Emphasis>—  vatthu upādānasampayuttakānaṃ khandhānaṃ vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. (2)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="3732">
Upādānavippayutto dhammo upādānasampayuttassa ca upādānavippayuttassa ca dhammassa vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. <Emphasis>Purejātaṃ</Emphasis>—  vatthu diṭṭhigatavippayuttalobhasahagatānaṃ khandhānaṃ lobhassa ca vippayuttapaccayena paccayo. (3)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="3733">
