<Paragraph number="1196" book="kv">
๐ Maṃsacakkhuṃ dhammupatthaddhaṃ dibbacakkhuṃ hotīti? Āmantā. Maṃsacakkhuṃ dibbacakkhuṃ, dibbacakkhuṃ maṃsacakkhunti? Na hevaṃ vattabbe …pe… .
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1197" book="kv">
๐ Maṃsacakkhuṃ dhammupatthaddhaṃ dibbacakkhuṃ hotīti? Āmantā. Yādisaṃ maṃsacakkhuṃ tādisaṃ dibbacakkhuṃ, yādisaṃ dibbacakkhuṃ tādisaṃ maṃsacakkhunti? Na hevaṃ vattabbe …pe… .
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1198" book="kv">
๐ Maṃsacakkhuṃ dhammupatthaddhaṃ dibbacakkhuṃ hotīti? Āmantā. Taññeva maṃsacakkhuṃ taṃ dibbacakkhuṃ, taṃ dibbacakkhuṃ taṃ maṃsacakkhunti? Na hevaṃ vattabbe …pe… .
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1199" book="kv">
๐ Maṃsacakkhuṃ dhammupatthaddhaṃ dibbacakkhuṃ hotīti? Āmantā. Yādiso maṃsacakkhussa visayo ānubhāvo gocaro tādiso dibbassa cakkhussa visayo ānubhāvo gocaroti? Na hevaṃ vattabbe …pe… .
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1200" book="kv">
๐ Maṃsacakkhuṃ dhammupatthaddhaṃ dibbacakkhuṃ hotīti? Āmantā. Upādinnaṃ hutvā anupādinnaṃ hotīti? Na hevaṃ vattabbe …pe… .
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1201" book="kv">
๐ Upādinnaṃ hutvā anupādinnaṃ hotīti? Āmantā. Kāmāvacaraṃ hutvā rūpāvacaraṃ hotīti? Na hevaṃ vattabbe …pe… .
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1202" book="kv">
๐ Kāmāvacaraṃ hutvā rūpāvacaraṃ hotīti? Āmantā. Rūpāvacaraṃ hutvā arūpāvacaraṃ hotīti? Na hevaṃ vattabbe …pe… .
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1203" book="kv">
๐ Rūpāvacaraṃ hutvā arūpāvacaraṃ hotīti? Āmantā. Pariyāpannaṃ hutvā apariyāpannaṃ hotīti? Na hevaṃ vattabbe …pe… .
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="374" book="kv">

<Paragraph number="1204" book="kv">
๐ Maṃsacakkhuṃ dhammupatthaddhaṃ dibbacakkhuṃ hotīti? Āmantā. Dibbacakkhuṃ dhammupatthaddhaṃ maṃsacakkhuṃ hotīti? Na hevaṃ vattabbe …pe… .
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1205" book="kv">
๐ Maṃsacakkhuṃ dhammupatthaddhaṃ dibbacakkhuṃ hotīti? Āmantā. Dibbacakkhuṃ dhammupatthaddhaṃ paññācakkhuṃ hotīti? Na hevaṃ vattabbe …pe… .
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1206" book="kv">
๐ Maṃsacakkhuṃ dhammupatthaddhaṃ dibbacakkhuṃ hotīti? Āmantā. Dibbacakkhuṃ dhammupatthaddhaṃ maṃsacakkhuṃ hotīti? Na hevaṃ vattabbe …pe… .
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1207" book="kv">
๐ Maṃsacakkhuṃ dhammupatthaddhaṃ dibbacakkhuṃ hotīti? Āmantā. Dveva cakkhūnīti? Na hevaṃ vattabbe …pe…  dveva cakkhūnīti? Āmantā. Nanu tīṇi cakkhūni vuttāni bhagavatā—  maṃsacakkhuṃ, dibbacakkhuṃ, paññācakkhunti? Āmantā. Hañci tīṇi cakkhūni vuttāni bhagavatā—  maṃsacakkhuṃ, dibbacakkhuṃ, paññācakkhuṃ, no ca vata re vattabbe—  “dveva cakkhūnī”ti.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1208" book="kv">
๐ Dveva cakkhūnīti? Āmantā. Nanu vuttaṃ bhagavatā—  “tīṇimāni, bhikkhave, cakkhūni. Katamāni tīṇi? Maṃsacakkhuṃ, dibbacakkhuṃ, paññācakkhunti—  imāni kho, bhikkhave, tīṇi cakkhūnī”ti.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1209" type="verses" book="kv">
_“Maṃsacakkhuṃ dibbacakkhuṃ,_  
_Paññācakkhuṃ anuttaraṃ;_  
_Etāni tīṇi cakkhūni,_  
_Akkhāsi purisuttamo._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1210" type="verses" book="kv">
_Maṃsacakkhussa uppādo,_  
_maggo dibbassa cakkhuno;_  
_Yadā ca ñāṇaṃ udapādi,_  
_paññācakkhuṃ anuttaraṃ;_  
_Tassa cakkhussa paṭilābhā,_  
_sabbadukkhā pamuccatī”ti._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1211" book="kv">
Attheva suttantoti? Āmantā. Tena hi na vattabbaṃ—  “dveva cakkhūnī”ti.

</Paragraph>
</Division>
