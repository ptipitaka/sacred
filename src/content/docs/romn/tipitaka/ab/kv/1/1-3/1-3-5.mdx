<Paragraph number="1171" book="kv">
๐ Aṭṭhamakassa puggalassa diṭṭhipariyuṭṭhānaṃ pahīnanti? Āmantā. Aṭṭhamakassa puggalassa diṭṭhipariyuṭṭhānapahānāya maggo bhāvitoti? Na hevaṃ vattabbe …pe…  aṭṭhamakassa puggalassa diṭṭhipariyuṭṭhānaṃ pahīnanti? Āmantā. Aṭṭhamakassa puggalassa diṭṭhipariyuṭṭhānapahānāya satipaṭṭhānā bhāvitā …pe…  sammappadhānā …pe…  bojjhaṅgā bhāvitāti? Na hevaṃ vattabbe.
</Paragraph>

</Paragraph>
