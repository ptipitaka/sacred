<Paragraph number="2779" book="kv">
๐ Bodhisatto issariyakāmakārikāhetu vinipātaṃ gacchatīti? Āmantā. Bodhisatto issariyakāmakārikāhetu nirayaṃ gacchati, sañjīvaṃ gacchati, kālasuttaṃ gacchati, tāpanaṃ gacchati, mahātāpanaṃ gacchati, saṅghātakaṃ gacchati, roruvaṃ gacchati …pe…  avīciṃ gacchatīti? Na hevaṃ vattabbe …pe… .
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2780" book="kv">
๐ Bodhisatto issariyakāmakārikāhetu vinipātaṃ gacchatīti? Āmantā. “Bodhisatto issariyakāmakārikāhetu vinipātaṃ gacchatī”ti—  attheva suttantoti? Natthi. Hañci “bodhisatto issariyakāmakārikāhetu vinipātaṃ gacchatī”ti—  nattheva suttanto, no ca vata re vattabbe—  “bodhisatto issariyakāmakārikāhetu vinipātaṃ gacchatī”ti.
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="911" book="kv" bookNo="32" page="451">

<Paragraph number="2781" book="kv">
๐ Bodhisatto issariyakāmakārikāhetu gabbhaseyyaṃ okkamatīti? Āmantā. Bodhisatto issariyakāmakārikāhetu nirayaṃ upapajjeyya, tiracchānayoniṃ upapajjeyyāti? Na hevaṃ vattabbe …pe… .
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2782" book="kv">
๐ Bodhisatto issariyakāmakārikāhetu gabbhaseyyaṃ okkamatīti? Āmantā. Bodhisatto iddhimāti? Na hevaṃ vattabbe …pe…  bodhisatto iddhimāti? Āmantā. Bodhisattena chandiddhipādo bhāvito …pe…  vīriyiddhipādo …pe…  cittiddhipādo …pe…  vīmaṃsiddhipādo bhāvitoti? Na hevaṃ vattabbe …pe… .
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2783" book="kv">
๐ Bodhisatto issariyakāmakārikāhetu gabbhaseyyaṃ okkamatīti? Āmantā. “Bodhisatto issariyakāmakārikāhetu gabbhaseyyaṃ okkamatī”ti—  attheva suttantoti? Natthi. </Paragraph>

</Division>

<Paragraph number="2784" book="kv">
๐ Bodhisatto issariyakāmakārikāhetu dukkarakārikaṃ akāsīti? Āmantā. Bodhisatto issariyakāmakārikāhetu “sassato loko”ti paccāgacchi, “asassato loko”ti …pe…  “antavā loko”ti …pe…  “anantavā loko”ti…  “taṃ jīvaṃ taṃ sarīran”ti…  “aññaṃ jīvaṃ aññaṃ sarīran”ti…  “hoti tathāgato paraṃ maraṇā”ti…  “na hoti tathāgato paraṃ maraṇā”ti…  “hoti ca na ca hoti tathāgato paraṃ maraṇā”ti …pe…  “neva hoti na na hoti tathāgato paraṃ maraṇā”ti paccāgacchīti? Na hevaṃ vattabbe …pe… .
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2785" book="kv">
๐ Bodhisatto issariyakāmakārikāhetu dukkarakārikaṃ akāsīti? Āmantā. “Bodhisatto issariyakāmakārikāhetu dukkarakārikaṃ akāsī”ti—  attheva suttantoti? Natthi. </Paragraph>

</Division>

<Paragraph number="2786" book="kv">
๐ Bodhisatto issariyakāmakārikāhetu aparantapaṃ akāsi, aññaṃ satthāraṃ uddisīti? Āmantā. Bodhisatto issariyakāmakārikāhetu “sassato loko”ti paccāgacchi …pe…  “neva hoti na na hoti tathāgato paraṃ maraṇā”ti paccāgacchīti? Na hevaṃ vattabbe …pe… .
</Paragraph>

</Division>
