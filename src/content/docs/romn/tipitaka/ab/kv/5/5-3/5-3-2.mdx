๐ Arahantānaṃ vaṇṇena amanussā methunaṃ dhammaṃ paṭisevantīti? Āmantā. Arahantānaṃ vaṇṇena amanussā pāṇaṃ hananti …pe…  adinnaṃ ādiyanti, musā bhaṇanti, pisuṇaṃ bhaṇanti, pharusaṃ bhaṇanti, samphaṃ palapanti, sandhiṃ chindanti, nillopaṃ haranti, ekāgārikaṃ karonti, paripanthe tiṭṭhanti, paradāraṃ gacchanti, gāmaghātakaṃ karonti …pe…  nigamaghātakaṃ karontīti? Na hevaṃ vattabbe …pe… .
