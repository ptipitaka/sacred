<Division number="521" book="paci">

<Paragraph number="1532" book="paci">
Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena āyasmā upanando sakyaputto ucce mañce sayati. Atha kho bhagavā sambahulehi bhikkhūhi saddhiṃ senāsanacārikaṃ āhiṇḍanto yenāyasmato upanandassa sakyaputtassa vihāro tenupasaṅkami. Addasā kho āyasmā upanando sakyaputto bhagavantaṃ dūratova āgacchantaṃ. Disvāna bhagavantaṃ etadavoca—  “āgacchatu me, bhante, bhagavā sayanaṃ passatū”ti. Atha kho bhagavā tatova paṭinivattitvā bhikkhū āmantesi—  “āsayato, bhikkhave, moghapuriso veditabbo”ti. Atha kho bhagavā āyasmantaṃ upanandaṃ sakyaputtaṃ anekapariyāyena vigarahitvā dubbharatāya …pe…  evañca pana, bhikkhave, imaṃ sikkhāpadaṃ uddiseyyātha—
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="522" book="paci">

<Paragraph number="1533" book="paci">
<Emphasis>“Navaṃ pana bhikkhunā mañcaṃ vā pīṭhaṃ vā kārayamānena aṭṭhaṅgulapādakaṃ kāretabbaṃ sugataṅgulena, aññatra heṭṭhimāya aṭaniyā; taṃ atikkāmayato chedanakaṃ pācittiyan”</Emphasis>ti. (*87*:<Emphasis>136</Emphasis>)
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="523" book="paci">

<Paragraph number="1534" book="paci">
<Emphasis>Navaṃ</Emphasis> nāma karaṇaṃ upādāya vuccati.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1535" book="paci">
<Emphasis>Mañco</Emphasis> nāma cattāro mañcā—  masārako, bundikābaddho, kuḷīrapādako, āhaccapādako.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1536" book="paci">
<Emphasis>Pīṭhaṃ</Emphasis> nāma cattāri pīṭhāni—  masārakaṃ, bundikābaddhaṃ, kuḷīrapādakaṃ, āhaccapādakaṃ.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1537" book="paci">
<Emphasis>Kārayamānenā</Emphasis>ti karonto vā kārāpento vā.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1538" book="paci">
<Emphasis>Aṭṭhaṅgulapādakaṃ kāretabbaṃ sugataṅgulena, aññatra heṭṭhimāya aṭaniyā</Emphasis>ti ṭhapetvā heṭṭhimaṃ aṭaniṃ; taṃ atikkāmetvā karoti vā kārāpeti vā, payoge dukkaṭaṃ, paṭilābhena chinditvā pācittiyaṃ desetabbaṃ.
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="524" book="paci">

<Paragraph number="1539" book="paci">
Attanā vippakataṃ attanā pariyosāpeti, āpatti pācittiyassa. Attanā vippakataṃ parehi pariyosāpeti, āpatti pācittiyassa. Parehi vippakataṃ attanā pariyosāpeti, āpatti pācittiyassa. Parehi vippakataṃ parehi pariyosāpeti, āpatti pācittiyassa.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1540" book="paci">
Aññassatthāya karoti vā kārāpeti vā, āpatti dukkaṭassa. Aññena kataṃ paṭilabhitvā paribhuñjati, āpatti dukkaṭassa.
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="525" book="paci">

<Paragraph number="1541" book="paci">
Anāpatti—  pamāṇikaṃ karoti, ūnakaṃ karoti, aññena kataṃ pamāṇātikkantaṃ paṭilabhitvā chinditvā paribhuñjati, ummattakassa, ādikammikassāti.

</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="1542" type="center" book="paci">
Mañcapīṭhasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ pañcamaṃ.
</Paragraph>
