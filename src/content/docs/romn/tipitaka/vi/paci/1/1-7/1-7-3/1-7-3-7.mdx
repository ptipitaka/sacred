Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū asakkaccaṃ piṇḍapātaṃ paṭiggaṇhanti chaḍḍetukāmā viya …pe… .

</Paragraph>
