Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū oṭṭhanillehakaṃ bhuñjanti …pe… .

<Paragraph number="1957" book="paci">

</Paragraph>
