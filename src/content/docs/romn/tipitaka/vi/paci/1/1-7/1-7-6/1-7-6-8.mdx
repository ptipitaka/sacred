<Paragraph number="1986" book="paci">
<Emphasis>Daṇḍo</Emphasis> nāma majjhimassa purisassa catuhattho daṇḍo. Tato ukkaṭṭho adaṇḍo, omako adaṇḍo.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1987" book="paci">

</Paragraph>
