<Division number="961" book="paci">

<Paragraph number="2952" book="paci">
Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena bhikkhuniyo antoraṭṭhe sāsaṅkasammate sappaṭibhaye asatthikā cārikaṃ caranti. Dhuttā dūsenti.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2953" book="paci">
Yā tā bhikkhuniyo appicchā …pe…  tā ujjhāyanti khiyyanti vipācenti—  “kathañhi nāma bhikkhuniyo antoraṭṭhe sāsaṅkasammate sappaṭibhaye asatthikā cārikaṃ carissantī”ti …pe…  “saccaṃ kira, bhikkhave, bhikkhuniyo antoraṭṭhe sāsaṅkasammate sappaṭibhaye asatthikā cārikaṃ carantī”ti? “Saccaṃ, bhagavā”ti. Vigarahi buddho bhagavā …pe…  kathañhi nāma, bhikkhave, bhikkhuniyo antoraṭṭhe sāsaṅkasammate sappaṭibhaye asatthikā cārikaṃ carissanti. Netaṃ, bhikkhave, appasannānaṃ vā pasādāya …pe…  evañca pana, bhikkhave, bhikkhuniyo imaṃ sikkhāpadaṃ uddisantu—
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="962" book="paci">

<Paragraph number="2954" book="paci">
<Emphasis>“Yā pana bhikkhunī antoraṭṭhe sāsaṅkasammate sappaṭibhaye asatthikā cārikaṃ careyya, pācittiyan”</Emphasis>ti. (*37*:<Emphasis>92</Emphasis>)
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="963" book="paci">

<Paragraph number="2955" book="paci">
<Emphasis>Yā panā</Emphasis>ti yā yādisā …pe…  <Emphasis>bhikkhunī</Emphasis>ti …pe…  ayaṃ imasmiṃ atthe adhippetā bhikkhunīti.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2956" book="paci">
<Emphasis>Antoraṭṭhe</Emphasis>ti yassa vijite viharati, tassa raṭṭhe.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2957" book="paci">
<Emphasis>Sāsaṅkaṃ</Emphasis> nāma tasmiṃ magge corānaṃ niviṭṭhokāso dissati, bhuttokāso dissati, ṭhitokāso dissati, nisinnokāso dissati, nipannokāso dissati.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2958" book="paci">
<Emphasis>Sappaṭibhayaṃ</Emphasis> nāma tasmiṃ magge corehi manussā hatā dissanti, viluttā dissanti, ākoṭitā dissanti.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2959" book="paci">
<Emphasis>Asatthikā</Emphasis> nāma vinā satthena.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2960" book="paci">
<Emphasis>Cārikaṃ careyyā</Emphasis>ti kukkuṭasampāte gāme gāmantare gāmantare āpatti pācittiyassa. Agāmake araññe addhayojane addhayojane āpatti pācittiyassa.
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="964" book="paci">

<Paragraph number="2961" book="paci">
Anāpatti—  satthena saha gacchati, kheme appaṭibhaye gacchati, āpadāsu, ummattikāya, ādikammikāyāti.

</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="2962" type="center" book="paci">
Sattamasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
</Paragraph>
