<Paragraph number="3285" book="paci">
Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena thullanandā bhikkhunī sahajīviniṃ vuṭṭhāpetvā neva vūpakāseti na vūpakāsāpeti. Sāmiko aggahesi.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="3286" book="paci">
Yā tā bhikkhuniyo appicchā …pe…  tā ujjhāyanti khiyyanti vipācenti—  “kathañhi nāma ayyā thullanandā sahajīviniṃ vuṭṭhāpetvā neva vūpakāsessati na vūpakāsāpessati, sāmiko aggahesi. Sacāyaṃ bhikkhunī pakkantā assa, na ca sāmiko gaṇheyyā”ti …pe…  “saccaṃ kira, bhikkhave, thullanandā bhikkhunī sahajīviniṃ vuṭṭhāpetvā neva vūpakāseti na vūpakāsāpeti, sāmiko aggahesī”ti? “Saccaṃ, bhagavā”ti. Vigarahi buddho bhagavā …pe…  kathañhi nāma, bhikkhave, thullanandā bhikkhunī sahajīviniṃ vuṭṭhāpetvā neva vūpakāsessati na vūpakāsāpessati, sāmiko aggahesi. Netaṃ, bhikkhave, appasannānaṃ vā pasādāya …pe…  evañca pana, bhikkhave, bhikkhuniyo imaṃ sikkhāpadaṃ uddisantu—
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="1116" book="paci" bookNo="2" page="432">

<Paragraph number="3287" book="paci">
<Emphasis>“Yā pana bhikkhunī sahajīviniṃ vuṭṭhāpetvā neva vūpakāseyya na vūpakāsāpeyya antamaso chappañcayojanānipi, pācittiyan”</Emphasis>ti. (*70*:<Emphasis>125</Emphasis>)
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="1117" book="paci" bookNo="2" page="432">

<Paragraph number="3288" book="paci">
<Emphasis>Yā panā</Emphasis>ti yā yādisā …pe…  <Emphasis>bhikkhunī</Emphasis>ti …pe…  ayaṃ imasmiṃ atthe adhippetā bhikkhunīti.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="3289" book="paci">
<Emphasis>Sahajīvinī</Emphasis> nāma saddhivihārinī vuccati. <Emphasis>Vuṭṭhāpetvā</Emphasis>ti upasampādetvā. <Emphasis>Neva</Emphasis> <Emphasis>vūpakāseyyā</Emphasis>ti na sayaṃ vūpakāseyya. <Emphasis>Na vūpakāsāpeyyā</Emphasis>ti na aññaṃ āṇāpeyya. “Neva vūpakāsessāmi na vūpakāsāpessāmi antamaso chappañcayojanānipī”ti dhuraṃ nikkhittamatte āpatti pācittiyassa.
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="1118" book="paci" bookNo="2" page="432">

<Paragraph number="3290" book="paci">
Anāpatti—  sati antarāye, pariyesitvā dutiyikaṃ bhikkhuniṃ na labhati, gilānāya, āpadāsu, ummattikāya, ādikammikāyāti.

</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="3291" type="center" book="paci">
Dasamasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.

  
Gabbhinivaggo sattamo.
</Paragraph>
