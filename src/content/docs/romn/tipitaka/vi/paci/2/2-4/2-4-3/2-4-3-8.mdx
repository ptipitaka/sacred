<Paragraph number="2851" book="paci">
Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena thullanandā bhikkhunī naṭānampi naṭakānampi laṅghakānampi sokajjhāyikānampi kumbhathūṇikānampi samaṇacīvaraṃ deti—  “mayhaṃ parisati vaṇṇaṃ bhāsathā”ti. Naṭāpi naṭakāpi laṅghakāpi sokajjhāyikāpi kumbhathūṇikāpi thullanandāya bhikkhuniyā parisati vaṇṇaṃ bhāsanti—  “ayyā thullanandā bahussutā bhāṇikā visāradā paṭṭā dhammiṃ kathaṃ kātuṃ; detha ayyāya, karotha ayyāyā”ti.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2852" book="paci">
Yā tā bhikkhuniyo appicchā, tā ujjhāyanti khiyyanti vipācenti—  “kathañhi nāma ayyā thullanandā agārikassa samaṇacīvaraṃ dassatī”ti …pe…  “saccaṃ kira, bhikkhave, thullanandā bhikkhunī agārikassa samaṇacīvaraṃ detī”ti? “Saccaṃ, bhagavā”ti. Vigarahi buddho bhagavā …pe…  kathañhi nāma, bhikkhave, thullanandā bhikkhunī agārikassa samaṇacīvaraṃ dassati. Netaṃ, bhikkhave, appasannānaṃ vā pasādāya …pe…  evañca pana, bhikkhave, bhikkhuniyo imaṃ sikkhāpadaṃ uddisantu—
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="917" book="paci" bookNo="2" page="374">

<Paragraph number="2853" book="paci">
<Emphasis>“Yā pana bhikkhunī agārikassa vā paribbājakassa vā paribbājikāya vā samaṇacīvaraṃ dadeyya, pācittiyan”</Emphasis>ti. (*28*:<Emphasis>83</Emphasis>)
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="918" book="paci" bookNo="2" page="374">

<Paragraph number="2854" book="paci">
<Emphasis>Yā panā</Emphasis>ti yā yādisā …pe…  <Emphasis>bhikkhunī</Emphasis>ti …pe…  ayaṃ imasmiṃ atthe adhippetā bhikkhunīti.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2855" book="paci">
<Emphasis>Agāriko</Emphasis> nāma yo koci agāraṃ ajjhāvasati.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2856" book="paci">
<Emphasis>Paribbājako</Emphasis> nāma bhikkhuñca sāmaṇerañca ṭhapetvā yo koci paribbājakasamāpanno.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2857" book="paci">
<Emphasis>Paribbājikā</Emphasis> nāma bhikkhuniñca sikkhamānañca sāmaṇeriñca ṭhapetvā yā kāci paribbājikasamāpannā.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2858" book="paci">
<Emphasis>Samaṇacīvaraṃ</Emphasis> nāma kappakataṃ vuccati. Deti, āpatti pācittiyassa.
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="919" book="paci" bookNo="2" page="375">

<Paragraph number="2859" book="paci">
Anāpatti—  mātāpitūnaṃ deti, tāvakālikaṃ deti, ummattikāya, ādikammikāyāti.

</Paragraph>
</Division>
