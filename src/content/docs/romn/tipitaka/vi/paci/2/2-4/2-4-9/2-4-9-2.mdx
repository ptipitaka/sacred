<Paragraph number="3439" book="paci">
Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhuniyo yānena yāyanti. Manussā ujjhāyanti khiyyanti vipācenti—  “kathañhi nāma bhikkhuniyo yānena yāyissanti, seyyathāpi gihiniyo kāmabhoginiyo”ti.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="3440" book="paci">
Assosuṃ kho bhikkhuniyo tesaṃ manussānaṃ ujjhāyantānaṃ khiyyantānaṃ vipācentānaṃ. Yā tā bhikkhuniyo appicchā …pe…  tā ujjhāyanti khiyyanti vipācenti—  “kathañhi nāma chabbaggiyā bhikkhuniyo yānena yāyissantī”ti …pe…  “saccaṃ kira, bhikkhave, chabbaggiyā bhikkhuniyo yānena yāyantī”ti? “Saccaṃ, bhagavā”ti. Vigarahi buddho bhagavā …pe…  kathañhi nāma, bhikkhave, chabbaggiyā bhikkhuniyo yānena yāyissanti. Netaṃ, bhikkhave, appasannānaṃ vā pasādāya …pe…  evañca pana, bhikkhave, bhikkhuniyo imaṃ sikkhāpadaṃ uddisantu—
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="3441" book="paci">
<Emphasis>“Yā pana bhikkhunī yānena yāyeyya, pācittiyan”</Emphasis>ti.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="3442" book="paci">
Evañcidaṃ bhagavatā bhikkhunīnaṃ sikkhāpadaṃ paññattaṃ hoti.
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="1185" book="paci" e="ch" v="2" p="452">

<Paragraph number="3443" book="paci">
Tena kho pana samayena aññatarā bhikkhunī gilānā hoti, na sakkoti padasā gantuṃ …pe…  bhagavato etamatthaṃ ārocesuṃ …pe…  anujānāmi, bhikkhave, gilānāya bhikkhuniyā yānaṃ. Evañca pana, bhikkhave, bhikkhuniyo imaṃ sikkhāpadaṃ uddisantu—
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="1186" book="paci" e="ch" v="2" p="453">

<Paragraph number="3444" book="paci">
<Emphasis>“Yā pana bhikkhunī agilānā yānena yāyeyya, pācittiyan”</Emphasis>ti. (*85*:<Emphasis>140</Emphasis>)
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="1187" book="paci" e="ch" v="2" p="453">

<Paragraph number="3445" book="paci">
<Emphasis>Yā panā</Emphasis>ti yā yādisā …pe…  <Emphasis>bhikkhunī</Emphasis>ti …pe…  ayaṃ imasmiṃ atthe adhippetā bhikkhunīti.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="3446" book="paci">
<Emphasis>Agilānā</Emphasis> nāma sakkoti padasā gantuṃ.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="3447" book="paci">
<Emphasis>Gilānā</Emphasis> nāma na sakkoti padasā gantuṃ.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="3448" book="paci">
<Emphasis>Yānaṃ</Emphasis> nāma vayhaṃ ratho sakaṭaṃ sandamānikā sivikā pāṭaṅkī.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="3449" book="paci">
<Emphasis>Yāyeyyā</Emphasis>ti sakimpi yānena yāyati, āpatti pācittiyassa.
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="1188" book="paci" e="ch" v="2" p="453">

<Paragraph number="3450" book="paci">
Agilānā agilānasaññā yānena yāyati, āpatti pācittiyassa. Agilānā vematikā yānena yāyati, āpatti pācittiyassa. Agilānā gilānasaññā yānena yāyati, āpatti pācittiyassa.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="3451" book="paci">
Gilānā agilānasaññā, āpatti dukkaṭassa. Gilānā vematikā, āpatti dukkaṭassa. Gilānā gilānasaññā, anāpatti.
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="1189" book="paci" e="ch" v="2" p="453">

<Paragraph number="3452" book="paci">
Anāpatti—  gilānāya, āpadāsu, ummattikāya, ādikammikāyāti.

</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="3453" type="center" book="paci">
Dutiyasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
</Paragraph>
