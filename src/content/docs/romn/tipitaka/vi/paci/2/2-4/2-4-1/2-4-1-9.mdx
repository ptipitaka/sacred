<Division number="828" book="paci">

<Paragraph number="2649" book="paci">
Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena aññatarassa brāhmaṇassa bhikkhunupassayaṃ nissāya yavakhettaṃ hoti. Bhikkhuniyo uccārampi passāvampi saṅkārampi vighāsampi khette chaḍḍenti. Atha kho so brāhmaṇo ujjhāyati khiyyati vipāceti—  “kathañhi nāma bhikkhuniyo amhākaṃ yavakhettaṃ dūsessantī”ti.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2650" book="paci">
Assosuṃ kho bhikkhuniyo tassa brāhmaṇassa ujjhāyantassa khiyyantassa vipācentassa. Yā tā bhikkhuniyo appicchā …pe…  tā ujjhāyanti khiyyanti vipācenti—  “kathañhi nāma bhikkhuniyo uccārampi passāvampi saṅkārampi vighāsampi harite chaḍḍessantī”ti …pe…  “saccaṃ kira, bhikkhave, bhikkhuniyo uccārampi passāvampi saṅkārampi vighāsampi harite chaḍḍentī”ti? “Saccaṃ, bhagavā”ti. Vigarahi buddho bhagavā …pe…  kathañhi nāma, bhikkhave, bhikkhuniyo uccārampi passāvampi saṅkārampi vighāsampi harite chaḍḍessanti. Netaṃ, bhikkhave, appasannānaṃ vā pasādāya …pe…  evañca pana, bhikkhave, bhikkhuniyo imaṃ sikkhāpadaṃ uddisantu—
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="829" book="paci">

<Paragraph number="2651" book="paci">
<Emphasis>“Yā pana bhikkhunī uccāraṃ vā passāvaṃ vā saṅkāraṃ vā vighāsaṃ vā harite chaḍḍeyya vā chaḍḍāpeyya vā, pācittiyan”</Emphasis>ti. (*9*:<Emphasis>64</Emphasis>)
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="830" book="paci">

<Paragraph number="2652" book="paci">
<Emphasis>Yā panā</Emphasis>ti yā yādisā …pe…  <Emphasis>bhikkhunī</Emphasis>ti …pe…  ayaṃ imasmiṃ atthe adhippetā bhikkhunīti.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2653" book="paci">
<Emphasis>Uccāro</Emphasis> nāma gūtho vuccati. <Emphasis>Passāvo</Emphasis> nāma muttaṃ vuccati.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2654" book="paci">
<Emphasis>Saṅkāraṃ</Emphasis> nāma kacavaraṃ vuccati.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2655" book="paci">
<Emphasis>Vighāsaṃ</Emphasis> nāma calakāni vā aṭṭhikāni vā ucchiṭṭhodakaṃ vā.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2656" book="paci">
<Emphasis>Haritaṃ</Emphasis> nāma pubbaṇṇaṃ aparaṇṇaṃ yaṃ manussānaṃ upabhogaparibhogaṃ ropimaṃ.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2657" book="paci">
<Emphasis>Chaḍḍeyyā</Emphasis>ti sayaṃ chaḍḍeti, āpatti pācittiyassa.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2658" book="paci">
<Emphasis>Chaḍḍāpeyyā</Emphasis>ti aññaṃ āṇāpeti, āpatti dukkaṭassa. Sakiṃ āṇattā bahukampi chaḍḍeti, āpatti pācittiyassa.
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="831" book="paci">

<Paragraph number="2659" book="paci">
Harite haritasaññā chaḍḍeti vā chaḍḍāpeti vā, āpatti pācittiyassa. Harite vematikā chaḍḍeti vā chaḍḍāpeti vā, āpatti pācittiyassa. Harite aharitasaññā chaḍḍeti vā chaḍḍāpeti vā, āpatti pācittiyassa.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2660" book="paci">
Aharite haritasaññā, āpatti dukkaṭassa. Aharite vematikā, āpatti dukkaṭassa. Aharite aharitasaññā, anāpatti.
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="832" book="paci">

<Paragraph number="2661" book="paci">
Anāpatti—  oloketvā chaḍḍeti, khettamariyāde chaḍḍeti sāmike āpucchitvā apaloketvā chaḍḍeti, ummattikāya, ādikammikāyāti.

</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="2662" type="center" book="paci">
Navamasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
</Paragraph>
