<Paragraph number="3019" book="paci">
Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena bhikkhuniyo gihiveyyāvaccaṃ karonti. Yā tā bhikkhuniyo appicchā …pe…  tā ujjhāyanti khiyyanti vipācenti—  “kathañhi nāma bhikkhuniyo gihiveyyāvaccaṃ karissantī”ti …pe…  “saccaṃ kira, bhikkhave, bhikkhuniyo gihiveyyāvaccaṃ karontī”ti? “Saccaṃ, bhagavā”ti. Vigarahi buddho bhagavā …pe…  kathañhi nāma, bhikkhave, bhikkhuniyo gihiveyyāvaccaṃ karissanti. Netaṃ, bhikkhave, appasannānaṃ vā pasādāya …pe…  evañca pana, bhikkhave, bhikkhuniyo imaṃ sikkhāpadaṃ uddisantu—
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="991" book="paci" e="ch" v="2" p="395">

<Paragraph number="3020" book="paci">
<Emphasis>“Yā pana bhikkhunī gihiveyyāvaccaṃ kareyya, pācittiyan”</Emphasis>ti. (*44*:<Emphasis>99</Emphasis>)
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="992" book="paci" e="ch" v="2" p="395">

<Paragraph number="3021" book="paci">
<Emphasis>Yā panā</Emphasis>ti yā yādisā …pe…  <Emphasis>bhikkhunī</Emphasis>ti …pe…  ayaṃ imasmiṃ atthe adhippetā bhikkhunīti.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="3022" book="paci">
<Emphasis>Gihiveyyāvaccaṃ</Emphasis> nāma agārikassa yāguṃ vā bhattaṃ vā khādanīyaṃ vā pacati, sāṭakaṃ vā veṭhanaṃ vā dhovati, āpatti pācittiyassa.
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="993" book="paci" e="ch" v="2" p="395">

<Paragraph number="3023" book="paci">
Anāpatti—  yāgupāne, saṃghabhatte, cetiyapūjāya, attano veyyāvaccakarassa yāguṃ vā bhattaṃ vā khādanīyaṃ vā pacati, sāṭakaṃ vā veṭhanaṃ vā dhovati, ummattikāya, ādikammikāyāti.

</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="3024" type="center" book="paci">
Catutthasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
</Paragraph>
