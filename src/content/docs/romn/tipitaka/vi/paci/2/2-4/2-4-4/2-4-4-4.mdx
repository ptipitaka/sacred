<Paragraph number="2912" book="paci">
Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena thullanandā bhikkhunī dukkhitaṃ sahajīviniṃ neva upaṭṭheti na upaṭṭhāpanāya ussukkaṃ karoti.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2913" book="paci">
Yā tā bhikkhuniyo appicchā …pe…  tā ujjhāyanti khiyyanti vipācenti—  “kathañhi nāma ayyā thullanandā dukkhitaṃ sahajīviniṃ neva upaṭṭhessati na upaṭṭhāpanāya ussukkaṃ karissatī”ti …pe…  “saccaṃ kira, bhikkhave, thullanandā bhikkhunī dukkhitaṃ sahajīviniṃ neva upaṭṭheti na upaṭṭhāpanāya ussukkaṃ karotī”ti? “Saccaṃ, bhagavā”ti. Vigarahi buddho bhagavā …pe…  kathañhi nāma, bhikkhave, thullanandā bhikkhunī dukkhitaṃ sahajīviniṃ neva upaṭṭhessati na upaṭṭhāpanāya ussukkaṃ karissati. Netaṃ, bhikkhave, appasannānaṃ vā pasādāya …pe…  evañca pana, bhikkhave, bhikkhuniyo imaṃ sikkhāpadaṃ uddisantu—
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="947" book="paci" bookNo="2" page="382">

<Paragraph number="2914" book="paci">
<Emphasis>“Yā pana bhikkhunī dukkhitaṃ sahajīviniṃ neva upaṭṭheyya na upaṭṭhāpanāya ussukkaṃ kareyya, pācittiyan”</Emphasis>ti. (*34*:<Emphasis>89</Emphasis>)
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="948" book="paci" bookNo="2" page="383">

<Paragraph number="2915" book="paci">
<Emphasis>Yā panā</Emphasis>ti yā yādisā …pe…  <Emphasis>bhikkhunī</Emphasis>ti …pe…  ayaṃ imasmiṃ atthe adhippetā bhikkhunīti.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2916" book="paci">
<Emphasis>Dukkhitā</Emphasis> nāma gilānā vuccati.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2917" book="paci">
<Emphasis>Sahajīvinī</Emphasis> nāma saddhivihārinī vuccati.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2918" book="paci">
<Emphasis>Neva upaṭṭheyyā</Emphasis>ti na sayaṃ upaṭṭheyya.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2919" book="paci">
<Emphasis>Na upaṭṭhāpanāya ussukkaṃ kareyyā</Emphasis>ti na aññaṃ āṇāpeyya.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2920" book="paci">
“Neva upaṭṭhessāmi, na upaṭṭhāpanāya ussukkaṃ karissāmī”ti dhuraṃ nikkhittamatte āpatti pācittiyassa. Antevāsiniṃ vā anupasampannaṃ vā neva upaṭṭheti, na upaṭṭhāpanāya ussukkaṃ karoti, āpatti dukkaṭassa.
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="949" book="paci" bookNo="2" page="383">

<Paragraph number="2921" book="paci">
Anāpatti—  sati antarāye, pariyesitvā na labhati, gilānāya, āpadāsu, ummattikāya, ādikammikāyāti.

</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="2922" type="center" book="paci">
Catutthasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
</Paragraph>
