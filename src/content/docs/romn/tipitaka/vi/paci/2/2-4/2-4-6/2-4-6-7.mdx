<Division number="1050" book="paci">

<Paragraph number="3152" book="paci">
Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena sambahulā bhikkhuniyo gāmakāvāse vassaṃvuṭṭhā sāvatthiṃ agamaṃsu. Bhikkhuniyo tā bhikkhuniyo etadavocuṃ—  “katthāyyāyo vassaṃvuṭṭhā; kattha bhikkhusaṃgho pavārito”ti? “Na mayaṃ, ayye, bhikkhusaṃghaṃ pavāremā”ti.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="3153" book="paci">
Yā tā bhikkhuniyo appicchā …pe…  tā ujjhāyanti khiyyanti vipācenti—  “kathañhi nāma bhikkhuniyo vassaṃvuṭṭhā bhikkhusaṃghaṃ na pavāressantī”ti …pe…  “saccaṃ kira, bhikkhave, bhikkhuniyo vassaṃvuṭṭhā bhikkhusaṃghaṃ na pavārentī”ti? “Saccaṃ, bhagavā”ti. Vigarahi buddho bhagavā …pe…  kathañhi nāma, bhikkhave, bhikkhuniyo vassaṃvuṭṭhā bhikkhusaṃghaṃ na pavāressanti. Netaṃ, bhikkhave, appasannānaṃ vā pasādāya …pe…  evañca pana, bhikkhave, bhikkhuniyo imaṃ sikkhāpadaṃ uddisantu—
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="1051" book="paci">

<Paragraph number="3154" book="paci">
<Emphasis>“Yā pana bhikkhunī vassaṃvuṭṭhā ubhatosaṃghe tīhi ṭhānehi na pavāreyya diṭṭhena vā sutena vā parisaṅkāya vā, pācittiyan”</Emphasis>ti. (*57*:<Emphasis>112</Emphasis>)
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="1052" book="paci">

<Paragraph number="3155" book="paci">
<Emphasis>Yā panā</Emphasis>ti yā yādisā …pe…  <Emphasis>bhikkhunī</Emphasis>ti …pe…  ayaṃ imasmiṃ atthe adhippetā bhikkhunīti.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="3156" book="paci">
<Emphasis>Vassaṃvuṭṭhā</Emphasis> nāma purimaṃ vā temāsaṃ pacchimaṃ vā temāsaṃ vuṭṭhā. Ubhatosaṃghe tīhi ṭhānehi na pavāressāmi diṭṭhena vā sutena vā parisaṅkāya vā”ti dhuraṃ nikkhittamatte āpatti pācittiyassa.
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="1053" book="paci">

<Paragraph number="3157" book="paci">
Anāpatti—  sati antarāye, pariyesitvā na labhati, gilānāya, āpadāsu, ummattikāya, ādikammikāyāti.

</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="3158" type="center" book="paci">
Sattamasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
</Paragraph>
