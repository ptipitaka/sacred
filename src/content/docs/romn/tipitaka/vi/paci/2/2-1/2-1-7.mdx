<Paragraph number="2155" book="paci">
Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena thullanandā bhikkhunī samaggena saṃghena ukkhittaṃ ariṭṭhaṃ bhikkhuṃ gaddhabādhipubbaṃ anuvattati.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2156" book="paci">
Yā tā bhikkhuniyo appicchā …pe…  tā ujjhāyanti khiyyanti vipācenti—  “kathañhi nāma ayyā thullanandā samaggena saṃghena ukkhittaṃ ariṭṭhaṃ bhikkhuṃ gaddhabādhipubbaṃ anuvattissatī”ti …pe…  “saccaṃ kira, bhikkhave, thullanandā bhikkhunī samaggena saṃghena ukkhittaṃ ariṭṭhaṃ bhikkhuṃ gaddhabādhipubbaṃ anuvattatī”ti? “Saccaṃ, bhagavā”ti. Vigarahi buddho bhagavā …pe…  kathañhi nāma, bhikkhave, thullanandā bhikkhunī samaggena saṃghena ukkhittaṃ ariṭṭhaṃ bhikkhuṃ gaddhabādhipubbaṃ anuvattissati. Netaṃ, bhikkhave, appasannānaṃ vā pasādāya …pe…  evañca pana, bhikkhave, bhikkhuniyo imaṃ sikkhāpadaṃ uddisantu—
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="669" book="paci" bookNo="2" page="282">

<Paragraph number="2157" book="paci">
<Emphasis>“Yā pana bhikkhunī samaggena saṃghena ukkhittaṃ bhikkhuṃ dhammena vinayena satthusāsanena anādaraṃ appaṭikāraṃ akatasahāyaṃ tamanuvatteyya, sā bhikkhunī bhikkhunīhi evamassa vacanīyā—  ‘eso kho, ayye, bhikkhu samaggena saṃghena ukkhitto dhammena vinayena satthusāsanena anādaro appaṭikāro akatasahāyo, māyye, etaṃ bhikkhuṃ anuvattī’ti. Evañca sā bhikkhunī bhikkhunīhi vuccamānā tatheva paggaṇheyya, sā bhikkhunī bhikkhunīhi yāvatatiyaṃ samanubhāsitabbā tassa paṭinissaggāya. Yāvatatiyañce samanubhāsiyamānā taṃ paṭinissajjeyya, iccetaṃ kusalaṃ. No ce paṭinissajjeyya, ayampi pārājikā hoti asaṃvāsā ukkhittānuvattikā”</Emphasis>ti. (*7*:<Emphasis>7</Emphasis>)
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="670" book="paci" bookNo="2" page="282">

<Paragraph number="2158" book="paci">
<Emphasis>Yā panā</Emphasis>ti yā yādisā …pe…  <Emphasis>bhikkhunī</Emphasis>ti …pe…  ayaṃ imasmiṃ atthe adhippetā bhikkhunīti.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2159" book="paci">
<Emphasis>Samaggo</Emphasis> nāma saṃgho samānasaṃvāsako samānasīmāyaṃ ṭhito.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2160" book="paci">
<Emphasis>Ukkhitto</Emphasis> nāma āpattiyā adassane vā appaṭikamme vā appaṭinissagge vā ukkhitto.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2161" book="paci">
<Emphasis>Dhammena vinayenā</Emphasis>ti yena dhammena yena vinayena.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2162" book="paci">
<Emphasis>Satthusāsanenā</Emphasis>ti jinasāsanena buddhasāsanena.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2163" book="paci">
<Emphasis>Anādaro</Emphasis> nāma saṃghaṃ vā gaṇaṃ vā puggalaṃ vā kammaṃ vā nādiyati.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2164" book="paci">
<Emphasis>Appaṭikāro</Emphasis> nāma ukkhitto anosārito.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2165" book="paci">
<Emphasis>Akatasahāyo</Emphasis> nāma samānasaṃvāsakā bhikkhū vuccanti sahāyā. So tehi saddhiṃ natthi, tena vuccati akatasahāyoti.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2166" book="paci">
<Emphasis>Tamanuvatteyyā</Emphasis>ti yaṃdiṭṭhiko so hoti yaṃkhantiko yaṃruciko, sāpi taṃdiṭṭhikā hoti taṃkhantikā taṃrucikā.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2167" book="paci">
<Emphasis>Sā bhikkhunī</Emphasis>ti yā sā ukkhittānuvattikā bhikkhunī.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2168" book="paci">
<Emphasis>Bhikkhunīhī</Emphasis>ti aññāhi bhikkhunīhi. Yā passanti yā suṇanti tāhi vattabbā—  “eso kho, ayye, bhikkhu samaggena saṃghena ukkhitto dhammena vinayena satthusāsanena anādaro appaṭikāro akatasahāyo. Māyye, etaṃ bhikkhuṃ anuvattī”ti. Dutiyampi vattabbā. Tatiyampi vattabbā. Sace paṭinissajjati, iccetaṃ kusalaṃ; no ce paṭinissajjati, āpatti dukkaṭassa. Sutvā na vadanti, āpatti dukkaṭassa. Sā bhikkhunī saṃghamajjhampi ākaḍḍhitvā vattabbā—  “eso kho, ayye, bhikkhu samaggena saṃghena ukkhitto dhammena vinayena satthusāsanena anādaro appaṭikāro akatasahāyo. Māyye, etaṃ bhikkhuṃ anuvattīti. Dutiyampi vattabbā. Tatiyampi vattabbā. Sace paṭinissajjati, iccetaṃ kusalaṃ. No ce paṭinissajjati, āpatti dukkaṭassa. Sā bhikkhunī samanubhāsitabbā. Evañca pana, bhikkhave, samanubhāsitabbā. Byattāya bhikkhuniyā paṭibalāya saṃgho ñāpetabbo—
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="671" book="paci" bookNo="2" page="283">

<Paragraph number="2169" book="paci">
‘Suṇātu me, ayye, saṃgho. Ayaṃ itthannāmā bhikkhunī samaggena saṃghena ukkhittaṃ bhikkhuṃ dhammena vinayena satthusāsanena anādaraṃ appaṭikāraṃ akatasahāyaṃ tamanuvattati, sā taṃ vatthuṃ na paṭinissajjati. Yadi saṃghassa pattakallaṃ, saṃgho itthannāmaṃ bhikkhuniṃ samanubhāseyya tassa vatthussa paṭinissaggāya. Esā ñatti.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2170" book="paci">
Suṇātu me, ayye, saṃgho. Ayaṃ itthannāmā bhikkhunī samaggena saṃghena ukkhittaṃ bhikkhuṃ dhammena vinayena satthusāsanena anādaraṃ appaṭikāraṃ akatasahāyaṃ tamanuvattati. Sā taṃ vatthuṃ na paṭinissajjati. Saṃgho itthannāmaṃ bhikkhuniṃ samanubhāsati tassa vatthussa paṭinissaggāya. Yassā ayyāya khamati itthannāmāya bhikkhuniyā samanubhāsanā tassa vatthussa paṭinissaggāya, sā tuṇhassa; yassā nakkhamati, sā bhāseyya.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2171" book="paci">
Dutiyampi etamatthaṃ vadāmi …pe…  tatiyampi etamatthaṃ vadāmi …pe… .
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2172" book="paci">
Samanubhaṭṭhā saṃghena itthannāmā bhikkhunī tassa vatthussa paṭinissaggāya. Khamati saṃghassa, tasmā tuṇhī, evametaṃ dhārayāmī’”ti.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2173" book="paci">
Ñattiyā dukkaṭaṃ, dvīhi kammavācāhi thullaccayā, kammavācā pariyosāne āpatti pārājikassa.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2174" book="paci">
<Emphasis>Ayampī</Emphasis>ti purimāyo upādāya vuccati.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2175" book="paci">
<Emphasis>Pārājikā hotī</Emphasis>ti seyyathāpi nāma puthusilā dvedhā bhinnā appaṭisandhikā hoti, evameva bhikkhunī yāvatatiyaṃ samanubhāsanāya na paṭinissajjantī assamaṇī hoti asakyadhītā. Tena vuccati pārājikā hotīti.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2176" book="paci">
<Emphasis>Asaṃvāsā</Emphasis>ti <Emphasis>saṃvāso</Emphasis> nāma ekakammaṃ ekuddeso samasikkhatā. Eso saṃvāso nāma. So tāya saddhiṃ natthi. Tena vuccati asaṃvāsāti.
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="672" book="paci" bookNo="2" page="284">

<Paragraph number="2177" book="paci">
Dhammakamme dhammakammasaññā na paṭinissajjati, āpatti pārājikassa. Dhammakamme vematikā na paṭinissajjati, āpatti pārājikassa. Dhammakamme adhammakammasaññā na paṭinissajjati, āpatti pārājikassa.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2178" book="paci">
Adhammakamme dhammakammasaññā, āpatti dukkaṭassa. Adhammakamme vematikā, āpatti dukkaṭassa. Adhammakamme adhammakammasaññā, āpatti dukkaṭassa.
</Paragraph>

</Division>

</Paragraph>
</Division>
