<Division number="982" book="paci">

<Paragraph number="3002" book="paci">
Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena bhikkhuniyo āsandimpi pallaṅkampi paribhuñjanti. Manussā vihāracārikaṃ āhiṇḍantā passitvā ujjhāyanti khiyyanti vipācenti—  “kathañhi nāma bhikkhuniyo āsandimpi pallaṅkampi paribhuñjissanti, seyyathāpi gihiniyo kāmabhoginiyo”ti.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="3003" book="paci">
Assosuṃ kho bhikkhuniyo tesaṃ manussānaṃ ujjhāyantānaṃ khiyyantānaṃ vipācentānaṃ. Yā tā bhikkhuniyo appicchā …pe…  tā ujjhāyanti khiyyanti vipācenti—  “kathañhi nāma bhikkhuniyo āsandimpi pallaṅkampi paribhuñjissantī”ti …pe…  “saccaṃ kira, bhikkhave, bhikkhuniyo āsandimpi pallaṅkampi paribhuñjantī”ti? “Saccaṃ, bhagavā”ti. Vigarahi buddho bhagavā …pe…  kathañhi nāma, bhikkhave, bhikkhuniyo āsandimpi pallaṅkampi paribhuñjissanti. Netaṃ, bhikkhave, appasannānaṃ vā pasādāya …pe…  evañca pana, bhikkhave, bhikkhuniyo imaṃ sikkhāpadaṃ uddisantu—
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="983" book="paci">

<Paragraph number="3004" book="paci">
<Emphasis>“Yā pana bhikkhunī āsandiṃ vā pallaṅkaṃ vā paribhuñjeyya, pācittiyan”</Emphasis>ti. (*42*:<Emphasis>97</Emphasis>)
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="984" book="paci">

<Paragraph number="3005" book="paci">
<Emphasis>Yā panā</Emphasis>ti yā yādisā …pe…  <Emphasis>bhikkhunī</Emphasis>ti …pe…  ayaṃ imasmiṃ atthe adhippetā bhikkhunīti.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="3006" book="paci">
<Emphasis>Āsandī</Emphasis> nāma atikkantappamāṇā vuccati.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="3007" book="paci">
<Emphasis>Pallaṅko</Emphasis> nāma āharimehi vāḷehi kato hoti.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="3008" book="paci">
<Emphasis>Paribhuñjeyyā</Emphasis>ti tasmiṃ abhinisīdati vā abhinipajjati vā, āpatti pācittiyassa.
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="985" book="paci">

<Paragraph number="3009" book="paci">
Anāpatti—  āsandiyā pāde chinditvā paribhuñjati, pallaṅkassa vāḷe bhinditvā paribhuñjati, ummattikāya, ādikammikāyāti.

</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="3010" type="center" book="paci">
Dutiyasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
</Paragraph>
