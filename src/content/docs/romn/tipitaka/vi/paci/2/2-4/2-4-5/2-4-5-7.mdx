<Paragraph number="3051" book="paci">
Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena thullanandā bhikkhunī āvasathacīvaraṃ anissajjitvā paribhuñjati. Aññā utuniyo bhikkhuniyo na labhanti.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="3052" book="paci">
Yā tā bhikkhuniyo appicchā …pe…  tā ujjhāyanti khiyyanti vipācenti—  “kathañhi nāma ayyā thullanandā āvasathacīvaraṃ anissajjitvā paribhuñjissatī”ti …pe…  “saccaṃ kira, bhikkhave, thullanandā bhikkhunī āvasathacīvaraṃ anissajjitvā paribhuñjatī”ti? “Saccaṃ, bhagavā”ti. Vigarahi buddho bhagavā …pe…  kathañhi nāma, bhikkhave, thullanandā bhikkhunī āvasathacīvaraṃ anissajjitvā paribhuñjissati. Netaṃ, bhikkhave, appasannānaṃ vā pasādāya …pe…  evañca pana, bhikkhave, bhikkhuniyo imaṃ sikkhāpadaṃ uddisantu—
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="1004" book="paci" bookNo="2" page="399">

<Paragraph number="3053" book="paci">
<Emphasis>“Yā pana bhikkhunī āvasathacīvaraṃ anissajjitvā paribhuñjeyya, pācittiyan”</Emphasis>ti. (*47*:<Emphasis>102</Emphasis>)
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="1005" book="paci" bookNo="2" page="399">

<Paragraph number="3054" book="paci">
<Emphasis>Yā panā</Emphasis>ti yā yādisā …pe…  <Emphasis>bhikkhunī</Emphasis>ti …pe…  ayaṃ imasmiṃ atthe adhippetā bhikkhunīti.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="3055" book="paci">
<Emphasis>Āvasathacīvaraṃ</Emphasis> nāma “utuniyo bhikkhuniyo paribhuñjantū”ti dinnaṃ hoti.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="3056" book="paci">
<Emphasis>Anissajjitvā paribhuñjeyyā</Emphasis>ti dvetisso rattiyo paribhuñjitvā catutthadivase dhovitvā bhikkhuniyā vā sikkhamānāya vā sāmaṇeriyā vā anissajjitvā paribhuñjati, āpatti pācittiyassa.
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="1006" book="paci" bookNo="2" page="399">

<Paragraph number="3057" book="paci">
Anissajjite anissajjitasaññā paribhuñjati, āpatti pācittiyassa. Anissajjite vematikā paribhuñjati, āpatti pācittiyassa. Anissajjite nissajjitasaññā paribhuñjati, āpatti pācittiyassa.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="3058" book="paci">
Nissajjite anissajjitasaññā, āpatti dukkaṭassa. Nissajjite vematikā, āpatti dukkaṭassa. Nissajjite nissajjitasaññā anāpatti.
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="1007" book="paci" bookNo="2" page="399">

<Paragraph number="3059" book="paci">
Anāpatti—  nissajjitvā paribhuñjati, puna pariyāyena paribhuñjati, aññā utuniyo bhikkhuniyo na honti, acchinnacīvarikāya, naṭṭhacīvarikāya, āpadāsu, ummattikāya, ādikammikāyāti.

</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="3060" type="center" book="paci">
Sattamasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
</Paragraph>
