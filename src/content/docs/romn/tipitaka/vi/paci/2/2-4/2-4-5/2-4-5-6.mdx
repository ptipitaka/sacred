<Paragraph number="3039" book="paci">
Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena thullanandā bhikkhunī naṭānampi naṭakānampi laṅghakānampi sokajjhāyikānampi kumbhathūṇikānampi sahatthā khādanīyaṃ bhojanīyaṃ deti—  “mayhaṃ parisati vaṇṇaṃ bhāsathā”ti. Naṭāpi naṭakāpi laṅghakāpi sokajjhāyikāpi kumbhathūṇikāpi thullanandāya bhikkhuniyā parisati vaṇṇaṃ bhāsanti—  “ayyā thullanandā bahussutā bhāṇikā visāradā paṭṭā dhammiṃ kathaṃ kātuṃ; detha ayyāya, karotha ayyāyā”ti.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="3040" book="paci">
Yā tā bhikkhuniyo appicchā …pe…  tā ujjhāyanti khiyyanti vipācenti—  “kathañhi nāma ayyā thullanandā agārikassa sahatthā khādanīyaṃ bhojanīyaṃ dassatī”ti …pe…  “saccaṃ kira, bhikkhave, thullanandā bhikkhunī agārikassa sahatthā khādanīyaṃ bhojanīyaṃ detī”ti? “Saccaṃ, bhagavā”ti. Vigarahi buddho bhagavā …pe…  kathañhi nāma, bhikkhave, thullanandā bhikkhunī agārikassa sahatthā khādanīyaṃ bhojanīyaṃ dassati. Netaṃ, bhikkhave, appasannānaṃ vā pasādāya …pe…  evañca pana, bhikkhave, bhikkhuniyo imaṃ sikkhāpadaṃ uddisantu—
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="1000" book="paci" bookNo="2" page="398">

<Paragraph number="3041" book="paci">
<Emphasis>“Yā pana bhikkhunī agārikassa vā paribbājakassa vā paribbājikāya vā sahatthā khādanīyaṃ vā bhojanīyaṃ vā dadeyya, pācittiyan”</Emphasis>ti. (*46*:<Emphasis>101</Emphasis>)
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="1001" book="paci" bookNo="2" page="398">

<Paragraph number="3042" book="paci">
<Emphasis>Yā panā</Emphasis>ti yā yādisā …pe…  <Emphasis>bhikkhunī</Emphasis>ti …pe…  ayaṃ imasmiṃ atthe adhippetā bhikkhunīti.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="3043" book="paci">
<Emphasis>Agāriko</Emphasis> nāma yo koci agāraṃ ajjhāvasati.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="3044" book="paci">
<Emphasis>Paribbājako</Emphasis> nāma bhikkhuñca sāmaṇerañca ṭhapetvā yo koci paribbājakasamāpanno.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="3045" book="paci">
<Emphasis>Paribbājikā</Emphasis> nāma bhikkhuniñca sikkhamānañca sāmaṇeriñca ṭhapetvā yā kāci paribbājikasamāpannā.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="3046" book="paci">
<Emphasis>Khādanīyaṃ</Emphasis> nāma pañca bhojanāni—  udakadantaponaṃ ṭhapetvā avasesaṃ khādanīyaṃ nāma.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="3047" book="paci">
<Emphasis>Bhojanīyaṃ</Emphasis> nāma pañca bhojanāni—  odano, kummāso, sattu, maccho, maṃsaṃ.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="3048" book="paci">
<Emphasis>Dadeyyā</Emphasis>ti kāyena vā kāyapaṭibaddhena vā nissaggiyena vā deti, āpatti pācittiyassa. Udakadantaponaṃ deti, āpatti dukkaṭassa.
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="1002" book="paci" bookNo="2" page="398">

<Paragraph number="3049" book="paci">
Anāpatti—  dāpeti na deti, upanikkhipitvā deti, bāhirālepaṃ deti, ummattikāya, ādikammikāyāti.

</Paragraph>
</Division>

Chaṭṭhasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
</Paragraph>
