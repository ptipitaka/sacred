<Paragraph number="3509" book="paci">
Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena bhikkhuniyo anokāsakataṃ bhikkhuṃ pañhaṃ pucchanti. Bhikkhū ujjhāyanti khiyyanti vipācenti—  “kathañhi nāma bhikkhuniyo anokāsakataṃ bhikkhuṃ pañhaṃ pucchissantī”ti …pe…  “saccaṃ kira, bhikkhave, bhikkhuniyo anokāsakataṃ bhikkhuṃ pañhaṃ pucchantī”ti? “Saccaṃ, bhagavā”ti. Vigarahi buddho bhagavā …pe…  kathañhi nāma, bhikkhave, bhikkhuniyo anokāsakataṃ bhikkhuṃ pañhaṃ pucchissanti. Netaṃ, bhikkhave, appasannānaṃ vā pasādāya …pe…  evañca pana, bhikkhave, bhikkhuniyo imaṃ sikkhāpadaṃ uddisantu—
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="1220" book="paci">

<Paragraph number="3510" book="paci">
<Emphasis>“Yā pana bhikkhunī anokāsakataṃ bhikkhuṃ pañhaṃ puccheyya, pācittiyan”</Emphasis>ti. (*95*:<Emphasis>150</Emphasis>)
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="1221" book="paci">

<Paragraph number="3511" book="paci">
<Emphasis>Yā panā</Emphasis>ti yā yādisā …pe…  <Emphasis>bhikkhunī</Emphasis>ti …pe…  ayaṃ imasmiṃ atthe adhippetā bhikkhunīti.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="3512" book="paci">
<Emphasis>Anokāsakatan</Emphasis>ti anāpucchā. <Emphasis>Bhikkhun</Emphasis>ti upasampannaṃ. <Emphasis>Pañhaṃ puccheyyā</Emphasis>ti suttante okāsaṃ kārāpetvā vinayaṃ vā abhidhammaṃ vā pucchati, āpatti pācittiyassa. Vinaye okāsaṃ kārāpetvā suttantaṃ vā abhidhammaṃ vā pucchati, āpatti pācittiyassa. Abhidhamme okāsaṃ kārāpetvā suttantaṃ vā vinayaṃ vā pucchati, āpatti pācittiyassa.
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="1222" book="paci">

<Paragraph number="3513" book="paci">
Anāpucchite anāpucchitasaññā pañhaṃ pucchati, āpatti pācittiyassa. Anāpucchite vematikā pañhaṃ pucchati, āpatti pācittiyassa. Anāpucchite āpucchitasaññā pañhaṃ pucchati, āpatti pācittiyassa.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="3514" book="paci">
Āpucchite anāpucchitasaññā, āpatti dukkaṭassa. Āpucchite vematikā, āpatti dukkaṭassa. Āpucchite āpucchitasaññā, anāpatti.
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="1223" book="paci">

<Paragraph number="3515" book="paci">
Anāpatti—  okāsaṃ kārāpetvā pucchati, anodissa okāsaṃ kārāpetvā yattha katthaci pucchati, ummattikāya, ādikammikāyāti.

</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="3516" type="center" book="paci">
Dvādasamasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
</Paragraph>
