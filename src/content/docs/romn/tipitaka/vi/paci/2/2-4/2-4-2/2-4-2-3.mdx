<Paragraph number="2695" book="paci">
Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena bhaddāya kāpilāniyā antevāsiniyā bhikkhuniyā ñātako puriso gāmakā sāvatthiṃ agamāsi kenacideva karaṇīyena. Atha kho sā bhikkhunī—  “bhagavatā paṭikkhittaṃ paṭicchanne okāse purisena saddhiṃ ekenekā santiṭṭhituṃ sallapitun”ti teneva purisena saddhiṃ ajjhokāse ekenekā santiṭṭhatipi sallapatipi.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2696" book="paci">
Yā tā bhikkhuniyo appicchā …pe…  tā ujjhāyanti khiyyanti vipācenti—  “kathañhi nāma bhikkhunī ajjhokāse purisena saddhiṃ ekenekā santiṭṭhissatipi sallapissatipī”ti …pe…  “saccaṃ kira, bhikkhave, bhikkhunī ajjhokāse purisena saddhiṃ ekenekā santiṭṭhatipi sallapatipī”ti? “Saccaṃ, bhagavā”ti. Vigarahi buddho bhagavā …pe…  kathañhi nāma, bhikkhave, bhikkhunī ajjhokāse purisena saddhiṃ ekenekā santiṭṭhissatipi sallapissatipi. Netaṃ, bhikkhave, appasannānaṃ vā pasādāya …pe…  evañca pana, bhikkhave, bhikkhuniyo imaṃ sikkhāpadaṃ uddisantu—
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="847" book="paci" e="ch" v="2" p="354">

<Paragraph number="2697" book="paci">
<Emphasis>“Yā pana bhikkhunī ajjhokāse purisena saddhiṃ ekenekā santiṭṭheyya vā sallapeyya vā, pācittiyan”</Emphasis>ti. (*13*:<Emphasis>68</Emphasis>)
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="848" book="paci" e="ch" v="2" p="354">

<Paragraph number="2698" book="paci">
<Emphasis>Yā panā</Emphasis>ti yā yādisā …pe…  <Emphasis>bhikkhunī</Emphasis>ti …pe…  ayaṃ imasmiṃ atthe adhippetā bhikkhunīti.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2699" book="paci">
<Emphasis>Ajjhokāso</Emphasis> nāma appaṭicchanno hoti kuṭṭena vā kavāṭena vā kilañjena vā sāṇipākārena vā rukkhena vā thambhena vā kotthaḷiyā vā, yena kenaci appaṭicchanno hoti.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2700" book="paci">
<Emphasis>Puriso</Emphasis> nāma manussapuriso, na yakkho na peto na tiracchānagato, viññū paṭibalo santiṭṭhituṃ sallapituṃ.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2701" book="paci">
<Emphasis>Saddhin</Emphasis>ti ekato. <Emphasis>Ekenekā</Emphasis>ti puriso ceva hoti bhikkhunī ca.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2702" book="paci">
<Emphasis>Santiṭṭheyya vā</Emphasis>ti purisassa hatthapāse tiṭṭhati, āpatti pācittiyassa.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2703" book="paci">
<Emphasis>Sallapeyya vā</Emphasis>ti purisassa hatthapāse ṭhitā sallapati, āpatti pācittiyassa.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2704" book="paci">
Hatthapāsaṃ vijahitvā santiṭṭhati vā sallapati vā, āpatti dukkaṭassa. Yakkhena vā petena vā paṇḍakena vā tiracchānagatamanussaviggahena vā saddhiṃ santiṭṭhati vā sallapati vā, āpatti dukkaṭassa.
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="849" book="paci" e="ch" v="2" p="355">

<Paragraph number="2705" book="paci">
Anāpatti—  yo koci viññū dutiyo hoti, arahopekkhā, aññavihitā santiṭṭhati vā sallapati vā, ummattikāya, ādikammikāyāti.

</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="2706" type="center" book="paci">
Tatiyasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
</Paragraph>
