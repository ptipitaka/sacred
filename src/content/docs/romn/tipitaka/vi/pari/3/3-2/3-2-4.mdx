<Paragraph number="1111" type="verses" book="pari">
_Bhedānuvattadubbaca-_  
_dūsaduṭṭhulladiṭṭhi ca;_  
_Chandaṃ ujjagghikā dve ca,_  
_dve ca saddā na byāhare._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1112" type="verses" book="pari">
_Chamā nīcāsane ṭhānaṃ,_  
_pacchato uppathena ca;_  
_Vajjānuvattigahaṇā,_  
_osāre paccācikkhanā._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1113" type="verses" book="pari">
_Kismiṃ saṃsaṭṭhā dve vadhi,_  
_Visibbe dukkhitāya ca;_  
_Puna saṃsaṭṭhā na vūpasame,_  
_Ārāmañca pavāraṇā._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1114" type="verses" book="pari">
_Anvaddhaṃ saha jīviniṃ dve,_  
_Cīvaraṃ anubandhanā;_  
_Sattatiṃsa ime dhammā,_  
_Kāyavācāya cittato;_  
_Sabbe ekasamuṭṭhānā,_  
_Samanubhāsanā yathā._  


</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="1115" type="center" book="pari">
Samanubhāsanāsamuṭṭhānaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
</Paragraph>
