<Paragraph number="1142" type="verses" book="pari">
_Bhūtaṃ kāyena jāyati,_  
_na vācā na ca cittato;_  
_Vācato ca samuṭṭhāti,_  
_na kāyā na ca cittato._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1143" type="verses" book="pari">
_Kāyavācāya jāyati,_  
_na tu jāyati cittato;_  
_Bhūtārocanakā nāma,_  
_tīhi ṭhānehi jāyati._  


</Paragraph>
</Division>
