<Paragraph number="1147" type="verses" book="pari">
_Ananuññātaṃ vācāya,_  
_na kāyā na ca cittato;_  
_Jāyati kāyavācāya,_  
_na taṃ jāyati cittato._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1148" type="verses" book="pari">
_Jāyati vācācittena,_  
_na taṃ jāyati kāyato;_  
_Jāyati tīhi dvārehi,_  
_akataṃ catuṭhānikaṃ._  


</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="1149" type="center" book="pari">
Ananuññātasamuṭṭhānaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.


</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1150" type="verses" book="pari">
_Samuṭṭhānañhi saṅkhepaṃ,_  
_dasa tīṇi sudesitaṃ;_  
_Asammohakaraṃ ṭhānaṃ,_  
_nettidhammānulomikaṃ;_  
_Dhārayanto imaṃ viññū,_  
_samuṭṭhāne na muyhatīti._  


</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1151" type="center" book="pari">
Samuṭṭhānasīsasaṅkhepo niṭṭhito.
</Paragraph>
