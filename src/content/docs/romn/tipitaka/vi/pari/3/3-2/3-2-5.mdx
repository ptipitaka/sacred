<Paragraph number="1116" type="verses" book="pari">
_Ubbhataṃ kathinaṃ tīṇi,_  
_paṭhamaṃ pattabhesajjaṃ;_  
_Accekaṃ cāpi sāsaṅkaṃ,_  
_pakkamantena vā duve._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1117" type="verses" book="pari">
_Upassayaṃ paramparā,_  
_anatirittaṃ nimantanā;_  
_Vikappaṃ rañño vikāle,_  
_vosāsāraññakena ca._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1118" type="verses" book="pari">
_Ussayāsannicayañca,_  
_pure pacchā vikāle ca;_  
_Pañcāhikā saṅkamanī,_  
_dvepi āvasathena ca._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1119" type="verses" book="pari">
_Pasākhe āsane ceva,_  
_tiṃsa ekūnakā ime;_  
_Kāyavācā na ca cittā,_  
_tīhi dvārehi jāyare;_  
_Dvisamuṭṭhānikā sabbe,_  
_kathinena sahā samā._  


</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="1120" type="center" book="pari">
Kathinasamuṭṭhānaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
</Paragraph>
