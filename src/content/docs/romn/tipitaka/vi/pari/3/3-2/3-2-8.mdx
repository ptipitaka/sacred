<Paragraph number="1131" type="verses" book="pari">
_Addhānanāvaṃ paṇītaṃ,_  
_mātugāmena saṃhare;_  
_Dhaññaṃ nimantitā ceva,_  
_aṭṭha ca pāṭidesanī._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1132" type="verses" book="pari">
_Sikkhā pannarasa ete,_  
_kāyā na vācā na manā;_  
_Kāyavācāhi jāyanti,_  
_na te cittena jāyare._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1133" type="verses" book="pari">
_Kāyacittena jāyanti,_  
_na te jāyanti vācato;_  
_Kāyavācāhi cittena,_  
_samuṭṭhānā catubbidhā;_  
_Paññattā buddhañāṇena,_  
_addhānena sahā samā._  


</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="1134" type="center" book="pari">
Addhānasamuṭṭhānaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
</Paragraph>
