<Paragraph number="1121" type="verses" book="pari">
_Eḷakalomā dve seyyā,_  
_āhacca piṇḍabhojanaṃ;_  
_Gaṇavikālasannidhi,_  
_dantaponena celakā._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1122" type="verses" book="pari">
_Uyyuttaṃ senaṃ uyyodhi,_  
_surā orena nhāyanā;_  
_Dubbaṇṇe dve desanikā,_  
_lasuṇupatiṭṭhe naccanā._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1123" type="verses" book="pari">
_Nhānamattharaṇaṃ seyyā,_  
_antoraṭṭhe tathā bahi;_  
_Antovassaṃ cittāgāraṃ,_  
_āsandi suttakantanā._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1124" type="verses" book="pari">
_Veyyāvaccaṃ sahatthā ca,_  
_abhikkhukāvāsena ca;_  
_Chattaṃ yānañca saṅghāṇiṃ,_  
_alaṅkāraṃ gandhavāsitaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1125" type="verses" book="pari">
_Bhikkhunī sikkhamānā ca,_  
_sāmaṇerī gihiniyā;_  
_Asaṅkaccikā āpatti,_  
_cattārīsā catuttari._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1126" type="verses" book="pari">
_Kāyena na vācācittena,_  
_kāyacittena na vācato;_  
_Dvisamuṭṭhānikā sabbe,_  
_samā eḷakalomikāti._  


</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="1127" type="center" book="pari">
Eḷakalomasamuṭṭhānaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
</Paragraph>
