<Paragraph number="1088" type="verses" book="pari">
_Methunaṃ sukkasaṃsaggo,_  
_aniyatā paṭhamikā;_  
_Pubbūpaparipācitā,_  
_raho bhikkhuniyā saha._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1089" type="verses" book="pari">
_Sabhojane raho dve ca,_  
_aṅguli udake hasaṃ;_  
_Pahāre uggire ceva,_  
_tepaññāsā ca sekhiyā._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1090" type="verses" book="pari">
_Adhakkhagāmāvassutā,_  
_talamaṭṭhañca suddhikā;_  
_Vassaṃvuṭṭhā ca ovādaṃ,_  
_nānubandhe pavattiniṃ._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1091" type="verses" book="pari">
_Chasattati ime sikkhā,_  
_kāyamānasikā katā;_  
_Sabbe ekasamuṭṭhānā,_  
_paṭhamaṃ pārājikaṃ yathā._  


</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="1092" type="center" book="pari">
Paṭhamapārājikasamuṭṭhānaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
</Paragraph>
