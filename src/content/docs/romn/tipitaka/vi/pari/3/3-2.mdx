<Paragraph number="1084" type="verses" book="pari">
_Vibhaṅge dvīsu paññattaṃ,_  
_uddisanti uposathe;_  
_Pavakkhāmi samuṭṭhānaṃ,_  
_yathāñāyaṃ suṇātha me._  



</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1085" type="verses" book="pari">
_Pārājikaṃ yaṃ paṭhamaṃ,_  
_dutiyañca tato paraṃ;_  
_Sañcarittānubhāsanañca,_  
_atirekañca cīvaraṃ._  



</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1086" type="verses" book="pari">
_Lomāni padasodhammo,_  
_bhūtaṃ saṃvidhānena ca;_  
_Theyyadesanacorī ca,_  
_ananuññātāya terasa._  



</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1087" type="verses" book="pari">
_Terasete samuṭṭhāna-_  
_nayā viññūhi cintitā;_  
_Ekekasmiṃ samuṭṭhāne,_  
_sadisā idha dissare._  


</Paragraph>
