<Paragraph number="1138" type="verses" book="pari">
_Chattapāṇissa saddhammaṃ,_  
_na desenti tathāgatā;_  
_Evameva daṇḍapāṇissa,_  
_satthaāvudhapāṇinaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1139" type="verses" book="pari">
_Pādukupāhanā yānaṃ,_  
_seyyapallatthikāya ca;_  
_Veṭhitoguṇṭhito ceva,_  
_ekādasamanūnakā._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="1140" type="verses" book="pari">
_Vācācittena jāyanti,_  
_na te jāyanti kāyato;_  
_Sabbe ekasamuṭṭhānā,_  
_samakā dhammadesane._  


</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="1141" type="center" book="pari">
Dhammadesanāsamuṭṭhānaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.
