<Paragraph number="1857" book="pari">
<Emphasis>Kathaṃ saññāpanīye ṭhāne saññāpeti</Emphasis>? Adhammaṃ adhammoti dīpento saññāpanīye ṭhāne saññāpeti, dhammaṃ dhammoti dīpento saññāpanīye ṭhāne saññāpeti …pe…  duṭṭhullaṃ āpattiṃ duṭṭhullā āpattīti dīpento saññāpanīye ṭhāne saññāpeti, aduṭṭhullaṃ āpattiṃ aduṭṭhullā āpattīti dīpento saññāpanīye ṭhāne saññāpeti. Evaṃ saññāpanīye ṭhāne saññāpeti.
</Paragraph>

</Division>
