<Paragraph number="140" type="verses" book="para">
_Makkaṭī vajjiputtā ca,_  
_gihī naggo ca titthiyā;_  
_Dārikuppalavaṇṇā ca,_  
_byañjanehipare duve._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="141" type="verses" book="para">
_Mātā dhītā bhaginī ca,_  
_jāyā ca mudu lambinā;_  
_Dve vaṇā lepacittañca,_  
_dārudhītalikāya ca._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="142" type="verses" book="para">
_Sundarena saha pañca,_  
_pañca sivathikaṭṭhikā;_  
_Nāgī yakkhī ca petī ca,_  
_paṇḍakopahato chupe._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="143" type="verses" book="para">
_Bhaddiye arahaṃ sutto,_  
_sāvatthiyā caturo pare;_  
_Vesāliyā tayo mālā,_  
_supine bhārukacchako._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="144" type="verses" book="para">
_Supabbā saddhā bhikkhunī,_  
_Sikkhamānā sāmaṇerī ca;_  
_Vesiyā paṇḍako gihī,_  
_Aññamaññaṃ vuḍḍhapabbajito migoti._
</Paragraph>
