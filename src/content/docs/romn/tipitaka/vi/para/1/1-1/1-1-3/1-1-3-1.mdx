<Paragraph number="451" type="verses" book="para">
_Saṃvaṇṇanā nisīdanto,_  
_musalodukkhalena ca;_  
_Vuḍḍhapabbajitābhisanno,_  
_aggavīmaṃsanāvisaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="452" type="verses" book="para">
_Tayo ca vatthukammehi,_  
_iṭṭhakāhipare tayo;_  
_Vāsī gopānasī ceva,_  
_aṭṭakotaraṇaṃ pati._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="453" type="verses" book="para">
_Sedaṃ natthuñca sambāho,_  
_nhāpanabbhañjanena ca;_  
_Uṭṭhāpento nipātento,_  
_annapānena māraṇaṃ._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="454" type="verses" book="para">
_Jāragabbho sapattī ca,_  
_mātā puttaṃ ubho vadhi;_  
_Ubho na miyyare maddā,_  
_tāpaṃ vañjhā vijāyinī._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="455" type="verses" book="para">
_Patodaṃ niggahe yakkho,_  
_vāḷayakkhañca pāhiṇi;_  
_Taṃ maññamāno pahari,_  
_saggañca nirayaṃ bhaṇe._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="456" type="verses" book="para">
_Āḷaviyā tayo rukkhā,_  
_dāyehi apare tayo;_  
_Mā kilamesi na tuyhaṃ,_  
_takkaṃ sovīrakena cāti._
</Paragraph>
