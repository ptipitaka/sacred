<Paragraph number="2121" book="para">
Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū—  “bhagavatā paṭikkhittaṃ suddhakāḷakānaṃ eḷakalomānaṃ santhataṃ kārāpetun”ti, te thokaññeva odātaṃ ante ādiyitvā tatheva suddhakāḷakānaṃ eḷakalomānaṃ santhataṃ kārāpenti. Ye te bhikkhū appicchā …pe…  te ujjhāyanti khiyyanti vipācenti—  “kathañhi nāma chabbaggiyā bhikkhū thokaññeva odātaṃ ante ādiyitvā tatheva suddhakāḷakānaṃ eḷakalomānaṃ santhataṃ kārāpessantī”ti.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2122" book="para">
Atha kho te bhikkhū chabbaggiye bhikkhū anekapariyāyena vigarahitvā bhagavato etamatthaṃ ārocesuṃ …pe…  “saccaṃ kira tumhe, bhikkhave, thokaññeva odātaṃ ante ādiyitvā tatheva suddhakāḷakānaṃ eḷakalomānaṃ santhataṃ kārāpethā”ti? “Saccaṃ, bhagavā”ti. Vigarahi buddho bhagavā …pe…  kathañhi nāma tumhe, moghapurisā, thokaññeva odātaṃ ante ādiyitvā tatheva suddhakāḷakānaṃ eḷakalomānaṃ santhataṃ kārāpessatha. Netaṃ, moghapurisā, appasannānaṃ vā pasādāya …pe…  evañca pana, bhikkhave, imaṃ sikkhāpadaṃ uddiseyyātha—
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="553" book="para" e="ch" v="1" p="332">

<Paragraph number="2123" book="para">
<Emphasis>“Navaṃ pana bhikkhunā santhataṃ kārayamānena dve bhāgā suddhakāḷakānaṃ eḷakalomānaṃ ādātabbā tatiyaṃ odātānaṃ catutthaṃ gocariyānaṃ. Anādā ce bhikkhu dve bhāge suddhakāḷakānaṃ eḷakalomānaṃ tatiyaṃ odātānaṃ catutthaṃ gocariyānaṃ navaṃ santhataṃ kārāpeyya, nissaggiyaṃ pācittiyan”</Emphasis>ti. (*13*:<Emphasis>32</Emphasis>)
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="554" book="para" e="ch" v="1" p="332">

<Paragraph number="2124" book="para">
<Emphasis>Navaṃ</Emphasis> nāma karaṇaṃ upādāya vuccati.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2125" book="para">
<Emphasis>Santhataṃ</Emphasis> nāma santharitvā kataṃ hoti avāyimaṃ.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2126" book="para">
<Emphasis>Kārayamānenā</Emphasis>ti karonto vā kārāpento vā.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2127" book="para">
<Emphasis>Dve bhāgā suddhakāḷakānaṃ eḷakalomānaṃ ādātabbā</Emphasis>ti dhārayitvā dve tulā ādātabbā.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2128" book="para">
<Emphasis>Tatiyaṃ odātānan</Emphasis>ti tulaṃ odātānaṃ.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2129" book="para">
<Emphasis>Catutthaṃ gocariyānan</Emphasis>ti tulaṃ gocariyānaṃ.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2130" book="para">
<Emphasis>Anādā ce bhikkhu dve bhāge suddhakāḷakānaṃ eḷakalomānaṃ tatiyaṃ odātānaṃ catutthaṃ gocariyānan</Emphasis>ti. Anādiyitvā dve tule suddhakāḷakānaṃ eḷakalomānaṃ tulaṃ odātānaṃ tulaṃ gocariyānaṃ navaṃ santhataṃ karoti vā kārāpeti vā payoge dukkaṭaṃ, paṭilābhena nissaggiyaṃ hoti. Nissajjitabbaṃ saṃghassa vā gaṇassa vā puggalassa vā. Evañca pana, bhikkhave, nissajjitabbaṃ …pe…  “idaṃ me, bhante, santhataṃ anādiyitvā dve tule suddhakāḷakānaṃ eḷakalomānaṃ tulaṃ odātānaṃ tulaṃ gocariyānaṃ kārāpitaṃ nissaggiyaṃ. Imāhaṃ saṃghassa nissajjāmī”ti …pe…  dadeyyāti …pe…  dadeyyunti …pe…  āyasmato dammīti.
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="555" book="para" e="ch" v="1" p="332">

<Paragraph number="2131" book="para">
Attanā vippakataṃ attanā pariyosāpeti, nissaggiyaṃ pācittiyaṃ. Attanā vippakataṃ parehi pariyosāpeti, nissaggiyaṃ pācittiyaṃ. Parehi vippakataṃ attanā pariyosāpeti, nissaggiyaṃ pācittiyaṃ. Parehi vippakataṃ parehi pariyosāpeti, nissaggiyaṃ pācittiyaṃ.
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2132" book="para">
Aññassatthāya karoti vā kārāpeti vā, āpatti dukkaṭassa. Aññena kataṃ paṭilabhitvā paribhuñjati, āpatti dukkaṭassa.
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="556" book="para" e="ch" v="1" p="333">

<Paragraph number="2133" book="para">
Anāpatti—  tulaṃ odātānaṃ tulaṃ gocariyānaṃ ādiyitvā karoti, bahutaraṃ odātānaṃ bahutaraṃ gocariyānaṃ ādiyitvā karoti, suddhaṃ odātānaṃ suddhaṃ gocariyānaṃ ādiyitvā karoti, vitānaṃ vā bhūmattharaṇaṃ vā sāṇipākāraṃ vā bhisiṃ vā bibbohanaṃ vā karoti, ummattakassa, ādikammikassāti.

</Paragraph>
</Division>

<Paragraph number="2134" type="center" book="para">
Dvebhāgasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ tatiyaṃ.
</Paragraph>
