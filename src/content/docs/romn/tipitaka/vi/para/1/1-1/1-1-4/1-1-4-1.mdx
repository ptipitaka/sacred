<Paragraph number="705" type="verses" book="para">
_Adhimāne araññamhi,_  
_piṇḍopajjhāriyāpatho;_  
_Saṃyojanā rahodhammā,_  
_vihāro paccupaṭṭhito._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="706" type="verses" book="para">
_Na dukkaraṃ viriyamathopi maccuno,_  
_Bhāyāvuso vippaṭisāri sammā;_  
_Viriyena yogena ārādhanāya,_  
_Atha vedanāya adhivāsanā duve._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="707" type="verses" book="para">
_Brāhmaṇe pañca vatthūni,_  
_aññaṃ byākaraṇā tayo;_  
_Agārāvaraṇā kāmā,_  
_rati cāpi apakkami._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="708" type="verses" book="para">
_Aṭṭhi pesi ubho gāvaghātakā,_  
_Piṇḍo sākuṇiko nicchavi orabbhi;_  
_Asi ca sūkariko satti māgavi,_  
_Usu ca kāraṇiko sūci sārathi._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="709" type="verses" book="para">
_Yo ca sibbīyati sūcako hi so,_  
_Aṇḍabhāri ahu gāmakūṭako;_  
_Kūpe nimuggo hi so pāradāriko,_  
_Gūthakhādī ahu duṭṭhabrāhmaṇo._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="710" type="verses" book="para">
_Nicchavitthī aticārinī ahu,_  
_Maṅgulitthī ahu ikkhaṇitthikā;_  
_Okilinī hi sapattaṅgārokiri,_  
_Sīsacchinno ahu coraghātako._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="711" type="verses" book="para">
_Bhikkhu bhikkhunī sikkhamānā,_  
_Sāmaṇero atha sāmaṇerikā;_  
_Kassapassa vinayasmiṃ pabbajaṃ,_  
_Pāpakammamakariṃsu tāvade._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="712" type="verses" book="para">
_Tapodā rājagahe yuddhaṃ,_  
_nāgānogāhanena ca;_  
_Sobhito arahaṃ bhikkhu,_  
_pañcakappasataṃ sareti._
</Paragraph>
