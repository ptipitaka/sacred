_သီဟာသနမကာသဟံ;_  
_သီဟာသနံ ကရိတွာန,_  
_ပါဒပီဌမကာသဟံ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="3" book="ap1" e="ch" v="20" p="60">

<Paragraph number="698" type="verses" book="ap1">

_ဃရံ တတ္ထ အကာသဟံ;_  
_တေန စိတ္တပ္ပသာဒေန,_  
_တုသိတံ ဥပပဇ္ဇဟံ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="4" book="ap1" e="ch" v="20" p="60">

_ယောဇနံ အာသိ တာဝဒေ;_  
_ဝိမာနံ သုကတံ မယှံ,_  
_ဝိတ္ထာရေန စတုဒ္ဒသ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="5" book="ap1" e="ch" v="20" p="60">

<Paragraph number="700" type="verses" book="ap1">
_သတံ ကညာသဟဿာနိ,_  
_ပရိဝါရေန္တိ မံ သဒါ;_  
_သောဏ္ဏမယဉ္စ ပလ္လင်္ကံ,_  
_ဗျမှေ အာသိ သုနိမ္မိတံ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="6" book="ap1" e="ch" v="20" p="60">

<Paragraph number="701" type="verses" book="ap1">
_ဟတ္ထိယာနံ အဿယာနံ,_  
_ဒိဗ္ဗယာနံ ဥပဋ္ဌိတံ;_  
_ပါသာဒါ သိဝိကာ စေဝ,_  
_နိဗ္ဗတ္တန္တိ ယဒိစ္ဆကံ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="7" book="ap1" e="ch" v="20" p="60">

<Paragraph number="702" type="verses" book="ap1">

_နိဗ္ဗတ္တန္တိ မမံ သဗ္ဗေ,_  
_သီဟာသနဿိဒံ ဖလံ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="8" book="ap1" e="ch" v="20" p="60">

<Paragraph number="703" type="verses" book="ap1">

_ပါဒုကာ အဘိရူဟာမိ,_  
_ပါဒပီဌဿိဒံ ဖလံ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="9" book="ap1" e="ch" v="20" p="60">

_ဒုဂ္ဂတိံ နာဘိဇာနာမိ,_  
_ပုညကမ္မဿိဒံ ဖလံ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="10" book="ap1" e="ch" v="20" p="60">

_ဒွေသတ္တတိမှိတော ကပ္ပေ,_  
_တယော သုမနနာမကာ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="11" book="ap1" e="ch" v="20" p="60">

_သတ္တရတနသမ္ပန္နာ,_  
_စတုဒီပမှိ ဣဿရာ._  

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="12" book="ap1" e="ch" v="20" p="61">

<Paragraph number="707" type="verses" book="ap1">

_ဝိမောက္ခာပိ စ အဋ္ဌိမေ;_  
_ဆဠဘိညာ သစ္ဆိကတာ,_  
_ကတံ ဗုဒ္ဓဿ သာသနံ”._  

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="708" book="ap1">

</Paragraph>
</Division>
