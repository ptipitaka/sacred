_ယံ ဖလမဒဒိံ တဒါ;_  
_ဒုဂ္ဂတိံ နာဘိဇာနာမိ,_

</Paragraph>

</Paragraph>

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="5735" book="ap1">

</Paragraph>
