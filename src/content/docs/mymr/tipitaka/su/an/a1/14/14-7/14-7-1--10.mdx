<Paragraph number="347" book="a1">
“ဧတဒဂ္ဂံ, ဘိက္ခဝေ, မမ သာဝိကာနံ ဥပါသိကာနံ ပဌမံ သရဏံ ဂစ္ဆန္တီနံ ယဒိဒံ သုဇာတာ သေနိယဓီတာ. (1)
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="259" book="a1">

<Paragraph number="348" book="a1">

</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="260" book="a1">

<Paragraph number="349" book="a1">
…  ဗဟုဿုတာနံ ယဒိဒံ ခုဇ္ဇုတ္တရာ. (3)
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="261" book="a1">

<Paragraph number="350" book="a1">
…  မေတ္တာဝိဟာရီနံ ယဒိဒံ သာမာဝတီ. (4)
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="262" book="a1">

<Paragraph number="351" book="a1">
…  ဈာယီနံ ယဒိဒံ ဥတ္တရာနန္ဒမာတာ. (5)
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="263" book="a1">

<Paragraph number="352" book="a1">
…  ပဏီတဒါယိကာနံ ယဒိဒံ သုပ္ပဝါသာ ကောလိယဓီတာ. (6)
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="264" book="a1">

<Paragraph number="353" book="a1">
…  ဂိလာနုပဋ္ဌာကီနံ ယဒိဒံ သုပ္ပိယာ ဥပါသိကာ. (7)
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="265" book="a1">

<Paragraph number="354" book="a1">
…  အဝေစ္စပ္ပသန္နာနံ ယဒိဒံ ကာတိယာနီ. (8)
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="266" book="a1">

<Paragraph number="355" book="a1">
…  ဝိဿာသိကာနံ ယဒိဒံ နကုလမာတာ ဂဟပတာနီ. (9)
</Paragraph>

</Division>
<Division number="267" book="a1">

<Paragraph number="356" book="a1">
…  အနုဿဝပ္ပသန္နာနံ ယဒိဒံ ကာဠီ ဥပါသိကာ ကုရရဃရိကာ”တိ. (10)

</Paragraph>
</Division>
