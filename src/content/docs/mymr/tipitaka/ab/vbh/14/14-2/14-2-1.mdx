</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2092" book="vbh">
အသံကိလိဋ္ဌသံကိလေသိကာ. (5)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2093" book="vbh">
သဝိတက္ကသဝိစာရာ. (6)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2094" book="vbh">

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2095" book="vbh">

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2096" book="vbh">

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2097" book="vbh">
အာစယဂါမိနော. (10)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2098" book="vbh">
နေဝသေက္ခနာသေက္ခာ. (11)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2099" book="vbh">
ပရိတ္တာ. (12)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2100" book="vbh">

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2101" book="vbh">

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2102" book="vbh">
အနိယတာ. (15)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2103" book="vbh">

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2104" book="vbh">

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2105" book="vbh">

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2106" book="vbh">

<Paragraph number="2107" book="vbh">

</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2108" book="vbh">
ဗဟိဒ္ဓါရမ္မဏာ. (21)
</Paragraph>

<Paragraph number="2109" book="vbh">
အနိဒဿနအပ္ပဋိဃာ. (22)
